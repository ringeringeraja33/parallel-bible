# Psalms 120

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | In my distress I cried unto the LORD, and he heard me. |
| 中文 | （上行（或作登阶下同）之诗我在急难中求告耶和华，他就应允我。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Deliver my soul, O LORD, from lying lips, and from a deceitful tongue. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你救我脱离说谎的嘴唇，和诡诈的舌头。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | What shall be given unto thee? or what shall be done unto thee, thou false tongue? |
| 中文 | 诡诈的舌头阿，要给你什么呢。要拿什么加给你呢。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Sharp arrows of the mighty, with coals of juniper. |
| 中文 | 就是勇士的利箭，和罗腾木（小树名松类）的炭火。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Woe is me, that I sojourn in Mesech, that I dwell in the tents of Kedar! |
| 中文 | 我寄居在米设，住在基达帐棚之中，有祸了。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My soul hath long dwelt with him that hateth peace. |
| 中文 | 我与那恨恶和睦的人，许久同住。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I am for peace: but when I speak, they are for war. |
| 中文 | 我愿和睦。但我发言，他们就要争战。 |
