# Psalms 16

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Preserve me, O God: for in thee do I put my trust. |
| 中文 | 神阿，求你保佑我，因为我投靠你。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O my soul, thou hast said unto the LORD, Thou art my Lord: my goodness extendeth not to thee; |
| 中文 | 我的心哪，你曾对耶和华说，你是我的主。我的好处不在你以外。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But to the saints that are in the earth, and to the excellent, in whom is all my delight. |
| 中文 | 论到世上的圣民，他们又美又善，是我最喜悦的。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Their sorrows shall be multiplied that hasten after another god: their drink offerings of blood will I not offer, nor take up their names into my lips. |
| 中文 | 以别神代替耶和华的，他们的愁苦必加增。他们所浇奠的血我不献上，我嘴唇也不题别神的名号。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD is the portion of mine inheritance and of my cup: thou maintainest my lot. |
| 中文 | 耶和华是我的产业，是我杯中的分。我所得的你为我持守。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The lines are fallen unto me in pleasant places; yea, I have a goodly heritage. |
| 中文 | 用绳量给我的地界，坐落在佳美之处。我的产业实在美好。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will bless the LORD, who hath given me counsel: my reins also instruct me in the night seasons. |
| 中文 | 我必称颂那指教我的耶和华。我的心肠在夜间也警戒我。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I have set the LORD always before me: because he is at my right hand, I shall not be moved. |
| 中文 | 我将耶和华常摆在我面前。因他在我右边，我便不至摇动。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Therefore my heart is glad, and my glory rejoiceth: my flesh also shall rest in hope. |
| 中文 | 因此我的心欢喜，我的灵快乐。我的肉身也要安然居住。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thou wilt not leave my soul in hell; neither wilt thou suffer thine Holy One to see corruption. |
| 中文 | 因为你必不将我的灵魂撇在阴间。也不叫你的圣者见朽坏。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou wilt shew me the path of life: in thy presence is fulness of joy; at thy right hand there are pleasures for evermore. |
| 中文 | 你必将生命的道路指示我。在你的面前有满足的喜乐。在你右手中有永远的福乐。 |
