# Ezekiel 25 / 以西结书 25

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The word of the LORD came again unto me, saying, |
| 中文 | 耶和华的话临到我说， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Son of man, set thy face against the Ammonites, and prophesy against them; |
| 中文 | 人子阿，你要面向亚扪人说预言，攻击他们， |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And say unto the Ammonites, Hear the word of the Lord GOD; Thus saith the Lord GOD; Because thou saidst, Aha, against my sanctuary, when it was profaned; and against the land of Israel, when it was desolate; and against the house of Judah, when they went into captivity; |
| 中文 | 说，你们当听主耶和华的话。主耶和华如此说，我的圣所被亵渎，以色列地变荒凉，犹大家被掳掠。那时，你便因这些事说，阿哈。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, therefore I will deliver thee to the men of the east for a possession, and they shall set their palaces in thee, and make their dwellings in thee: they shall eat thy fruit, and they shall drink thy milk. |
| 中文 | 所以我必将你的地交给东方人为业。他们必在你的地上安营居住，吃你的果子，喝你的奶。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And I will make Rabbah a stable for camels, and the Ammonites a couching place for flocks: and ye shall know that I am the LORD. |
| 中文 | 我必使拉巴为骆驼场，使亚扪人的地为羊群躺卧之处，你们就知道我是耶和华。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thus saith the Lord GOD; Because thou hast clapped thine hands, and stamped with the feet, and rejoiced in heart with all thy despite against the land of Israel; |
| 中文 | 主耶和华如此说，因你拍手顿足，以满心的恨恶向以色列地欢喜， |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, therefore I will stretch out mine hand upon thee, and will deliver thee for a spoil to the heathen; and I will cut thee off from the people, and I will cause thee to perish out of the countries: I will destroy thee; and thou shalt know that I am the LORD. |
| 中文 | 所以我伸手攻击你，将你交给列国作为掳物。我必从万民中剪除你，使你从万国中败亡。我必除灭你，你就知道我是耶和华。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thus saith the Lord GOD; Because that Moab and Seir do say, Behold, the house of Judah is like unto all the heathen; |
| 中文 | 主耶和华如此说，因摩押和西珥人说，看哪，犹大家与列国无异， |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Therefore, behold, I will open the side of Moab from the cities, from his cities which are on his frontiers, the glory of the country, Bethjeshimoth, Baalmeon, and Kiriathaim, |
| 中文 | 所以我要破开摩押边界上的城邑，就是摩押人看为本国之荣耀的伯耶西末，巴力免，基列亭， |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Unto the men of the east with the Ammonites, and will give them in possession, that the Ammonites may not be remembered among the nations. |
| 中文 | 好使东方人来攻击亚扪人。我必将亚扪人之地交给他们为业，使亚扪人在列国中不再被记念。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And I will execute judgments upon Moab; and they shall know that I am the LORD. |
| 中文 | 我必向摩押施行审判，他们就知道我是耶和华。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thus saith the Lord GOD; Because that Edom hath dealt against the house of Judah by taking vengeance, and hath greatly offended, and revenged himself upon them; |
| 中文 | 主耶和华如此说，因为以东报仇雪恨，攻击犹大家，向他们报仇，大大有罪， |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Therefore thus saith the Lord GOD; I will also stretch out mine hand upon Edom, and will cut off man and beast from it; and I will make it desolate from Teman; and they of Dedan shall fall by the sword. |
| 中文 | 所以主耶和华如此说，我必伸手攻击以东，剪除人与牲畜，使以东从毯螅起，人必倒在刀下，地要变为荒凉，直到底但。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And I will lay my vengeance upon Edom by the hand of my people Israel: and they shall do in Edom according to mine anger and according to my fury; and they shall know my vengeance, saith the Lord GOD. |
| 中文 | 我必藉我民以色列的手报复以东。以色列民必照我的怒气，按我的忿怒在以东施报，以东人就知道是我施报。这是主耶和华说的。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thus saith the Lord GOD; Because the Philistines have dealt by revenge, and have taken vengeance with a despiteful heart, to destroy it for the old hatred; |
| 中文 | 主耶和华如此说，因非利士人向犹大人报仇，就是以恨恶的心报仇雪恨，永怀仇恨，要毁灭他们， |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Therefore thus saith the Lord GOD; Behold, I will stretch out mine hand upon the Philistines, and I will cut off the Cherethims, and destroy the remnant of the sea coast. |
| 中文 | 所以主耶和华如此说，我必伸手攻击非利士人，剪除基利提人，灭绝沿海剩下的居民。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And I will execute great vengeance upon them with furious rebukes; and they shall know that I am the LORD, when I shall lay my vengeance upon them. |
| 中文 | 我向他们大施报应，发怒斥责他们。我报复他们的时候，他们就知道我是耶和华。 |
