# Psalms 83

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Keep not thou silence, O God: hold not thy peace, and be not still, O God. |
| 中文 | （亚萨的诗歌）神阿，求你不要静默。神阿，求你不要闭口，也不要不作声。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For, lo, thine enemies make a tumult: and they that hate thee have lifted up the head. |
| 中文 | 因为你的仇敌喧囔。恨你的抬起头来。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They have taken crafty counsel against thy people, and consulted against thy hidden ones. |
| 中文 | 他们同谋奸诈，要害你的百姓，彼此商议，要害你所隐藏的人。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They have said, Come, and let us cut them off from being a nation; that the name of Israel may be no more in remembrance. |
| 中文 | 他们说，来吧，我们将他们剪灭，使他们不再成国。使以色列的名，不再被人记念。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For they have consulted together with one consent: they are confederate against thee: |
| 中文 | 他们同心商议，彼此结盟，要抵挡你。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The tabernacles of Edom, and the Ishmaelites; of Moab, and the Hagarenes; |
| 中文 | 就是住帐棚的以东人，和以实玛利人。摩押和夏甲人。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Gebal, and Ammon, and Amalek; the Philistines with the inhabitants of Tyre; |
| 中文 | 迦巴勒，亚扪，和亚玛力，非利士，并推罗的居民。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Assur also is joined with them: they have holpen the children of Lot. Selah. |
| 中文 | 亚述也与他们连合。他们作罗得子孙的帮手。（细拉） |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Do unto them as unto the Midianites; as to Sisera, as to Jabin, at the brook of Kison: |
| 中文 | 求你待他们如待米甸，如在基顺河待西西拉和耶宾一样。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Which perished at Endor: they became as dung for the earth. |
| 中文 | 他们在隐多珥灭亡，成了地上的粪土。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Make their nobles like Oreb, and like Zeeb: yea, all their princes as Zebah, and as Zalmunna: |
| 中文 | 求你叫他们的首领，像俄立和西伊伯，叫他们的王子，都像西巴和撒慕拿。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who said, Let us take to ourselves the houses of God in possession. |
| 中文 | 他们说，我们要得神的住处，作为自己的产业。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O my God, make them like a wheel; as the stubble before the wind. |
| 中文 | 我的神阿，求你叫他们像旋风的尘土，像风前的碎秸。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | As the fire burneth a wood, and as the flame setteth the mountains on fire; |
| 中文 | 火怎样焚烧树林，火焰怎样烧着山岭。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | So persecute them with thy tempest, and make them afraid with thy storm. |
| 中文 | 求你也照样用狂风追赶他们，用暴雨恐吓他们。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Fill their faces with shame; that they may seek thy name, O LORD. |
| 中文 | 愿你使他们满面羞耻，好叫他们寻求你耶和华的名。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let them be confounded and troubled for ever; yea, let them be put to shame, and perish: |
| 中文 | 愿他们永远羞愧惊惶。愿他们惭愧灭亡。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | That men may know that thou, whose name alone is JEHOVAH, art the most high over all the earth. |
| 中文 | 使他们知道惟独你名为耶和华的，是全地以上的至高者。 |
