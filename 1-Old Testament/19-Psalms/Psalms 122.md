# Psalms 122

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I was glad when they said unto me, Let us go into the house of the LORD. |
| 中文 | （大卫上行之诗）人对我说，我们往耶和华的殿去，我就欢喜。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Our feet shall stand within thy gates, O Jerusalem. |
| 中文 | 耶路撒冷阿，我们的脚站在你的门内。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Jerusalem is builded as a city that is compact together: |
| 中文 | 耶路撒冷被建造，如同连络整齐的一座城。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Whither the tribes go up, the tribes of the LORD, unto the testimony of Israel, to give thanks unto the name of the LORD. |
| 中文 | 众支派，就是耶和华的支派，上那里去，按以色列的常例（或作作以色列的证据）称赞耶和华的名。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For there are set thrones of judgment, the thrones of the house of David. |
| 中文 | 因为在那里设立审判的宝座，就是大卫家的宝座。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Pray for the peace of Jerusalem: they shall prosper that love thee. |
| 中文 | 你们要为耶路撒冷求平安。耶路撒冷阿，爱你的人必然兴旺。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Peace be within thy walls, and prosperity within thy palaces. |
| 中文 | 愿你城中平安，愿你宫内兴旺。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For my brethren and companions' sakes, I will now say, Peace be within thee. |
| 中文 | 因我弟兄和同伴的缘故，我要说，愿平安在你中间。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Because of the house of the LORD our God I will seek thy good. |
| 中文 | 因耶和华我们神殿的缘故，我要为你求福。 |
