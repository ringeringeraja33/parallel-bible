# Job 23

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then Job answered and said, |
| 中文 | 约伯回答说， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Even to day is my complaint bitter: my stroke is heavier than my groaning. |
| 中文 | 如今我的哀告还算为悖逆。我的责罚比我的唉哼还重。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Oh that I knew where I might find him! that I might come even to his seat! |
| 中文 | 惟愿我能知道在那里可以寻见神，能到他的台前。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I would order my cause before him, and fill my mouth with arguments. |
| 中文 | 我就在他面前将我的案件陈明，满口辩白。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I would know the words which he would answer me, and understand what he would say unto me. |
| 中文 | 我必知道他回答我的言语，明白他向我所说的话。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Will he plead against me with his great power? No; but he would put strength in me. |
| 中文 | 他岂用大能与我争辩吗。必不这样，他必理会我。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | There the righteous might dispute with him; so should I be delivered for ever from my judge. |
| 中文 | 在他那里，正直人可以与他辩论。这样，我必永远脱离那审判我的。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, I go forward, but he is not there; and backward, but I cannot perceive him: |
| 中文 | 只是，我往前行，他不在那里，往后退，也不能见他。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | On the left hand, where he doth work, but I cannot behold him: he hideth himself on the right hand, that I cannot see him: |
| 中文 | 他在左边行事，我却不能看见，在右边隐藏，我也不能见他。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But he knoweth the way that I take: when he hath tried me, I shall come forth as gold. |
| 中文 | 然而他知道我所行的路。他试炼我之后，我必如精金。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My foot hath held his steps, his way have I kept, and not declined. |
| 中文 | 我脚追随他的步履。我谨守他的道，并不偏离。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Neither have I gone back from the commandment of his lips; I have esteemed the words of his mouth more than my necessary food. |
| 中文 | 他嘴唇的命令，我未曾背弃。我看重他口中的言语，过于我需用的饮食。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But he is in one mind, and who can turn him? and what his soul desireth, even that he doeth. |
| 中文 | 只是他心志已定，谁能使他转意呢。他心里所愿的，就行出来。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For he performeth the thing that is appointed for me: and many such things are with him. |
| 中文 | 他向我所定的，就必做成。这类的事他还有许多。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Therefore am I troubled at his presence: when I consider, I am afraid of him. |
| 中文 | 所以我在他面前惊惶，我思念这事，便惧怕他。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For God maketh my heart soft, and the Almighty troubleth me: |
| 中文 | 神使我丧胆，全能者使我惊惶。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Because I was not cut off before the darkness, neither hath he covered the darkness from my face. |
| 中文 | 我的恐惧，不是因为黑暗，也不是因为幽暗蒙蔽我的脸。 |
