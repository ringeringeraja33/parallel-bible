# Job 41 / 约伯记 41

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Canst thou draw out leviathan with an hook? or his tongue with a cord which thou lettest down? |
| 中文 | 你能用鱼钩钓上鳄鱼吗。能用绳子压下它的舌头吗。 |

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 你能用绳索穿它的鼻子吗。能用钩穿它的腮骨吗。 |

| Language | Content |
