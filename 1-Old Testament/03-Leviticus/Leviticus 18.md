# Leviticus 18 / 利未记 18

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the LORD spake unto Moses, saying, |
| 中文 | 耶和华对摩西说， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Speak unto the children of Israel, and say unto them, I am the LORD your God. |
| 中文 | 你晓谕以色列人说，我是耶和华你们的神。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | After the doings of the land of Egypt, wherein ye dwelt, shall ye not do: and after the doings of the land of Canaan, whither I bring you, shall ye not do: neither shall ye walk in their ordinances. |
| 中文 | 你们从前住的埃及地，那里人的行为，你们不可效法，我要领你们到的迦南地，那里人的行为也不可效法，也不可照他们的恶俗行。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ye shall do my judgments, and keep mine ordinances, to walk therein: I am the LORD your God. |
| 中文 | 你们要遵我的典章，守我的律例，按此而行。我是耶和华你们的神。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ye shall therefore keep my statutes, and my judgments: which if a man do, he shall live in them: I am the LORD. |
| 中文 | 所以，你们要守我的律例典章，人若遵行，就必因此活着。我是耶和华。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | None of you shall approach to any that is near of kin to him, to uncover their nakedness: I am the LORD. |
| 中文 | 你们都不可露骨肉之亲的下体，亲近他们。我是耶和华。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The nakedness of thy father, or the nakedness of thy mother, shalt thou not uncover: she is thy mother; thou shalt not uncover her nakedness. |
| 中文 | 不可露你母亲的下体，羞辱了你父亲。她是你的母亲，不可露她的下体。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The nakedness of thy father's wife shalt thou not uncover: it is thy father's nakedness. |
| 中文 | 不可露你继母的下体，这本是你父亲的下体。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The nakedness of thy sister, the daughter of thy father, or daughter of thy mother, whether she be born at home, or born abroad, even their nakedness thou shalt not uncover. |
| 中文 | 你的姊妹，不拘是异母同父的，是异父同母的，无论是生在家生在外的，都不可露她们的下体。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The nakedness of thy son's daughter, or of thy daughter's daughter, even their nakedness thou shalt not uncover: for theirs is thine own nakedness. |
| 中文 | 不可露你孙女或是外孙女的下体，露了她们的下体就是露了自己的下体。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The nakedness of thy father's wife's daughter, begotten of thy father, she is thy sister, thou shalt not uncover her nakedness. |
| 中文 | 你继母从你父亲生的女儿本是你的妹妹，不可露她的下体。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt not uncover the nakedness of thy mother's sister: for she is thy mother's near kinswoman. |

## 14

| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 不可亲近你伯叔之妻，羞辱了你伯叔，她是你的伯叔母。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt not uncover the nakedness of thy daughter in law: she is thy son's wife; thou shalt not uncover her nakedness. |
| 中文 | 不可露你儿妇的下体，她是你儿子的妻，不可露她的下体。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt not uncover the nakedness of thy brother's wife: it is thy brother's nakedness. |
| 中文 | 不可露你弟兄妻子的下体，这本是你弟兄的下体。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt not uncover the nakedness of a woman and her daughter, neither shalt thou take her son's daughter, or her daughter's daughter, to uncover her nakedness; for they are her near kinswomen: it is wickedness. |
| 中文 | 不可露了妇人的下体，又露她女儿的下体，也不可娶她孙女或是外孙女，露她们的下体，她们是骨肉之亲，这本是大恶。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Neither shalt thou take a wife to her sister, to vex her, to uncover her nakedness, beside the other in her life time. |
| 中文 | 你妻还在的时候，不可另娶她的姊妹作对头，露她的下体。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Also thou shalt not approach unto a woman to uncover her nakedness, as long as she is put apart for her uncleanness. |
| 中文 | 女人行经不洁净的时候，不可露她的下体，与她亲近。 |

## 20

| --- | --- |

## 21

| --- | --- |

## 30

| 中文 | 所以，你们要守我所吩咐的，免得你们随从那些可憎的恶俗，就是在你们以先的人所常行的，以致玷污了自己。我是耶和华你们的神。 |
