# Job 26

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But Job answered and said, |
| 中文 | 约伯回答说， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | How hast thou helped him that is without power? how savest thou the arm that hath no strength? |
| 中文 | 无能的人，蒙你何等的帮助。膀臂无力的人，蒙你何等的拯救。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | How hast thou counselled him that hath no wisdom? and how hast thou plentifully declared the thing as it is? |
| 中文 | 无智慧的人，蒙你何等的指教。你向他多显大知识。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | To whom hast thou uttered words? and whose spirit came from thee? |
| 中文 | 你向谁发出言语来。谁的灵从你而出。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Dead things are formed from under the waters, and the inhabitants thereof. |
| 中文 | 在大水，和水族以下的阴魂，战兢。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hell is naked before him, and destruction hath no covering. |
| 中文 | 在神面前，阴间显露。灭亡也不得遮掩。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He stretcheth out the north over the empty place, and hangeth the earth upon nothing. |
| 中文 | 神将北极铺在空中，将大地悬在虚空。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He bindeth up the waters in his thick clouds; and the cloud is not rent under them. |
| 中文 | 将水包在密云中，云却不破裂。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He holdeth back the face of his throne, and spreadeth his cloud upon it. |
| 中文 | 遮蔽他的宝座，将云铺在其上。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He hath compassed the waters with bounds, until the day and night come to an end. |
| 中文 | 在水面的周围划出界限，直到光明黑暗的交界。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The pillars of heaven tremble and are astonished at his reproof. |
| 中文 | 天的柱子，因他的斥责，震动惊奇。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He divideth the sea with his power, and by his understanding he smiteth through the proud. |
| 中文 | 他以能力搅动（或作平静）大海，他藉知识打伤拉哈伯。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | By his spirit he hath garnished the heavens; his hand hath formed the crooked serpent. |
| 中文 | 藉他的灵使天有妆饰，他的手刺杀快蛇。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Lo, these are parts of his ways: but how little a portion is heard of him? but the thunder of his power who can understand? |
| 中文 | 看哪，这不过是神工作的些微。我们所听于他的，是何等细微的声音。他大能的雷声谁能明透呢。 |
