# Exodus 21 / 出埃及记 21

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Now these are the judgments which thou shalt set before them. |
| 中文 | 你在百姓面前所要立的典章是这样， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If thou buy an Hebrew servant, six years he shall serve: and in the seventh he shall go out free for nothing. |
| 中文 | 你若买希伯来人作奴仆，他必服事你六年，第七年他可以自由，白白地出去。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If he came in by himself, he shall go out by himself: if he were married, then his wife shall go out with him. |
| 中文 | 他若孤身来，就可以孤身去，他若有妻，他的妻就可以同他出去。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If his master have given him a wife, and she have born him sons or daughters; the wife and her children shall be her master's, and he shall go out by himself. |
| 中文 | 他主人若给他妻子，妻子给他生了儿子或女儿，妻子和儿女要归主人，他要独自出去。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And if the servant shall plainly say, I love my master, my wife, and my children; I will not go out free: |
| 中文 | 倘或奴仆明说，我爱我的主人和我的妻子儿女，不愿意自由出去。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then his master shall bring him unto the judges; he shall also bring him to the door, or unto the door post; and his master shall bore his ear through with an aul; and he shall serve him for ever. |
| 中文 | 他的主人就要带他到审判官那里（审判官或作神下同），又要带他到门前，靠近门框，用锥子穿他的耳朵，他就永远服事主人。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And if a man sell his daughter to be a maidservant, she shall not go out as the menservants do. |
| 中文 | 人若卖女儿作婢女，婢女不可像男仆那样出去。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If she please not her master, who hath betrothed her to himself, then shall he let her be redeemed: to sell her unto a strange nation he shall have no power, seeing he hath dealt deceitfully with her. |
| 中文 | 主人选定她归自己，若不喜欢她，就要许她赎身，主人既然用诡诈待她，就没有权柄卖给外邦人。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And if he have betrothed her unto his son, he shall deal with her after the manner of daughters. |
| 中文 | 主人若选定她给自己的儿子，就当待她如同女儿。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 若另娶一个，那女子的吃食，衣服，并好合的事，仍不可减少。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 若不向她行这三样，她就可以不用钱赎，白白地出去。 |
