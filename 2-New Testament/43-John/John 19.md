| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then Pilate therefore took Jesus, and scourged him. |
| 中文 | 当下彼拉多将耶稣鞭打了。 |

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 兵丁用荆棘编作冠冕，戴在他头上，给他穿上紫袍。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And said, Hail, King of the Jews! and they smote him with their hands. |
| 中文 | 又挨近他说，恭喜犹太人的王阿。他们就用手掌打他。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Pilate therefore went forth again, and saith unto them, Behold, I bring him forth to you, that ye may know that I find no fault in him. |
| 中文 | 彼拉多又出来对众人说，我带他出来见你们，叫你们知道我查不出他有什么罪来。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then came Jesus forth, wearing the crown of thorns, and the purple robe. And Pilate saith unto them, Behold the man! |
| 中文 | 耶稣出来，戴着荆棘冠冕，穿着紫袍。彼拉多对他们说，你们看这个人。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When the chief priests therefore and officers saw him, they cried out, saying, Crucify him, crucify him. Pilate saith unto them, Take ye him, and crucify him: for I find no fault in him. |
| 中文 | 祭司长和差役看见他，就喊着说，钉他十字架，钉他十字架。彼拉多说，你们自己把他钉十字架吧。我查不出他有什么罪来。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The Jews answered him, We have a law, and by our law he ought to die, because he made himself the Son of God. |
| 中文 | 犹太人回答说，我们有律法，按那律法，他是该死的，因他以自己为神的儿子。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When Pilate therefore heard that saying, he was the more afraid; |
| 中文 | 彼拉多听见这话，越发害怕。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And went again into the judgment hall, and saith unto Jesus, Whence art thou? But Jesus gave him no answer. |
| 中文 | 又进衙门，对耶稣说，你是那里来的。耶稣却不回答。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then saith Pilate unto him, Speakest thou not unto me? knowest thou not that I have power to crucify thee, and have power to release thee? |
| 中文 | 彼拉多说，你不对我说话吗。你岂不知我有权柄释放你，也有权柄把你钉十字架吗。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Jesus answered, Thou couldest have no power at all against me, except it were given thee from above: therefore he that delivered me unto thee hath the greater sin. |
| 中文 | 耶稣回答说，若不是从上头赐给你的，你就毫无权柄辨我。所以把我交给你的那人，罪更重了。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 从此彼拉多想要释放耶稣。无奈犹太人喊着说，你若释放这个人，就不是凯撒的忠臣。（原文作朋友）凡以自己为王的，就是背叛凯撒了。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |

## 14

| Language | Content |

| 中文 | 那日是逾越节的日子，约有午正。彼拉多对犹太人说，看哪，这是你们的王。 |

## 16

| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 有许多犹太人念这名号。因为耶稣被钉十字架的地方，与城相近，并且是用希伯来，罗马，希腊，三样文字写的。 |
