# Ezekiel 34 / 以西结书 34

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the word of the LORD came unto me, saying, |
| 中文 | 耶和华的话临到我说， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Son of man, prophesy against the shepherds of Israel, prophesy, and say unto them, Thus saith the Lord GOD unto the shepherds; Woe be to the shepherds of Israel that do feed themselves! should not the shepherds feed the flocks? |
| 中文 | 人子阿，你要向以色列的牧人发预言，攻击他们，说，主耶和华如此说，祸哉。以色列的牧人只知牧养自己。牧人岂不当牧养群羊吗。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ye eat the fat, and ye clothe you with the wool, ye kill them that are fed: but ye feed not the flock. |
| 中文 | 你们吃脂油，穿羊毛，宰肥壮的，却不牧养群羊。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The diseased have ye not strengthened, neither have ye healed that which was sick, neither have ye bound up that which was broken, neither have ye brought again that which was driven away, neither have ye sought that which was lost; but with force and with cruelty have ye ruled them. |
| 中文 | 瘦弱的，你们没有养壮。有病的，你们没有医治。受伤的，你们没有缠裹。被逐的，你们没有领回。失丧的，你们没有寻梢。但用强暴严严地辖制。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And they were scattered, because there is no shepherd: and they became meat to all the beasts of the field, when they were scattered. |
| 中文 | 因无牧人，羊就分散。既分散，便作了一切野兽的食物。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My sheep wandered through all the mountains, and upon every high hill: yea, my flock was scattered upon all the face of the earth, and none did search or seek after them. |
| 中文 | 我的羊在诸山间，在各高冈上流离，在全地上分散，无人去寻，无人去找。 |

| Language | Content |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | As I live, saith the Lord GOD, surely because my flock became a prey, and my flock became meat to every beast of the field, because there was no shepherd, neither did my shepherds search for my flock, but the shepherds fed themselves, and fed not my flock; |
| 中文 | 主耶和华说，我指着我的永生起誓，我的羊因无牧人就成为掠物，也作了一切野兽的食物。我的牧人不寻梢我的羊。这些牧人只知牧养自己，并不牧养我的羊。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thus saith the Lord GOD; Behold, I am against the shepherds; and I will require my flock at their hand, and cause them to cease from feeding the flock; neither shall the shepherds feed themselves any more; for I will deliver my flock from their mouth, that they may not be meat for them. |
| 中文 | 主耶和华如此说，我必与牧人为敌，必向他们的手追讨我的羊，使他们不再牧放群羊。牧人也不再牧养自己。我必救我的羊脱离他们的口，不再作他们的食物。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thus saith the Lord GOD; Behold, I, even I, will both search my sheep, and seek them out. |
| 中文 | 主耶和华如此说，看哪，我必亲自寻梢我的羊，将它们寻见。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | As a shepherd seeketh out his flock in the day that he is among his sheep that are scattered; so will I seek out my sheep, and will deliver them out of all places where they have been scattered in the cloudy and dark day. |
| 中文 | 牧人在羊群四散的日子怎样寻梢他的羊，我必照样寻梢我的羊。这些羊在密云黑暗的日子散到各处，我必从那里救回它们来。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
