# Psalms 98

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O sing unto the LORD a new song; for he hath done marvellous things: his right hand, and his holy arm, hath gotten him the victory. |
| 中文 | （一篇诗）你们要向耶和华唱新歌。因为他行过奇妙的事。他的右手和圣臂，施行救恩。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD hath made known his salvation: his righteousness hath he openly shewed in the sight of the heathen. |
| 中文 | 耶和华发明了他的救恩，在列邦人眼前显出公义。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He hath remembered his mercy and his truth toward the house of Israel: all the ends of the earth have seen the salvation of our God. |
| 中文 | 记念他向以色列家所发的慈爱，所凭的信实。地的四极，都看见我们神的救恩。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Make a joyful noise unto the LORD, all the earth: make a loud noise, and rejoice, and sing praise. |
| 中文 | 全地都要向耶和华欢乐。要发起大声欢呼歌颂。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Sing unto the LORD with the harp; with the harp, and the voice of a psalm. |
| 中文 | 要用琴歌颂耶和华，用琴和诗歌的声音歌颂他。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | With trumpets and sound of cornet make a joyful noise before the LORD, the King. |
| 中文 | 用号和角声，在大君王耶和华面前欢呼。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let the sea roar, and the fulness thereof; the world, and they that dwell therein. |
| 中文 | 愿海和其中所充满的澎湃。世界和住在其间的，也要发声。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let the floods clap their hands: let the hills be joyful together |
| 中文 | 愿大水拍手。愿诸山在耶和华面前一同欢呼。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Before the LORD; for he cometh to judge the earth: with righteousness shall he judge the world, and the people with equity. |
| 中文 | 因为他来，要审判遍地。他要按公义审判世界，按公正审判万民。 |
