# Psalms 94

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O Lord God, to whom vengeance belongeth; O God, to whom vengeance belongeth, shew thyself. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，你是伸冤的神。伸冤的神阿，求你发出光来。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Lift up thyself, thou judge of the earth: render a reward to the proud. |
| 中文 | 审判世界的主阿，求你挺身而立，使骄傲人受应得的报应。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | LORD, how long shall the wicked, how long shall the wicked triumph? |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，恶人夸胜要到几时呢，要到几时呢。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | How long shall they utter and speak hard things? and all the workers of iniquity boast themselves? |
| 中文 | 他们絮絮叨叨，说傲慢的话。一切作孽的人，都自己夸张。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They break in pieces thy people, O LORD, and afflict thine heritage. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，他们强压你的百姓，苦害你的产业。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They slay the widow and the stranger, and murder the fatherless. |
| 中文 | 他们杀死寡妇和寄居的，又杀害孤儿。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Yet they say, The LORD shall not see, neither shall the God of Jacob regard it. |
| 中文 | 他们说，耶和华必不看见，雅各的神必不思念。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Understand, ye brutish among the people: and ye fools, when will ye be wise? |
| 中文 | 你们民间的畜类人当思想。你们愚顽人，到几时才有智慧呢。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that planted the ear, shall he not hear? he that formed the eye, shall he not see? |
| 中文 | 造耳朵的，难道自己不听见吗。造眼睛的，难道自己不看见吗。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that chastiseth the heathen, shall not he correct? he that teacheth man knowledge, shall not he know? |
| 中文 | 管教列邦的，就是叫人得知识的，难道自己不惩治人吗。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD knoweth the thoughts of man, that they are vanity. |
| 中文 | 耶和华知道人的意念是虚妄的。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Blessed is the man whom thou chastenest, O LORD, and teachest him out of thy law; |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，你所管教，用律法所教训的人，是有福的。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | That thou mayest give him rest from the days of adversity, until the pit be digged for the wicked. |
| 中文 | 你使他在遭难的日子，得享平安。惟有恶人陷在所挖的坑中。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the LORD will not cast off his people, neither will he forsake his inheritance. |
| 中文 | 因为耶和华必不丢弃他的百姓，也不离弃他的产业。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But judgment shall return unto righteousness: and all the upright in heart shall follow it. |
| 中文 | 审判要转向公义。心里正直的，必都随从。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who will rise up for me against the evildoers? or who will stand up for me against the workers of iniquity? |
| 中文 | 谁肯为我起来攻击作恶的。谁肯为我站起抵挡作孽的。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Unless the LORD had been my help, my soul had almost dwelt in silence. |
| 中文 | 若不是耶和华帮助我，我就住在寂静之中了。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When I said, My foot slippeth; thy mercy, O LORD, held me up. |
| 中文 | 我正说我失了脚，耶和华阿，那时你的慈爱扶助我。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | In the multitude of my thoughts within me thy comforts delight my soul. |
| 中文 | 我心里多忧多疑，你安慰我，就使我欢乐。 |

## 20

| 中文 | 那藉着律例架弄残害，在位上行奸恶的，岂能与你相交吗。 |

## 23

| --- | --- |
