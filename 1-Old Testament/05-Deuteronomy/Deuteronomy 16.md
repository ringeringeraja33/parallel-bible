# Deuteronomy 16 / 申命记 16

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Observe the month of Abib, and keep the passover unto the LORD thy God: for in the month of Abib the LORD thy God brought thee forth out of Egypt by night. |
| 中文 | 你要注意亚笔月，向耶和华你的神守逾越节，因为耶和华你的神在亚笔月夜间领你出埃及。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt therefore sacrifice the passover unto the LORD thy God, of the flock and the herd, in the place which the LORD shall choose to place his name there. |
| 中文 | 你当在耶和华所选择要立为他名的居所，从牛群羊群中，将逾越节的祭牲献给耶和华你的神。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt eat no leavened bread with it; seven days shalt thou eat unleavened bread therewith, even the bread of affliction; for thou camest forth out of the land of Egypt in haste: that thou mayest remember the day when thou camest forth out of the land of Egypt all the days of thy life. |
| 中文 | 你吃这祭牲，不可吃有酵的饼。七日之内要吃无酵饼，就是困苦饼。（你本是急忙出了埃及地）要叫你一生一世记念你从埃及地出来的日子。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And there shall be no leavened bread seen with thee in all thy coast seven days; neither shall there any thing of the flesh, which thou sacrificedst the first day at even, remain all night until the morning. |
| 中文 | 在你四境之内，七日不可见面酵，头一日晚上所献的肉，一点不可留到早晨。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou mayest not sacrifice the passover within any of thy gates, which the LORD thy God giveth thee: |
| 中文 | 在耶和华你神所赐的各城中，你不可献逾越节的祭。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But at the place which the LORD thy God shall choose to place his name in, there thou shalt sacrifice the passover at even, at the going down of the sun, at the season that thou camest forth out of Egypt. |
| 中文 | 只当在耶和华你神所选择要立为他名的居所，晚上日落的时候，乃是你出埃及的时候，献逾越节的祭。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And thou shalt roast and eat it in the place which the LORD thy God shall choose: and thou shalt turn in the morning, and go unto thy tents. |
| 中文 | 当在耶和华你神所选择的地方把肉烤了吃（烤或作煮），次日早晨就回到你的帐棚去。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Six days thou shalt eat unleavened bread: and on the seventh day shall be a solemn assembly to the LORD thy God: thou shalt do no work therein. |
| 中文 | 你要吃无酵饼六日，第七日要向耶和华你的神守严肃会，不可作工。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Seven weeks shalt thou number unto thee: begin to number the seven weeks from such time as thou beginnest to put the sickle to the corn. |
| 中文 | 你要计算七七日，从你开镰收割禾稼时算起，共计七七日。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And thou shalt keep the feast of weeks unto the LORD thy God with a tribute of a freewill offering of thine hand, which thou shalt give unto the LORD thy God, according as the LORD thy God hath blessed thee: |
| 中文 | 你要照耶和华你神所赐你的福，手里拿着甘心祭，献在耶和华你的神面前，守七七节。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And thou shalt rejoice before the LORD thy God, thou, and thy son, and thy daughter, and thy manservant, and thy maidservant, and the Levite that is within thy gates, and the stranger, and the fatherless, and the widow, that are among you, in the place which the LORD thy God hath chosen to place his name there. |
| 中文 | 你和你儿女，仆婢，并住在你城里的利未人，以及在你们中间寄居的与孤儿寡妇，都要在耶和华你神所选择立为他名的居所，在耶和华你的神面前欢乐。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And thou shalt remember that thou wast a bondman in Egypt: and thou shalt observe and do these statutes. |
| 中文 | 你也要记念你在埃及作过奴仆。你要谨守遵行这些律例。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt observe the feast of tabernacles seven days, after that thou hast gathered in thy corn and thy wine: |
| 中文 | 你把禾场的谷，酒榨的酒收藏以后，就要守住棚节七日。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And thou shalt rejoice in thy feast, thou, and thy son, and thy daughter, and thy manservant, and thy maidservant, and the Levite, the stranger, and the fatherless, and the widow, that are within thy gates. |
| 中文 | 守节的时候，你和你儿女，仆婢，并住在你城里的利未人，以及寄居的与孤儿寡妇，都要欢乐。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Seven days shalt thou keep a solemn feast unto the LORD thy God in the place which the LORD shall choose: because the LORD thy God shall bless thee in all thine increase, and in all the works of thine hands, therefore thou shalt surely rejoice. |
| 中文 | 在耶和华所选择的地方，你当向耶和华你的神守节七日。因为耶和华你神在你一切的土产上和你手里所办的事上要赐福与你，你就非常地欢乐。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Three times in a year shall all thy males appear before the LORD thy God in the place which he shall choose; in the feast of unleavened bread, and in the feast of weeks, and in the feast of tabernacles: and they shall not appear before the LORD empty: |
| 中文 | 你一切的男丁要在除酵节，七七节，住棚节，一年三次，在耶和华你神所选择的地方朝见他，却不可空手朝见。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Every man shall give as he is able, according to the blessing of the LORD thy God which he hath given thee. |
| 中文 | 各人要按自己的力量，照耶和华你神所赐的福分，奉献礼物。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Judges and officers shalt thou make thee in all thy gates, which the LORD thy God giveth thee, throughout thy tribes: and they shall judge the people with just judgment. |
| 中文 | 你要在耶和华你神所赐的各城里，按着各支派设立审判官和官长。他们必按公义的审判判断百姓。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt not wrest judgment; thou shalt not respect persons, neither take a gift: for a gift doth blind the eyes of the wise, and pervert the words of the righteous. |
| 中文 | 不可屈枉正直。不可看人的外貌。也不可受贿赂。因为贿赂能叫智慧人的眼变瞎了，又能颠倒义人的话。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 你要追求至公至义，好叫你存活，承受耶和华你神所赐你的地。 |
