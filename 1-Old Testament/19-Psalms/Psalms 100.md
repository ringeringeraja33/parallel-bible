# Psalms 100

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Make a joyful noise unto the LORD, all ye lands. |
| 中文 | （称谢诗）普天下当向耶和华欢呼。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Serve the LORD with gladness: come before his presence with singing. |
| 中文 | 你们当乐意事奉耶和华。当来向他歌唱。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Know ye that the LORD he is God: it is he that hath made us, and not we ourselves; we are his people, and the sheep of his pasture. |
| 中文 | 你们当晓得耶和华是神。我们是他造的，也是属他的。我们是他的民，也是他草场的羊。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Enter into his gates with thanksgiving, and into his courts with praise: be thankful unto him, and bless his name. |
| 中文 | 当称谢进入他的门，当赞美进入他的院。当感谢他，称颂他的名。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the LORD is good; his mercy is everlasting; and his truth endureth to all generations. |
| 中文 | 因为耶和华本为善，他的慈爱，存到永远，他的信实，直到万代。 |
