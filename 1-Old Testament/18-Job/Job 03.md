# Job 3 / 约伯记 3

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | After this opened Job his mouth, and cursed his day. |
| 中文 | 此后，约伯开口咒诅自己的生日，说， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And Job spake, and said, |
| 中文 | 愿我生的那日， |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let the day perish wherein I was born, and the night in which it was said, There is a man child conceived. |
| 中文 | *和说怀了男胎的那夜都灭没。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let that day be darkness; let not God regard it from above, neither let the light shine upon it. |
| 中文 | 愿那日变为黑暗。愿神不从上面寻梢它。愿亮光不照于其上。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let darkness and the shadow of death stain it; let a cloud dwell upon it; let the blackness of the day terrify it. |
| 中文 | 愿黑暗和死荫索取那日。愿密云停在其上。愿日蚀恐吓它。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | As for that night, let darkness seize upon it; let it not be joined unto the days of the year, let it not come into the number of the months. |
| 中文 | 愿那夜被幽暗夺取，不在年中的日子同乐，也不入月中的数目。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Lo, let that night be solitary, let no joyful voice come therein. |
| 中文 | 愿那夜没有生育，其间也没有欢乐的声音。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let them curse it that curse the day, who are ready to raise up their mourning. |
| 中文 | 愿那咒诅日子且能惹动鳄鱼的，咒诅那夜。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let the stars of the twilight thereof be dark; let it look for light, but have none; neither let it see the dawning of the day: |
| 中文 | 愿那夜黎明的星宿变为黑暗，盼亮却不亮，也不见早晨的光线（光线原文作眼皮）。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Because it shut not up the doors of my mother's womb, nor hid sorrow from mine eyes. |
| 中文 | 因没有把怀我胎的门关闭，也没有将患难对我的眼隐藏。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Why died I not from the womb? why did I not give up the ghost when I came out of the belly? |
| 中文 | 我为何不出母胎而死。为何不出母腹绝气。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Why did the knees prevent me? or why the breasts that I should suck? |
| 中文 | 为何有膝接收我。为何有奶哺养我。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For now should I have lain still and been quiet, I should have slept: then had I been at rest, |
| 中文 | 不然，我就早已躺卧安睡。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | With kings and counsellors of the earth, which built desolate places for themselves; |
| 中文 | 和地上为自己重造荒邱的君王，谋士。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Or with princes that had gold, who filled their houses with silver: |
| 中文 | 或与有金子，将银子装满了房屋的王子一同安息。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Or as an hidden untimely birth I had not been; as infants which never saw light. |
| 中文 | 或像隐而未现，不到期而落的胎，归于无有，如同未见光的婴孩。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | There the wicked cease from troubling; and there the weary be at rest. |
| 中文 | 在那里恶人止息搅扰，困乏人得享安息。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | There the prisoners rest together; they hear not the voice of the oppressor. |
| 中文 | 被囚的人同得安逸，不听见督工的声音。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The small and great are there; and the servant is free from his master. |
| 中文 | 大小都在那里。奴仆脱离主人的辖制。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Wherefore is light given to him that is in misery, and life unto the bitter in soul; |
| 中文 | 受患难的人，为何有光赐给他呢。心中愁苦的人，为何有生命赐给他呢。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Which long for death, but it cometh not; and dig for it more than for hid treasures; |
| 中文 | 他们切望死，却不得死。求死，胜于求隐藏的珍宝。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Which rejoice exceedingly, and are glad, when they can find the grave? |
| 中文 | 他们寻见坟墓就快乐，极其欢喜。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Why is light given to a man whose way is hid, and whom God hath hedged in? |
| 中文 | 人的道路既然遮隐，神又把他四面围困，为何有光赐给他呢。 |

## 24

| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 我未曾吃饭就发出叹息。我唉哼的声音涌出如水。 |

## 25

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 因我所恐惧的临到我身，我所惧怕的迎我而来。 |

## 26

| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 我不得安逸，不得平静，也不得安息，却有患难来到。 |
