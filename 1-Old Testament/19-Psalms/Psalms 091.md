# Psalms 91 / 诗篇 91

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that dwelleth in the secret place of the most High shall abide under the shadow of the Almighty. |
| 中文 | 住在至高者隐密处的，必住在全能者的荫下。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will say of the LORD, He is my refuge and my fortress: my God; in him will I trust. |
| 中文 | 我要论到耶和华说，他是我的避难所，是我的山寨，是我的神，是我所倚靠的。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Surely he shall deliver thee from the snare of the fowler, and from the noisome pestilence. |
| 中文 | 他必救你脱离捕鸟人的网罗，和毒害的瘟疫。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He shall cover thee with his feathers, and under his wings shalt thou trust: his truth shall be thy shield and buckler. |
| 中文 | 他必用自己的翎毛遮蔽你。你要投靠在他的翅膀底下。他的诚实，是大小的盾牌。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt not be afraid for the terror by night; nor for the arrow that flieth by day; |
| 中文 | 你必不怕黑夜的惊骇，或是白日飞的箭。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Nor for the pestilence that walketh in darkness; nor for the destruction that wasteth at noonday. |
| 中文 | 也不怕黑夜行的瘟疫，或是午间灭人的毒病。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A thousand shall fall at thy side, and ten thousand at thy right hand; but it shall not come nigh thee. |
| 中文 | 虽有千人仆倒在你旁边，万人仆倒在你右边，这灾却不得临近你。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Only with thine eyes shalt thou behold and see the reward of the wicked. |
| 中文 | 你惟亲眼观看，见恶人遭报。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Because thou hast made the LORD, which is my refuge, even the most High, thy habitation; |
| 中文 | 耶和华是我的避难所。你已将至高者当你的居所。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | There shall no evil befall thee, neither shall any plague come nigh thy dwelling. |
| 中文 | 祸患必不临到你，灾害也不挨近你的帐棚。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For he shall give his angels charge over thee, to keep thee in all thy ways. |
| 中文 | 因他要为你吩咐他的使者，在你行的一切道路上保护你。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They shall bear thee up in their hands, lest thou dash thy foot against a stone. |
| 中文 | 他们要用手托着你，免得你的脚碰在石头上。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt tread upon the lion and adder: the young lion and the dragon shalt thou trample under feet. |
| 中文 | 你要踹在狮子和虺蛇的身上，践踏少壮狮子和大蛇。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Because he hath set his love upon me, therefore will I deliver him: I will set him on high, because he hath known my name. |
| 中文 | 神说，因为他专心爱我，我就搭救他。因为他知道我的名，我要把他安置在高处。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He shall call upon me, and I will answer him: I will be with him in trouble; I will deliver him, and honour him. |
| 中文 | 他若求告我，我就应允他。他在急难中，我要与他同在。我要搭救他，使他尊贵。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | With long life will I satisfy him, and shew him my salvation. |
| 中文 | 我要使他足享长寿，将我的救恩显明给他。 |
