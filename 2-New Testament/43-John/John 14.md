# John 14 / 约翰福音 14

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let not your heart be troubled: ye believe in God, believe also in me. |
| 中文 | 你们心里不要忧愁。你们信神，也当信我。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | In my Father's house are many mansions: if it were not so, I would have told you. I go to prepare a place for you. |
| 中文 | 在我父的家里，有许多住处。若是没有，我就早已告诉你们了。我去原是为你们预备地方去。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And if I go and prepare a place for you, I will come again, and receive you unto myself; that where I am, there ye may be also. |
| 中文 | 我若去为你们预备了地方，就必再来接你们到我那里去，我在那里，叫你们也在那里。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And whither I go ye know, and the way ye know. |
| 中文 | 我往那里去，你们知道。那条路，你们也知道。（有古卷作我往那里去你们知道那条路） |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thomas saith unto him, Lord, we know not whither thou goest; and how can we know the way? |
| 中文 | 多马对他说，主阿，我们不知道你往那里去，怎吗知道那条路呢。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Jesus saith unto him, I am the way, the truth, and the life: no man cometh unto the Father, but by me. |
| 中文 | 耶稣说，我就是道路，真理，生命。若不藉着我。没有人能到父那里去。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If ye had known me, ye should have known my Father also: and from henceforth ye know him, and have seen him. |
| 中文 | 你们若认识我，也就认识我的父。从今以后，你们认识他，并且已经看见他。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Philip saith unto him, Lord, shew us the Father, and it sufficeth us. |
| 中文 | 腓力对他说，求主将父显给我们看，我们就知足了。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Jesus saith unto him, Have I been so long time with you, and yet hast thou not known me, Philip? he that hath seen me hath seen the Father; and how sayest thou then, Shew us the Father? |
| 中文 | 耶稣对他说，腓力，我与你们同在这样长久，你还不认识我吗。人看见了我，就是看见了父。你怎吗说，将父显给我们看呢。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Believest thou not that I am in the Father, and the Father in me? the words that I speak unto you I speak not of myself: but the Father that dwelleth in me, he doeth the works. |
| 中文 | 我在父里面，父在我里面，你不信吗。我对你们所说的话，不是凭着自己说的，乃是住在我里面的父作他自己的事。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Believe me that I am in the Father, and the Father in me: or else believe me for the very works' sake. |
| 中文 | 你们当信我，我在父里面，父在我里面。既或不信，也当因我所作的事信我。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Verily, verily, I say unto you, He that believeth on me, the works that I do shall he do also; and greater works than these shall he do; because I go unto my Father. |
| 中文 | 我实实在在的告诉你们，我所作的事，信我的人也要作。并且要作比这更大的事。因为我往父那里去。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And whatsoever ye shall ask in my name, that will I do, that the Father may be glorified in the Son. |
| 中文 | 你们奉我的名，无论求什么，我必成就，叫父因子得荣耀。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If ye shall ask any thing in my name, I will do it. |
| 中文 | 你们若奉我的名求什么，我必成就。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If ye love me, keep my commandments. |
| 中文 | 你们若爱我，就必遵守我的命令。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And I will pray the Father, and he shall give you another Comforter, that he may abide with you for ever; |
| 中文 | 我要求父，父就另外赐给你们一位保惠师，（或作训慰师下同）叫他永远与你们同在。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Even the Spirit of truth; whom the world cannot receive, because it seeth him not, neither knoweth him: but ye know him; for he dwelleth with you, and shall be in you. |
| 中文 | 就是真理的圣灵，乃世人不能接受的。因为不见他，也不认识他。你们却认识他。因他常与你们同在，也要在你们里面。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will not leave you comfortless: I will come to you. |
| 中文 | 我不撇下你们为孤儿，我必到你们这里来。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Yet a little while, and the world seeth me no more; but ye see me: because I live, ye shall live also. |
| 中文 | 还有不多的时候，世人不再看见我。你们却看见我。因为我活着，你们也活着。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | At that day ye shall know that I am in my Father, and ye in me, and I in you. |
| 中文 | 到那日，你们就知道我在父里面，你们在我里面，我也在你们里面。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that hath my commandments, and keepeth them, he it is that loveth me: and he that loveth me shall be loved of my Father, and I will love him, and will manifest myself to him. |
| 中文 | 有了我的命令又遵守的，这人就是爱我的。爱我的必蒙我父爱他，我也要爱他，并且要向他显现。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Judas saith unto him, not Iscariot, Lord, how is it that thou wilt manifest thyself unto us, and not unto the world? |
| 中文 | 犹大（不是加略人犹大）问耶稣说，主阿，为什么要向我们显现，不向世人显现呢。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Jesus answered and said unto him, If a man love me, he will keep my words: and my Father will love him, and we will come unto him, and make our abode with him. |
| 中文 | 耶稣回答说，人若爱我，就必遵守我的道。我父也必爱他，并且我们要到他那里去，与他同住。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that loveth me not keepeth not my sayings: and the word which ye hear is not mine, but the Father's which sent me. |
| 中文 | 不爱我的人就不遵守我的道。你们所听见的道不是我的，乃是差我来之父的道。 |

## 25

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | These things have I spoken unto you, being yet present with you. |
| 中文 | 我还与你们同住的时候，已将这些话对你们说了。 |

## 26

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But the Comforter, which is the Holy Ghost, whom the Father will send in my name, he shall teach you all things, and bring all things to your remembrance, whatsoever I have said unto you. |
| 中文 | 但保惠师，就是父因我的名所要差来的圣灵，他要将一切的事，指教你们，并且要叫你们想起我对你们所说的一切话。 |

## 27

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Peace I leave with you, my peace I give unto you: not as the world giveth, give I unto you. Let not your heart be troubled, neither let it be afraid. |
| 中文 | 我留下平安给你们，我将我的平安赐给你们。我所赐的，不像世人所赐的。你们心里不要忧愁，也不要胆怯。 |

## 28

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ye have heard how I said unto you, I go away, and come again unto you. If ye loved me, ye would rejoice, because I said, I go unto the Father: for my Father is greater than I. |
| 中文 | 你们听见我对你们说了，我去还要到你们这里来。你们若爱我，因我到父那里去，就必喜乐，因为父是比我大的。 |

## 29

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And now I have told you before it come to pass, that, when it is come to pass, ye might believe. |
| 中文 | 现在事情还没有成就，我豫先告诉你们，叫你们到事情成就的时候，就可以信。 |

## 30

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hereafter I will not talk much with you: for the prince of this world cometh, and hath nothing in me. |
| 中文 | 以后我不再和你们多说话，因为这世界的王将到。他在我里面是毫无所有。 |

## 31

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But that the world may know that I love the Father; and as the Father gave me commandment, even so I do. Arise, let us go hence. |
| 中文 | 但要叫世人知道我爱父，并且父怎样吩咐我，我就怎样行。起来我们走吧。 |
