# Genesis 22 / 创世记 22

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And it came to pass after these things, that God did tempt Abraham, and said unto him, Abraham: and he said, Behold, here I am. |
| 中文 | 这些事以后，神要试验亚伯拉罕，就呼叫他说，亚伯拉罕，他说，我在这里。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And he said, Take now thy son, thine only son Isaac, whom thou lovest, and get thee into the land of Moriah; and offer him there for a burnt offering upon one of the mountains which I will tell thee of. |
| 中文 | 神说，你带着你的儿子，就是你独生的儿子，你所爱的以撒，往摩利亚地去，在我所要指示你的山上，把他献为燔祭。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And Abraham rose up early in the morning, and saddled his ass, and took two of his young men with him, and Isaac his son, and clave the wood for the burnt offering, and rose up, and went unto the place of which God had told him. |
| 中文 | 亚伯拉罕清早起来，备上驴，带着两个仆人和他儿子以撒，也劈好了燔祭的柴，就起身往神所指示他的地方去了。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then on the third day Abraham lifted up his eyes, and saw the place afar off. |
| 中文 | 到了第三日，亚伯拉罕举目远远地看见那地方。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 亚伯拉罕对他的仆人说，你们和驴在此等候，我与童子往那里去拜一拜，就回到你们这里来。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 亚伯拉罕把燔祭的柴放在他儿子以撒身上，自己手里拿着火与刀。于是二人同行。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 亚伯拉罕说，我儿，神必自己预备作燔祭的羊羔。于是二人同行。 |
