# Hebrews 12 / 希伯来书 12

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Wherefore seeing we also are compassed about with so great a cloud of witnesses, let us lay aside every weight, and the sin which doth so easily beset us, and let us run with patience the race that is set before us, |
| 中文 | 我们既有这许多的见证人，如同云彩围着我们，就当放下各样的重担，脱去容易缠累我们的罪，存心忍耐，奔那摆在我们前头的路程， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Looking unto Jesus the author and finisher of our faith; who for the joy that was set before him endured the cross, despising the shame, and is set down at the right hand of the throne of God. |
| 中文 | 仰望为我们信心创始成终的耶稣。（或作仰望那将真道创始成终的耶稣）他因那摆在前头的喜乐，就轻看羞辱，忍受了十字架的苦难，便坐在神宝座的右边。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For consider him that endured such contradiction of sinners against himself, lest ye be wearied and faint in your minds. |
| 中文 | 那忍受罪人这样顶撞的，你们要思想，免得疲倦灰心。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ye have not yet resisted unto blood, striving against sin. |
| 中文 | 你们与罪恶相争，还没有抵挡到留血的地步。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And ye have forgotten the exhortation which speaketh unto you as unto children, My son, despise not thou the chastening of the Lord, nor faint when thou art rebuked of him: |
| 中文 | 你们又忘了那劝你们如同劝儿子的话，说，我儿，你不可轻看主的管教，被他责备的时候，也不可灰心。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For whom the Lord loveth he chasteneth, and scourgeth every son whom he receiveth. |
| 中文 | 因为主所爱的他必管教，又鞭打凡所收纳的儿子。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If ye endure chastening, God dealeth with you as with sons; for what son is he whom the father chasteneth not? |
| 中文 | 你们所忍受的，是神管教你们，待你们如同待儿子。焉有儿子不被父亲管教的呢。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But if ye be without chastisement, whereof all are partakers, then are ye bastards, and not sons. |
| 中文 | 管教原是众子所共受的，你们若不受管教，就是私子，不是儿子了。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Furthermore we have had fathers of our flesh which corrected us, and we gave them reverence: shall we not much rather be in subjection unto the Father of spirits, and live? |
| 中文 | 再者，我们曾有生身的父管教我们，我们尚且敬重他，何况万灵的父，我们岂不更当顺服他得生吗。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For they verily for a few days chastened us after their own pleasure; but he for our profit, that we might be partakers of his holiness. |
| 中文 | 生身的父都是暂随己意管教我们。惟有万灵的父管教我们，是要我们得益处，使我们在他的圣洁上有分。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Now no chastening for the present seemeth to be joyous, but grievous: nevertheless afterward it yieldeth the peaceable fruit of righteousness unto them which are exercised thereby. |
| 中文 | 凡管教的事，当时不觉得快乐，凡觉得愁苦。后来却为那经练过的人，结出平安的果子，就是义。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 所以你们要把下垂的手，发酸的腿，梃起来。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

## 15
