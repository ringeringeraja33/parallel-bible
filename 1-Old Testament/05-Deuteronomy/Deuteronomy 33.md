# Deuteronomy 33 / 申命记 33

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And this is the blessing, wherewith Moses the man of God blessed the children of Israel before his death. |
| 中文 | 以下是神人摩西在未死之先为以色列人所祝的福， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And he said, The LORD came from Sinai, and rose up from Seir unto them; he shined forth from mount Paran, and he came with ten thousands of saints: from his right hand went a fiery law for them. |
| 中文 | 他说，耶和华从西奈而来，从西珥向他们显现，从巴兰山发出光辉，从万万圣者中来临，从他右手为百姓传出烈火的律法。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Yea, he loved the people; all his saints are in thy hand: and they sat down at thy feet; every one shall receive of thy words. |
| 中文 | 他疼爱百姓。众圣徒都在他手中。他们坐在他的脚下，领受他的言语。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Moses commanded us a law, even the inheritance of the congregation of Jacob. |
| 中文 | 摩西将律法传给我们，作为雅各会众的产业。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And he was king in Jeshurun, when the heads of the people and the tribes of Israel were gathered together. |
| 中文 | 百姓的众首领，以色列的各支派，一同聚会的时候，耶和华（原文作他）在耶书仑中为王。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let Reuben live, and not die; and let not his men be few. |
| 中文 | 愿流便存活，不致死亡。愿他人数不致稀少。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And this is the blessing of Judah: and he said, Hear, LORD, the voice of Judah, and bring him unto his people: let his hands be sufficient for him; and be thou an help to him from his enemies. |
| 中文 | 为犹大祝福说，求耶和华俯听犹大的声音，引导他归于本族。他曾用手为自己争战，你必帮助他攻击敌人。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And of Levi he said, Let thy Thummim and thy Urim be with thy holy one, whom thou didst prove at Massah, and with whom thou didst strive at the waters of Meribah; |
| 中文 | 论利未说，耶和华阿，你的土明和乌陵都在你的虔诚人那里。你在玛撒曾试验他，在米利巴水与他争论。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who said unto his father and to his mother, I have not seen him; neither did he acknowledge his brethren, nor knew his own children: for they have observed thy word, and kept thy covenant. |
| 中文 | 他论自己的父母说，我未曾看见。他也不承认弟兄，也不认识自己的儿女。这是因利未人遵行你的话，谨守你的约。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They shall teach Jacob thy judgments, and Israel thy law: they shall put incense before thee, and whole burnt sacrifice upon thine altar. |
| 中文 | 他们要将你的典章教训雅各，将你的律法教训以色列。他们要把香焚在你面前，把全牲的燔祭献在你的坛上。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Bless, LORD, his substance, and accept the work of his hands: smite through the loins of them that rise against him, and of them that hate him, that they rise not again. |
| 中文 | 求耶和华降福在他的财物上，悦纳他手里所办的事。那些起来攻击他和恨恶他的人，愿你刺透他们的腰，使他们不得再起来。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And of Benjamin he said, The beloved of the LORD shall dwell in safety by him; and the Lord shall cover him all the day long, and he shall dwell between his shoulders. |
| 中文 | 论便雅悯说，耶和华所亲爱的必同耶和华安然居住。耶和华终日遮蔽他，也住在他两肩之中。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |

| 中文 | 论约瑟说，愿他的地蒙耶和华赐福，得天上的宝物，甘露，以及地里所藏的泉水。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And for the chief things of the ancient mountains, and for the precious things of the lasting hills, |
| 中文 | 得上古之山的至宝，永世之岭的宝物。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 得地和其中所充满的宝物，并住荆棘中上主的喜悦。愿这些福都归于约瑟的头上，归于那与弟兄迥别之人的顶上。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 他为牛群中头生的，有威严。他的角是野牛的角，用以抵触万邦，直到地极。这角是以法莲的万万，玛拿西的千千。 |
