# 2 Corinthians 11 / 哥林多后书 11

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Would to God ye could bear with me a little in my folly: and indeed bear with me. |
| 中文 | 但愿你们宽容我这一点愚妄。其实你们原是宽容我的。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For I am jealous over you with godly jealousy: for I have espoused you to one husband, that I may present you as a chaste virgin to Christ. |
| 中文 | 我为你们起的愤恨，原是神那样的愤恨。因为我曾把你们许配一个丈夫，要把你们如同贞洁的童女，献给基督。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But I fear, lest by any means, as the serpent beguiled Eve through his subtilty, so your minds should be corrupted from the simplicity that is in Christ. |
| 中文 | 我只怕你们的心或偏于邪，失去那向基督所存纯一清洁的心，就像蛇用诡诈诱惑了夏娃一样。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For if he that cometh preacheth another Jesus, whom we have not preached, or if ye receive another spirit, which ye have not received, or another gospel, which ye have not accepted, ye might well bear with him. |
| 中文 | 假如有人来，另传一个耶稣，不是我们所传过的。或着你们另受一个灵，不是你们所受过的。或者另得一个福音，不是你们所得过的。你们容让他也就吧了。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For I suppose I was not a whit behind the very chiefest apostles. |
| 中文 | 但我想，我一点不在那些最大的使徒以下。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But though I be rude in speech, yet not in knowledge; but we have been throughly made manifest among you in all things. |
| 中文 | 我的言语虽然粗俗，我的知识却不粗俗。这是我们在凡事上，向你们众人显明出来的。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Have I committed an offence in abasing myself that ye might be exalted, because I have preached to you the gospel of God freely? |
| 中文 | 我因为白白传神的福音给你们，就自居卑微，叫你们高升，这算是我犯罪吗。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I robbed other churches, taking wages of them, to do you service. |
| 中文 | 我亏负了别的教会，向他们取了工价来，给你们效力。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And when I was present with you, and wanted, I was chargeable to no man: for that which was lacking to me the brethren which came from Macedonia supplied: and in all things I have kept myself from being burdensome unto you, and so will I keep myself. |
| 中文 | 我在你们那里缺乏的时候，并没有累着你们一个人。因我所缺乏的，那从马其顿来的弟兄们都补足了。我向来凡事谨守，后来也必谨守，总不至于累着你们。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | As the truth of Christ is in me, no man shall stop me of this boasting in the regions of Achaia. |
| 中文 | 既有基督的诚实在我里面，就无人能在亚该亚一带地方阻挡我这自夸。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 为什么呢。是因我不爱你们吗。这有神知道。 |

| 中文 | 我现在所作的，后来还要作，为要断绝那些寻机会人的机会，使他们在所夸的事上，也不过与我们一样。 |
