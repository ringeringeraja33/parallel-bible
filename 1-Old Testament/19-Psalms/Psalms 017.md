# Psalms 17

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hear the right, O LORD, attend unto my cry, give ear unto my prayer, that goeth not out of feigned lips. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你听闻公义，侧耳听我的呼吁。求你留心听我这不出于诡诈嘴唇的祈祷。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let my sentence come forth from thy presence; let thine eyes behold the things that are equal. |
| 中文 | 愿我的判语从你面前发出。愿你的眼睛观看公正。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou hast proved mine heart; thou hast visited me in the night; thou hast tried me, and shalt find nothing; I am purposed that my mouth shall not transgress. |
| 中文 | 你已经试验我的心。你在夜间鉴察我。你熬炼我，却找不着什么。我立志叫我口中没有过失。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Concerning the works of men, by the word of thy lips I have kept me from the paths of the destroyer. |
| 中文 | 论到人的行为，我藉着你嘴唇的言语，自己谨守，不行强暴人的道路。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hold up my goings in thy paths, that my footsteps slip not. |
| 中文 | 我的脚踏定了你的路径，我的两脚未曾滑跌。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I have called upon thee, for thou wilt hear me, O God: incline thine ear unto me, and hear my speech. |
| 中文 | 神阿，我曾求告你，因为你必应允我。求你向我侧耳，听我的言语。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Shew thy marvellous lovingkindness, O thou that savest by thy right hand them which put their trust in thee from those that rise up against them. |
| 中文 | 求你显出你奇妙的慈爱来。你是那用右手拯救投靠你的，脱离起来攻击他们的人。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Keep me as the apple of the eye, hide me under the shadow of thy wings, |
| 中文 | 求你保护我，如同保护眼中的瞳人。将我隐藏在你翅膀的荫下， |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | From the wicked that oppress me, from my deadly enemies, who compass me about. |
| 中文 | 使我脱离那欺压我的恶人，就是围困我，要害我命的仇敌。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They are inclosed in their own fat: with their mouth they speak proudly. |
| 中文 | 他们的心被脂油包裹。他们用口说骄傲的话。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They have now compassed us in our steps: they have set their eyes bowing down to the earth; |
| 中文 | 他们围困了我们的脚步。他们瞪着眼，要把我们推倒在地。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Like as a lion that is greedy of his prey, and as it were a young lion lurking in secret places. |
| 中文 | 他像狮子急要抓食，又像少壮狮子蹲伏在暗处。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Arise, O LORD, disappoint him, cast him down: deliver my soul from the wicked, which is thy sword: |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你起来，前去迎敌，将他打倒。用你的刀救护我命脱离恶人。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | From men which are thy hand, O LORD, from men of the world, which have their portion in this life, and whose belly thou fillest with thy hid treasure: they are full of children, and leave the rest of their substance to their babes. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你用手救我脱离世人，脱离那只在今生有福分的世人。你把你的财宝充满他们的肚腹。他们因有儿女就心满意足，将其馀的财物留给他们的婴孩。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | As for me, I will behold thy face in righteousness: I shall be satisfied, when I awake, with thy likeness. |
| 中文 | 至于我，我必在义中见你的面。我醒了的时候，得见你的形像，就心满意足了。 |
