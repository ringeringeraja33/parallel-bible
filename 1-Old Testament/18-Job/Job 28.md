# Job 28

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Surely there is a vein for the silver, and a place for gold where they fine it. |
| 中文 | 银子有矿，炼金有方。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Iron is taken out of the earth, and brass is molten out of the stone. |
| 中文 | 铁从地里挖出，铜从石中熔化。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He setteth an end to darkness, and searcheth out all perfection: the stones of darkness, and the shadow of death. |
| 中文 | 人为黑暗定界限，查究幽暗阴翳的石头，直到极处。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The flood breaketh out from the inhabitant; even the waters forgotten of the foot: they are dried up, they are gone away from men. |
| 中文 | 在无人居住之处刨开矿穴，过路的人也想不到他们。又与人远离，悬在空中摇来摇去。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | As for the earth, out of it cometh bread: and under it is turned up as it were fire. |
| 中文 | 至于地，能出粮食，地内好像被火翻起来。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The stones of it are the place of sapphires: and it hath dust of gold. |
| 中文 | 地中的石头有蓝宝石，并有金沙。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | There is a path which no fowl knoweth, and which the vulture's eye hath not seen: |
| 中文 | 矿中的路鸷鸟不得知道，鹰眼也未见过。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The lion's whelps have not trodden it, nor the fierce lion passed by it. |
| 中文 | 狂傲的野兽未曾行过。猛烈的狮子也未曾经过。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He putteth forth his hand upon the rock; he overturneth the mountains by the roots. |
| 中文 | 人伸手凿开坚石，倾倒山根。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He cutteth out rivers among the rocks; and his eye seeth every precious thing. |
| 中文 | 在磐石中凿出水道，亲眼看见各样宝物。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He bindeth the floods from overflowing; and the thing that is hid bringeth he forth to light. |
| 中文 | 他封闭水不得滴流，使隐藏的物显露出来。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But where shall wisdom be found? and where is the place of understanding? |
| 中文 | 然而，智慧有何处可寻。聪明之处在那里呢。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Man knoweth not the price thereof; neither is it found in the land of the living. |
| 中文 | 智慧的价值无人能知，在活人之地也无处可寻。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The depth saith, It is not in me: and the sea saith, It is not with me. |
| 中文 | 深渊说，不在我内。沧海说，不在我中。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | It cannot be gotten for gold, neither shall silver be weighed for the price thereof. |
| 中文 | 智慧非用黄金可得，也不能平白银为它的价值。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | It cannot be valued with the gold of Ophir, with the precious onyx, or the sapphire. |
| 中文 | 俄斐金，和贵重的红玛瑙，并蓝宝石，不足与较量。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 黄金，和玻璃，不足与比较。精金的器皿，不足与兑换。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 珊瑚，水晶都不足论。智慧的价值胜过珍珠（或作红宝石）。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 古实的红璧玺，不足与比较。精金，也不足与较量。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 智慧从何处来呢。聪明之处在那里呢。 |
