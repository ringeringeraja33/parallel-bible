# Psalms 141 / 诗篇 141

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Lord, I cry unto thee: make haste unto me; give ear unto my voice, when I cry unto thee. |
| 中文 | （大卫的诗）耶和华阿，我曾求告你，求你快快临到我这里。我求告你的时候，愿你留心听我的声音。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let my prayer be set forth before thee as incense; and the lifting up of my hands as the evening sacrifice. |
| 中文 | 愿我的祷告，如香陈列在你面前。愿我举手祈求，如献晚祭。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Set a watch, O LORD, before my mouth; keep the door of my lips. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你禁止我的口，把守我的嘴。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Incline not my heart to any evil thing, to practise wicked works with men that work iniquity: and let me not eat of their dainties. |
| 中文 | 求你不叫我的心，偏向邪恶，以致我和作孽的人同行恶事。也不叫我吃他们的美食。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let the righteous smite me; it shall be a kindness: and let him reprove me; it shall be an excellent oil, which shall not break my head: for yet my prayer also shall be in their calamities. |
| 中文 | 任凭义人击打我，这算为仁慈。任凭他责备我，这算为头上的膏油。我的头不要躲闪。正在他们行恶的时候，我仍要祈祷。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When their judges are overthrown in stony places, they shall hear my words; for they are sweet. |
| 中文 | 他们的审判官，被扔在岩下。众人要听我的话，因为这话甘甜。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Our bones are scattered at the grave's mouth, as when one cutteth and cleaveth wood upon the earth. |
| 中文 | 我们的骨头散在墓旁，好像人耕田，刨地的土块。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But mine eyes are unto thee, O GOD the Lord: in thee is my trust; leave not my soul destitute. |
| 中文 | 主耶和华阿，我的眼目仰望你。我投靠你，求你不要将我撇得孤苦。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Keep me from the snares which they have laid for me, and the gins of the workers of iniquity. |
| 中文 | 求你保护我脱离恶人为我设的网罗，和作孽之人的圈套。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let the wicked fall into their own nets, whilst that I withal escape. |
| 中文 | 愿恶人落在自己的网里，我却得以逃脱。 |
