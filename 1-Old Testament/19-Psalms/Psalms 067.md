# Psalms 67

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | God be merciful unto us, and bless us; and cause his face to shine upon us; Selah. |
| 中文 | （一篇诗歌，交与伶长。用丝弦的乐器）愿神怜悯我们，赐福与我们，用脸光照我们。（细拉） |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | That thy way may be known upon earth, thy saving health among all nations. |
| 中文 | 好叫世界得知你的道路，万国得知你的救恩。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let the people praise thee, O God; let all the people praise thee. |
| 中文 | 神阿，愿列邦称赞你，愿万民都称赞你。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O let the nations be glad and sing for joy: for thou shalt judge the people righteously, and govern the nations upon earth. Selah. |
| 中文 | 愿万国都快乐欢呼。因为你必按公正审判万民，引导世上的万国。（细拉） |
