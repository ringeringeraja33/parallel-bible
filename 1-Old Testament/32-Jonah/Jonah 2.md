# Jonah 2 / 约拿书 2

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then Jonah prayed unto the LORD his God out of the fish's belly, |
| 中文 | 约拿在鱼腹中祷告耶和华他的神， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And said, I cried by reason of mine affliction unto the LORD, and he heard me; out of the belly of hell cried I, and thou heardest my voice. |
| 中文 | 说，我遭遇患难求告耶和华，你就应允我。从阴间的深处呼求，你就俯听我的声音。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thou hadst cast me into the deep, in the midst of the seas; and the floods compassed me about: all thy billows and thy waves passed over me. |
| 中文 | 你将我投下深渊，就是海的深处。大水环绕我。你的波浪洪涛都漫过我身。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then I said, I am cast out of thy sight; yet I will look again toward thy holy temple. |
| 中文 | 我说，我从你眼前虽被驱逐，我仍要仰望你的圣殿。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The waters compassed me about, even to the soul: the depth closed me round about, the weeds were wrapped about my head. |
| 中文 | 诸水环绕我，几乎淹没我。深渊围住我，海草缠绕我的头。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I went down to the bottoms of the mountains; the earth with her bars was about me for ever: yet hast thou brought up my life from corruption, O LORD my God. |
| 中文 | 我下到山根。地的门将我永远关住。耶和华我的神阿，你却将我的性命，从坑中救出来。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When my soul fainted within me I remembered the LORD: and my prayer came in unto thee, into thine holy temple. |
| 中文 | 我心在我里面发昏的时候，我就想念耶和华。我的祷告进入你的圣殿，达到你的面前。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They that observe lying vanities forsake their own mercy. |
| 中文 | 那信奉虚无之神的人，离弃怜爱他们的主。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But I will sacrifice unto thee with the voice of thanksgiving; I will pay that that I have vowed. Salvation is of the LORD. |
| 中文 | 但我必用感谢的声音献祭与你。我所许的愿，我必偿还。救恩出于耶和华。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the LORD spake unto the fish, and it vomited out Jonah upon the dry land. |
| 中文 | 耶和华吩咐鱼，鱼就把约拿吐在旱地上。 |
