# Psalms 9 / 诗篇 9

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will praise thee, O LORD, with my whole heart; I will shew forth all thy marvellous works. |
| 中文 | 我要一心称谢耶和华。我要传扬你一切奇妙的作为。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will be glad and rejoice in thee: I will sing praise to thy name, O thou most High. |
| 中文 | 我要因你欢喜快乐。至高者阿，我要歌颂你的名。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When mine enemies are turned back, they shall fall and perish at thy presence. |
| 中文 | 我的仇敌转身退去的时候，他们一见你的面，就跌倒灭亡。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thou hast maintained my right and my cause; thou satest in the throne judging right. |
| 中文 | 因你已经为我伸冤，为我辨屈。你坐在宝座上，按公义审判。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou hast rebuked the heathen, thou hast destroyed the wicked, thou hast put out their name for ever and ever. |
| 中文 | 你曾斥责外邦。你曾灭绝恶人。你曾涂抹他们名，直到永永远远。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O thou enemy, destructions are come to a perpetual end: and thou hast destroyed cities; their memorial is perished with them. |
| 中文 | 仇敌到了尽头。他们被毁坏，直到永远。你拆毁他们的城邑。连他们的名号，都归于无有。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But the LORD shall endure for ever: he hath prepared his throne for judgment. |
| 中文 | 惟耶和华坐着为王，直到永远。他已经为审判设摆他的宝座。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And he shall judge the world in righteousness, he shall minister judgment to the people in uprightness. |
| 中文 | 他要按公义审判世界，按正直判断万民。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD also will be a refuge for the oppressed, a refuge in times of trouble. |
| 中文 | 耶和华又要给受欺压的人作高台，在患难的时候作高台。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And they that know thy name will put their trust in thee: for thou, LORD, hast not forsaken them that seek thee. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，认识你名的人要倚靠你。因你没有离弃寻求你的人。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Sing praises to the LORD, which dwelleth in Zion: declare among the people his doings. |
| 中文 | 应当歌颂居锡安的耶和华，将他所行的传扬在众民中。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When he maketh inquisition for blood, he remembereth them: he forgetteth not the cry of the humble. |
| 中文 | 因为那追讨流人血之罪的，他记念受屈的人，不忘记困苦人的哀求。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Have mercy upon me, O LORD; consider my trouble which I suffer of them that hate me, thou that liftest me up from the gates of death: |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，你是从死门把我提拔起来的。求你怜恤我。看那恨我的人所加给我的苦难。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | That I may shew forth all thy praise in the gates of the daughter of Zion: I will rejoice in thy salvation. |
| 中文 | 好叫我述说你一切的美德。我必在锡安城的门因你的救恩欢乐。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The heathen are sunk down in the pit that they made: in the net which they hid is their own foot taken. |
| 中文 | 外邦人陷在自己所掘的坑中。他们的脚，在自己暗设的网罗里缠住了。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD is known by the judgment which he executeth: the wicked is snared in the work of his own hands. Higgaion. Selah. |
| 中文 | 耶和华已将自己显明了，他已施行审判。恶人被自己手所作的缠住了。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The wicked shall be turned into hell, and all the nations that forget God. |
| 中文 | 恶人，就是忘记神的外邦人，都必归到阴间。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the needy shall not alway be forgotten: the expectation of the poor shall not perish for ever. |
| 中文 | 穷乏人必不永久被忘，困苦人的指望，必不永远落空。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Arise, O LORD; let not man prevail: let the heathen be judged in thy sight. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你起来，不容人得胜。愿外邦人在你面前受审判。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Put them in fear, O LORD: that the nations may know themselves to be but men. Selah. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你使外邦人恐惧。愿他们知道自己不过是人。（细拉） |
