# Psalms 87 / 诗篇 87

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | His foundation is in the holy mountains. |
| 中文 | （可拉后裔的诗歌）耶和华所立的根基在圣山上。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD loveth the gates of Zion more than all the dwellings of Jacob. |
| 中文 | 他爱锡安的门，胜于爱雅各一切的住处。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Glorious things are spoken of thee, O city of God. Selah. |
| 中文 | 神的城阿，有荣耀的事乃指着你说的。（细拉） |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will make mention of Rahab and Babylon to them that know me: behold Philistia, and Tyre, with Ethiopia; this man was born there. |
| 中文 | 我要题起拉哈伯，和巴比伦人是在认识我之中的，看哪，非利士，和推罗，并古实人，个个生在那里。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And of Zion it shall be said, This and that man was born in her: and the highest himself shall establish her. |
| 中文 | 论到锡安必说，这一个那一个都生在其中。而且至高者必亲自坚立这城。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD shall count, when he writeth up the people, that this man was born there. Selah. |
| 中文 | 当耶和华记录万民的时候，他要点出这一个生在那里。（细拉） |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | As well the singers as the players on instruments shall be there: all my springs are in thee. |
| 中文 | 歌唱的，跳舞的，都要说，我的泉源都在你这里。 |
