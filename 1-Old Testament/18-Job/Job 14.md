# Job 14 / 约伯记 14

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Man that is born of a woman is of few days, and full of trouble. |
| 中文 | 人为妇人所生，日子短少，多有患难。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He cometh forth like a flower, and is cut down: he fleeth also as a shadow, and continueth not. |
| 中文 | 出来如花，又被割下。飞去如影，不能存留。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And dost thou open thine eyes upon such an one, and bringest me into judgment with thee? |
| 中文 | 这样的人你岂睁眼看他吗。又叫我来受审吗。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who can bring a clean thing out of an unclean? not one. |
| 中文 | 谁能使洁净之物出于污秽之中呢。无论谁也不能。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Seeing his days are determined, the number of his months are with thee, thou hast appointed his bounds that he cannot pass; |
| 中文 | 人的日子既然限定，他的月数在你那里，你也派定他的界限，使他不能越过。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Turn from him, that he may rest, till he shall accomplish, as an hireling, his day. |
| 中文 | 便求你转眼不看他，使他得歇息。直等他像雇工人完毕他的日子。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For there is hope of a tree, if it be cut down, that it will sprout again, and that the tender branch thereof will not cease. |
| 中文 | 树若被砍下，还可指望发芽，嫩枝生长不息。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Though the root thereof wax old in the earth, and the stock thereof die in the ground; |
| 中文 | 其根虽然衰老在地里，干也死在土中。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Yet through the scent of water it will bud, and bring forth boughs like a plant. |
| 中文 | 及至得了水气，还要发芽，又长枝条，像新栽的树一样。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But man dieth, and wasteth away: yea, man giveth up the ghost, and where is he? |
| 中文 | 但人死亡而消灭。他气绝，竟在何处呢。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | As the waters fail from the sea, and the flood decayeth and drieth up: |
| 中文 | 海中的水绝尽，江河消散乾涸。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | So man lieth down, and riseth not: till the heavens be no more, they shall not awake, nor be raised out of their sleep. |
| 中文 | 人也是如此，躺下不再起来。等到天没有了，仍不得复醒，也不得从睡中唤醒。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O that thou wouldest hide me in the grave, that thou wouldest keep me secret, until thy wrath be past, that thou wouldest appoint me a set time, and remember me! |
| 中文 | 惟愿你把我藏在阴间，存于隐密处，等你的忿怒过去。愿你为我定了日期，记念我。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If a man die, shall he live again? all the days of my appointed time will I wait, till my change come. |
| 中文 | 人若死了岂能再活呢。我只要在我一切争战的日子，等我被释放（被释放或作改变）的时候来到。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt call, and I will answer thee: thou wilt have a desire to the work of thine hands. |
| 中文 | 你呼叫，我便回答。你手所做的，你必羡慕。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For now thou numberest my steps: dost thou not watch over my sin? |
| 中文 | 但如今你数点我的脚步，岂不窥察我的罪过吗。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My transgression is sealed up in a bag, and thou sewest up mine iniquity. |
| 中文 | 我的过犯被你封在囊中，也缝严了我的罪孽。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And surely the mountain falling cometh to nought, and the rock is removed out of his place. |
| 中文 | 山崩变为无有。磐石挪开原处。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The waters wear the stones: thou washest away the things which grow out of the dust of the earth; and thou destroyest the hope of man. |
| 中文 | 水流消磨石头，所流溢的洗去地上的尘土。你也照样灭绝人的指望。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou prevailest for ever against him, and he passeth: thou changest his countenance, and sendest him away. |
| 中文 | 你攻击人常常得胜，使他去世。你改变他的容貌，叫他往而不回。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | His sons come to honour, and he knoweth it not; and they are brought low, but he perceiveth it not of them. |
| 中文 | 他儿子得尊荣，他也不知道。降为卑，他也不觉得。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But his flesh upon him shall have pain, and his soul within him shall mourn. |
| 中文 | 但知身上疼痛，心中悲哀。 |
