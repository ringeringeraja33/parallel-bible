# 1 Corinthians 12

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Now concerning spiritual gifts, brethren, I would not have you ignorant. |
| 中文 | 弟兄们，论到属灵的恩赐，我不愿意你们不明白。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ye know that ye were Gentiles, carried away unto these dumb idols, even as ye were led. |
| 中文 | 你们作外邦人的时候，随事被牵引受迷惑，去服事那哑吧偶像。这是你们知道的。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Wherefore I give you to understand, that no man speaking by the Spirit of God calleth Jesus accursed: and that no man can say that Jesus is the Lord, but by the Holy Ghost. |
| 中文 | 所以我告诉你们，被神的灵感动的，没有说耶稣是可咒诅的。若不是被圣灵感动的，也没有能说耶稣是主的。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Now there are diversities of gifts, but the same Spirit. |
| 中文 | 恩赐原有分别，圣灵却是一位。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And there are differences of administrations, but the same Lord. |
| 中文 | 职事也有分别，主却是一位。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And there are diversities of operations, but it is the same God which worketh all in all. |
| 中文 | 功用也有分别，神却是一位，在众人里面运行一切的事。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But the manifestation of the Spirit is given to every man to profit withal. |
| 中文 | 圣灵显在各人身上，是叫人得益处。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For to one is given by the Spirit the word of wisdom; to another the word of knowledge by the same Spirit; |
| 中文 | 这人蒙圣灵赐他智慧的言语。那人也蒙这位圣灵赐他知识的言语。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | To another faith by the same Spirit; to another the gifts of healing by the same Spirit; |
| 中文 | 又有一人蒙这位圣灵赐他信心。还有一人蒙这位圣灵赐他医病的恩赐。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | To another the working of miracles; to another prophecy; to another discerning of spirits; to another divers kinds of tongues; to another the interpretation of tongues: |
| 中文 | 又有一人能行异能。又叫一人能作先知。又叫一人能辨别诸灵。又叫一人能说方言。又叫一人能翻方言。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But all these worketh that one and the selfsame Spirit, dividing to every man severally as he will. |
| 中文 | 这一切都是这位圣灵所运行，随己意分给各人的。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For as the body is one, and hath many members, and all the members of that one body, being many, are one body: so also is Christ. |
| 中文 | 就如身子是一个，却有许多肢体。而且肢体虽多，仍是一个身子。基督也是这样。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For by one Spirit are we all baptized into one body, whether we be Jews or Gentiles, whether we be bond or free; and have been all made to drink into one Spirit. |
| 中文 | 我们不拘是犹太人，是希腊人，是为奴的，是自主的，都从一位圣灵受洗，成了一个身体。饮于一位圣灵。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the body is not one member, but many. |
| 中文 | 身子原不是一个肢体，乃是许多肢体。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If the foot shall say, Because I am not the hand, I am not of the body; is it therefore not of the body? |
| 中文 | 设若脚说，我不是手，所以不属乎身子。他不能因此就不属乎身子。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And if the ear shall say, Because I am not the eye, I am not of the body; is it therefore not of the body? |
| 中文 | 设若耳说，我不是眼，所以不属乎身子。他也不能因此就不属乎身子。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If the whole body were an eye, where were the hearing? If the whole were hearing, where were the smelling? |
| 中文 | 若全身是眼，从那里听声呢。若全身是耳，从那里闻味呢。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But now hath God set the members every one of them in the body, as it hath pleased him. |
| 中文 | 但如今神随自己的意思，把肢体俱各安排在身上。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And if they were all one member, where were the body? |
| 中文 | 若都是一个肢体，身子在那里呢。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But now are they many members, yet but one body. |
| 中文 | 但如今肢体是多的，身子却是一个。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the eye cannot say unto the hand, I have no need of thee: nor again the head to the feet, I have no need of you. |
| 中文 | 眼不能对手说，我用不着你。头也不能对脚说，我用不着你。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Nay, much more those members of the body, which seem to be more feeble, are necessary: |
| 中文 | 不但如此，身上肢体人以为软弱的，更是不可少的。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And those members of the body, which we think to be less honourable, upon these we bestow more abundant honour; and our uncomely parts have more abundant comeliness. |
| 中文 | 身上肢体，我们看为不体面的，越发给他加上体面。不俊美的，越发得着俊美。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For our comely parts have no need: but God hath tempered the body together, having given more abundant honour to that part which lacked: |
| 中文 | 我们俊美的肢体，自然用不着装饰。但神配搭这身子，把加倍的体面给那有缺欠的肢体。 |

## 25

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | That there should be no schism in the body; but that the members should have the same care one for another. |
| 中文 | 免得身上分门别类。总要肢体彼此相顾。 |

## 26

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 若一个肢体受苦，所有的肢体就一同受苦。若一个肢体得荣耀，所有的肢体就一同快乐。 |

## 27

| Language | Content |

| 中文 | 你们就是基督的身子，并且各作肢体。 |
