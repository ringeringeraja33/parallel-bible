# Revelation 14

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And I looked, and, lo, a Lamb stood on the mount Sion, and with him an hundred forty and four thousand, having his Father's name written in their foreheads. |
| 中文 | 我又观看，见羔羊站在锡安山，同他又有十四万四千人，都有他的名，和他父的名，写在额上。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And I heard a voice from heaven, as the voice of many waters, and as the voice of a great thunder: and I heard the voice of harpers harping with their harps: |
| 中文 | 我听见从天上有声音，像众水的声音，和大雷的声音。并且我所听见的好像弹琴的所弹的琴声。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And they sung as it were a new song before the throne, and before the four beasts, and the elders: and no man could learn that song but the hundred and forty and four thousand, which were redeemed from the earth. |
| 中文 | 他们在宝座前，并在四活物和众长老前唱歌，彷佛是新歌。除了从地上买来的那十四万四千人以外，没有人能学这歌。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | These are they which were not defiled with women; for they are virgins. These are they which follow the Lamb whithersoever he goeth. These were redeemed from among men, being the firstfruits unto God and to the Lamb. |
| 中文 | 这些人未曾沾染妇女，他们原是童身。羔羊无论往那里去，他们都跟随他。他们是从人间买来的，作初熟的果子归与神和羔羊。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 在他们口中察不出谎言来。他们是没有瑕疵的。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
