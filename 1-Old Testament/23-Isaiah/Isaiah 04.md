# Isaiah 4 / 以赛亚书 4

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And in that day seven women shall take hold of one man, saying, We will eat our own bread, and wear our own apparel: only let us be called by thy name, to take away our reproach. |
| 中文 | 在那日，七个女人必拉住一个男人，说，我们吃自己的食物，穿自己的衣服。但求你许我们归你名下。求你除掉我们的羞耻。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | In that day shall the branch of the LORD be beautiful and glorious, and the fruit of the earth shall be excellent and comely for them that are escaped of Israel. |
| 中文 | 到那日，耶和华发生的苗，必华美尊荣，地的出产，必为以色列逃脱的人显为荣华茂盛。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And it shall come to pass, that he that is left in Zion, and he that remaineth in Jerusalem, shall be called holy, even every one that is written among the living in Jerusalem: |
| 中文 | 主以公义的灵，和焚烧的灵，将锡安女子的污秽洗去，又将耶路撒冷中杀人的血除净， |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When the Lord shall have washed away the filth of the daughters of Zion, and shall have purged the blood of Jerusalem from the midst thereof by the spirit of judgment, and by the spirit of burning. |
| 中文 | *那时，剩在锡安留在耶路撒冷的，就是一切住耶路撒冷，在生命册上记名的，必称为圣。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the LORD will create upon every dwelling place of mount Zion, and upon her assemblies, a cloud and smoke by day, and the shining of a flaming fire by night: for upon all the glory shall be a defence. |
| 中文 | 耶和华也必在锡安全山，并各会众以上，使白日有烟云，黑夜有火焰的光。因为在全荣耀之上必有遮蔽。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And there shall be a tabernacle for a shadow in the day time from the heat, and for a place of refuge, and for a covert from storm and from rain. |
| 中文 | 必有亭子，白日可以得荫避暑，也可以作为藏身之处，躲避狂风暴雨。 |
