# Job 13 / 约伯记 13

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Lo, mine eye hath seen all this, mine ear hath heard and understood it. |
| 中文 | 这一切，我眼都见过。我耳都听过，而且明白。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | What ye know, the same do I know also: I am not inferior unto you. |
| 中文 | 你们所知道的，我也知道，并非不及你们。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Surely I would speak to the Almighty, and I desire to reason with God. |
| 中文 | 我真要对全能者说话。我愿与神理论。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But ye are forgers of lies, ye are all physicians of no value. |
| 中文 | 你们是编造谎言的，都是无用的医生。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O that ye would altogether hold your peace! and it should be your wisdom. |
| 中文 | 惟愿你们全然不作声。这就算为你们的智慧。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hear now my reasoning, and hearken to the pleadings of my lips. |
| 中文 | 请你们听我的辩论，留心听我口中的分诉。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Will ye speak wickedly for God? and talk deceitfully for him? |
| 中文 | 你们要为神说不义的话吗，为他说诡诈的言语吗。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Will ye accept his person? will ye contend for God? |
| 中文 | 你们要为神徇情吗，要为他争论吗。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Is it good that he should search you out? or as one man mocketh another, do ye so mock him? |
| 中文 | 他查出你们来，这岂是好吗。人欺哄人，你们也要照样欺哄他吗。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He will surely reprove you, if ye do secretly accept persons. |
| 中文 | 你们若暗中徇情，他必要责备你们。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Shall not his excellency make you afraid? and his dread fall upon you? |
| 中文 | 他的尊荣岂不叫你们惧怕吗。他的惊吓岂不临到你们吗。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Your remembrances are like unto ashes, your bodies to bodies of clay. |
| 中文 | 你们以为可记念的箴言是炉灰的箴言。你们以为可靠的坚垒是淤泥的坚垒。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hold your peace, let me alone, that I may speak, and let come on me what will. |
| 中文 | 你们不要作声，任凭我吧。让我说话，无论如何我都承当。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Wherefore do I take my flesh in my teeth, and put my life in mine hand? |
| 中文 | 我何必把我的肉挂在牙上，将我的命放在手中。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Though he slay me, yet will I trust in him: but I will maintain mine own ways before him. |
| 中文 | 他必杀我。我虽无指望，然而我在他面前还要辩明我所行的。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He also shall be my salvation: for an hypocrite shall not come before him. |
| 中文 | 这要成为我的拯救，因为不虔诚的人，不得到他面前。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hear diligently my speech, and my declaration with your ears. |
| 中文 | 你们要细听我的言语，使我所辩论的入你们的耳中。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold now, I have ordered my cause; I know that I shall be justified. |
| 中文 | 我已陈明我的案，知道自己有义。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who is he that will plead with me? for now, if I hold my tongue, I shall give up the ghost. |
| 中文 | 有谁与我争论，我就情愿缄默不言，气绝而亡。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 惟有两件不要向我施行，我就不躲开你的面。 |

| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 就是把你的手缩回，远离我身。又不使你的惊惶威吓我。 |

## 22

| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 这样，你呼叫，我就回答。或是让我说话，你回答我。 |
