# Psalms 85 / 诗篇 85

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Lord, thou hast been favourable unto thy land: thou hast brought back the captivity of Jacob. |
| 中文 | （可拉后裔的诗，交与伶长）耶和华阿，你已经向你的地施恩，救回被掳的雅各。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou hast forgiven the iniquity of thy people, thou hast covered all their sin. Selah. |
| 中文 | 你赦免了你百姓的罪孽，遮盖了他们一切的过犯。（细拉） |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou hast taken away all thy wrath: thou hast turned thyself from the fierceness of thine anger. |
| 中文 | 你收转了所发的忿怒，和你猛烈的怒气。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Turn us, O God of our salvation, and cause thine anger toward us to cease. |
| 中文 | 拯救我们的神阿，求你使我们回转，叫你的恼恨向我们止息。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Wilt thou be angry with us for ever? wilt thou draw out thine anger to all generations? |
| 中文 | 你要向我们发怒到永远吗，你要将你的怒气延留到万代吗。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Wilt thou not revive us again: that thy people may rejoice in thee? |
| 中文 | 你不再将我们救活，使你的百姓靠你欢喜吗。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Shew us thy mercy, O LORD, and grant us thy salvation. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你使我们得见你的慈爱，又将你的救恩赐给我们。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will hear what God the LORD will speak: for he will speak peace unto his people, and to his saints: but let them not turn again to folly. |
| 中文 | 我要听神耶和华所说的话。因为他必应许将平安赐给他的百姓，他的圣民。他们却不可再转去妄行。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Surely his salvation is nigh them that fear him; that glory may dwell in our land. |
| 中文 | 他的救恩，诚然与敬畏他的人相近，叫荣耀住在我们的地上。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Mercy and truth are met together; righteousness and peace have kissed each other. |
| 中文 | 慈爱和诚实，彼此相遇。公义和平安，彼此相亲。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Truth shall spring out of the earth; and righteousness shall look down from heaven. |
| 中文 | 诚实从地而生，公义从天而现。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Yea, the LORD shall give that which is good; and our land shall yield her increase. |
| 中文 | 耶和华必将好处赐给我们。我们的地，也要多出土产。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Righteousness shall go before him; and shall set us in the way of his steps. |
| 中文 | 公义要行在他面前，叫他的脚踪，成为可走的路。 |
