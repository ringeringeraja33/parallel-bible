# Job 24 / 约伯记 24

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Why, seeing times are not hidden from the Almighty, do they that know him not see his days? |
| 中文 | 全能者既定期罚恶，为何不使认识他的人看见那日子呢。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Some remove the landmarks; they violently take away flocks, and feed thereof. |
| 中文 | 有人挪移地界，抢夺群畜而牧养。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They drive away the ass of the fatherless, they take the widow's ox for a pledge. |
| 中文 | 他们拉去孤儿的驴，强取寡妇的牛为当头。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They turn the needy out of the way: the poor of the earth hide themselves together. |
| 中文 | 他们使穷人离开正道，世上的贫民尽都隐藏。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, as wild asses in the desert, go they forth to their work; rising betimes for a prey: the wilderness yieldeth food for them and for their children. |
| 中文 | 这些贫穷人，如同野驴出到旷野，殷勤寻梢食物。他们靠着野地给儿女糊口， |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They reap every one his corn in the field: and they gather the vintage of the wicked. |
| 中文 | 收割别人田间的禾稼，摘取恶人馀剩的葡萄。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They cause the naked to lodge without clothing, that they have no covering in the cold. |
| 中文 | 终夜赤身无衣，天气寒冷毫无遮盖， |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They are wet with the showers of the mountains, and embrace the rock for want of a shelter. |
| 中文 | 在山上被大雨淋湿，因没有避身之处就挨近磐石。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They pluck the fatherless from the breast, and take a pledge of the poor. |
| 中文 | 又有人从母怀中抢夺孤儿，强取穷人的衣服为当头。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They cause him to go naked without clothing, and they take away the sheaf from the hungry; |
| 中文 | 使人赤身无衣，到处流行，且因饥饿扛抬禾捆， |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Which make oil within their walls, and tread their winepresses, and suffer thirst. |
| 中文 | 在那些人的围墙内造油，榨酒，自己还口渴。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Men groan from out of the city, and the soul of the wounded crieth out: yet God layeth not folly to them. |
| 中文 | 在多民的城内有人唉哼，受伤的人哀号。神却不理会（那恶人的愚妄。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They are of those that rebel against the light; they know not the ways thereof, nor abide in the paths thereof. |
| 中文 | 又有人背弃光明，不认识光明的道，不住在光明的路上。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The murderer rising with the light killeth the poor and needy, and in the night is as a thief. |
| 中文 | 杀人的黎明起来，杀害困苦穷乏人，夜间又作盗贼。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The eye also of the adulterer waiteth for the twilight, saying, No eye shall see me: and disguiseth his face. |
| 中文 | 奸夫等候黄昏，说，必无眼能见我，就把脸蒙蔽。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | In the dark they dig through houses, which they had marked for themselves in the daytime: they know not the light. |
| 中文 | 盗贼黑夜挖窟窿，白日躲藏，并不认识光明。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the morning is to them even as the shadow of death: if one know them, they are in the terrors of the shadow of death. |
| 中文 | 他们看早晨如幽暗，因为他们晓得幽暗的惊骇。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He is swift as the waters; their portion is cursed in the earth: he beholdeth not the way of the vineyards. |
| 中文 | 这些恶人犹如浮萍快快飘去。他们所得的分在世上被咒诅。他们不得再走葡萄园的路。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Drought and heat consume the snow waters: so doth the grave those which have sinned. |
| 中文 | 乾旱炎热消没雪水，阴间也如此消没犯罪之辈。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The womb shall forget him; the worm shall feed sweetly on him; he shall be no more remembered; and wickedness shall be broken as a tree. |
| 中文 | 怀他的母（原文是胎）要忘记他。虫子要吃他，觉得甘甜。他不再被人记念。不义的人必如树折断。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He evil entreateth the barren that beareth not: and doeth not good to the widow. |
| 中文 | 他恶待（或作他吞灭）不怀孕不生养的妇人，不善待寡妇。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He draweth also the mighty with his power: he riseth up, and no man is sure of life. |
| 中文 | 然而神用能力保全有势力的人，那性命难保的人仍然兴起。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Though it be given him to be in safety, whereon he resteth; yet his eyes are upon their ways. |
| 中文 | 神使他们安稳，他们就有所倚靠。神的眼目也看顾他们的道路。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They are exalted for a little while, but are gone and brought low; they are taken out of the way as all other, and cut off as the tops of the ears of corn. |
| 中文 | 他们被高举，不过片时就没有了。他们降为卑，被除灭，与众人一样，又如谷穗被割。 |

## 25

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And if it be not so now, who will make me a liar, and make my speech nothing worth? |
| 中文 | 若不是这样，谁能证实我是说谎的，将我的言语驳为虚空呢。 |
