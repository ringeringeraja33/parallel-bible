# Psalms 134

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, bless ye the LORD, all ye servants of the LORD, which by night stand in the house of the LORD. |
| 中文 | （上行之诗）耶和华的仆人，夜间站在耶和华殿中的，你们当称颂耶和华。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Lift up your hands in the sanctuary, and bless the LORD. |
| 中文 | 你们当向圣所举手，称颂耶和华。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD that made heaven and earth bless thee out of Zion. |
| 中文 | 愿造天地的耶和华，从锡安赐福给你们。 |
