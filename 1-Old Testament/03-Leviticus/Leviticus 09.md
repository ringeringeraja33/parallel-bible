# Leviticus 9 / 利未记 9

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And it came to pass on the eighth day, that Moses called Aaron and his sons, and the elders of Israel; |
| 中文 | 到了第八天，摩西召了亚伦和他儿子，并以色列的众长老来， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And he said unto Aaron, Take thee a young calf for a sin offering, and a ram for a burnt offering, without blemish, and offer them before the LORD. |
| 中文 | 对亚伦说，你当取牛群中的一只公牛犊作赎罪祭，一只公绵羊作燔祭，都要没有残疾的，献在耶和华面前。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And unto the children of Israel thou shalt speak, saying, Take ye a kid of the goats for a sin offering; and a calf and a lamb, both of the first year, without blemish, for a burnt offering; |
| 中文 | 你也要对以色列人说，你们当取一只公山羊作赎罪祭，又取一只牛犊和一只绵羊羔，都要一岁，没有残疾的，作燔祭， |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Also a bullock and a ram for peace offerings, to sacrifice before the LORD; and a meat offering mingled with oil: for to day the LORD will appear unto you. |
| 中文 | 又取一只公牛，一只公绵羊作平安祭，献在耶和华面前，并取调油的素祭，因为今天耶和华要向你们显现。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And they brought that which Moses commanded before the tabernacle of the congregation: and all the congregation drew near and stood before the LORD. |
| 中文 | 于是他们把摩西所吩咐的，带到会幕前，全会众都近前来，站在耶和华面前。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And Moses said, This is the thing which the LORD commanded that ye should do: and the glory of the LORD shall appear unto you. |
| 中文 | 摩西说，这是耶和华吩咐你们所当行的，耶和华的荣光就要向你们显现。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And Moses said unto Aaron, Go unto the altar, and offer thy sin offering, and thy burnt offering, and make an atonement for thyself, and for the people: and offer the offering of the people, and make an atonement for them; as the LORD commanded. |
| 中文 | 摩西对亚伦说，你就近坛前，献你的赎罪祭和燔祭，为自己与百姓赎罪，又献上百姓的供物，为他们赎罪，都照耶和华所吩咐的。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Aaron therefore went unto the altar, and slew the calf of the sin offering, which was for himself. |
| 中文 | 于是，亚伦就近坛前，宰了为自己作赎罪祭的牛犊。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the sons of Aaron brought the blood unto him: and he dipped his finger in the blood, and put it upon the horns of the altar, and poured out the blood at the bottom of the altar: |
| 中文 | 亚伦的儿子把血奉给他，他就把指头蘸在血中，抹在坛的四角上，又把血倒在坛脚那里。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But the fat, and the kidneys, and the caul above the liver of the sin offering, he burnt upon the altar; as the LORD commanded Moses. |
| 中文 | 惟有赎罪祭的脂油和腰子，并肝上取的网子，都烧在坛上，是照耶和华所吩咐摩西的。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the flesh and the hide he burnt with fire without the camp. |
| 中文 | 又用火将肉和皮烧在营外。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And he slew the burnt offering; and Aaron's sons presented unto him the blood, which he sprinkled round about upon the altar. |
| 中文 | 亚伦宰了燔祭牲，他儿子把血递给他，他就洒在坛的周围， |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And they presented the burnt offering unto him, with the pieces thereof, and the head: and he burnt them upon the altar. |
| 中文 | 又把燔祭一块一块地，连头递给他，他都烧在坛上。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 又洗了脏腑和腿，烧在坛上的燔祭上。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And he brought the people's offering, and took the goat, which was the sin offering for the people, and slew it, and offered it for sin, as the first. |
| 中文 | 他奉上百姓的供物，把那给百姓作赎罪祭的公山羊宰了，为罪献上，和先献的一样。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| --- | --- |

## 19

| --- | --- |
