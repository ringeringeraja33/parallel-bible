# 1 Corinthians 7 / 哥林多前书 7

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Now concerning the things whereof ye wrote unto me: It is good for a man not to touch a woman. |
| 中文 | 论到你们信上所题的事，我说男不近女倒好。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Nevertheless, to avoid fornication, let every man have his own wife, and let every woman have her own husband. |
| 中文 | 但要免淫乱的事，男人当各有自己的妻子，女子也当各有自己的丈夫。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let the husband render unto the wife due benevolence: and likewise also the wife unto the husband. |
| 中文 | 丈夫当用合宜之分待妻子，妻子待丈夫也要如此。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The wife hath not power of her own body, but the husband: and likewise also the husband hath not power of his own body, but the wife. |
| 中文 | 妻子没有权柄主张自己的身子，乃在丈夫。丈夫也没有权柄主张自己的身子，乃在妻子。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Defraud ye not one the other, except it be with consent for a time, that ye may give yourselves to fasting and prayer; and come together again, that Satan tempt you not for your incontinency. |
| 中文 | 夫妻不可彼此亏负，除非两相情愿，暂时分房，为要专心祷告方可，以后仍要同房，免得撒但趁着你们情不自禁，引诱你们。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But I speak this by permission, and not of commandment. |
| 中文 | 我说这话，原是准你们的，不是命你们的。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For I would that all men were even as I myself. But every man hath his proper gift of God, one after this manner, and another after that. |
| 中文 | 我愿意众人像我一样。只是各人领受神的恩赐，一个是这样，一个是那样。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I say therefore to the unmarried and widows, It is good for them if they abide even as I. |
| 中文 | 我对着没有嫁娶的和寡妇说，若他们常像我就好。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But if they cannot contain, let them marry: for it is better to marry than to burn. |
| 中文 | 倘若自己禁止不住，就可以嫁娶。与其欲火攻心，倒不如嫁娶为妙。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And unto the married I command, yet not I, but the Lord, Let not the wife depart from her husband: |
| 中文 | 至于那已经嫁娶的，我吩咐他们，其实不是我吩咐，乃是主吩咐，说，妻子不可离开丈夫。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But and if she depart, let her remain unmarried, or be reconciled to her husband: and let not the husband put away his wife. |
| 中文 | 若是离开了，不可再嫁。或是同丈夫和好。丈夫也不可离弃妻子。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But to the rest speak I, not the Lord: If any brother hath a wife that believeth not, and she be pleased to dwell with him, let him not put her away. |
| 中文 | 我对其馀的人说，不是主说，倘若某弟兄有不信的妻子，妻子也情愿和他住，他就不要离弃妻子。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the woman which hath an husband that believeth not, and if he be pleased to dwell with her, let her not leave him. |
| 中文 | 妻子有不信的丈夫，丈夫也情愿和他同住，他就不要离弃丈夫。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the unbelieving husband is sanctified by the wife, and the unbelieving wife is sanctified by the husband: else were your children unclean; but now are they holy. |
| 中文 | 因为不信的丈夫，就因着妻子成了圣洁。并且不信的妻子，就因着丈夫成了圣洁。（丈夫原文作弟兄）不然，你们的儿女就不洁净。但如今他们是圣洁的了。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But if the unbelieving depart, let him depart. A brother or a sister is not under bondage in such cases: but God hath called us to peace. |
| 中文 | 倘若那不信的人要离去，就由他离去吧。无论是弟兄，是姐妹，遇着这样的事，都不必拘束。神召我们原是要我们和睦。 |

## 16

| 中文 | 你这作妻子的，怎吗知道不能救你的丈夫呢。你这作丈夫的，怎吗知道不能救你的妻子呢。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 只要照主所分给各人的，和神所召各人的而行。我吩咐各教会都是这样。 |
