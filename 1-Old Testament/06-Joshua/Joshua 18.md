# Joshua 18

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the whole congregation of the children of Israel assembled together at Shiloh, and set up the tabernacle of the congregation there. And the land was subdued before them. |
| 中文 | 以色列的全会众都聚集在示罗，把会幕设立在那里，那地已经被他们制伏了。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And there remained among the children of Israel seven tribes, which had not yet received their inheritance. |
| 中文 | 以色列人中其馀的七个支派还没有分给他们地业。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And Joshua said unto the children of Israel, How long are ye slack to go to possess the land, which the LORD God of your fathers hath given you? |
| 中文 | 约书亚对以色列人说，耶和华你们列祖的神所赐给你们的地，你们耽延不去得，要到几时呢。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Give out from among you three men for each tribe: and I will send them, and they shall rise, and go through the land, and describe it according to the inheritance of them; and they shall come again to me. |
| 中文 | 你们每支派当选举三个人，我要打发他们去，他们就要起身走遍那地，按着各支派应得的地业写明（或作画图），就回到我这里来。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And they shall divide it into seven parts: Judah shall abide in their coast on the south, and the house of Joseph shall abide in their coasts on the north. |
| 中文 | 他们要将地分作七分。犹大仍在南方，住在他的境内。约瑟家仍在北方，住在他的境内。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ye shall therefore describe the land into seven parts, and bring the description hither to me, that I may cast lots for you here before the LORD our God. |
| 中文 | 你们要将地分作七分，写明了拿到我这里来。我要在耶和华我们神面前，为你们拈阄。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But the Levites have no part among you; for the priesthood of the LORD is their inheritance: and Gad, and Reuben, and half the tribe of Manasseh, have received their inheritance beyond Jordan on the east, which Moses the servant of the LORD gave them. |
| 中文 | 利未人在你们中间没有分，因为供耶和华祭司的职任就是他们的产业。迦得支派，流便支派，和玛拿西半支派已经在约旦河东得了地业，就是耶和华仆人摩西所给他们的。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the men arose, and went away: and Joshua charged them that went to describe the land, saying, Go and walk through the land, and describe it, and come again to me, that I may here cast lots for you before the LORD in Shiloh. |
| 中文 | 划地势的人起身去的时候，约书亚嘱咐他们说，你们去走遍那地，划明地势，就回到我这里来。我要在示罗这里，耶和华面前，为你们拈阄。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the men went and passed through the land, and described it by cities into seven parts in a book, and came again to Joshua to the host at Shiloh. |
| 中文 | 他们就去了，走遍那地，按着城邑分作七分，写在册子上，回到示罗营中见约书亚。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 约书亚就在示罗，耶和华面前，为他们拈阄。约书亚在那里，按着以色列人的支派，将地分给他们。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| Language | Content |

## 13
