# Matthew 20 / 马太福音 20

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the kingdom of heaven is like unto a man that is an householder, which went out early in the morning to hire labourers into his vineyard. |
| 中文 | 因为天国好像家主，清早去雇人，进他的葡萄园作工。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And when he had agreed with the labourers for a penny a day, he sent them into his vineyard. |
| 中文 | 和工人讲定一天一银子，就打发他们进葡萄园去。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And he went out about the third hour, and saw others standing idle in the marketplace, |
| 中文 | 约在巳初出去，看见市上还有闲站的人。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And said unto them; Go ye also into the vineyard, and whatsoever is right I will give you. And they went their way. |
| 中文 | 就对他们说，你们也进葡萄园去，所当给的，我必给你们。他们也进去了。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Again he went out about the sixth and ninth hour, and did likewise. |
| 中文 | 约在午正和申初又出去，也是这样行。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And about the eleventh hour he went out, and found others standing idle, and saith unto them, Why stand ye here all the day idle? |
| 中文 | 约在酉初出去，看见还有人站在那里。就问他们说，你们为什么整天在这里闲站呢。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They say unto him, Because no man hath hired us. He saith unto them, Go ye also into the vineyard; and whatsoever is right, that shall ye receive. |
| 中文 | 他们说，因为没有人雇我们。他说，你们也进葡萄园去。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | So when even was come, the lord of the vineyard saith unto his steward, Call the labourers, and give them their hire, beginning from the last unto the first. |
| 中文 | 到了晚上，园主对管事的说，叫工人都来，给他们工钱，从后来的起，到先来的为止。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And when they came that were hired about the eleventh hour, they received every man a penny. |
| 中文 | 约在酉初雇来的人来了，各人得了一钱银子。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But when the first came, they supposed that they should have received more; and they likewise received every man a penny. |
| 中文 | 及至那先雇的来了，他们以为必要多得。谁知也是各得一钱。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And when they had received it, they murmured against the goodman of the house, |
| 中文 | 他们得了，就埋怨家主说， |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Saying, These last have wrought but one hour, and thou hast made them equal unto us, which have borne the burden and heat of the day. |
| 中文 | 我们整天劳苦受热，那后来的只做了一小时，你竟叫他们和我们一样吗。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But he answered one of them, and said, Friend, I do thee no wrong: didst not thou agree with me for a penny? |
| 中文 | 家主回答其中的一人说，朋友，我不亏负你。你与我讲定的，不是一钱银子吗。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Take that thine is, and go thy way: I will give unto this last, even as unto thee. |
| 中文 | 拿你的走吧。我给那后来的和给你一样，这是我愿意的。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Is it not lawful for me to do what I will with mine own? Is thine eye evil, because I am good? |
| 中文 | 我的东西难道不可随我的意思用吗。因为我作好人，你就红了眼吗。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | So the last shall be first, and the first last: for many be called, but few chosen. |
| 中文 | 这样，那在后的将要在前，在前的将要在后了。（有古卷在此有因为被召的人多，选上的人少） |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 耶稣上耶路撒冷去的时候，在路上把十二个门徒带到一边对他们说， |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
