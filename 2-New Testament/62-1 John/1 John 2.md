# 1 John 2 / 约翰一书 2

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My little children, these things write I unto you, that ye sin not. And if any man sin, we have an advocate with the Father, Jesus Christ the righteous: |
| 中文 | 我小子们哪，我将这些话写给你们，是要叫你们不犯罪。若有人犯罪，在父那里我们有一位中保，就是那义者耶稣基督。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And he is the propitiation for our sins: and not for ours only, but also for the sins of the whole world. |
| 中文 | 他为我们的罪作了挽回祭。不是单为我们的罪，也是为普天下人的罪。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And hereby we do know that we know him, if we keep his commandments. |
| 中文 | 我们若遵守他的诫命，就晓得是认识他。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that saith, I know him, and keepeth not his commandments, is a liar, and the truth is not in him. |
| 中文 | 人若说我认识他，却不遵守他的诫命，便是说谎话的。真里也不在他心里了。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But whoso keepeth his word, in him verily is the love of God perfected: hereby know we that we are in him. |
| 中文 | 凡遵守主道的，爱神的心在他里面实在是完全的，从此我们知道我们是在主里面。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that saith he abideth in him ought himself also so to walk, even as he walked. |
| 中文 | 人若说他住在主里面，就该自己照主所行的去行。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Brethren, I write no new commandment unto you, but an old commandment which ye had from the beginning. The old commandment is the word which ye have heard from the beginning. |
| 中文 | 亲爱的弟兄阿，我写给你们的，不是一条新命令，乃是你们从起初所受的旧命令。这旧命令就是你们所听见的道。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Again, a new commandment I write unto you, which thing is true in him and in you: because the darkness is past, and the true light now shineth. |
| 中文 | 再者，我写给你们的，是一条新命令，在主是真的，在你们也是真的。因为黑暗渐渐过去，真光已经照耀。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that saith he is in the light, and hateth his brother, is in darkness even until now. |
| 中文 | 人若说自己在光明中。却恨他的弟兄，他到如今还是在黑暗里。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that loveth his brother abideth in the light, and there is none occasion of stumbling in him. |
| 中文 | 爱弟兄的就是住在光明中，在他并没有绊跌的缘由。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But he that hateth his brother is in darkness, and walketh in darkness, and knoweth not whither he goeth, because that darkness hath blinded his eyes. |
| 中文 | 唯独恨弟兄的是在黑暗里，且在黑暗里行，也不知道往那里去，因为黑暗叫他眼睛瞎了。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I write unto you, little children, because your sins are forgiven you for his name's sake. |
| 中文 | 小子们哪，我写信给你们，因为你们的罪藉着主名得了赦免。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I write unto you, fathers, because ye have known him that is from the beginning. I write unto you, young men, because ye have overcome the wicked one. I write unto you, little children, because ye have known the Father. |
| 中文 | 父老阿，我写信给你们，因为你们认识那从起初原有的。少年人哪，我写信给你们，因为你们胜了那恶者。小子们哪，我曾写信给你们，因为你们认识父。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I have written unto you, fathers, because ye have known him that is from the beginning. I have written unto you, young men, because ye are strong, and the word of God abideth in you, and ye have overcome the wicked one. |
| 中文 | 父老阿，我曾写信给你们，因为你们认识那从起初原有的。少年人哪，我曾写信给你们，因为你们刚强，神的道常存在你们心里，你们也胜了那恶者。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Love not the world, neither the things that are in the world. If any man love the world, the love of the Father is not in him. |
| 中文 | 不要爱世界，和世界上的事。人若爱世界，爱父的心就不在他里面了。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For all that is in the world, the lust of the flesh, and the lust of the eyes, and the pride of life, is not of the Father, but is of the world. |
| 中文 | 因为凡世界上的事，就像肉体的情欲，眼目的情欲，并今生的骄傲，都不是从父来的，乃是从世界来的。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the world passeth away, and the lust thereof: but he that doeth the will of God abideth for ever. |
| 中文 | 这世界，和其上的情欲，都要过去。唯独遵行神旨意的，是永远常存。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Little children, it is the last time: and as ye have heard that antichrist shall come, even now are there many antichrists; whereby we know that it is the last time. |
| 中文 | 小子们哪，如今是末时了。你们曾听见说，那敌基督的要来现在已经有好些敌基督的出来了。从此我们就知道如今是末时了。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
