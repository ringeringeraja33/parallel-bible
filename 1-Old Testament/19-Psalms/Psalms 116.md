# Psalms 116

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I love the LORD, because he hath heard my voice and my supplications. |
| 中文 | 我爱耶和华，因为他听了我的声音，和我的恳求。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Because he hath inclined his ear unto me, therefore will I call upon him as long as I live. |
| 中文 | 他既向我侧耳，我一生要求告他。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The sorrows of death compassed me, and the pains of hell gat hold upon me: I found trouble and sorrow. |
| 中文 | 死亡的绳索缠绕我，阴间的痛苦抓住我。我遭遇患难愁苦。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then called I upon the name of the LORD; O LORD, I beseech thee, deliver my soul. |
| 中文 | 那时，我便求告耶和华的名，说，耶和华阿，求你救我的灵魂。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Gracious is the LORD, and righteous; yea, our God is merciful. |
| 中文 | 耶和华有恩惠，有公义。我们的神以怜悯为怀。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD preserveth the simple: I was brought low, and he helped me. |
| 中文 | 耶和华保护愚人。我落到卑微的地步，他救了我。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Return unto thy rest, O my soul; for the LORD hath dealt bountifully with thee. |
| 中文 | 我的心哪，你要仍归安乐，因为耶和华用厚恩待你。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thou hast delivered my soul from death, mine eyes from tears, and my feet from falling. |
| 中文 | 主阿，你救我的命，免了死亡，救我的眼，免了流泪，救我的脚，免了跌倒。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will walk before the LORD in the land of the living. |
| 中文 | 我要在耶和华面前，行活人之路。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I believed, therefore have I spoken: I was greatly afflicted: |
| 中文 | 我因信，所以如此说话。我受了极大的困苦。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I said in my haste, All men are liars. |
| 中文 | 我曾急促地说，人都是说谎的。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | What shall I render unto the LORD for all his benefits toward me? |
| 中文 | 我拿什么报答耶和华向我所赐的一切厚恩。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 我要举起救恩的杯，称扬耶和华的名。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 我要在他众民面前向耶和华还我的愿。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Precious in the sight of the LORD is the death of his saints. |
| 中文 | 在耶和华眼中，看圣民之死，极为宝贵。 |

| 中文 | 耶和华阿，我真是你的仆人。我是你的仆人，是你婢女的儿子。你已经解开我的绑索。 |
