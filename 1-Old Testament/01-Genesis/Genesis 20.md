# Genesis 20 / 创世记 20

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And Abraham journeyed from thence toward the south country, and dwelled between Kadesh and Shur, and sojourned in Gerar. |
| 中文 | 亚伯拉罕从那里向南地迁去，寄居在加低斯和书珥中间的基拉耳。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And Abraham said of Sarah his wife, She is my sister: and Abimelech king of Gerar sent, and took Sarah. |
| 中文 | 亚伯拉罕称他的妻撒拉为妹子，基拉耳王亚比米勒差人把撒拉取了去。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But God came to Abimelech in a dream by night, and said to him, Behold, thou art but a dead man, for the woman which thou hast taken; for she is a man's wife. |
| 中文 | 但夜间神来在梦中对亚比米勒说，你是个死人哪，因为你取了那女人来。她原是别人的妻子。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But Abimelech had not come near her: and he said, LORD, wilt thou slay also a righteous nation? |
| 中文 | 亚比米勒却还没有亲近撒拉。他说，主阿，连有义的国，你也要毁灭吗。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Said he not unto me, She is my sister? and she, even she herself said, He is my brother: in the integrity of my heart and innocency of my hands have I done this. |
| 中文 | 那人岂不是自己对我说，她是我的妹子吗。就是女人也自己说，他是我的哥哥。我作这事是心正手洁的。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And God said unto him in a dream, Yea, I know that thou didst this in the integrity of thy heart; for I also withheld thee from sinning against me: therefore suffered I thee not to touch her. |
| 中文 | 神在梦中对他说，我知道你作这事是心中正直。我也拦阻了你，免得你得罪我，所以我不容你沾着她。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Now therefore restore the man his wife; for he is a prophet, and he shall pray for thee, and thou shalt live: and if thou restore her not, know thou that thou shalt surely die, thou, and all that are thine. |
| 中文 | 现在你把这人的妻子归还他。因为他是先知，他要为你祷告，使你存活。你若不归还他，你当知道，你和你所有的人都必要死。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Therefore Abimelech rose early in the morning, and called all his servants, and told all these things in their ears: and the men were sore afraid. |
| 中文 | 亚比米勒清早起来，召了众臣仆来，将这些事都说给他们听，他们都甚惧怕。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then Abimelech called Abraham, and said unto him, What hast thou done unto us? and what have I offended thee, that thou hast brought on me and on my kingdom a great sin? thou hast done deeds unto me that ought not to be done. |
| 中文 | 亚比米勒召了亚伯拉罕来，对他说，你怎吗向我这样行呢。我在什么事上得罪了你，你竟使我和我国里的人陷在大罪里。你向我行不当行的事了。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And Abimelech said unto Abraham, What sawest thou, that thou hast done this thing? |
| 中文 | 亚比米勒又对亚伯拉罕说，你见了什么才作这事呢。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 亚伯拉罕说，我以为这地方的人总不惧怕神，必为我妻子的缘故杀我。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |

| 中文 | 况且她也实在是我的妹子。她与我是同父异母，后来作了我的妻子。 |
