# Psalms 124

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If it had not been the LORD who was on our side, now may Israel say; |
| 中文 | （大卫上行之诗）以色列人要说，若不是耶和华帮助我们， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If it had not been the LORD who was on our side, when men rose up against us: |
| 中文 | 若不是耶和华帮助我们，当人起来攻击我们， |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then they had swallowed us up quick, when their wrath was kindled against us: |
| 中文 | 向我们发怒的时候，就把我们活活地吞了。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then the waters had overwhelmed us, the stream had gone over our soul: |
| 中文 | 那时，波涛必漫过我们，河水必淹没我们。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then the proud waters had gone over our soul. |
| 中文 | 狂傲的水必淹没我们。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Blessed be the LORD, who hath not given us as a prey to their teeth. |
| 中文 | 耶和华是应当称颂的。他没有把我们当野食交给他们吞吃（原文作牙齿）。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Our soul is escaped as a bird out of the snare of the fowlers: the snare is broken, and we are escaped. |
| 中文 | 我们好像雀鸟，从捕鸟人的网罗里逃脱。网罗破裂，我们逃脱了。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Our help is in the name of the LORD, who made heaven and earth. |
| 中文 | 我们得帮助，是在乎倚靠造天地之耶和华的名。 |
