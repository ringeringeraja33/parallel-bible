# Psalms 63

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O God, thou art my God; early will I seek thee: my soul thirsteth for thee, my flesh longeth for thee in a dry and thirsty land, where no water is; |
| 中文 | （大卫在犹大旷野的时候，作了这诗）神阿，你是我的神，我要切切的寻求你。在乾旱疲乏无水之地，我渴想你，我的心切慕你。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | To see thy power and thy glory, so as I have seen thee in the sanctuary. |
| 中文 | 我在圣所中曾如此瞻仰你，为要见你的能力，和你的荣耀。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Because thy lovingkindness is better than life, my lips shall praise thee. |
| 中文 | 因你的慈爱比生命更好，我的嘴唇要颂赞你。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thus will I bless thee while I live: I will lift up my hands in thy name. |
| 中文 | 我还活的时候，要这样称颂你。我要奉你的名举手。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My soul shall be satisfied as with marrow and fatness; and my mouth shall praise thee with joyful lips: |
| 中文 | 我在床上记念你，在夜更的时候思想你，我的心就像饱足了骨髓肥油。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When I remember thee upon my bed, and meditate on thee in the night watches. |
| 中文 | 我也要以欢乐的嘴唇赞美你。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Because thou hast been my help, therefore in the shadow of thy wings will I rejoice. |
| 中文 | 因为你曾帮助我，我就在你翅膀的荫下欢呼。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My soul followeth hard after thee: thy right hand upholdeth me. |
| 中文 | 我心紧紧的跟随你。你的右手扶持我。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But those that seek my soul, to destroy it, shall go into the lower parts of the earth. |
| 中文 | 但那些寻索要灭我命的人，必往地底下去。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They shall fall by the sword: they shall be a portion for foxes. |
| 中文 | 他们必被刀剑所杀，被野狗所吃。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But the king shall rejoice in God; every one that sweareth by him shall glory: but the mouth of them that speak lies shall be stopped. |
| 中文 | 但是王必因神欢喜。凡指着他发誓的，必要夸口。因为说谎之人的口，必被塞住。 |
