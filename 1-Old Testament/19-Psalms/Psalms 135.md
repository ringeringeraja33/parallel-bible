# Psalms 135 / 诗篇 135

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Praise ye the LORD. Praise ye the name of the LORD; praise him, O ye servants of the LORD. |
| 中文 | 你们要赞美耶和华。你们要赞美耶和华的名。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ye that stand in the house of the LORD, in the courts of the house of our God, |
| 中文 | 耶和华的仆人站在耶和华殿中，站在我们神殿院中的，你们要赞美他。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Praise the LORD; for the LORD is good: sing praises unto his name; for it is pleasant. |
| 中文 | 你们要赞美耶和华。耶和华本为善。要歌颂他的名，因为这是美好的。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the LORD hath chosen Jacob unto himself, and Israel for his peculiar treasure. |
| 中文 | 耶和华拣选雅各归自己，拣选以色列特作自己的子民。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For I know that the LORD is great, and that our Lord is above all gods. |
| 中文 | 原来我知道耶和华为大，也知道我们的主超乎万神之上。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Whatsoever the LORD pleased, that did he in heaven, and in earth, in the seas, and all deep places. |
| 中文 | 耶和华在天上，在地下，在海中，在一切的深处，都随自己的意旨而行。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He causeth the vapours to ascend from the ends of the earth; he maketh lightnings for the rain; he bringeth the wind out of his treasuries. |
| 中文 | 他使云雾从地极上腾，造电随雨而闪，从府库中带出风来。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who smote the firstborn of Egypt, both of man and beast. |
| 中文 | 他将埃及头生的，连人带牲畜都击杀了。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who sent tokens and wonders into the midst of thee, O Egypt, upon Pharaoh, and upon all his servants. |
| 中文 | 埃及阿，他施行神迹奇事，在你当中，在法老和他一切臣仆身上。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who smote great nations, and slew mighty kings; |
| 中文 | 他击杀许多的民，又杀戮大能的王， |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Sihon king of the Amorites, and Og king of Bashan, and all the kingdoms of Canaan: |
| 中文 | 就是亚摩利王西宏，和巴珊王噩，并迦南一切的国王， |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And gave their land for an heritage, an heritage unto Israel his people. |
| 中文 | 将他们的地，赏赐他的百姓以色列为业。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thy name, O LORD, endureth for ever; and thy memorial, O LORD, throughout all generations. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，你的名存到永远。耶和华阿，你可记念的名存到万代。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the LORD will judge his people, and he will repent himself concerning his servants. |
| 中文 | 耶和华要为他的百姓伸冤，为他的仆人后悔。 |

| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 外邦的偶像是金的，银的，是人手所造的。 |
