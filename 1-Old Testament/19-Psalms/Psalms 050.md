# Psalms 50 / 诗篇 50

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The mighty God, even the LORD, hath spoken, and called the earth from the rising of the sun unto the going down thereof. |
| 中文 | （亚萨的诗）大能者神耶和华，已经发言招呼天下，从日出之地到日落之处。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Out of Zion, the perfection of beauty, God hath shined. |
| 中文 | 从全美的锡安中，神已经发光了。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Our God shall come, and shall not keep silence: a fire shall devour before him, and it shall be very tempestuous round about him. |
| 中文 | 我们的神要来，决不闭口。有烈火在他们面前吞灭，有暴风在他四围大刮。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He shall call to the heavens from above, and to the earth, that he may judge his people. |
| 中文 | 他招呼上天下地，为要审判他的民， |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Gather my saints together unto me; those that have made a covenant with me by sacrifice. |
| 中文 | 说，招聚我的圣民到我这里来，就是那些用祭物与我立约的人。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the heavens shall declare his righteousness: for God is judge himself. Selah. |
| 中文 | 诸天必表明他的公义。因为神是施行审判的。（细拉） |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hear, O my people, and I will speak; O Israel, and I will testify against thee: I am God, even thy God. |
| 中文 | 我的民哪，你们当听我的话。以色列阿，我要劝戒你。我是神，是你的神。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will not reprove thee for thy sacrifices or thy burnt offerings, to have been continually before me. |
| 中文 | 我并不因你的祭物责备你。你的燔祭常在我面前。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will take no bullock out of thy house, nor he goats out of thy folds. |
| 中文 | 我不从你家中取公牛，也不从你圈内取山羊。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For every beast of the forest is mine, and the cattle upon a thousand hills. |
| 中文 | 因为树林中的百兽是我的，千山的牲畜也是我的。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I know all the fowls of the mountains: and the wild beasts of the field are mine. |
| 中文 | 山中的飞鸟，我都知道。野地的走兽，也都属我。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If I were hungry, I would not tell thee: for the world is mine, and the fulness thereof. |
| 中文 | 我若是饥饿，我不用告诉你。因为世界，和其中所充满的，都是我的。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Will I eat the flesh of bulls, or drink the blood of goats? |
| 中文 | 我岂吃公牛的肉呢。我岂喝山羊的血呢。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Offer unto God thanksgiving; and pay thy vows unto the most High: |
| 中文 | 你们要以感谢为祭献与神。又要向至高者还你的愿。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And call upon me in the day of trouble: I will deliver thee, and thou shalt glorify me. |
| 中文 | 并要在患难之日求告我，我必搭救你。你也要荣耀我。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But unto the wicked God saith, What hast thou to do to declare my statutes, or that thou shouldest take my covenant in thy mouth? |
| 中文 | 但神对恶人说，你怎敢传说我的律例，口中题到我的约呢。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Seeing thou hatest instruction, and casteth my words behind thee. |
| 中文 | 其实你恨恶管教，将我的言语丢在背后。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When thou sawest a thief, then thou consentedst with him, and hast been partaker with adulterers. |
| 中文 | 你见了盗贼，就乐意与他同夥，又与行奸淫的人，一同有分。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou givest thy mouth to evil, and thy tongue frameth deceit. |
| 中文 | 你口任说恶言，你舌编造诡诈。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou sittest and speakest against thy brother; thou slanderest thine own mother's son. |
| 中文 | 你坐着毁谤你的兄弟，谗毁你亲母的儿子。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | These things hast thou done, and I kept silence; thou thoughtest that I was altogether such an one as thyself: but I will reprove thee, and set them in order before thine eyes. |
| 中文 | 你行了这些事，我还闭口不言。你想我恰和你一样。其实我要责备你，将这些事摆在你眼前。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Now consider this, ye that forget God, lest I tear you in pieces, and there be none to deliver. |
| 中文 | 你们忘记神的，要思想这事，免得我把你们撕碎，无人搭救。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Whoso offereth praise glorifieth me: and to him that ordereth his conversation aright will I shew the salvation of God. |
| 中文 | 凡以剐谢献上为祭的，便是荣耀我。那按正路而行的，我便使他得着我的救恩。 |
