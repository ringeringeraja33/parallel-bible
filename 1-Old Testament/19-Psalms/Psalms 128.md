# Psalms 128

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Blessed is every one that feareth the LORD; that walketh in his ways. |
| 中文 | （上行之诗）凡敬畏耶和华，遵行他道的人便为有福。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thou shalt eat the labour of thine hands: happy shalt thou be, and it shall be well with thee. |
| 中文 | 你要吃劳碌得来的。你要享福，事情顺利。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thy wife shall be as a fruitful vine by the sides of thine house: thy children like olive plants round about thy table. |
| 中文 | 你妻子在你的内室，好像多结果子的葡萄树。你儿女围绕你的桌子，好像橄榄栽子。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, that thus shall the man be blessed that feareth the LORD. |
| 中文 | 看哪，敬畏耶和华的人，必要这样蒙福。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD shall bless thee out of Zion: and thou shalt see the good of Jerusalem all the days of thy life. |
| 中文 | 愿耶和华从锡安赐福给你。愿你一生一世，看见耶路撒冷的好处。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Yea, thou shalt see thy children's children, and peace upon Israel. |
| 中文 | 愿你看见你儿女的儿女。愿平安归于以色列。 |
