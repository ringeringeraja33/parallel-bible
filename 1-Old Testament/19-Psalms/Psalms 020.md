# Psalms 20

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD hear thee in the day of trouble; the name of the God of Jacob defend thee; |
| 中文 | 愿耶和华在你遭难的日子应允你。愿名为雅各神的高举你。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Send thee help from the sanctuary, and strengthen thee out of Zion; |
| 中文 | 愿他从圣所救助你，从锡安坚固你。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Remember all thy offerings, and accept thy burnt sacrifice; Selah. |
| 中文 | 记念你的一切供献，悦纳你的燔祭。（细拉） |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Grant thee according to thine own heart, and fulfil all thy counsel. |
| 中文 | 将你心所愿的赐给你，成就你的一切筹算。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | We will rejoice in thy salvation, and in the name of our God we will set up our banners: the LORD fulfil all thy petitions. |
| 中文 | 我们要因你的救恩夸胜，要奉我们神的名竖立旌旗。愿耶和华成就你一切所求的。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Now know I that the LORD saveth his anointed; he will hear him from his holy heaven with the saving strength of his right hand. |
| 中文 | 现在我知道耶和华救护他的受膏者，必从他的圣天上应允他，用右手的能力救护他。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Some trust in chariots, and some in horses: but we will remember the name of the LORD our God. |
| 中文 | 有人靠车，有人靠马。但我们要题到耶和华我们神的名。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They are brought down and fallen: but we are risen, and stand upright. |
| 中文 | 他们都屈身仆倒，我们却起来，立得正直。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Save, LORD: let the king hear us when we call. |
| 中文 | 求耶和华施行拯救。我们呼求的时候，愿王应允我们。 |
