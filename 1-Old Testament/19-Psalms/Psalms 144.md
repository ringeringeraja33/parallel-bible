# Psalms 144

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Blessed be the LORD my strength, which teacheth my hands to war, and my fingers to fight: |
| 中文 | （大卫的诗）耶和华我的磐石，是应当称颂的。他教导我的手争战，教导我的指头打仗。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My goodness, and my fortress; my high tower, and my deliverer; my shield, and he in whom I trust; who subdueth my people under me. |
| 中文 | 他是我慈爱的主，我的山寨，我的高台，我的救主，我的盾牌，是我所投靠的。他使我的百姓服在我以下。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | LORD, what is man, that thou takest knowledge of him! or the son of man, that thou makest account of him! |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，人算什么，你竟认识他。世人算什么，你竟顾念他。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Man is like to vanity: his days are as a shadow that passeth away. |
| 中文 | 人好像一口气。他的年日，如同影儿快快过去。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Bow thy heavens, O LORD, and come down: touch the mountains, and they shall smoke. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你使天下垂，亲自降临，摸山，山就冒烟。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Cast forth lightning, and scatter them: shoot out thine arrows, and destroy them. |
| 中文 | 求你发出闪电，使他们四散，射出你的箭，使他们扰乱。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Send thine hand from above; rid me, and deliver me out of great waters, from the hand of strange children; |
| 中文 | 求你从上伸手救拔我，救我出离大水，救我脱离外邦人的手。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Whose mouth speaketh vanity, and their right hand is a right hand of falsehood. |
| 中文 | 他们的口说谎话。他们的右手起假誓。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will sing a new song unto thee, O God: upon a psaltery and an instrument of ten strings will I sing praises unto thee. |
| 中文 | 神阿，我要向你唱新歌，用十弦瑟向你歌颂。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | It is he that giveth salvation unto kings: who delivereth David his servant from the hurtful sword. |
| 中文 | 你是那拯救君王的。你是那救仆人大卫脱离害命之刀的。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Rid me, and deliver me from the hand of strange children, whose mouth speaketh vanity, and their right hand is a right hand of falsehood: |
| 中文 | 求你救拔我，救我脱离外邦人的手。他们的口说谎话。他们的右手起假誓。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | That our sons may be as plants grown up in their youth; that our daughters may be as corner stones, polished after the similitude of a palace: |
| 中文 | 我们的儿子，从幼年好像树栽子长大。我们的女儿如同殿角石，是按建宫的样式凿成的。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | That our garners may be full, affording all manner of store: that our sheep may bring forth thousands and ten thousands in our streets: |
| 中文 | 我们的仓盈满，能出各样的粮食。我们的羊，在田间孳生千万。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | That our oxen may be strong to labour; that there be no breaking in, nor going out; that there be no complaining in our streets. |
| 中文 | 我们的牛驮着满驮，没有人闯进来抢夺，也没有人出去争战。我们的街市上，也没有哭号的声音。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Happy is that people, that is in such a case: yea, happy is that people, whose God is the LORD. |
| 中文 | 遇见这光景的百姓，便为有福。有耶和华为他们的神，这百姓便为有福。 |
