# Psalms 142

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I cried unto the LORD with my voice; with my voice unto the LORD did I make my supplication. |
| 中文 | （大卫在洞里作的训诲诗，乃是祈祷）我发声哀告耶和华，发声恳求耶和华。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I poured out my complaint before him; I shewed before him my trouble. |
| 中文 | 我在他面前吐露我的苦情，陈说我的患难。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When my spirit was overwhelmed within me, then thou knewest my path. In the way wherein I walked have they privily laid a snare for me. |
| 中文 | 我的灵在我里面发昏的时候，你知道我的道路。在我行的路上，敌人为我暗设网罗。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I looked on my right hand, and beheld, but there was no man that would know me: refuge failed me; no man cared for my soul. |
| 中文 | 求你向我右边观看，因为没有人认识我。我无处避难，也没有人眷顾我。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I cried unto thee, O LORD: I said, Thou art my refuge and my portion in the land of the living. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，我曾向你哀求。我说，你是我的避难所。在活人之地，你是我的福分。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Attend unto my cry; for I am brought very low: deliver me from my persecutors; for they are stronger than I. |
| 中文 | 求你侧耳听我的呼求，因我落到极卑之地。求你救我脱离逼迫我的人，因为他们比我强盛。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Bring my soul out of prison, that I may praise thy name: the righteous shall compass me about; for thou shalt deal bountifully with me. |
| 中文 | 求你领我出离被囚之地，我好称赞你的名。义人必环绕我，因为你是用厚恩待我。 |
