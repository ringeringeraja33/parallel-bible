# Psalms 125

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They that trust in the LORD shall be as mount Zion, which cannot be removed, but abideth for ever. |
| 中文 | （上行之诗）倚靠耶和华的人，好像锡安山，永不动摇。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | As the mountains are round about Jerusalem, so the LORD is round about his people from henceforth even for ever. |
| 中文 | 众山怎样围绕耶路撒冷，耶和华也照样围绕他的百姓，从今时直到永远。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the rod of the wicked shall not rest upon the lot of the righteous; lest the righteous put forth their hands unto iniquity. |
| 中文 | 恶人的杖不常落在义人的分上，免得义人伸手作恶。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Do good, O LORD, unto those that be good, and to them that are upright in their hearts. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你善待那些为善，和心里正直的人。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | As for such as turn aside unto their crooked ways, the LORD shall lead them forth with the workers of iniquity: but peace shall be upon Israel. |
| 中文 | 至于那偏行弯曲道路的人，耶和华必使他和作恶的人一同出去受刑。愿平安归于以色列。 |
