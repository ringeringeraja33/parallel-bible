# Leviticus 3

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And if his oblation be a sacrifice of peace offering, if he offer it of the herd; whether it be a male or female, he shall offer it without blemish before the LORD. |
| 中文 | 人献供物为平安祭（平安或作酬恩下同），若是从牛群中献，无论是公的是母的，必用没有残疾的献在耶和华面前。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And he shall lay his hand upon the head of his offering, and kill it at the door of the tabernacle of the congregation: and Aaron's sons the priests shall sprinkle the blood upon the altar round about. |
| 中文 | 他要按手在供物的头上，宰于会幕门口。亚伦子孙作祭司的，要把血洒在坛的周围。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And he shall offer of the sacrifice of the peace offering an offering made by fire unto the LORD; the fat that covereth the inwards, and all the fat that is upon the inwards, |
| 中文 | 从平安祭中，将火祭献给耶和华，也要把盖脏的脂油和脏上所有的脂油， |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the two kidneys, and the fat that is on them, which is by the flanks, and the caul above the liver, with the kidneys, it shall he take away. |
| 中文 | 并两个腰子和腰子上的脂油，就是靠腰两旁的脂油，与肝上的网子和腰子，一概取下。 |
