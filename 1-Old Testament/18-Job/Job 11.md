# Job 11

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then answered Zophar the Naamathite, and said, |
| 中文 | 拿玛人琐法回答说， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Should not the multitude of words be answered? and should a man full of talk be justified? |
| 中文 | 这许多的言语岂不该回答吗。多嘴多舌的人岂可称为义吗。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Should thy lies make men hold their peace? and when thou mockest, shall no man make thee ashamed? |
| 中文 | 你夸大的话，岂能使人不作声吗ⅶ你戏笑的时候，岂没有人叫你害羞吗。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thou hast said, My doctrine is pure, and I am clean in thine eyes. |
| 中文 | 你说，我的道理纯全，我在你眼前洁净。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But oh that God would speak, and open his lips against thee; |
| 中文 | 惟愿神说话，愿他开口攻击你。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And that he would shew thee the secrets of wisdom, that they are double to that which is! Know therefore that God exacteth of thee less than thine iniquity deserveth. |
| 中文 | 并将智慧的奥秘指示你，他有诸般的智识。所以当知道神追讨你，比你罪孽该得的还少。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Canst thou by searching find out God? canst thou find out the Almighty unto perfection? |
| 中文 | 你考察，就能测透神吗。你岂能尽情测透全能者吗。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | It is as high as heaven; what canst thou do? deeper than hell; what canst thou know? |
| 中文 | 他的智慧高于天，你还能做什么。深于阴间，你还能知道什么。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The measure thereof is longer than the earth, and broader than the sea. |
| 中文 | 其量比地长，比海宽。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If he cut off, and shut up, or gather together, then who can hinder him? |
| 中文 | 他若经过，将人拘禁，招人受审，谁能阻挡他呢。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For he knoweth vain men: he seeth wickedness also; will he not then consider it? |
| 中文 | 他本知道虚妄的人。人的罪孽，他虽不留意，还是无所不见。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For vain man would be wise, though man be born like a wild ass's colt. |
| 中文 | 空虚的人却毫无知识。人生在世好像野驴的驹子。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If thou prepare thine heart, and stretch out thine hands toward him; |
| 中文 | 你若将心安正，又向主举手。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If iniquity be in thine hand, put it far away, and let not wickedness dwell in thy tabernacles. |
| 中文 | 你手里若有罪孽，就当远远地除掉，也不容非义住在你帐棚之中。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For then shalt thou lift up thy face without spot; yea, thou shalt be stedfast, and shalt not fear: |
| 中文 | 那时，你必仰起脸来毫无斑点。你也必坚固，无所惧怕。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Because thou shalt forget thy misery, and remember it as waters that pass away: |
| 中文 | 你必忘记你的苦楚，就是想起也如流过去的水一样。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And thine age shall be clearer than the noonday: thou shalt shine forth, thou shalt be as the morning. |
| 中文 | 你在世的日子要比正午更明，虽有黑暗仍像早晨。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And thou shalt be secure, because there is hope; yea, thou shalt dig about thee, and thou shalt take thy rest in safety. |
| 中文 | 你因有指望就必稳固，也必四围巡查，坦然安息。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Also thou shalt lie down, and none shall make thee afraid; yea, many shall make suit unto thee. |
| 中文 | 你躺卧，无人惊吓，且有许多人向你求恩。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But the eyes of the wicked shall fail, and they shall not escape, and their hope shall be as the giving up of the ghost. |
| 中文 | 但恶人的眼目必要失明。他们无路可逃。他们的指望就是气绝。 |
