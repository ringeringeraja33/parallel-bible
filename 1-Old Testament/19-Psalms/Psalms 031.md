# Psalms 31

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | In thee, O LORD, do I put my trust; let me never be ashamed: deliver me in thy righteousness. |
| 中文 | （大卫的诗交与伶长）耶和华阿，我投靠你，求你使我永不羞愧，凭你的公义搭救我。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Bow down thine ear to me; deliver me speedily: be thou my strong rock, for an house of defence to save me. |
| 中文 | 求你侧耳而听，快快救我，作我坚固的磐石，拯救我的保障。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thou art my rock and my fortress; therefore for thy name's sake lead me, and guide me. |
| 中文 | 因为你是我的岩石，我的山寨。所以求你为你名的缘故，引导我，指点我。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Pull me out of the net that they have laid privily for me: for thou art my strength. |
| 中文 | 求你救我脱离人为我暗设的网罗。因为你是我的保障。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Into thine hand I commit my spirit: thou hast redeemed me, O LORD God of truth. |
| 中文 | 我将我的灵魂交在你手里。耶和华诚实的神阿，你救赎了我。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I have hated them that regard lying vanities: but I trust in the LORD. |
| 中文 | 我恨恶那信奉虚无之神的人。我却倚靠耶和华。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will be glad and rejoice in thy mercy: for thou hast considered my trouble; thou hast known my soul in adversities; |
| 中文 | 我要为你的慈爱高兴欢喜。因为你见过我的困苦，知道我心中的艰难。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And hast not shut me up into the hand of the enemy: thou hast set my feet in a large room. |
| 中文 | 你未曾把我交在仇敌手里。你使我的脚站在宽阔之处。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Have mercy upon me, O LORD, for I am in trouble: mine eye is consumed with grief, yea, my soul and my belly. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你怜恤我，因为我在急难之中。我的眼睛因忧愁而乾瘪，连我的身心，也不安舒。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For my life is spent with grief, and my years with sighing: my strength faileth because of mine iniquity, and my bones are consumed. |
| 中文 | 我的生命为愁苦所消耗。我的年岁为叹息所旷废。我的力量因我的罪孽衰败。我的骨头也枯乾。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I was a reproach among all mine enemies, but especially among my neighbours, and a fear to mine acquaintance: they that did see me without fled from me. |
| 中文 | 我因一切敌人成了羞辱，在我的邻舍跟前更甚。那认识我的都惧怕我。在外头看见我的都躲避我。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I am forgotten as a dead man out of mind: I am like a broken vessel. |
| 中文 | 我被人忘记，如同死人，无人记念。我好像破碎的器皿。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For I have heard the slander of many: fear was on every side: while they took counsel together against me, they devised to take away my life. |
| 中文 | 我听见了许多人的谗谤，四围都是惊吓。他们一同商议攻击我的时候，就图谋要害我的性命。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But I trusted in thee, O LORD: I said, Thou art my God. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，我仍旧倚靠你。我说，你是我的神。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My times are in thy hand: deliver me from the hand of mine enemies, and from them that persecute me. |
| 中文 | 我终身的事在你手中。求你救我脱离仇敌的手，和那些逼迫我的人。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Make thy face to shine upon thy servant: save me for thy mercies' sake. |
| 中文 | 求你使你的脸光照仆人，凭你的慈爱拯救我。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let me not be ashamed, O LORD; for I have called upon thee: let the wicked be ashamed, and let them be silent in the grave. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你叫我不至羞愧。因为我曾呼吁你。求你使恶人羞愧，使他们在阴间缄默无声。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let the lying lips be put to silence; which speak grievous things proudly and contemptuously against the righteous. |
| 中文 | 那撒谎的人，逞骄傲轻慢，出狂妄的话攻击义人。愿他的嘴哑而无言。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Oh how great is thy goodness, which thou hast laid up for them that fear thee; which thou hast wrought for them that trust in thee before the sons of men! |
| 中文 | 敬畏你投靠你的人，你为他们所积存的，在世人面前所施行的恩惠，是何等大呢。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt hide them in the secret of thy presence from the pride of man: thou shalt keep them secretly in a pavilion from the strife of tongues. |
| 中文 | 你必把他们藏在你面前的隐密处，免得遇见人的计谋。你必暗暗的保守他们在亭子里，免受口舌的争闹。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Blessed be the LORD: for he hath shewed me his marvellous kindness in a strong city. |
| 中文 | 耶和华是应当称颂的。因为他在坚固城里，向我施展奇妙的慈爱。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For I said in my haste, I am cut off from before thine eyes: nevertheless thou heardest the voice of my supplications when I cried unto thee. |
| 中文 | 至于我，我曾急促的说，我从你眼前被隔绝。然而我呼求你的时候，你仍听我恳求的声音。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O love the LORD, all ye his saints: for the LORD preserveth the faithful, and plentifully rewardeth the proud doer. |
| 中文 | 耶和华的圣民哪，你们都要爱他。耶和华保护诚实人，足足报应行事骄傲的人。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Be of good courage, and he shall strengthen your heart, all ye that hope in the LORD. |
| 中文 | 凡仰望耶和华的人，你们都要壮胆，坚固你们的心。 |
