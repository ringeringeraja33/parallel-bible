# Galatians 6 / 加拉太书 6

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Brethren, if a man be overtaken in a fault, ye which are spiritual, restore such an one in the spirit of meekness; considering thyself, lest thou also be tempted. |
| 中文 | 弟兄们，若有人偶然被过犯所胜，你们属灵的人，就应当用温柔的心，把他挽回过来。又当自己小心，恐怕也被引诱。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Bear ye one another's burdens, and so fulfil the law of Christ. |
| 中文 | 你们各人的重担要互相担当，如此就完全了基督的律法。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For if a man think himself to be something, when he is nothing, he deceiveth himself. |
| 中文 | 人若无有，自己还以为有，就是自欺了。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But let every man prove his own work, and then shall he have rejoicing in himself alone, and not in another. |
| 中文 | 各人应当察验自己的行为，这样，他所夸的就专在自己，不在别人了。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For every man shall bear his own burden. |
| 中文 | 因为各人必担当自己的担子。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let him that is taught in the word communicate unto him that teacheth in all good things. |
| 中文 | 在道理上受教的，当把一切需用的供给施教的人。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Be not deceived; God is not mocked: for whatsoever a man soweth, that shall he also reap. |
| 中文 | 不要自欺，神是轻慢不得的。人种的是什么，收的也是什么。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For he that soweth to his flesh shall of the flesh reap corruption; but he that soweth to the Spirit shall of the Spirit reap life everlasting. |
| 中文 | 顺着情欲撒种的，必从情欲收败坏顺着圣灵撒种的，必从圣灵收永生。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And let us not be weary in well doing: for in due season we shall reap, if we faint not. |
| 中文 | 我们行善，不可丧志。若不灰心，到了时候，就要收成。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | As we have therefore opportunity, let us do good unto all men, especially unto them who are of the household of faith. |
| 中文 | 所以有了机会，就当向众人行善。向信徒一家的人更当这样。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ye see how large a letter I have written unto you with mine own hand. |
| 中文 | 请看我亲手写给你们的字，是何等的大呢。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | As many as desire to make a fair shew in the flesh, they constrain you to be circumcised; only lest they should suffer persecution for the cross of Christ. |
| 中文 | 凡希图外貌体面的人，都勉强你们受割礼。无非是怕自己为基督的十字架受逼迫。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For neither they themselves who are circumcised keep the law; but desire to have you circumcised, that they may glory in your flesh. |
| 中文 | 他们那些受割礼的，连自己也不守律法。他们愿意你们受割礼，不过要藉着你们的肉体夸口。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But God forbid that I should glory, save in the cross of our Lord Jesus Christ, by whom the world is crucified unto me, and I unto the world. |
| 中文 | 但我断不以别的夸口，只夸我们主耶稣基督的十字架。因这十字架，就我而论，世界已经钉在十字架上。就世界而论，我已经钉在十字架上。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For in Christ Jesus neither circumcision availeth any thing, nor uncircumcision, but a new creature. |
| 中文 | 受割礼不受割礼都无关紧要，要紧的就是作新造的人。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And as many as walk according to this rule, peace be on them, and mercy, and upon the Israel of God. |
| 中文 | 凡照此理而行的，愿平安怜悯加给他们，和神的以色列民。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | From henceforth let no man trouble me: for I bear in my body the marks of the Lord Jesus. |
| 中文 | 从今以后，人都不要搅扰我。因为我身上带着耶稣的印记。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Brethren, the grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with your spirit. Amen. |
| 中文 | 弟兄们，愿我主耶稣基督的恩常在你们心里。阿们。 |
