# Psalms 108

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O God, my heart is fixed; I will sing and give praise, even with my glory. |
| 中文 | （大卫的诗歌）神阿，我心坚定。我口（原文作荣耀）要唱诗歌颂。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Awake, psaltery and harp: I myself will awake early. |
| 中文 | 琴瑟阿，你们当醒起。我自己要极早醒起。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will praise thee, O LORD, among the people: and I will sing praises unto thee among the nations. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，我要在万民中称谢你，在列邦中歌颂你。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thy mercy is great above the heavens: and thy truth reacheth unto the clouds. |
| 中文 | 因为，你的慈爱，大过诸天；你的诚实，达到穹苍。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Be thou exalted, O God, above the heavens: and thy glory above all the earth; |
| 中文 | 神阿，愿你崇高过于诸天。愿你的荣耀，高过全地。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | That thy beloved may be delivered: save with thy right hand, and answer me. |
| 中文 | 求你应允我们，用右手拯救我们，好叫你所亲爱的人得救。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | God hath spoken in his holiness; I will rejoice, I will divide Shechem, and mete out the valley of Succoth. |
| 中文 | 神已经指着他的圣洁说（说或作应许我），我要欢乐。我要分开示剑，丈量疏割谷。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Gilead is mine; Manasseh is mine; Ephraim also is the strength of mine head; Judah is my lawgiver; |
| 中文 | 基列是我的。玛拿西是我的。以法莲是护卫我头的。犹大是我的杖。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Moab is my washpot; over Edom will I cast out my shoe; over Philistia will I triumph. |
| 中文 | 摩押是我的沭浴盆。我要向以东抛鞋。我必因胜非利士呼喊。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who will bring me into the strong city? who will lead me into Edom? |
| 中文 | 谁能领我进坚固城。谁能引我到以东地。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Wilt not thou, O God, who hast cast us off? and wilt not thou, O God, go forth with our hosts? |
| 中文 | 神阿，你不是丢弃了我们吗。神阿，你不和我们的军兵同去吗。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Give us help from trouble: for vain is the help of man. |
| 中文 | 求你帮助我们攻击敌人，因为人的帮助是枉然的。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Through God we shall do valiantly: for he it is that shall tread down our enemies. |
| 中文 | 我们倚靠神，才得施展大能，因为践踏我们敌人的就是他。 |
