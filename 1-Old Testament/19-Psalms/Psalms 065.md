# Psalms 65

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Praise waiteth for thee, O God, in Sion: and unto thee shall the vow be performed. |
| 中文 | （大卫的诗歌，交与伶长）神阿，锡安的人都等候赞美你，所许的愿，也要向你偿还。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O thou that hearest prayer, unto thee shall all flesh come. |
| 中文 | 听祷告的主阿，凡有血气的，都要来就你。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Iniquities prevail against me: as for our transgressions, thou shalt purge them away. |
| 中文 | 罪孽胜了我。至于我们的过犯，你都要赦免。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Blessed is the man whom thou choosest, and causest to approach unto thee, that he may dwell in thy courts: we shall be satisfied with the goodness of thy house, even of thy holy temple. |
| 中文 | 你所拣选，使他亲近你，住在你院中的，这人便为有福。我们必因你居所，你圣殿的美福知足了。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | By terrible things in righteousness wilt thou answer us, O God of our salvation; who art the confidence of all the ends of the earth, and of them that are afar off upon the sea: |
| 中文 | 拯救我们的神阿，你必以威严秉公义应允我们。你本是一切地极，和海上远处的人所倚靠的。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Which by his strength setteth fast the mountains; being girded with power: |
| 中文 | 他既以大能束腰，就用力量安定诸山。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Which stilleth the noise of the seas, the noise of their waves, and the tumult of the people. |
| 中文 | 使诸海的响声，和其中波浪的响声，并万民的喧哗，都平静了。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They also that dwell in the uttermost parts are afraid at thy tokens: thou makest the outgoings of the morning and evening to rejoice. |
| 中文 | 住在地极的人，因你的神迹惧怕。你使日出日落之地都欢呼。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou visitest the earth, and waterest it: thou greatly enrichest it with the river of God, which is full of water: thou preparest them corn, when thou hast so provided for it. |
| 中文 | 你眷顾地，降下透雨，使地大得肥美。神的河满了水。你这样浇灌了地，好为人豫备五谷。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou waterest the ridges thereof abundantly: thou settlest the furrows thereof: thou makest it soft with showers: thou blessest the springing thereof. |
| 中文 | 你浇透地的犁沟，润平犁脊。降甘霖，使地软和。其中发长的，蒙你赐福。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou crownest the year with thy goodness; and thy paths drop fatness. |
| 中文 | 你以恩典为年岁的冠冕。你的路径都滴下脂油。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They drop upon the pastures of the wilderness: and the little hills rejoice on every side. |
| 中文 | 滴在旷野的草场上。小山以欢乐束腰。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The pastures are clothed with flocks; the valleys also are covered over with corn; they shout for joy, they also sing. |
| 中文 | 草场以羊群为衣。谷中也长满了五谷。这一切都欢呼歌唱。 |
