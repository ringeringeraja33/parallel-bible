# Job 33 / 约伯记 33

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Wherefore, Job, I pray thee, hear my speeches, and hearken to all my words. |
| 中文 | 约伯阿，请听我的话，留心听我一切的言语。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, now I have opened my mouth, my tongue hath spoken in my mouth. |
| 中文 | 我现在开口，用舌发言。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My words shall be of the uprightness of my heart: and my lips shall utter knowledge clearly. |
| 中文 | 我的言语要发明心中所存的正直。我所知道的，我嘴唇要诚实地说出。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The spirit of God hath made me, and the breath of the Almighty hath given me life. |
| 中文 | 神的灵造我，全能者的气使我得生。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If thou canst answer me, set thy words in order before me, stand up. |
| 中文 | 你若回答我，就站起来，在我面前陈明。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, I am according to thy wish in God's stead: I also am formed out of the clay. |
| 中文 | 我在神面前与你一样，也是用土造成。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, my terror shall not make thee afraid, neither shall my hand be heavy upon thee. |
| 中文 | 我不用威严惊吓你，也不用势力重压你。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Surely thou hast spoken in mine hearing, and I have heard the voice of thy words, saying, |
| 中文 | 你所说的，我听见了，也听见你的言语，说， |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I am clean without transgression, I am innocent; neither is there iniquity in me. |
| 中文 | 我是清洁无过的，我是无辜的。在我里面也没有罪孽。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, he findeth occasions against me, he counteth me for his enemy, |
| 中文 | 神找机会攻击我，以我为仇敌， |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He putteth my feet in the stocks, he marketh all my paths. |
| 中文 | 把我的脚上了木狗，窥察我一切的道路。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, in this thou art not just: I will answer thee, that God is greater than man. |
| 中文 | 我要回答你说，你这话无理，因神比世人更大。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Why dost thou strive against him? for he giveth not account of any of his matters. |
| 中文 | 你为何与他争论呢。因他的事都不对人解说。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For God speaketh once, yea twice, yet man perceiveth it not. |
| 中文 | 神说一次，两次，世人却不理会。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | In a dream, in a vision of the night, when deep sleep falleth upon men, in slumberings upon the bed; |
| 中文 | 人躺在床上沉睡的时候，神就用梦，和夜间的异象， |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then he openeth the ears of men, and sealeth their instruction, |
| 中文 | 开通他们的耳朵，将当受的教训印在他们心上， |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | That he may withdraw man from his purpose, and hide pride from man. |
| 中文 | 好叫人不从自己的谋算，不行骄傲的事。（原文作将骄傲向人隐藏） |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He keepeth back his soul from the pit, and his life from perishing by the sword. |
| 中文 | 拦阻人不陷于坑里，不死在刀下。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He is chastened also with pain upon his bed, and the multitude of his bones with strong pain: |
| 中文 | 人在床上被惩治，骨头中不住地疼痛。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | So that his life abhorreth bread, and his soul dainty meat. |
| 中文 | 以致他的口厌弃食物，心厌恶美味。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | His flesh is consumed away, that it cannot be seen; and his bones that were not seen stick out. |
| 中文 | 他的肉消瘦，不得再见。先前不见的骨头都凸出来。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Yea, his soul draweth near unto the grave, and his life to the destroyers. |
| 中文 | 他的灵魂临近深坑。他的生命近于灭命的。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If there be a messenger with him, an interpreter, one among a thousand, to shew unto man his uprightness: |
| 中文 | 一千天使中，若有一个作传话的与神同在，指示人所当行的事。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then he is gracious unto him, and saith, Deliver him from going down to the pit: I have found a ransom. |
| 中文 | 神就给他开恩，说，救赎他免得下坑。我已经得了赎价。 |

## 25

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | His flesh shall be fresher than a child's: he shall return to the days of his youth: |
| 中文 | 他的肉要比孩童的肉更嫩。他就返老还童。 |

## 26

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He shall pray unto God, and he will be favourable unto him: and he shall see his face with joy: for he will render unto man his righteousness. |
| 中文 | 他祷告神，神就喜悦他，使他欢呼朝见神的面。神又看他为义。 |

## 27

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He looketh upon men, and if any say, I have sinned, and perverted that which was right, and it profited me not; |
| 中文 | 他在人前歌唱说，我犯了罪，颠倒是非，这竟与我无益。 |

## 28

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He will deliver his soul from going into the pit, and his life shall see the light. |
| 中文 | 神救赎我的灵魂免入深坑。我的生命也必见光。 |

## 29

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Lo, all these things worketh God oftentimes with man, |
| 中文 | 神两次，三次，向人行这一切的事。 |

## 30

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | To bring back his soul from the pit, to be enlightened with the light of the living. |
| 中文 | 为要从深坑救回人的灵魂，使他被光照耀与活人一样。 |

## 31

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Mark well, O Job, hearken unto me: hold thy peace, and I will speak. |
| 中文 | 约伯阿，你当侧耳听我的话，不要作声，等我讲说。 |

## 32

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If thou hast anything to say, answer me: speak, for I desire to justify thee. |
| 中文 | 你若有话说，就可以回答我。你只管说，因我愿以你为是。 |

## 33

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If not, hearken unto me: hold thy peace, and I shall teach thee wisdom. |
| 中文 | 若不然，你就听我说。你不要作声，我便将智慧教训你。 |
