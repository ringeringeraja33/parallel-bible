# Job 22 / 约伯记 22

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then Eliphaz the Temanite answered and said, |
| 中文 | 毯螅人以利法回答说， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Can a man be profitable unto God, as he that is wise may be profitable unto himself? |
| 中文 | 人岂能使神有益呢。智慧人但能有益于己。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Is it any pleasure to the Almighty, that thou art righteous? or is it gain to him, that thou makest thy ways perfect? |
| 中文 | 你为人公义，岂叫全能者喜悦呢。你行为完全，岂能使他得利呢。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Will he reprove thee for fear of thee? will he enter with thee into judgment? |
| 中文 | 岂是因你敬畏他，就责备你，审判你吗。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Is not thy wickedness great? and thine iniquities infinite? |
| 中文 | 你的罪恶岂不是大吗。你的罪孽也没有穷尽。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thou hast taken a pledge from thy brother for nought, and stripped the naked of their clothing. |
| 中文 | 因你无故强取弟兄的物为当头，剥去贫寒人的衣服。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou hast not given water to the weary to drink, and thou hast withholden bread from the hungry. |
| 中文 | 困乏的人，你没有给他水喝。饥饿的人，你没有给他食物。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But as for the mighty man, he had the earth; and the honourable man dwelt in it. |
| 中文 | 有能力的人就得地土。尊贵的人也住在其中。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou hast sent widows away empty, and the arms of the fatherless have been broken. |
| 中文 | 你打发寡妇空手回去，折断孤儿的膀臂。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Therefore snares are round about thee, and sudden fear troubleth thee; |
| 中文 | 因此，有网罗环绕你，有恐惧忽然使你惊惶。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Or darkness, that thou canst not see; and abundance of waters cover thee. |
| 中文 | 或有黑暗蒙蔽你，并有洪水淹没你。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Is not God in the height of heaven? and behold the height of the stars, how high they are! |
| 中文 | 神岂不是在高天吗。你看星宿何其高呢。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And thou sayest, How doth God know? can he judge through the dark cloud? |
| 中文 | 你说，神知道什么。他岂能看透幽暗施行审判呢。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thick clouds are a covering to him, that he seeth not; and he walketh in the circuit of heaven. |
| 中文 | 密云将他遮盖，使他不能看见。他周游穹苍。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hast thou marked the old way which wicked men have trodden? |
| 中文 | 你要依从上古的道吗。这道是恶人所行的。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Which were cut down out of time, whose foundation was overflown with a flood: |
| 中文 | 他们未到死期，忽然除灭。根基毁坏，好像被江河冲去。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Which said unto God, Depart from us: and what can the Almighty do for them? |
| 中文 | 他们向神说，离开我们吧。又说，全能者能把我们怎吗样呢。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Yet he filled their houses with good things: but the counsel of the wicked is far from me. |
| 中文 | 那知，神以美物充满他们的房屋。但恶人所谋定的离我好远。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The righteous see it, and are glad: and the innocent laugh them to scorn. |
| 中文 | 义人看见他们的结局就欢喜。无辜的人嗤笑他们。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Whereas our substance is not cut down, but the remnant of them the fire consumeth. |
| 中文 | 说，那起来攻击我们的果然被剪除，其馀的都被火烧灭。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Acquaint now thyself with him, and be at peace: thereby good shall come unto thee. |
| 中文 | 你若认识神，就得平安。福气也必临到你。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Receive, I pray thee, the law from his mouth, and lay up his words in thine heart. |
| 中文 | 你当领受他口中的教训，将他的言语存在心里。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If thou return to the Almighty, thou shalt be built up, thou shalt put away iniquity far from thy tabernacles. |
| 中文 | 你若向全能者，从你帐棚中远除不义，就必得建立。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then shalt thou lay up gold as dust, and the gold of Ophir as the stones of the brooks. |
| 中文 | 要将你的珍宝丢在尘土里，将俄斐的黄金丢在溪河石头之间。 |

## 25

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Yea, the Almighty shall be thy defence, and thou shalt have plenty of silver. |
| 中文 | 全能者就必为你的珍宝，作你的宝银。 |

## 26

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For then shalt thou have thy delight in the Almighty, and shalt lift up thy face unto God. |
| 中文 | 你就要以全能者为喜乐，向神仰起脸来。 |

## 27

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt make thy prayer unto him, and he shall hear thee, and thou shalt pay thy vows. |
| 中文 | 你要祷告他，他就听你。你也要还你的愿。 |

## 28

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt also decree a thing, and it shall be established unto thee: and the light shall shine upon thy ways. |
| 中文 | 你定意要作何事，必然给你成就。亮光也必照耀你的路。 |

## 29

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When men are cast down, then thou shalt say, There is lifting up; and he shall save the humble person. |
| 中文 | 人使你降卑，你仍可说，必得高升。谦卑的人神必拯救。 |

## 30

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He shall deliver the island of the innocent: and it is delivered by the pureness of thine hands. |
| 中文 | 人非无辜，神且要搭救他。他因你手中清洁，必蒙拯救。 |
