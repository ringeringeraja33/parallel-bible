# Isaiah 35

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The wilderness and the solitary place shall be glad for them; and the desert shall rejoice, and blossom as the rose. |
| 中文 | 旷野和乾旱之地，必然欢喜。沙漠也必快乐。又像玫瑰开花。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | It shall blossom abundantly, and rejoice even with joy and singing: the glory of Lebanon shall be given unto it, the excellency of Carmel and Sharon, they shall see the glory of the LORD, and the excellency of our God. |
| 中文 | 必开花繁盛，乐上加乐，而且欢呼。黎巴嫩的荣耀，并迦密与沙仑的华美，必赐给他。人必看见耶和华的荣耀，我们神的华美。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Strengthen ye the weak hands, and confirm the feeble knees. |
| 中文 | 你们要使软弱的手坚壮，无力的膝稳固。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Say to them that are of a fearful heart, Be strong, fear not: behold, your God will come with vengeance, even God with a recompence; he will come and save you. |
| 中文 | 对胆怯的人说，你们要刚强，不要惧怕。看哪，你们的神必来报仇，必来施行极大的报应，他必来拯救你们。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then the eyes of the blind shall be opened, and the ears of the deaf shall be unstopped. |
| 中文 | 那时瞎子的眼必睁开，聋子的耳必开通。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then shall the lame man leap as an hart, and the tongue of the dumb sing: for in the wilderness shall waters break out, and streams in the desert. |
| 中文 | 那时瘸子必跳跃像鹿，哑吧的舌头必能歌唱。在旷野必有水发出，在沙漠必有河涌流。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the parched ground shall become a pool, and the thirsty land springs of water: in the habitation of dragons, where each lay, shall be grass with reeds and rushes. |
| 中文 | 发光的沙，（或作蜃楼）要变为水池，乾渴之地，要变为泉源。在野狗躺卧之处，必有青草，芦苇，和蒲草。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And an highway shall be there, and a way, and it shall be called The way of holiness; the unclean shall not pass over it; but it shall be for those: the wayfaring men, though fools, shall not err therein. |
| 中文 | 在那里必有一条大道，称为圣路。污秽人不得经过，必专为赎民行走，行路的人虽愚昧，也不至失迷。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | No lion shall be there, nor any ravenous beast shall go up thereon, it shall not be found there; but the redeemed shall walk there: |
| 中文 | 在那里必没有狮子，猛兽也不登这路，在那里都遇不见。只有赎民在那里行走。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the ransomed of the LORD shall return, and come to Zion with songs and everlasting joy upon their heads: they shall obtain joy and gladness, and sorrow and sighing shall flee away. |
| 中文 | 并且耶和华救赎的民必归回，歌唱来到锡安。永乐必归到他们的头上，他们必得着欢喜快乐，忧愁叹息尽都逃避。 |
