# Psalms 84 / 诗篇 84

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | How amiable are thy tabernacles, O LORD of hosts! |
| 中文 | （可拉后裔的诗，交与伶长，用迦特乐器）万军之耶和华阿，你的居所何等可爱。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My soul longeth, yea, even fainteth for the courts of the LORD: my heart and my flesh crieth out for the living God. |
| 中文 | 我羡慕渴想耶和华的院宇。我的心肠，我的肉体，向永生神呼吁（或作欢呼）。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Yea, the sparrow hath found an house, and the swallow a nest for herself, where she may lay her young, even thine altars, O LORD of hosts, my King, and my God. |
| 中文 | 万军之耶和华我的王，我的神阿，在你祭坛那里，麻雀为自己找着房屋。燕子为自己找着抱雏之窝。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Blessed are they that dwell in thy house: they will be still praising thee. Selah. |
| 中文 | 如此住在你殿中的，便为有福。他们仍要赞美你。（细拉） |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Blessed is the man whose strength is in thee; in whose heart are the ways of them. |
| 中文 | 靠你有力量心中想往锡安大道的，这人便为有福。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who passing through the valley of Baca make it a well; the rain also filleth the pools. |
| 中文 | 他们经过流泪谷，叫这谷变为泉源之地。并有秋雨之福，盖满了全谷。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They go from strength to strength, every one of them in Zion appeareth before God. |
| 中文 | 他们行走，力上加力，各人到锡安朝见神。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O LORD God of hosts, hear my prayer: give ear, O God of Jacob. Selah. |
| 中文 | 耶和华万军之神阿，求你听我的祷告。雅各的神阿，求你留心听。（细拉） |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, O God our shield, and look upon the face of thine anointed. |
| 中文 | 神阿，你是我们的盾牌。求你垂顾观看你受膏者的面。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For a day in thy courts is better than a thousand. I had rather be a doorkeeper in the house of my God, than to dwell in the tents of wickedness. |
| 中文 | 在你院宇住一日，胜似在别处住千日。宁可在我神殿中看门，不愿住在恶人的帐棚里。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the LORD God is a sun and shield: the LORD will give grace and glory: no good thing will he withhold from them that walk uprightly. |
| 中文 | 因为耶和华神是日头，是盾牌，要赐下恩惠和荣耀。他未尝留下一样好处，不给那些行动正直的人。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O LORD of hosts, blessed is the man that trusteth in thee. |
| 中文 | 万军之耶和华阿，倚靠你的人，便为有福。 |
