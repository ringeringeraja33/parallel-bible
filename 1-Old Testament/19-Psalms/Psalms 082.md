# Psalms 82 / 诗篇 82

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | God standeth in the congregation of the mighty; he judgeth among the gods. |
| 中文 | （亚萨的诗）神站在有权力者的会中。在诸神中行审判。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | How long will ye judge unjustly, and accept the persons of the wicked? Selah. |
| 中文 | 说，你们审判不秉公义，徇恶人的情面，要到几时呢。（细拉） |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Defend the poor and fatherless: do justice to the afflicted and needy. |
| 中文 | 你们当为贫寒的人和孤儿伸冤。当为困苦和穷乏的人施行公义。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Deliver the poor and needy: rid them out of the hand of the wicked. |
| 中文 | 当保护贫寒和穷乏的人，救他们脱离恶人的手。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They know not, neither will they understand; they walk on in darkness: all the foundations of the earth are out of course. |
| 中文 | 你们仍不知道，也不明白，在黑暗中走来走去。地的根基都摇动了。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I have said, Ye are gods; and all of you are children of the most High. |
| 中文 | 我曾说，你们是神，都是至高者的儿子。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But ye shall die like men, and fall like one of the princes. |
| 中文 | 然而你们要死，与世人一样。要仆倒，像王子中的一位。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Arise, O God, judge the earth: for thou shalt inherit all nations. |
| 中文 | 神阿，求你起来，审判世界。因为你要得万邦为业。 |
