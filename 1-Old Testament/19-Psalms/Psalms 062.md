# Psalms 62

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Truly my soul waiteth upon God: from him cometh my salvation. |
| 中文 | （大卫的诗，照耶杜顿的作法，交与伶长）我的心默默无声，专等候神。我的救恩是从他而来。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He only is my rock and my salvation; he is my defence; I shall not be greatly moved. |
| 中文 | 惟独他是我的磐石，我的拯救。他是我的高台。我必不很动摇。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | How long will ye imagine mischief against a man? ye shall be slain all of you: as a bowing wall shall ye be, and as a tottering fence. |
| 中文 | 你们大家攻击一人，把他毁坏。如同毁坏歪斜的墙，将倒的壁，要到几时呢。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They only consult to cast him down from his excellency: they delight in lies: they bless with their mouth, but they curse inwardly. Selah. |
| 中文 | 他们彼此商议，专要从他的尊位上把他推下。他们喜爱谎话。口虽祝福，心却咒诅。（细拉） |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My soul, wait thou only upon God; for my expectation is from him. |
| 中文 | 我的心哪，你当默默无声，专等候神。因为我的盼望是从他而来。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He only is my rock and my salvation: he is my defence; I shall not be moved. |
| 中文 | 惟独他是我的磐石，我的拯救。他是我的高台。我必不动摇。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | In God is my salvation and my glory: the rock of my strength, and my refuge, is in God. |
| 中文 | 我的拯救，我的荣耀，都在乎神。我力量的磐石，我的避难所，都在乎神。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Trust in him at all times; ye people, pour out your heart before him: God is a refuge for us. Selah. |
| 中文 | 你们众民当时时倚靠他，在他面前倾心吐意。神是我们的避难所。（细拉） |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Surely men of low degree are vanity, and men of high degree are a lie: to be laid in the balance, they are altogether lighter than vanity. |
| 中文 | 下流人真是虚空，上流人也是虚假。放在天平里就必浮起。他们一共比空气还轻。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Trust not in oppression, and become not vain in robbery: if riches increase, set not your heart upon them. |
| 中文 | 不要仗势欺人，也不要因抢夺而骄傲。若财宝加增，不要放在心上。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | God hath spoken once; twice have I heard this; that power belongeth unto God. |
| 中文 | 神说了一次，两次，我都听见，就是能力都属乎神。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Also unto thee, O Lord, belongeth mercy: for thou renderest to every man according to his work. |
| 中文 | 主阿，慈爱也是属乎你。因为你照着各人所行的报应他。 |
