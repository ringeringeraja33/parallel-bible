# Numbers 17

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the LORD spake unto Moses, saying, |
| 中文 | 耶和华对摩西说， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Speak unto the children of Israel, and take of every one of them a rod according to the house of their fathers, of all their princes according to the house of their fathers twelve rods: write thou every man's name upon his rod. |
| 中文 | 你晓谕以色列人，从他们手下取杖，每支派一根。从他们所有的首领，按着支派，共取十二根。你要将各人的名字写在各人的杖上， |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And thou shalt write Aaron's name upon the rod of Levi: for one rod shall be for the head of the house of their fathers. |
| 中文 | 并要将亚伦的名字写在利未的杖上，因为各族长必有一根杖。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And thou shalt lay them up in the tabernacle of the congregation before the testimony, where I will meet with you. |
| 中文 | 你要把这些杖存在会幕内法柜前，就是我与你们相会之处。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And it shall come to pass, that the man's rod, whom I shall choose, shall blossom: and I will make to cease from me the murmurings of the children of Israel, whereby they murmur against you. |
| 中文 | 后来我所拣选的那人，他的杖必发芽。这样，我必使以色列人向你们所发的怨言止息，不再达到我耳中。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And Moses spake unto the children of Israel, and every one of their princes gave him a rod apiece, for each prince one, according to their fathers' houses, even twelve rods: and the rod of Aaron was among their rods. |
| 中文 | 于是摩西晓谕以色列人，他们的首领就把杖交给他，按着支派，每首领一根，共有十二根。亚伦的杖也在其中。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And Moses laid up the rods before the LORD in the tabernacle of witness. |
| 中文 | 摩西就把杖存在法柜的帐幕内，在耶和华面前。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And it came to pass, that on the morrow Moses went into the tabernacle of witness; and, behold, the rod of Aaron for the house of Levi was budded, and brought forth buds, and bloomed blossoms, and yielded almonds. |
| 中文 | 第二天，摩西进法柜的帐幕去。谁知利未族亚伦的杖已经发了芽，生了花苞，开了花，结了熟杏。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And Moses brought out all the rods from before the LORD unto all the children of Israel: and they looked, and took every man his rod. |
| 中文 | 摩西就把所有的杖从耶和华面前拿出来，给以色列众人看。他们看见了，各首领就把自己的杖拿去。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the LORD said unto Moses, Bring Aaron's rod again before the testimony, to be kept for a token against the rebels; and thou shalt quite take away their murmurings from me, that they die not. |
| 中文 | 耶和华吩咐摩西说，把亚伦的杖还放在法柜前，给这些背叛之子留作记号。这样，你就使他们向我发的怨言止息，免得他们死亡。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And Moses did so: as the LORD commanded him, so did he. |
| 中文 | 摩西就这样行。耶和华怎样吩咐他，他就怎样行了。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the children of Israel spake unto Moses, saying, Behold, we die, we perish, we all perish. |
| 中文 | 以色列人对摩西说，我们死喇。我们灭亡喇。都灭亡喇。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Whosoever cometh any thing near unto the tabernacle of the LORD shall die: shall we be consumed with dying? |
| 中文 | 凡挨近耶和华帐幕的是必死的。我们都要死亡吗， |
