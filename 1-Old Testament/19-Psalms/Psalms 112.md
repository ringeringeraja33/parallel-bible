# Psalms 112 / 诗篇 112

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Praise ye the LORD. Blessed is the man that feareth the LORD, that delighteth greatly in his commandments. |
| 中文 | 你们要赞美耶和华。敬畏耶和华，甚喜爱他命令的，这人便为有福。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | His seed shall be mighty upon earth: the generation of the upright shall be blessed. |
| 中文 | 他的后裔在世必强盛。正直人的后代，必要蒙福。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Wealth and riches shall be in his house: and his righteousness endureth for ever. |
| 中文 | 他家中有货物，有钱财。他的公义存到永远。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Unto the upright there ariseth light in the darkness: he is gracious, and full of compassion, and righteous. |
| 中文 | 正直人在黑暗中，有光向他发现。他有恩惠，有怜悯，有公义。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A good man sheweth favour, and lendeth: he will guide his affairs with discretion. |
| 中文 | 施恩与人，借贷与人的，这人事情顺利。他被审判的时候，要诉明自己的冤。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Surely he shall not be moved for ever: the righteous shall be in everlasting remembrance. |
| 中文 | 他永不动摇。义人被记念，直到永远。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He shall not be afraid of evil tidings: his heart is fixed, trusting in the LORD. |
| 中文 | 他必不怕凶恶的信息。他心坚定，倚靠耶和华。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | His heart is established, he shall not be afraid, until he see his desire upon his enemies. |
| 中文 | 他心确定，总不惧怕，直到他看见敌人遭报。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He hath dispersed, he hath given to the poor; his righteousness endureth for ever; his horn shall be exalted with honour. |
| 中文 | 他施舍钱财，周济贫穷。他的仁义存到永远。他的角必被高举，大有荣耀。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The wicked shall see it, and be grieved; he shall gnash with his teeth, and melt away: the desire of the wicked shall perish. |
| 中文 | 恶人看见便恼恨。必咬牙而消化。恶人的心愿，要归灭绝。 |
