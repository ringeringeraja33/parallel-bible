# Exodus 23 / 出埃及记 23

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt not raise a false report: put not thine hand with the wicked to be an unrighteous witness. |
| 中文 | 不可随夥布散谣言，不可与恶人连手妄作见证。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt not follow a multitude to do evil; neither shalt thou speak in a cause to decline after many to wrest judgment: |
| 中文 | 不可随众行恶，不可在争讼的事上随众偏行，作见证屈枉正直， |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Neither shalt thou countenance a poor man in his cause. |
| 中文 | 也不可在争讼的事上偏护穷人。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If thou meet thine enemy's ox or his ass going astray, thou shalt surely bring it back to him again. |
| 中文 | 若遇见你仇敌的牛或驴失迷了路，总要牵回来交给他。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If thou see the ass of him that hateth thee lying under his burden, and wouldest forbear to help him, thou shalt surely help with him. |
| 中文 | 若看见恨你人的驴压卧在重驮之下，不可走开，务要和驴主一同抬开重驮。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt not wrest the judgment of thy poor in his cause. |
| 中文 | 不可在穷人争讼的事上屈枉正直。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Keep thee far from a false matter; and the innocent and righteous slay thou not: for I will not justify the wicked. |
| 中文 | 当远离虚假的事。不可杀无辜和有义的人，因我必不以恶人为义。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And thou shalt take no gift: for the gift blindeth the wise, and perverteth the words of the righteous. |
| 中文 | 不可受贿赂，因为贿赂能叫明眼人变瞎了，又能颠倒义人的话。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Also thou shalt not oppress a stranger: for ye know the heart of a stranger, seeing ye were strangers in the land of Egypt. |
| 中文 | 不可欺压寄居的，因为你们在埃及地作过寄居的，知道寄居的心。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And six years thou shalt sow thy land, and shalt gather in the fruits thereof: |
| 中文 | 六年你要耕种田地，收藏土产， |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But the seventh year thou shalt let it rest and lie still; that the poor of thy people may eat: and what they leave the beasts of the field shall eat. In like manner thou shalt deal with thy vineyard, and with thy oliveyard. |
| 中文 | 只是第七年要叫地歇息，不耕不种，使你民中的穷人有吃的，他们所剩下的，野兽可以吃。你的葡萄园和橄榄园也要照样办理。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Six days thou shalt do thy work, and on the seventh day thou shalt rest: that thine ox and thine ass may rest, and the son of thy handmaid, and the stranger, may be refreshed. |
| 中文 | 六日你要作工，第七日要安息，使牛，驴可以歇息，并使你婢女的儿子和寄居的都可以舒畅。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And in all things that I have said unto you be circumspect: and make no mention of the name of other gods, neither let it be heard out of thy mouth. |
| 中文 | 凡我对你们说的话，你们要谨守。别神的名，你不可提，也不可从你口中传说。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Three times thou shalt keep a feast unto me in the year. |
| 中文 | 一年三次，你要向我守节。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt keep the feast of unleavened bread: (thou shalt eat unleavened bread seven days, as I commanded thee, in the time appointed of the month Abib; for in it thou camest out from Egypt: and none shall appear before me empty:) |
| 中文 | 你要守除酵节，照我所吩咐你的，在亚笔月内所定的日期，吃无酵饼七天。谁也不可空手朝见我，因为你是这月出了埃及。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the feast of harvest, the firstfruits of thy labours, which thou hast sown in the field: and the feast of ingathering, which is in the end of the year, when thou hast gathered in thy labours out of the field. |
| 中文 | 又要守收割节，所收的是你田间所种，劳碌得来初熟之物。并在年底收藏，要守收藏节。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Three times in the year all thy males shall appear before the LORD God. |
| 中文 | 一切的男丁要一年三次朝见主耶和华。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt not offer the blood of my sacrifice with leavened bread; neither shall the fat of my sacrifice remain until the morning. |
| 中文 | 不可将我祭牲的血和有酵的饼一同献上，也不可将我节上祭牲的脂油留到早晨。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The first of the firstfruits of thy land thou shalt bring into the house of the LORD thy God. Thou shalt not seethe a kid in his mother's milk. |
| 中文 | 地里首先初熟之物要送到耶和华你神的殿。不可用山羊羔母的奶煮山羊羔。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, I send an Angel before thee, to keep thee in the way, and to bring thee into the place which I have prepared. |
| 中文 | 看哪，我差遣使者在你前面，在路上保护你，领你到我所预备的地方去。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Beware of him, and obey his voice, provoke him not; for he will not pardon your transgressions: for my name is in him. |
| 中文 | 他是奉我名来的，你们要在他面前谨慎，听从他的话，不可惹（惹或作违背）他，因为他必不赦免你们的过犯。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But if thou shalt indeed obey his voice, and do all that I speak; then I will be an enemy unto thine enemies, and an adversary unto thine adversaries. |
| 中文 | 你若实在听从他的话，照着我一切所说的去行，我就向你的仇敌作仇敌，向你的敌人作敌人。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
