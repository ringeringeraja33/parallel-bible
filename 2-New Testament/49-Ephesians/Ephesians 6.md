# Ephesians 6 / 以弗所书 6

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Children, obey your parents in the Lord: for this is right. |
| 中文 | 你们作儿女的，要在主里听从父母，这是理所当然的。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Honour thy father and mother; which is the first commandment with promise; |
| 中文 | 要孝敬父母，使你们得福，在世长寿。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | That it may be well with thee, and thou mayest live long on the earth. |
| 中文 | *这是第一条带应许的诫命。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And, ye fathers, provoke not your children to wrath: but bring them up in the nurture and admonition of the Lord. |
| 中文 | 你们作父亲的，不要惹儿女的气，只要照着主的教训和警戒，养育他们。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Servants, be obedient to them that are your masters according to the flesh, with fear and trembling, in singleness of your heart, as unto Christ; |
| 中文 | 你们作仆人的，要惧怕战兢，用诚实的心听从你们肉身的主人，好像听从基督一般。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Not with eyeservice, as menpleasers; but as the servants of Christ, doing the will of God from the heart; |
| 中文 | 不要只在眼前事奉，像是讨人喜欢的，要像基督的仆人，从心里遵行神的旨意。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | With good will doing service, as to the Lord, and not to men: |
| 中文 | 甘心事奉，好像服事主，不像服事人。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Knowing that whatsoever good thing any man doeth, the same shall he receive of the Lord, whether he be bond or free. |
| 中文 | 因为晓得各人所行的善事，不论是为奴的，是自主的，都必按所行的得主的赏赐 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And, ye masters, do the same things unto them, forbearing threatening: knowing that your Master also is in heaven; neither is there respect of persons with him. |
| 中文 | 你们作主人的待仆人，也是一理，不要威吓他们。因为知道他们和你们，同有一位主在天上，他并不偏待人。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Finally, my brethren, be strong in the Lord, and in the power of his might. |
| 中文 | 我还有末了的话，你们要靠着主，倚赖他的大能大力，作刚强的人。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Put on the whole armour of God, that ye may be able to stand against the wiles of the devil. |
| 中文 | 要穿戴神所赐的全副军装，就能抵挡魔鬼的诡计。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For we wrestle not against flesh and blood, but against principalities, against powers, against the rulers of the darkness of this world, against spiritual wickedness in high places. |
| 中文 | 因我们并不是与属血气的争战，乃是与那些执政的，掌权的，管辖这幽暗世界的，以及天空属灵气的恶魔争战。（两争战原都作摔跤） |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Wherefore take unto you the whole armour of God, that ye may be able to withstand in the evil day, and having done all, to stand. |
| 中文 | 所以要拿起神所赐的全副军装，好在磨难的日子，抵挡仇敌，并且成就了一切，还能站立得住。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 所以要站稳了，用真理当作带子束腰，用公义当作护心镜遮胸。 |
