# Psalms 126

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When the LORD turned again the captivity of Zion, we were like them that dream. |
| 中文 | （上行之诗）当耶和华将那些被掳的带回锡安的时候，我们好像做梦的人。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then was our mouth filled with laughter, and our tongue with singing: then said they among the heathen, The LORD hath done great things for them. |
| 中文 | 我们满口喜笑，满舌欢呼的时候，外邦中就有人说，耶和华为他们行了大事。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD hath done great things for us; whereof we are glad. |
| 中文 | 耶和华果然为我们行了大事，我们就欢喜。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Turn again our captivity, O LORD, as the streams in the south. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你使我们被掳的人归回，好像南地的河水复流。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They that sow in tears shall reap in joy. |
| 中文 | 流泪撒种的，必欢呼收割。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that goeth forth and weepeth, bearing precious seed, shall doubtless come again with rejoicing, bringing his sheaves with him. |
| 中文 | 那带种流泪出去的，必要欢欢乐乐地带禾捆回来。 |
