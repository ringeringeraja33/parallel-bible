# Psalms 4

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hear me when I call, O God of my righteousness: thou hast enlarged me when I was in distress; have mercy upon me, and hear my prayer. |
| 中文 | 显我为义的神阿，我呼吁的时候，求你应允我。我在困苦中，你曾使我宽广。现在求你怜恤我，听我的祷告。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O ye sons of men, how long will ye turn my glory into shame? how long will ye love vanity, and seek after leasing? Selah. |
| 中文 | 你们这上流人哪，你们将我的尊荣变为羞辱，要到几时呢你们喜爱虚妄，寻梢虚假，要到几时呢。（细拉） |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But know that the LORD hath set apart him that is godly for himself: the LORD will hear when I call unto him. |
| 中文 | 你们要知道耶和华已经分别虔诚人归他自己。我求告耶和华，他必听我。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Stand in awe, and sin not: commune with your own heart upon your bed, and be still. Selah. |
| 中文 | 你们应当畏惧，不可犯罪。在床上的时候，要心里思想，并要肃静。（细拉） |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Offer the sacrifices of righteousness, and put your trust in the LORD. |
| 中文 | 当献上公义的祭，又当倚靠耶和华。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | There be many that say, Who will shew us any good? LORD, lift thou up the light of thy countenance upon us. |
| 中文 | 有许多人说，谁能指示我们什么好处。耶和华阿，求你仰起脸来，光照我们。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou hast put gladness in my heart, more than in the time that their corn and their wine increased. |
| 中文 | 你使我心里快乐，胜过那丰收五谷新酒的人。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will both lay me down in peace, and sleep: for thou, LORD, only makest me dwell in safety. |
| 中文 | 我必安然躺下睡觉，因为独有你耶和华使我安然居住。 |
