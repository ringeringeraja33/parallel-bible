# 2 Corinthians 6

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | We then, as workers together with him, beseech you also that ye receive not the grace of God in vain. |
| 中文 | 我们与神同工的，也劝你们，不可徒受他的恩典。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | (For he saith, I have heard thee in a time accepted, and in the day of salvation have I succoured thee: behold, now is the accepted time; behold, now is the day of salvation.) |
| 中文 | 因为他说，在悦纳的时候，我应允了你。在拯救的日子，我搭救了你。看哪，现在正是悦纳的时候，现在正是拯救的日子。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Giving no offence in any thing, that the ministry be not blamed: |
| 中文 | 我们凡事都不叫人有妨碍，免得这职分被人毁谤。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But in all things approving ourselves as the ministers of God, in much patience, in afflictions, in necessities, in distresses, |
| 中文 | 反倒在各样的事上，表明自己是神的用人，就如在许多的忍耐，患难，穷乏，困苦， |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | In stripes, in imprisonments, in tumults, in labours, in watchings, in fastings; |
| 中文 | 鞭打，监禁，扰乱，勤劳，儆醒，不食， |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | By pureness, by knowledge, by longsuffering, by kindness, by the Holy Ghost, by love unfeigned, |
| 中文 | 廉洁，知识，恒忍，恩慈，圣灵的感化，无伪的爱心， |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | By the word of truth, by the power of God, by the armour of righteousness on the right hand and on the left, |
| 中文 | 真实的道理，神的大能。仁义的兵器在左在右。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | By honour and dishonour, by evil report and good report: as deceivers, and yet true; |
| 中文 | 荣耀羞辱，恶名美名。似乎是诱惑人的，却是诚实的。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | As unknown, and yet well known; as dying, and, behold, we live; as chastened, and not killed; |
| 中文 | 似乎不为人所知，却是人所共知的。似乎要死却是活着的。似乎受责罚，却是不至丧命的。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | As sorrowful, yet alway rejoicing; as poor, yet making many rich; as having nothing, and yet possessing all things. |
| 中文 | 似乎忧愁，却是常常快乐的。似乎贫穷，却是叫许多人富足的。似乎一无所有，却是样样都有的。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O ye Corinthians, our mouth is open unto you, our heart is enlarged. |
| 中文 | 哥林多人哪，我们向你们，口是张开的，心是宽宏的。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ye are not straitened in us, but ye are straitened in your own bowels. |
| 中文 | 你们狭窄，原不在乎我们，是在乎自己的心肠狭窄。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Now for a recompence in the same, (I speak as unto my children,) be ye also enlarged. |
| 中文 | 你们也要用宽宏的心报答我。我这话升像对自己的孩子说的。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Be ye not unequally yoked together with unbelievers: for what fellowship hath righteousness with unrighteousness? and what communion hath light with darkness? |
| 中文 | 你们和不信的原不相配，不要同负一轭。义和不义有什么相交呢。光明和黑暗有什么相通呢。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And what concord hath Christ with Belial? or what part hath he that believeth with an infidel? |
| 中文 | 基督和彼列（彼列就是撒但的别名）有什么相和呢。信主的和不信主的有什么相干呢。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And what agreement hath the temple of God with idols? for ye are the temple of the living God; as God hath said, I will dwell in them, and walk in them; and I will be their God, and they shall be my people. |
| 中文 | 神的殿和偶像有什么相同呢。因为我们是永生神的殿。就如神曾说，我要在他们中间居住，在他们中间来往。我要作他们的神，他们要作我的子民。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Wherefore come out from among them, and be ye separate, saith the Lord, and touch not the unclean thing; and I will receive you, |
| 中文 | 又说，你们务要从他们中间出来，与他们分别，不要沾不洁净的物，我就收纳你们。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And will be a Father unto you, and ye shall be my sons and daughters, saith the Lord Almighty. |
| 中文 | 我要作你们的父，你们要作我的儿女。这是全能的主说的。 |
