# James 1

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | James, a servant of God and of the Lord Jesus Christ, to the twelve tribes which are scattered abroad, greeting. |
| 中文 | 作神和主耶稣基督仆人的雅各，请散住十二个支派之人的安。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My brethren, count it all joy when ye fall into divers temptations; |
| 中文 | 我的弟兄们，你们落在百般的试炼中，都要以为大喜乐。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Knowing this, that the trying of your faith worketh patience. |
| 中文 | 因为知道你们的信心经过试验就生忍耐。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But let patience have her perfect work, that ye may be perfect and entire, wanting nothing. |
| 中文 | 但忍耐也当成功，使你们成全完备，毫无缺欠。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If any of you lack wisdom, let him ask of God, that giveth to all men liberally, and upbraideth not; and it shall be given him. |
| 中文 | 你们中间若有缺少智慧的，应当求那厚赐与众人，也不斥责人的神，主就必赐给他。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But let him ask in faith, nothing wavering. For he that wavereth is like a wave of the sea driven with the wind and tossed. |
| 中文 | 只要凭着信心求，一点不疑惑。因为那疑惑的人，就像海中的波浪，被风吹动翻腾。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For let not that man think that he shall receive any thing of the Lord. |
| 中文 | 这样的人，不要想从主那里得什么。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A double minded man is unstable in all his ways. |
| 中文 | 心怀二意的人，在他一切所行的路上，都没有定见。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let the brother of low degree rejoice in that he is exalted: |
| 中文 | 卑微的弟兄升高，就该喜乐。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But the rich, in that he is made low: because as the flower of the grass he shall pass away. |
| 中文 | 富足的降卑，也该如此。因为他必要过去，如同草上的花一样。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the sun is no sooner risen with a burning heat, but it withereth the grass, and the flower thereof falleth, and the grace of the fashion of it perisheth: so also shall the rich man fade away in his ways. |
| 中文 | 太阳出来，热风刮起，草就枯乾，花也凋谢，美容就消没了。那富足的人，在他所行的事上，也要这样衰残。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Blessed is the man that endureth temptation: for when he is tried, he shall receive the crown of life, which the Lord hath promised to them that love him. |
| 中文 | 忍受试探的人是有福的。因为他经过试验以后，必得生命的冠冕，这是主应许给那些爱他之人的。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let no man say when he is tempted, I am tempted of God: for God cannot be tempted with evil, neither tempteth he any man: |
| 中文 | 人被试探，不可说，我是被神试探。因为神不能被恶试探，他也不试探人。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But every man is tempted, when he is drawn away of his own lust, and enticed. |
| 中文 | 但各人被试探，乃是被自己的私欲牵引诱惑的。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then when lust hath conceived, it bringeth forth sin: and sin, when it is finished, bringeth forth death. |
| 中文 | 私欲既怀了胎，就生出罪来。罪既长成，就生出死来。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Do not err, my beloved brethren. |
| 中文 | 我亲爱的弟兄们，不要看错了。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Every good gift and every perfect gift is from above, and cometh down from the Father of lights, with whom is no variableness, neither shadow of turning. |
| 中文 | 各样美善的恩赐，和各样全备的赏赐，都是从上头来的。从众光之父那里降下来的。在他并没有改变，也没有转动的影儿。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Of his own will begat he us with the word of truth, that we should be a kind of firstfruits of his creatures. |
| 中文 | 他按自己的旨意，用真道生了我们，叫我们在他所造的万物中，好像初熟的果子。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Wherefore, my beloved brethren, let every man be swift to hear, slow to speak, slow to wrath: |
| 中文 | 我亲爱的弟兄们，这是你们所知道的。但你们各人要快快的听，慢慢的说，慢慢的动怒。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the wrath of man worketh not the righteousness of God. |
| 中文 | 因为人的怒气，并不成就神的义。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Wherefore lay apart all filthiness and superfluity of naughtiness, and receive with meekness the engrafted word, which is able to save your souls. |
| 中文 | 所以你们要脱去一切的污秽，和盈馀的邪恶，存温柔的心领受那所栽种的道，就是能救你们灵魂的道。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But be ye doers of the word, and not hearers only, deceiving your own selves. |
| 中文 | 只是你们要行道，不要单单听道，自己欺哄自己。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For if any be a hearer of the word, and not a doer, he is like unto a man beholding his natural face in a glass: |
| 中文 | 因为听道而不行道的，就像人对着镜子看自己本来的面目。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For he beholdeth himself, and goeth his way, and straightway forgetteth what manner of man he was. |
| 中文 | 看见，走后，随即忘了他的相貌如何。 |

## 25

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But whoso looketh into the perfect law of liberty, and continueth therein, he being not a forgetful hearer, but a doer of the work, this man shall be blessed in his deed. |
| 中文 | 惟有那详细察看那全备使人自由之律法的，并且时常如此，这人既不是听了就忘，乃是实在行出来，就在他所行的事上必然得福。 |

## 26

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If any man among you seem to be religious, and bridleth not his tongue, but deceiveth his own heart, this man's religion is vain. |
| 中文 | 若有人自以为虔诚，却不勒住他的舌头，反欺哄自己的心，这人的虔诚是虚的。 |

## 27

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Pure religion and undefiled before God and the Father is this, To visit the fatherless and widows in their affliction, and to keep himself unspotted from the world. |
| 中文 | 在神我们的父面前，那清洁没有玷污的虔诚，就是看雇在患难中的孤儿寡妇，并且保守自己不沾染世俗。 |
