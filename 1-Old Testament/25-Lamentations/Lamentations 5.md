# Lamentations 5

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Remember, O LORD, what is come upon us: consider, and behold our reproach. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你记念我们所遭遇的事，观看我们所受的凌辱。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Our inheritance is turned to strangers, our houses to aliens. |
| 中文 | 我们的产业归与外邦人。我们的房屋归与外路人。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | We are orphans and fatherless, our mothers are as widows. |
| 中文 | 我们是无父的孤儿。我们的母亲好像寡妇。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | We have drunken our water for money; our wood is sold unto us. |
| 中文 | 我们出钱才得水喝。我们的柴是人卖给我们的。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Our necks are under persecution: we labour, and have no rest. |
| 中文 | 追赶我们的，到了我们的颈项上。我们疲乏不得歇息。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | We have given the hand to the Egyptians, and to the Assyrians, to be satisfied with bread. |
| 中文 | 我们投降埃及人和亚述人，为要得粮吃饱。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Our fathers have sinned, and are not; and we have borne their iniquities. |
| 中文 | 我们列祖犯罪，而今不在了。我们担当他们的罪孽。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Servants have ruled over us: there is none that doth deliver us out of their hand. |
| 中文 | 奴仆辖制我们，无人救我们脱离他们的手。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | We gat our bread with the peril of our lives because of the sword of the wilderness. |
| 中文 | 因为旷野的刀剑，我们冒着险才得粮食。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Our skin was black like an oven because of the terrible famine. |
| 中文 | 因饥饿燥热，我们的皮肤就黑如炉。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They ravished the women in Zion, and the maids in the cities of Judah. |
| 中文 | 敌人在锡安玷污妇人，在犹大的城邑玷污处女。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Princes are hanged up by their hand: the faces of elders were not honoured. |
| 中文 | 他们吊起首领的手，也不尊敬老人的面。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They took the young men to grind, and the children fell under the wood. |
| 中文 | 少年人扛磨石，孩童背木柴，都绊跌了。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The elders have ceased from the gate, the young men from their musick. |
| 中文 | 老年人在城门口断绝。少年人不再作乐。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The joy of our heart is ceased; our dance is turned into mourning. |
| 中文 | 我们心中的快乐止息，跳舞变为悲哀。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The crown is fallen from our head: woe unto us, that we have sinned! |
| 中文 | 冠冕从我们的头上落下。我们犯罪了，我们有祸了。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For this our heart is faint; for these things our eyes are dim. |
| 中文 | 这些事我们心里发昏，我们的眼睛昏花。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 锡安山荒凉，野狗（或作狐狸）行在其上。 |

| 中文 | 耶和华阿，你存到永远。你的宝座存到万代。 |

| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 你为何永远忘记我们。为何许久离弃我们。 |
