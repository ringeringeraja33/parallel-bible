# Proverbs 7

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My son, keep my words, and lay up my commandments with thee. |
| 中文 | 我儿，你要遵守我的言语，将我的命令存记在心。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Keep my commandments, and live; and my law as the apple of thine eye. |
| 中文 | 遵守我的命令，就得存活。保守我的法则，（或作指教）好像保守眼中的瞳人。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Bind them upon thy fingers, write them upon the table of thine heart. |
| 中文 | 系在你指头上，刻在你心版上。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Say unto wisdom, Thou art my sister; and call understanding thy kinswoman: |
| 中文 | 对智慧说，你是我的姊妹。称呼聪明为你的亲人。 |

| Language | Content |

| 中文 | 他就保你远离淫妇，远离说谄媚话的外女。 |
