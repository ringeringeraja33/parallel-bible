# Psalms 37

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Fret not thyself because of evildoers, neither be thou envious against the workers of iniquity. |
| 中文 | （大卫的诗）不要为作恶的，心怀不平，也不要向那行不义的，生出嫉妒。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For they shall soon be cut down like the grass, and wither as the green herb. |
| 中文 | 因为他们如草快被割下，又如青菜快要枯乾。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Trust in the LORD, and do good; so shalt thou dwell in the land, and verily thou shalt be fed. |
| 中文 | 你当倚靠耶和华而行善。住在地上，以他的信实为粮。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Delight thyself also in the LORD: and he shall give thee the desires of thine heart. |
| 中文 | 又要以耶和华为乐。他就将你心里所求的赐给你。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Commit thy way unto the LORD; trust also in him; and he shall bring it to pass. |
| 中文 | 当将你的事交托耶和华，并倚靠他，他就必成全。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And he shall bring forth thy righteousness as the light, and thy judgment as the noonday. |
| 中文 | 他要使你的公义，如光发出，使你的公平，明如正午。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Rest in the LORD, and wait patiently for him: fret not thyself because of him who prospereth in his way, because of the man who bringeth wicked devices to pass. |
| 中文 | 你当默然倚靠耶和华，耐性等候他。不要因那道路通达的，和那恶谋成就的，心怀不平。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Cease from anger, and forsake wrath: fret not thyself in any wise to do evil. |
| 中文 | 当止住怒气，离弃忿怒。不要心怀不平，以致作恶。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For evildoers shall be cut off: but those that wait upon the LORD, they shall inherit the earth. |
| 中文 | 因为作恶的，必被剪除。惟有等候耶和华的，必承受地土。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For yet a little while, and the wicked shall not be: yea, thou shalt diligently consider his place, and it shall not be. |
| 中文 | 还有片时，恶人要归于无有。你就细察他的住处，也要归于无有。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But the meek shall inherit the earth; and shall delight themselves in the abundance of peace. |
| 中文 | 但谦卑人必承受地土，以丰盛的平安为乐。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The wicked plotteth against the just, and gnasheth upon him with his teeth. |
| 中文 | 恶人设谋害义人，又向他咬牙。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD shall laugh at him: for he seeth that his day is coming. |
| 中文 | 主要笑他，因见他受罚的日子将要来到。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The wicked have drawn out the sword, and have bent their bow, to cast down the poor and needy, and to slay such as be of upright conversation. |
| 中文 | 恶人已经弓上弦，刀出鞘，要打倒困苦穷乏的人，要杀害行动正直的人。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Their sword shall enter into their own heart, and their bows shall be broken. |
| 中文 | 他们的刀，必刺入自己的心，他们的弓，必被折断。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A little that a righteous man hath is better than the riches of many wicked. |
| 中文 | 一个义人所有的虽少，强过许多恶人的富馀。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the arms of the wicked shall be broken: but the LORD upholdeth the righteous. |
| 中文 | 因为恶人的膀臂，必被折断。但耶和华是扶持义人。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD knoweth the days of the upright: and their inheritance shall be for ever. |
| 中文 | 耶和华知道完全人的日子。他们的产业，要存到永远。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They shall not be ashamed in the evil time: and in the days of famine they shall be satisfied. |
| 中文 | 他们在急难的时候，不至羞愧，在饥荒的日子，必得饱足。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But the wicked shall perish, and the enemies of the LORD shall be as the fat of lambs: they shall consume; into smoke shall they consume away. |
| 中文 | 恶人却要灭亡。耶和华的仇敌，要像羊羔的脂油。（或作像草地的华美）他们要消灭，要如烟消灭。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The wicked borroweth, and payeth not again: but the righteous sheweth mercy, and giveth. |
| 中文 | 恶人借货而不偿还。义人却恩待人，并且施舍。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For such as be blessed of him shall inherit the earth; and they that be cursed of him shall be cut off. |
| 中文 | 蒙耶和华赐福的，必承受地土。被他咒诅的，必被剪除。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The steps of a good man are ordered by the LORD: and he delighteth in his way. |
| 中文 | 义人的脚步，被耶和华立定。他的道路，耶和华也喜爱。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Though he fall, he shall not be utterly cast down: for the LORD upholdeth him with his hand. |
| 中文 | 他虽失脚，也不至全身仆倒。因为耶和华用手搀扶他。（或作搀扶他的手） |

## 25

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I have been young, and now am old; yet have I not seen the righteous forsaken, nor his seed begging bread. |
| 中文 | 我从前年幼，现在年老，却未见过义人被弃。也未见过他的后裔讨饭。 |

## 26

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He is ever merciful, and lendeth; and his seed is blessed. |
| 中文 | 他终日恩待人，借给人。他的后裔也蒙福。 |

## 27

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Depart from evil, and do good; and dwell for evermore. |
| 中文 | 你当离恶行善，就可永远安居。 |

## 28

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the LORD loveth judgment, and forsaketh not his saints; they are preserved for ever: but the seed of the wicked shall be cut off. |
| 中文 | 因为耶和华喜爱公平，不撇弃他的圣民。他们永蒙保佑。但恶人的后裔，必被剪除。 |

## 29

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The righteous shall inherit the land, and dwell therein for ever. |
| 中文 | 义人必承受地土，永居其上。 |

## 30

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The mouth of the righteous speaketh wisdom, and his tongue talketh of judgment. |
| 中文 | 义人的口谈论智慧，他的舌头讲说公平。 |

## 31

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The law of his God is in his heart; none of his steps shall slide. |
| 中文 | 神的律法在他心里。他的脚总不滑跌。 |

## 32

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The wicked watcheth the righteous, and seeketh to slay him. |
| 中文 | 恶人窥探义人，想要杀他。 |

## 33

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD will not leave him in his hand, nor condemn him when he is judged. |
| 中文 | 耶和华必不撇他在恶人手中，当审判的时候，也不定他的罪。 |

## 34

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Wait on the LORD, and keep his way, and he shall exalt thee to inherit the land: when the wicked are cut off, thou shalt see it. |
| 中文 | 你当等候耶和华，遵守他的道，他就抬举你，使你承受地土。恶人被剪除的时候，你必看见。 |

## 35

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I have seen the wicked in great power, and spreading himself like a green bay tree. |
| 中文 | 我见过恶人大有势力，好像一根青翠树在本土生发。 |

## 36

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Yet he passed away, and, lo, he was not: yea, I sought him, but he could not be found. |
| 中文 | 有人从那里经过，不料，他没有了。我也寻梢他，却寻不着。 |

## 37

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Mark the perfect man, and behold the upright: for the end of that man is peace. |
| 中文 | 你要细察那完全人，观看那正直人。因为和平人有好结局。 |

## 38

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But the transgressors shall be destroyed together: the end of the wicked shall be cut off. |
| 中文 | 至于犯法的人，必一同灭绝。恶人终必剪除。 |

## 39

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But the salvation of the righteous is of the LORD: he is their strength in the time of trouble. |
| 中文 | 但义人得救，是由于耶和华。他在患难时作他们的营寨。 |

## 40

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the LORD shall help them, and deliver them: he shall deliver them from the wicked, and save them, because they trust in him. |
| 中文 | 耶和华帮助他们，解救他们。他解救他们脱离恶人，把他们救出来，因为他们投靠他。 |
