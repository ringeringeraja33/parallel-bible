# John 2

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the third day there was a marriage in Cana of Galilee; and the mother of Jesus was there: |
| 中文 | 第三日，在加利利的迦拿有娶亲的筵席。耶稣的母亲在那里。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And both Jesus was called, and his disciples, to the marriage. |
| 中文 | 耶稣和他的门徒也被请去赴席。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And when they wanted wine, the mother of Jesus saith unto him, They have no wine. |
| 中文 | 酒用尽了，耶稣的母亲对他说，他们没有酒了。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Jesus saith unto her, Woman, what have I to do with thee? mine hour is not yet come. |
| 中文 | 耶稣说，母亲，（原文作妇人）我与你有什么相干。我的时候还没有到。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | His mother saith unto the servants, Whatsoever he saith unto you, do it. |
| 中文 | 他母亲对用人说，他告诉你们什么，你们就作什么。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And there were set there six waterpots of stone, after the manner of the purifying of the Jews, containing two or three firkins apiece. |
| 中文 | 照犹太人洁净的规矩，有六口缸摆在那里，每口可以盛两三桶水。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Jesus saith unto them, Fill the waterpots with water. And they filled them up to the brim. |
| 中文 | 耶稣对用人说，把缸倒满了水。他们就倒满了，直到缸口。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And he saith unto them, Draw out now, and bear unto the governor of the feast. And they bare it. |
| 中文 | 耶稣又说，现在可以舀出来，送给管筵席的。他们就送了去。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When the ruler of the feast had tasted the water that was made wine, and knew not whence it was: (but the servants which drew the water knew;) the governor of the feast called the bridegroom, |
| 中文 | 管筵席尝了那水变的酒，并不知道是那里来的，只有舀水的用人知道。管筵席的便叫新郎来。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And saith unto him, Every man at the beginning doth set forth good wine; and when men have well drunk, then that which is worse: but thou hast kept the good wine until now. |
| 中文 | 对他说，人都是先摆上好酒。等客喝足了，才摆上次的。你倒把好酒留到如今。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | This beginning of miracles did Jesus in Cana of Galilee, and manifested forth his glory; and his disciples believed on him. |
| 中文 | 这是耶稣所行的头一件神迹，是在加利利的迦拿行的，显出他的荣耀来。他的门徒就信他了。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | After this he went down to Capernaum, he, and his mother, and his brethren, and his disciples: and they continued there not many days. |
| 中文 | 这事以后，耶稣与他的母亲弟兄和门徒，都下迦百农去。在那里住了不多几日。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the Jews' passover was at hand, and Jesus went up to Jerusalem, |
| 中文 | 犹太人的逾越节近了，耶稣就上耶路撒冷去。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And found in the temple those that sold oxen and sheep and doves, and the changers of money sitting: |
| 中文 | 看见殿里有卖牛羊鸽子的，并有兑换银钱的人，坐在那里。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And when he had made a scourge of small cords, he drove them all out of the temple, and the sheep, and the oxen; and poured out the changers' money, and overthrew the tables; |
| 中文 | 耶稣就拿绳子作成鞭子，把牛羊都赶出殿去。倒出兑换银钱之人的银钱，推翻他们的桌子。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And said unto them that sold doves, Take these things hence; make not my Father's house an house of merchandise. |
| 中文 | 又对卖鸽子的说，把这些东西拿去。不要将我父的殿，当作买卖的地方。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And his disciples remembered that it was written, The zeal of thine house hath eaten me up. |
| 中文 | 他的门徒就想起经上记着说，我为你的殿，心里焦急，如同火烧。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then answered the Jews and said unto him, What sign shewest thou unto us, seeing that thou doest these things? |
| 中文 | 因此，犹太人问他说，你既作这些事，还显什么神迹给我们看呢。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Jesus answered and said unto them, Destroy this temple, and in three days I will raise it up. |
| 中文 | 耶稣回答说，你们拆毁这殿，我三日内要再建立起来。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 犹太人便说，这殿是四十六年才造成的，你三日内就再建立起来吗。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 但耶稣这话，是以他的身体为殿。 |

## 22

| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 所以到他从死里复活以后，门徒就想起他说过这话，便信了圣经和耶稣所说的。 |

## 23

| 中文 | 当耶稣在耶路撒冷过逾越节的时候，有许多人看见他所行的神迹，就信了他的名。 |
