# Job 5 / 约伯记 5

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Call now, if there be any that will answer thee; and to which of the saints wilt thou turn? |
| 中文 | 你且呼求，有谁答应你。诸圣者之中，你转向那一位呢。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For wrath killeth the foolish man, and envy slayeth the silly one. |
| 中文 | 忿怒害死愚妄人，嫉妒杀死痴迷人。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I have seen the foolish taking root: but suddenly I cursed his habitation. |
| 中文 | 我曾见愚妄人扎下根，但我忽然咒诅他的住处。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | His children are far from safety, and they are crushed in the gate, neither is there any to deliver them. |
| 中文 | 他的儿女远离稳妥的地步，在城门口被压，并无人搭救。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Whose harvest the hungry eateth up, and taketh it even out of the thorns, and the robber swalloweth up their substance. |
| 中文 | 他的庄稼有饥饿的人吃尽了，就是在荆棘里的也抢去了。他的财宝有网罗张口吞灭了。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Although affliction cometh not forth of the dust, neither doth trouble spring out of the ground; |
| 中文 | 祸患原不是从土中出来。患难也不是从地里发生。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Yet man is born unto trouble, as the sparks fly upward. |
| 中文 | 人生在世必遇患难，如同火星飞腾。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I would seek unto God, and unto God would I commit my cause: |
| 中文 | 至于我，我必仰望神，把我的事情托付他。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Which doeth great things and unsearchable; marvellous things without number: |
| 中文 | 他行大事不可测度，行奇事不可胜数。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who giveth rain upon the earth, and sendeth waters upon the fields: |
| 中文 | 降雨在地上，赐水于田里。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | To set up on high those that be low; that those which mourn may be exalted to safety. |
| 中文 | 将卑微的安置在高处，将哀痛的举到稳妥之地。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He disappointeth the devices of the crafty, so that their hands cannot perform their enterprise. |
| 中文 | 破坏狡猾人的计谋，使他们所谋的不得成就。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He taketh the wise in their own craftiness: and the counsel of the froward is carried headlong. |
| 中文 | 他叫有智慧的中了自己的诡计，使狡诈人的计谋速速灭亡。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They meet with darkness in the day time, and grope in the noonday as in the night. |
| 中文 | 他们白昼遇见黑暗，午间摸索如在夜间。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But he saveth the poor from the sword, from their mouth, and from the hand of the mighty. |
| 中文 | 神拯救穷乏人，脱离他们口中的刀，和强暴人的手。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | So the poor hath hope, and iniquity stoppeth her mouth. |
| 中文 | 这样，贫寒的人有指望，罪孽之辈必塞口无言。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, happy is the man whom God correcteth: therefore despise not thou the chastening of the Almighty: |
| 中文 | 神所惩治的人是有福的。所以你不可轻看全能者的管教。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For he maketh sore, and bindeth up: he woundeth, and his hands make whole. |
| 中文 | 因为他打破，又缠裹。他击伤，用手医治。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He shall deliver thee in six troubles: yea, in seven there shall no evil touch thee. |
| 中文 | 你六次遭难，他必救你。就是七次，灾祸也无法害你。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | In famine he shall redeem thee from death: and in war from the power of the sword. |
| 中文 | 在饥荒中，他必救你脱离死亡。在争战中，他必救你脱离刀剑的权力。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt be hid from the scourge of the tongue: neither shalt thou be afraid of destruction when it cometh. |
| 中文 | 你必被隐藏，不受口舌之害。灾殃临到，你也不惧怕。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | At destruction and famine thou shalt laugh: neither shalt thou be afraid of the beasts of the earth. |
| 中文 | 你遇见灾害饥馑，就必嬉笑。地上的野兽，你也不惧怕。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thou shalt be in league with the stones of the field: and the beasts of the field shall be at peace with thee. |
| 中文 | 因为你必与田间的石头立约，田里的野兽也必与你和好。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And thou shalt know that thy tabernacle shall be in peace; and thou shalt visit thy habitation, and shalt not sin. |
| 中文 | 你必知道你帐棚平安，要查看你的羊圈，一无所失。 |

## 25

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt know also that thy seed shall be great, and thine offspring as the grass of the earth. |
| 中文 | 也必知道你的后裔将来发达，你的子孙像地上的青草。 |

## 26

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt come to thy grave in a full age, like as a shock of corn cometh in in his season. |
| 中文 | 你必寿高年迈才归坟墓，好像禾捆到时收藏。 |

## 27

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Lo this, we have searched it, so it is; hear it, and know thou it for thy good. |
| 中文 | 这理，我们已经考察，本是如此。你须要听，要知道是与自己有益。 |
