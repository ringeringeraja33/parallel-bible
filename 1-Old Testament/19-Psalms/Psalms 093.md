# Psalms 93

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD reigneth, he is clothed with majesty; the LORD is clothed with strength, wherewith he hath girded himself: the world also is stablished, that it cannot be moved. |
| 中文 | 耶和华作王。他以威严为衣穿上。耶和华以能力为衣，以能力束腰。世界就坚定，不得动摇。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thy throne is established of old: thou art from everlasting. |
| 中文 | 你的宝座从太初立定。你从亘古就有。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The floods have lifted up, O LORD, the floods have lifted up their voice; the floods lift up their waves. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，大水杨起，大水发声，波浪澎湃。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD on high is mightier than the noise of many waters, yea, than the mighty waves of the sea. |
| 中文 | 耶和华在高处大有能力，胜过诸水的响声，洋海的大浪。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thy testimonies are very sure: holiness becometh thine house, O LORD, for ever. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，你的法度最的确。你的殿永称为圣，是合宜的。 |
