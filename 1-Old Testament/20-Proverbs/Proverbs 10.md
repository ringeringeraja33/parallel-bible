# Proverbs 10 / 箴言 10

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The proverbs of Solomon. A wise son maketh a glad father: but a foolish son is the heaviness of his mother. |
| 中文 | 所罗门的箴言。智慧之子，使父亲欢乐。愚昧之子，叫母亲担忧。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Treasures of wickedness profit nothing: but righteousness delivereth from death. |
| 中文 | 不义之财，毫无益处。惟有公义，能救人脱离死亡。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD will not suffer the soul of the righteous to famish: but he casteth away the substance of the wicked. |
| 中文 | 耶和华不使义人受饥饿。恶人所欲的他必推开。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 手懒的要受贫穷。手勤的却要富足。 |
