# Psalms 33 / 诗篇 33

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Rejoice in the LORD, O ye righteous: for praise is comely for the upright. |
| 中文 | 义人哪，你们应当靠耶和华欢乐。正直人的赞美是合宜的。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Praise the LORD with harp: sing unto him with the psaltery and an instrument of ten strings. |
| 中文 | 你们应当弹琴称谢耶和华，用十弦瑟歌颂他。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Sing unto him a new song; play skilfully with a loud noise. |
| 中文 | 应当向他唱新歌，弹得巧妙，声音洪亮。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the word of the LORD is right; and all his works are done in truth. |
| 中文 | 因为耶和华的言语正直。凡他所作的，尽都诚实。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He loveth righteousness and judgment: the earth is full of the goodness of the LORD. |
| 中文 | 他喜爱仁义公平。遍地满了耶和华的慈爱。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | By the word of the LORD were the heavens made; and all the host of them by the breath of his mouth. |
| 中文 | 诸天藉耶和华的命而造，万象藉他口中的气而成。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He gathereth the waters of the sea together as an heap: he layeth up the depth in storehouses. |
| 中文 | 他聚集海水如垒，收藏深洋在库房。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let all the earth fear the LORD: let all the inhabitants of the world stand in awe of him. |
| 中文 | 愿全地都敬畏耶和华。愿世上的居民，都惧怕他。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For he spake, and it was done; he commanded, and it stood fast. |
| 中文 | 因为他说有，就有。命立，就立。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD bringeth the counsel of the heathen to nought: he maketh the devices of the people of none effect. |
| 中文 | 耶和华使列国的寿算归于无有，使众民的思念无有功效。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The counsel of the LORD standeth for ever, the thoughts of his heart to all generations. |
| 中文 | 耶和华的寿算永远立定，他心中的思念万带常存。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Blessed is the nation whose God is the LORD; and the people whom he hath chosen for his own inheritance. |
| 中文 | 以耶和华为神的，那国是有福的。他所拣选为自己产业的，那民是有福的。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD looketh from heaven; he beholdeth all the sons of men. |
| 中文 | 耶和华从天上观看。他看见一切的世人。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | From the place of his habitation he looketh upon all the inhabitants of the earth. |
| 中文 | 从他的居所，往外察看地上的居民。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He fashioneth their hearts alike; he considereth all their works. |
| 中文 | 他是那造成他们众人心的，留意他们一切作为的。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | There is no king saved by the multitude of an host: a mighty man is not delivered by much strength. |
| 中文 | 君王不能因兵多得胜。勇士不能因力大得救。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | An horse is a vain thing for safety: neither shall he deliver any by his great strength. |
| 中文 | 靠马得救是枉然的。马也不能因力大救人。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, the eye of the LORD is upon them that fear him, upon them that hope in his mercy; |
| 中文 | 耶和华的眼目，看顾敬畏他的人，和仰望他慈爱的人， |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | To deliver their soul from death, and to keep them alive in famine. |
| 中文 | 要救他们的命脱离死亡，并使他们在饥荒中存活。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Our soul waiteth for the LORD: he is our help and our shield. |
| 中文 | 我们的心向来等候耶和华。他是我们的帮助，我们的盾牌。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For our heart shall rejoice in him, because we have trusted in his holy name. |
| 中文 | 我们的心必靠他欢喜，因为我们向来倚靠他的圣名。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let thy mercy, O LORD, be upon us, according as we hope in thee. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你照着我们所仰望你的，向我们施行慈爱。 |
