# 1 Corinthians 5 / 哥林多前书 5

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | It is reported commonly that there is fornication among you, and such fornication as is not so much as named among the Gentiles, that one should have his father's wife. |
| 中文 | 风闻在你们中间有淫乱的事。这样的淫乱，连外邦人中也没有，就是有人收了他的继母。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And ye are puffed up, and have not rather mourned, that he that hath done this deed might be taken away from among you. |
| 中文 | 你们还是自高自大，并不哀痛，把行这事的人从你们中间赶出去。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For I verily, as absent in body, but present in spirit, have judged already, as though I were present, concerning him that hath so done this deed, |
| 中文 | 我身子虽不在你们那里，心却在你们那里，好像我亲自与你们同在，已经判断了行这事的人， |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | In the name of our Lord Jesus Christ, when ye are gathered together, and my spirit, with the power of our Lord Jesus Christ, |
| 中文 | 就是你们聚会的时候，我的心也同在，奉我们主耶稣的名，并用我们主耶稣的权能， |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | To deliver such an one unto Satan for the destruction of the flesh, that the spirit may be saved in the day of the Lord Jesus. |
| 中文 | 要把这样的人交给撒但，败坏他的肉体，使他的灵魂在主耶稣的日子可以得救。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Your glorying is not good. Know ye not that a little leaven leaveneth the whole lump? |
| 中文 | 你们这自夸是不好的。岂不知一点面酵能使全团发起来吗。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Purge out therefore the old leaven, that ye may be a new lump, as ye are unleavened. For even Christ our passover is sacrificed for us: |
| 中文 | 你们既是无酵的面，应当把旧酵除净，好使你们成为新团。因为我们逾越节的羔羊基督已经被杀献祭了。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Therefore let us keep the feast, not with old leaven, neither with the leaven of malice and wickedness; but with the unleavened bread of sincerity and truth. |
| 中文 | 所以，我们守这节不可用旧酵，也不可用恶毒（或作阴毒），邪恶的酵，只用诚实真正的无酵饼。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I wrote unto you in an epistle not to company with fornicators: |
| 中文 | 我先前写信给你们说，不可与淫乱的人相交。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Yet not altogether with the fornicators of this world, or with the covetous, or extortioners, or with idolaters; for then must ye needs go out of the world. |
| 中文 | 此话不是指这世上一概行淫乱的，或贪婪的，勒索的，或拜偶像的，若是这样，你们除非离开世界方可。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But now I have written unto you not to keep company, if any man that is called a brother be a fornicator, or covetous, or an idolater, or a railer, or a drunkard, or an extortioner; with such an one no not to eat. |
| 中文 | 但如今我写信给你们说，若有称弟兄，是行淫乱的，或贪婪的，或拜偶像的，或辱骂的，或醉酒的，或勒索的。这样的人不可与他相交。就是与他吃饭都不可。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For what have I to do to judge them also that are without? do not ye judge them that are within? |
| 中文 | 因为审判教外的人与我何干。教内的人岂不是你们审判的吗。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But them that are without God judgeth. Therefore put away from among yourselves that wicked person. |
| 中文 | 至于外人有神审判他们。你们应当把那恶人从你们中间赶出去。 |
