# Revelation 18

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And after these things I saw another angel come down from heaven, having great power; and the earth was lightened with his glory. |
| 中文 | 此后，我看见另有一位有大权柄的天使从天降下。地就因他的荣耀发光。 |

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
