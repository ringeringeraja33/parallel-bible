# Matthew 7 / 马太福音 7

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Judge not, that ye be not judged. |
| 中文 | 你们不要论断人，免得你们被论断。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For with what judgment ye judge, ye shall be judged: and with what measure ye mete, it shall be measured to you again. |
| 中文 | 因为你们怎样论断人，也必怎样被论断。你们用什么量器量给人，也必用什么量器量给你们。 |

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| Language | Content |
