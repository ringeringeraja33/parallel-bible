# 1 Thessalonians 5

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But of the times and the seasons, brethren, ye have no need that I write unto you. |
| 中文 | 弟兄们，论到时候日期，不用写信给你们。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For yourselves know perfectly that the day of the Lord so cometh as a thief in the night. |
| 中文 | 因为你们自己明明晓得，主的日子来到，好像夜间的贼一样。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For when they shall say, Peace and safety; then sudden destruction cometh upon them, as travail upon a woman with child; and they shall not escape. |
| 中文 | 人正说平安稳妥的时候，灾祸忽然临到他们，如同叁难临到怀胎的妇人一样，他们绝不能逃脱。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But ye, brethren, are not in darkness, that that day should overtake you as a thief. |
| 中文 | 弟兄们，你们却不在黑暗里，叫那日子临到你们像贼一样。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ye are all the children of light, and the children of the day: we are not of the night, nor of darkness. |
| 中文 | 你们都是光明之子，都是白昼之子，我们不是属黑夜的，也不是属幽暗的。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Therefore let us not sleep, as do others; but let us watch and be sober. |
| 中文 | 所以我们不要睡觉，像别人一样，总要儆醒谨守。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For they that sleep sleep in the night; and they that be drunken are drunken in the night. |
| 中文 | 因为睡了的人是在夜间睡。醉了的人是在夜间醉。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But let us, who are of the day, be sober, putting on the breastplate of faith and love; and for an helmet, the hope of salvation. |
| 中文 | 但我们既然属乎白昼，就应当谨守，把信和爱当作护心镜遮胸。把得救的盼望当作头盔戴上。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For God hath not appointed us to wrath, but to obtain salvation by our Lord Jesus Christ, |
| 中文 | 因为神不是豫定我们受刑，乃是豫定我们藉着我们主耶稣基督得救。 |
