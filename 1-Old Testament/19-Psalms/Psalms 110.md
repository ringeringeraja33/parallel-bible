# Psalms 110

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD said unto my Lord, Sit thou at my right hand, until I make thine enemies thy footstool. |
| 中文 | （大卫的诗）耶和华对我主说，你坐在我的右边，等我使你仇敌作你的脚凳。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD shall send the rod of thy strength out of Zion: rule thou in the midst of thine enemies. |
| 中文 | 耶和华必使你从锡安伸出能力的杖来。你要在你仇敌中掌权。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thy people shall be willing in the day of thy power, in the beauties of holiness from the womb of the morning: thou hast the dew of thy youth. |
| 中文 | 当你掌权的日子（或作行军的日子），你的民要以圣洁的妆饰为衣（或作以圣洁为妆饰），甘心牺牲自己。你的民多如清晨的甘露（或作你少年时光耀如清晨的甘露）。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD hath sworn, and will not repent, Thou art a priest for ever after the order of Melchizedek. |
| 中文 | 耶和华起了誓，决不后悔，说，你是照着麦基洗德的等次，永远为祭司。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The Lord at thy right hand shall strike through kings in the day of his wrath. |
| 中文 | 在你右边的主，当他发怒的日子，必打伤列王。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He shall judge among the heathen, he shall fill the places with the dead bodies; he shall wound the heads over many countries. |
| 中文 | 他要在列邦中刑罚恶人，尸首就遍满各处。他要在许多国中打破仇敌的头 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He shall drink of the brook in the way: therefore shall he lift up the head. |
| 中文 | 他要喝路旁的河水，因此必抬起头来。 |
