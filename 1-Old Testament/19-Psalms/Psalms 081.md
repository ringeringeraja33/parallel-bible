# Psalms 81

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Sing aloud unto God our strength: make a joyful noise unto the God of Jacob. |
| 中文 | （亚萨的诗，交与伶长，用迦特乐器）你们当向神我们的力量大声欢呼，向雅各的神发声欢乐， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Take a psalm, and bring hither the timbrel, the pleasant harp with the psaltery. |
| 中文 | 唱起诗歌，打手鼓，弹美琴与瑟。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Blow up the trumpet in the new moon, in the time appointed, on our solemn feast day. |
| 中文 | 当在月朔，并月望，我们过节的日期，吹角。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For this was a statute for Israel, and a law of the God of Jacob. |
| 中文 | 因这是为以色列定的律例，是雅各神的典章。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | This he ordained in Joseph for a testimony, when he went out through the land of Egypt: where I heard a language that I understood not. |
| 中文 | 他去攻击埃及地的时候，在约瑟中间立此为证。我在那里听见我所不明白的言语。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I removed his shoulder from the burden: his hands were delivered from the pots. |
| 中文 | 神说，我使你的肩得脱重担，你的手放下筐子。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou calledst in trouble, and I delivered thee; I answered thee in the secret place of thunder: I proved thee at the waters of Meribah. Selah. |
| 中文 | 你在急难中呼求，我就搭救你。我在雷的隐密处应允你，在米利巴水那里试验你。（细拉） |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hear, O my people, and I will testify unto thee: O Israel, if thou wilt hearken unto me; |
| 中文 | 我的民哪，你当听。我要劝戒你。以色列阿，甚愿你肯听从我。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | There shall no strange god be in thee; neither shalt thou worship any strange god. |
| 中文 | 在你当中不可有别的神。外邦的神，你也不可下拜。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I am the LORD thy God, which brought thee out of the land of Egypt: open thy mouth wide, and I will fill it. |
| 中文 | 我是耶和华你的神，曾把你从埃及地领上来。你要大大张口，我就给你充满。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But my people would not hearken to my voice; and Israel would none of me. |
| 中文 | 无奈我的民不听我的声音。以色列全不理我。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | So I gave them up unto their own hearts' lust: and they walked in their own counsels. |
| 中文 | 我便任凭他们心里刚硬，随自己的计谋而行。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Oh that my people had hearkened unto me, and Israel had walked in my ways! |
| 中文 | 甚愿我的民肯听从我，以色列肯行我的道。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I should soon have subdued their enemies, and turned my hand against their adversaries. |
| 中文 | 我便速速治服他们的仇敌，反手攻击他们的敌人。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The haters of the LORD should have submitted themselves unto him: but their time should have endured for ever. |
| 中文 | 恨耶和华的人必来投降，但他的百姓，必永久长存。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He should have fed them also with the finest of the wheat: and with honey out of the rock should I have satisfied thee. |
| 中文 | 他也必拿上好的麦子给他们吃。有拿从磐石出的蜂蜜，叫他们饱足。 |
