# Psalms 114

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When Israel went out of Egypt, the house of Jacob from a people of strange language; |
| 中文 | 以色列出了埃及，雅各家离开说异言之民。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Judah was his sanctuary, and Israel his dominion. |
| 中文 | 那时，犹大为主的圣所，以色列为他所治理的国度。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The sea saw it, and fled: Jordan was driven back. |
| 中文 | 沧海看见就奔逃。约旦河也倒流。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The mountains skipped like rams, and the little hills like lambs. |
| 中文 | 大山踊跃，如公羊。小山跳舞，如羊羔。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | What ailed thee, O thou sea, that thou fleddest? thou Jordan, that thou wast driven back? |
| 中文 | 沧海阿，你为何奔逃。约旦哪，你为何倒流。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ye mountains, that ye skipped like rams; and ye little hills, like lambs? |
| 中文 | 大山哪，你为何踊跃，如公羊。小山哪，你为何跳舞，如羊羔。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 大地阿，你因见主的面，就是雅各神的面，便要震动。 |

## 8
