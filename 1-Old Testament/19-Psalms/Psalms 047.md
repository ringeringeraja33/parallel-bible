# Psalms 47 / 诗篇 47

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O clap your hands, all ye people; shout unto God with the voice of triumph. |
| 中文 | （可拉后裔的诗，交与伶长）万民哪，你们都要拍掌。要用夸胜的声音向神呼喊。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the LORD most high is terrible; he is a great King over all the earth. |
| 中文 | 因为耶和华至高者是可畏的。他是治理全地的大君王。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He shall subdue the people under us, and the nations under our feet. |
| 中文 | 他叫万民服在我们以下，又叫列邦服在我们脚下。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He shall choose our inheritance for us, the excellency of Jacob whom he loved. Selah. |
| 中文 | 他为我们选择产业，就是他所爱之雅各的荣耀。（细拉）。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | God is gone up with a shout, the LORD with the sound of a trumpet. |
| 中文 | 神上升，有喊声相送。耶和华上升，有角声相送。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Sing praises to God, sing praises: sing praises unto our King, sing praises. |
| 中文 | 你们要向神歌颂，歌颂，向我们王歌颂，歌颂。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For God is the King of all the earth: sing ye praises with understanding. |
| 中文 | 因为神是全地的王。你们要用悟性歌颂。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | God reigneth over the heathen: God sitteth upon the throne of his holiness. |
| 中文 | 神作王治理万国。神坐在他的圣宝座上。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The princes of the people are gathered together, even the people of the God of Abraham: for the shields of the earth belong unto God: he is greatly exalted. |
| 中文 | 列邦的君王聚集，要作亚伯拉罕之神的民。因为世界的盾牌，是属神的。他为至高。 |
