# Romans 12 / 罗马书 12

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I beseech you therefore, brethren, by the mercies of God, that ye present your bodies a living sacrifice, holy, acceptable unto God, which is your reasonable service. |
| 中文 | 所以弟兄们，我以神的慈悲劝你们，将身体献上，当作活祭，是圣洁的，是神所喜悦的。你们如此事奉，乃是理所当然的。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And be not conformed to this world: but be ye transformed by the renewing of your mind, that ye may prove what is that good, and acceptable, and perfect, will of God. |
| 中文 | 不要效法这世界。只要心意更新而变化，叫你们察验何为神的善良，纯全可喜悦的旨意。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For I say, through the grace given unto me, to every man that is among you, not to think of himself more highly than he ought to think; but to think soberly, according as God hath dealt to every man the measure of faith. |
| 中文 | 我凭着所赐我的恩，对你们各人说，不要看自己过于所当看的。要照着神所分给各人信心的大小，看得合乎中道。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For as we have many members in one body, and all members have not the same office: |
| 中文 | 正如我们一个身子上有好些肢体，肢体也不都是一样的用处。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | So we, being many, are one body in Christ, and every one members one of another. |
| 中文 | 我们这许多人，在基督里成为一身，互相联络作肢体，也是如此。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Having then gifts differing according to the grace that is given to us, whether prophecy, let us prophesy according to the proportion of faith; |
| 中文 | 按我们所得的恩赐，各有不同。或说预言，就当照着信心的程度说预言 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Or ministry, let us wait on our ministering: or he that teacheth, on teaching; |
| 中文 | 或作执事，就当专一执事。或作教导的，就当专一教导。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Or he that exhorteth, on exhortation: he that giveth, let him do it with simplicity; he that ruleth, with diligence; he that sheweth mercy, with cheerfulness. |
| 中文 | 或作劝化的，就当专一劝化。施舍的就当诚实。治理的，就当殷勤。怜悯人的，就当甘心。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let love be without dissimulation. Abhor that which is evil; cleave to that which is good. |
| 中文 | 爱人不可虚假，恶要厌恶，善要亲近。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Be kindly affectioned one to another with brotherly love; in honour preferring one another; |
| 中文 | 爱弟兄，要彼此亲热。恭敬人，要彼此推让。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Not slothful in business; fervent in spirit; serving the Lord; |
| 中文 | 殷勤不可懒惰。要心里火热。常常服事主。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Rejoicing in hope; patient in tribulation; continuing instant in prayer; |
| 中文 | 在指望中要喜乐。在患难中要忍耐。祷告要恒切。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Distributing to the necessity of saints; given to hospitality. |
| 中文 | 圣徒缺乏要帮补。客要一味的款待。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Bless them which persecute you: bless, and curse not. |
| 中文 | 逼迫你们的，要给他们祝福。只要祝福，不可咒诅。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Rejoice with them that do rejoice, and weep with them that weep. |
| 中文 | 与喜乐的人要同乐。与哀哭的人要同哭。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Be of the same mind one toward another. Mind not high things, but condescend to men of low estate. Be not wise in your own conceits. |
| 中文 | 要彼此同心。不要志气高大，倒要俯就卑微的人。（人或作事）不要自以为聪明。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Recompense to no man evil for evil. Provide things honest in the sight of all men. |
| 中文 | 不要以恶报恶，众人以为美的事，要留心去作。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If it be possible, as much as lieth in you, live peaceably with all men. |
| 中文 | 若行，总要尽力与众人和睦。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Dearly beloved, avenge not yourselves, but rather give place unto wrath: for it is written, Vengeance is mine; I will repay, saith the Lord. |
| 中文 | 亲串的弟兄，不要自己伸冤，宁可让步，听凭主怒。（或作让人发怒）因为经上记着，主说，伸冤在我。我必报应。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Therefore if thine enemy hunger, feed him; if he thirst, give him drink: for in so doing thou shalt heap coals of fire on his head. |
| 中文 | 所以，你的仇敌若饿了，就给他吃。若渴了，就给他喝。因为陪这样行，就是把炭火堆在他的头上。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Be not overcome of evil, but overcome evil with good. |
| 中文 | 你不可为恶所胜，反要以善胜恶。 |
