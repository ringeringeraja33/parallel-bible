# Psalms 45

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My heart is inditing a good matter: I speak of the things which I have made touching the king: my tongue is the pen of a ready writer. |
| 中文 | （可拉后裔的训诲诗，又是爱慕歌，交与伶长，调用百合花）。我心里涌出美辞。我论到我为王作的事。我的舌头是快手笔。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou art fairer than the children of men: grace is poured into thy lips: therefore God hath blessed thee for ever. |
| 中文 | 你比世人更美，在你嘴里满有恩惠。所以神赐福给你，直到永远。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Gird thy sword upon thy thigh, O most mighty, with thy glory and thy majesty. |
| 中文 | 大能者阿，愿你腰间佩刀，大有荣耀和威严。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And in thy majesty ride prosperously because of truth and meekness and righteousness; and thy right hand shall teach thee terrible things. |
| 中文 | 为真理，谦卑，公义，赫然坐车前往，无不得胜。你的右手必显明可畏的事。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thine arrows are sharp in the heart of the king's enemies; whereby the people fall under thee. |
| 中文 | 你的箭锋快，射中王敌之心。万民仆倒在你以下。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thy throne, O God, is for ever and ever: the sceptre of thy kingdom is a right sceptre. |
| 中文 | 神阿，你的宝座是永永远远的，你的国权是正直的。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou lovest righteousness, and hatest wickedness: therefore God, thy God, hath anointed thee with the oil of gladness above thy fellows. |
| 中文 | 你喜爱公义，恨恶罪恶。所以神，就是你的神，用喜乐油膏你，胜过膏你的同伴。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | All thy garments smell of myrrh, and aloes, and cassia, out of the ivory palaces, whereby they have made thee glad. |
| 中文 | 你的衣服，都有没药沉香肉桂的香气。象牙宫中有丝弦乐器的声音，使你欢喜。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Kings' daughters were among thy honourable women: upon thy right hand did stand the queen in gold of Ophir. |
| 中文 | 有君王的女儿，在你尊贵妇女之中。王后佩戴俄斐金饰，站在你右边。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hearken, O daughter, and consider, and incline thine ear; forget also thine own people, and thy father's house; |
| 中文 | 女子阿，你要听，要想，要侧耳而听。不要记念你的民，和你的父家。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | So shall the king greatly desire thy beauty: for he is thy Lord; and worship thou him. |
| 中文 | 王就羡慕你的美貌。因为他是你的主。你当敬拜他。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the daughter of Tyre shall be there with a gift; even the rich among the people shall intreat thy favour. |
| 中文 | 推罗的民，（民原文作女子），必来送礼。民中富足人，也必向你求恩。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The king's daughter is all glorious within: her clothing is of wrought gold. |
| 中文 | 王女在宫里，极其荣华。他的衣服是用金线绣的。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | She shall be brought unto the king in raiment of needlework: the virgins her companions that follow her shall be brought unto thee. |
| 中文 | 他要穿锦绣的衣服，被引到王前。随从他的陪伴童女，也要被带到你面前。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | With gladness and rejoicing shall they be brought: they shall enter into the king's palace. |
| 中文 | 他们要欢喜快乐被引导。他们要进入王宫。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Instead of thy fathers shall be thy children, whom thou mayest make princes in all the earth. |
| 中文 | 你的子孙要接续你的列祖。你要立他们在全地作王。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will make thy name to be remembered in all generations: therefore shall the people praise thee for ever and ever. |
| 中文 | 我必叫你的名被万代记念。所以万民要永永远远称谢你。 |
