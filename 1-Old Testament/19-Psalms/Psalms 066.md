# Psalms 66 / 诗篇 66

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Make a joyful noise unto God, all ye lands: |
| 中文 | （一篇诗歌，交与伶长）全地都当向神欢呼。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Sing forth the honour of his name: make his praise glorious. |
| 中文 | 歌颂他名的荣耀。用赞美的言语，将他的荣耀发明。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Say unto God, How terrible art thou in thy works! through the greatness of thy power shall thine enemies submit themselves unto thee. |
| 中文 | 当对神说，你的作为何等可畏。因你的大能仇敌要投降你。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | All the earth shall worship thee, and shall sing unto thee; they shall sing to thy name. Selah. |
| 中文 | 全地要敬拜你，歌颂你，要歌颂你的名。（细拉） |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Come and see the works of God: he is terrible in his doing toward the children of men. |
| 中文 | 你们来看神所行的。他向世人所作的事，是可畏的。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He turned the sea into dry land: they went through the flood on foot: there did we rejoice in him. |
| 中文 | 他将海变成乾地。众民步行过河。我们在那里因他欢喜。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He ruleth by his power for ever; his eyes behold the nations: let not the rebellious exalt themselves. Selah. |
| 中文 | 他用权能治理万民，直到永远。他的眼睛鉴察列邦。悖逆的人不可自高。（细拉） |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O bless our God, ye people, and make the voice of his praise to be heard: |
| 中文 | 万民哪，你们应当称颂我们的神。使人得听赞美他的声音。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Which holdeth our soul in life, and suffereth not our feet to be moved. |
| 中文 | 他使我们的生命存活，也不叫我们的脚摇动。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thou, O God, hast proved us: thou hast tried us, as silver is tried. |
| 中文 | 神阿，你曾试验我们，熬炼我们，如熬炼银子一样。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou broughtest us into the net; thou laidst affliction upon our loins. |
| 中文 | 你使我们进入网罗，把重担放在我们的身上。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou hast caused men to ride over our heads; we went through fire and through water: but thou broughtest us out into a wealthy place. |
| 中文 | 你使人坐车轧我们的头。我们经过水火。你却使我们到丰富之地。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will go into thy house with burnt offerings: I will pay thee my vows, |
| 中文 | 我要用燔祭进你的殿，向你还我的愿。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Which my lips have uttered, and my mouth hath spoken, when I was in trouble. |
| 中文 | 就是在急难时我嘴唇所发的，口中所许的。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will offer unto thee burnt sacrifices of fatlings, with the incense of rams; I will offer bullocks with goats. Selah. |
| 中文 | 我要把肥牛作燔祭，将公羊的香祭献给你。又把公牛和山羊献上。（细拉） |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Come and hear, all ye that fear God, and I will declare what he hath done for my soul. |
| 中文 | 凡敬畏神的人，你们都来听。我要述说他为我所行的事。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I cried unto him with my mouth, and he was extolled with my tongue. |
| 中文 | 我曾用口求告他，我的舌头，也称他为高。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If I regard iniquity in my heart, the Lord will not hear me: |
| 中文 | 我若心里注重罪孽，主必不听。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But verily God hath heard me; he hath attended to the voice of my prayer. |
| 中文 | 但神实在听见了。他侧耳听了我祷告的声音。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Blessed be God, which hath not turned away my prayer, nor his mercy from me. |
| 中文 | 神是应当称颂的。他并没有推却我的祷告，也没有叫他的慈爱离开我。 |
