# Job 8

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then answered Bildad the Shuhite, and said, |
| 中文 | 书亚人比勒达回答说， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | How long wilt thou speak these things? and how long shall the words of thy mouth be like a strong wind? |
| 中文 | 这些话你要说到几时。口中的言语如狂风要到几时呢。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Doth God pervert judgment? or doth the Almighty pervert justice? |
| 中文 | 神岂能偏离公平。全能者岂能偏离公义。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If thy children have sinned against him, and he have cast them away for their transgression; |
| 中文 | 或者你的儿女得罪了他，他使他们受报应。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If thou wouldest seek unto God betimes, and make thy supplication to the Almighty; |
| 中文 | 你若殷勤地寻求神，向全能者恳求。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If thou wert pure and upright; surely now he would awake for thee, and make the habitation of thy righteousness prosperous. |
| 中文 | 你若清洁正直，他必定为你起来，使你公义的居所兴旺。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Though thy beginning was small, yet thy latter end should greatly increase. |
| 中文 | 你起初虽然微小，终久必甚发达。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For enquire, I pray thee, of the former age, and prepare thyself to the search of their fathers: |
| 中文 | 请你考问前代，追念他们的列祖所查究的。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | (For we are but of yesterday, and know nothing, because our days upon earth are a shadow:) |
| 中文 | 我们不过从昨日才有，一无所知。我们在世的日子好像影儿。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Shall not they teach thee, and tell thee, and utter words out of their heart? |
| 中文 | 他们岂不指教你，告诉你，从心里发出言语来呢。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Can the rush grow up without mire? can the flag grow without water? |
| 中文 | 蒲草没有泥，岂能发长。芦荻没有水，岂能生发。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Whilst it is yet in his greenness, and not cut down, it withereth before any other herb. |
| 中文 | 尚青的时候，还没有割下，比百样的草先枯槁。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | So are the paths of all that forget God; and the hypocrite's hope shall perish: |
| 中文 | 凡忘记神的人，景况也是这样。不虔敬人的指望要灭没。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Whose hope shall be cut off, and whose trust shall be a spider's web. |
| 中文 | 他所仰赖的必折断，他所倚靠的是蜘蛛网。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He shall lean upon his house, but it shall not stand: he shall hold it fast, but it shall not endure. |
| 中文 | 他要倚靠房屋，房屋却站立不住。他要抓住房屋，房屋却不能存留。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He is green before the sun, and his branch shooteth forth in his garden. |
| 中文 | 他在日光之下发青，蔓子爬满了园子。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 他的根盘绕石堆，扎入石地。 |

| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 他若从本地被拔出，那地就不认识他，说，我没有见过你。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| --- | --- |
