# Psalms 86

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Bow down thine ear, O LORD, hear me: for I am poor and needy. |
| 中文 | （大卫的祈祷）耶和华阿，求你侧耳应允我，因我是困苦穷乏的。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Preserve my soul; for I am holy: O thou my God, save thy servant that trusteth in thee. |
| 中文 | 求你保存我的性命。因我是虔诚人。我的神阿，求你拯救这倚靠你的仆人。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Be merciful unto me, O Lord: for I cry unto thee daily. |
| 中文 | 主阿，求你怜悯我，因我终日求告你。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Rejoice the soul of thy servant: for unto thee, O Lord, do I lift up my soul. |
| 中文 | 主阿，求你使仆人心里欢喜。因为我的心仰望你。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thou, Lord, art good, and ready to forgive; and plenteous in mercy unto all them that call upon thee. |
| 中文 | 主阿，你本为良善，乐意饶恕人，有丰盛的慈爱，赐给凡求告你的人。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Give ear, O LORD, unto my prayer; and attend to the voice of my supplications. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你留心听我的祷告，垂听我恳求的声音。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | In the day of my trouble I will call upon thee: for thou wilt answer me. |
| 中文 | 我在患难之日要求告你。因为你必应允我。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Among the gods there is none like unto thee, O Lord; neither are there any works like unto thy works. |
| 中文 | 主阿，诸神之中，没有可比你的。你的作为也无可比。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | All nations whom thou hast made shall come and worship before thee, O Lord; and shall glorify thy name. |
| 中文 | 主阿，你所造的万民，都要来敬拜你。他们也要荣耀你的名。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thou art great, and doest wondrous things: thou art God alone. |
| 中文 | 因你为大，且行奇妙的事。惟独你是神。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Teach me thy way, O LORD; I will walk in thy truth: unite my heart to fear thy name. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你将你的道指教我。我要照你的真理行。求你使我专心敬畏你的名。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will praise thee, O Lord my God, with all my heart: and I will glorify thy name for evermore. |
| 中文 | 主我的神阿，我要一心称赞你。我要荣耀你的名，直到永远。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For great is thy mercy toward me: and thou hast delivered my soul from the lowest hell. |
| 中文 | 因为你向我发的慈爱是大的。你救了我的灵魂，免入极深的阴间。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O God, the proud are risen against me, and the assemblies of violent men have sought after my soul; and have not set thee before them. |
| 中文 | 神阿，骄傲的人起来攻击我，又有一党强横的人寻索我的命。他们没有将你放在眼中。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But thou, O Lord, art a God full of compassion, and gracious, long suffering, and plenteous in mercy and truth. |
| 中文 | 主阿，你是有怜悯有恩典的神，不轻易发怒，并有丰盛的慈爱和诚实。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O turn unto me, and have mercy upon me; give thy strength unto thy servant, and save the son of thine handmaid. |
| 中文 | 求你向我转脸，怜恤我，将你的力量赐给仆人，救你婢女的儿子。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Shew me a token for good; that they which hate me may see it, and be ashamed: because thou, LORD, hast holpen me, and comforted me. |
| 中文 | 求你向我显出恩待我的凭据，叫恨我的人看见，便羞愧，因为你耶和华帮助我，安慰我。 |
