# Proverbs 6

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My son, if thou be surety for thy friend, if thou hast stricken thy hand with a stranger, |
| 中文 | 我儿，你若为朋友作保，替外人击掌， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou art snared with the words of thy mouth, thou art taken with the words of thy mouth. |
| 中文 | 你就被口中的话语缠住，被嘴里的言语捉住。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Do this now, my son, and deliver thyself, when thou art come into the hand of thy friend; go, humble thyself, and make sure thy friend. |
| 中文 | 我儿，你既落在朋友手中，就当这样行，才可救自己。你要自卑，去恳求你的朋友。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Give not sleep to thine eyes, nor slumber to thine eyelids. |
| 中文 | 不要容你的眼睛睡觉，不要容你的眼皮打盹。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Deliver thyself as a roe from the hand of the hunter, and as a bird from the hand of the fowler. |
| 中文 | 要救自己，如鹿脱离猎户的手，如鸟脱离捕鸟人的手。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Go to the ant, thou sluggard; consider her ways, and be wise: |
| 中文 | 懒惰人哪，你去察看蚂蚁的动作，就可得智慧。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Which having no guide, overseer, or ruler, |
| 中文 | 蚂蚁没有元帅，没有官长，没有君王， |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Provideth her meat in the summer, and gathereth her food in the harvest. |
| 中文 | 尚且在夏天豫备食物，在收割时聚敛粮食。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | How long wilt thou sleep, O sluggard? when wilt thou arise out of thy sleep? |
| 中文 | 懒惰人哪，你要睡到几时呢。你何时睡醒呢。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Yet a little sleep, a little slumber, a little folding of the hands to sleep: |
| 中文 | 再睡片时，打盹片时，抱着手躺卧片时， |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | So shall thy poverty come as one that travelleth, and thy want as an armed man. |
| 中文 | 你的贪穷就必如强盗速来，你的缺乏仿佛拿兵器的人来到。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A naughty person, a wicked man, walketh with a froward mouth. |
| 中文 | 无赖的恶徒，行动就用乖僻的口。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He winketh with his eyes, he speaketh with his feet, he teacheth with his fingers; |
| 中文 | 用眼传神，用脚示意，用指点划。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Frowardness is in his heart, he deviseth mischief continually; he soweth discord. |
| 中文 | 心中乖僻，常设恶谋，布散分争。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Therefore shall his calamity come suddenly; suddenly shall he be broken without remedy. |
| 中文 | 所以灾难必忽然临到他身。他必顷刻败坏无法可治。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | These six things doth the LORD hate: yea, seven are an abomination unto him: |
| 中文 | 耶和华所恨恶的有六样，连他心所憎恶的共有七样。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A proud look, a lying tongue, and hands that shed innocent blood, |
| 中文 | 就是高傲的眼，撒谎的舌，流无辜人血的手， |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | An heart that deviseth wicked imaginations, feet that be swift in running to mischief, |
| 中文 | 图谋恶计的心，飞跑行恶的脚， |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A false witness that speaketh lies, and he that soweth discord among brethren. |
| 中文 | 吐谎言的假见证，并弟兄中布散分争的人。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My son, keep thy father's commandment, and forsake not the law of thy mother: |
| 中文 | 我儿，要谨守你父亲的诫命，不可离弃你母亲的法则。（或作指教） |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Bind them continually upon thine heart, and tie them about thy neck. |
| 中文 | 要常系在你心上，挂在你项上。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When thou goest, it shall lead thee; when thou sleepest, it shall keep thee; and when thou awakest, it shall talk with thee. |
| 中文 | 你行走，他必引导你。你躺卧，他必保守你。你睡醒，他必与你谈论。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the commandment is a lamp; and the law is light; and reproofs of instruction are the way of life: |
| 中文 | 因为诫命是灯，法则（或作指教）是光。训诲的责备是生命的道。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | To keep thee from the evil woman, from the flattery of the tongue of a strange woman. |
| 中文 | 能保你远离恶妇，远离外女谄媚的舌头。 |

## 25

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Lust not after her beauty in thine heart; neither let her take thee with her eyelids. |
| 中文 | 你心中不要恋慕他的美色，也不要被他的眼皮勾引。 |

## 26

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 因为妓女能使人只剩一块饼，淫妇猎取人宝贵的生命。 |

## 27

| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 人若怀里搋火，衣服岂能不烧呢。 |

## 28

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 人若在火炭上走，脚岂能不烫呢。 |

| 中文 | 亲近邻舍之妻的，也是如此。凡挨近他的，不免受罚。 |
