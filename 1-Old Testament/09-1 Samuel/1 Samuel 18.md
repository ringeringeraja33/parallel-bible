## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And it came to pass, when he had made an end of speaking unto Saul, that the soul of Jonathan was knit with the soul of David, and Jonathan loved him as his own soul. |
| 中文 | 大卫对扫罗说完了话，约拿单的心与大卫的心深相契合。约拿单爱大卫，如同爱自己的性命。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then Jonathan and David made a covenant, because he loved him as his own soul. |
| 中文 | 约拿单爱大卫如同爱自己的性命，就与他结盟。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And Jonathan stripped himself of the robe that was upon him, and gave it to David, and his garments, even to his sword, and to his bow, and to his girdle. |
| 中文 | 约拿单从身上脱下外袍，给了大卫，又将战衣，刀，弓，腰带都给了他。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And David went out whithersoever Saul sent him, and behaved himself wisely: and Saul set him over the men of war, and he was accepted in the sight of all the people, and also in the sight of Saul's servants. |
| 中文 | 扫罗无论差遣大卫往何处去，他都作事精明。扫罗就立他作战士长，众百姓和扫罗的臣仆无不喜悦。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And it came to pass as they came, when David was returned from the slaughter of the Philistine, that the women came out of all cities of Israel, singing and dancing, to meet king Saul, with tabrets, with joy, and with instruments of musick. |
| 中文 | 大卫打死了那非利士人，同众人回来的时候，妇女们从以色列各城里出来，欢欢喜喜，打鼓击磬，歌唱跳舞，迎接扫罗王。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |

| 中文 | 众妇女舞蹈唱和，说，扫罗杀死千千，大卫杀死万万。 |
