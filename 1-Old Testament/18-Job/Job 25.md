# Job 25 / 约伯记 25

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then answered Bildad the Shuhite, and said, |
| 中文 | 书亚人比勒达回答说， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Dominion and fear are with him, he maketh peace in his high places. |
| 中文 | 神有治理之权，有威严可畏。他在高处施行和平。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Is there any number of his armies? and upon whom doth not his light arise? |
| 中文 | 他的诸军，岂能数算。他的光亮一发，谁不蒙照呢。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | How then can man be justified with God? or how can he be clean that is born of a woman? |
| 中文 | 这样在神面前，人怎能称义。妇人所生的怎能洁净。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 在神眼前，月亮也无光亮，星宿也不清洁。 |

## 6
