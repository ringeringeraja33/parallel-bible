# Psalms 71 / 诗篇 71

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | In thee, O LORD, do I put my trust: let me never be put to confusion. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，我投靠你。求你叫我永不羞愧。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Deliver me in thy righteousness, and cause me to escape: incline thine ear unto me, and save me. |
| 中文 | 求你凭你的公义搭救我，救拔我，侧耳听我，拯救我。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Be thou my strong habitation, whereunto I may continually resort: thou hast given commandment to save me; for thou art my rock and my fortress. |
| 中文 | 求你作我常住的磐石。你已经命定要救我。因为你是我的岩石，我的山寨。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Deliver me, O my God, out of the hand of the wicked, out of the hand of the unrighteous and cruel man. |
| 中文 | 我的神阿，求你救我脱离恶人的手，脱离不义和残暴之人的手。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thou art my hope, O Lord GOD: thou art my trust from my youth. |
| 中文 | 主耶和华阿，你是我所盼望的。从我年幼你是我所倚靠的。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | By thee have I been holden up from the womb: thou art he that took me out of my mother's bowels: my praise shall be continually of thee. |
| 中文 | 我从出母胎被你扶持。使我出母腹的是你。我必常常赞美你。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I am as a wonder unto many; but thou art my strong refuge. |
| 中文 | 许多人以我为怪。但你是我坚固的避难所。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let my mouth be filled with thy praise and with thy honour all the day. |
| 中文 | 你的赞美，你的荣耀，终日必满了我的口。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Cast me not off in the time of old age; forsake me not when my strength faileth. |
| 中文 | 我年老的时候，求你不要丢弃我。我力气衰弱的时候，求你不要离弃我。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For mine enemies speak against me; and they that lay wait for my soul take counsel together, |
| 中文 | 我的仇敌议论我。那些窥探要害我命的彼此商议， |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Saying, God hath forsaken him: persecute and take him; for there is none to deliver him. |
| 中文 | 说，神已经离弃他。我们追赶他，捉拿他吧。因为没有人搭救。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O God, be not far from me: O my God, make haste for my help. |
| 中文 | 神阿，求你不要远离我。我的神阿，求你速速帮助我。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let them be confounded and consumed that are adversaries to my soul; let them be covered with reproach and dishonour that seek my hurt. |
| 中文 | 愿那与我性命为敌的，羞愧被灭。愿那谋害我的，受辱蒙羞。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But I will hope continually, and will yet praise thee more and more. |
| 中文 | 我却要常常盼望，并要越发赞美你。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My mouth shall shew forth thy righteousness and thy salvation all the day; for I know not the numbers thereof. |
| 中文 | 我的口终日要述说你的公义，和你的救恩。因我不计其数。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will go in the strength of the Lord GOD: I will make mention of thy righteousness, even of thine only. |
| 中文 | 我要来说主耶和华大能的事。我单要题说你的公义。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O God, thou hast taught me from my youth: and hitherto have I declared thy wondrous works. |
| 中文 | 神阿，自我年幼时，你就教训我。直到如今，我传扬你奇妙的作为。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Now also when I am old and greyheaded, O God, forsake me not; until I have shewed thy strength unto this generation, and thy power to every one that is to come. |
| 中文 | 神阿，我到年老发白的时候，求你不要离弃我，等我将你的能力指示下代，将你的大能指示后世的人。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thy righteousness also, O God, is very high, who hast done great things: O God, who is like unto thee! |
| 中文 | 神阿，你的公义甚高。行过大事的神阿，谁能像你。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou, which hast shewed me great and sore troubles, shalt quicken me again, and shalt bring me up again from the depths of the earth. |
| 中文 | 你是叫我们多经历重大急难的，必使我们复活。从地的深处救上来。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt increase my greatness, and comfort me on every side. |
| 中文 | 求你使我越发昌大，又转来安慰我。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will also praise thee with the psaltery, even thy truth, O my God: unto thee will I sing with the harp, O thou Holy One of Israel. |
| 中文 | 我的神阿，我要鼓瑟称赞你，称赞你的诚实。以色列的圣者阿，我要弹琴歌颂你。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My lips shall greatly rejoice when I sing unto thee; and my soul, which thou hast redeemed. |
| 中文 | 我歌颂你的时候，我的嘴唇，和你所赎我的灵魂，都必欢呼。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My tongue also shall talk of thy righteousness all the day long: for they are confounded, for they are brought unto shame, that seek my hurt. |
| 中文 | 并且我的舌头，必终日讲论你的公义，因为那些谋害我的人，已经蒙羞受辱了。 |
