# Ecclesiastes 1 / 传道书 1

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The words of the Preacher, the son of David, king in Jerusalem. |
| 中文 | 在耶路撒冷作王，大卫的儿子，传道者的言语。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Vanity of vanities, saith the Preacher, vanity of vanities; all is vanity. |
| 中文 | 传道者说，虚空的虚空，虚空的虚空。凡事都是虚空。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | What profit hath a man of all his labour which he taketh under the sun? |
| 中文 | 人一切的劳碌，就是他在日光之下的劳碌，有什么益处呢。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | One generation passeth away, and another generation cometh: but the earth abideth for ever. |
| 中文 | 一代过去，一代又来。地却永远长存。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The sun also ariseth, and the sun goeth down, and hasteth to his place where he arose. |
| 中文 | 日头出来，日头落下，急归所出之地。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The wind goeth toward the south, and turneth about unto the north; it whirleth about continually, and the wind returneth again according to his circuits. |
| 中文 | 风往南刮，又向北转，不住的旋转，而且返回转行原道。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | All the rivers run into the sea; yet the sea is not full; unto the place from whence the rivers come, thither they return again. |
| 中文 | 江河都往海里流，海却不满。江河从何处流，仍归何处。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | All things are full of labour; man cannot utter it: the eye is not satisfied with seeing, nor the ear filled with hearing. |
| 中文 | 万事令人厌烦。（或作万物满有困乏）人不能说尽。眼看，看不饱，耳听，听不足。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The thing that hath been, it is that which shall be; and that which is done is that which shall be done: and there is no new thing under the sun. |
| 中文 | 已有的事，后必再有。已行的事，后必再行。日光之下并无新事。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Is there any thing whereof it may be said, See, this is new? it hath been already of old time, which was before us. |
| 中文 | 岂有一件事人能指着说，这是新的。那知，在我们以前的世代，早已有了。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | There is no remembrance of former things; neither shall there be any remembrance of things that are to come with those that shall come after. |
| 中文 | 已过的世代，无人记念，将来的世代，后来的人也不记念。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I the Preacher was king over Israel in Jerusalem. |
| 中文 | 我传道者在耶路撒冷作过以色列的王。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And I gave my heart to seek and search out by wisdom concerning all things that are done under heaven: this sore travail hath God given to the sons of man to be exercised therewith. |
| 中文 | 我专心用智慧寻求查究天下所作的一切事，乃知神叫世人所经练的，是极重的劳苦。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I have seen all the works that are done under the sun; and, behold, all is vanity and vexation of spirit. |
| 中文 | 我见日光之下所作的一切事，都是虚空，都是捕风。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | That which is crooked cannot be made straight: and that which is wanting cannot be numbered. |
| 中文 | 弯曲的不能变直，缺少的不能足数。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I communed with mine own heart, saying, Lo, I am come to great estate, and have gotten more wisdom than all they that have been before me in Jerusalem: yea, my heart had great experience of wisdom and knowledge. |
| 中文 | 我心里议论，说，我得了大智慧，胜过我以前在耶路撒冷的众人。而且我心中多经历智慧，和知识的事。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And I gave my heart to know wisdom, and to know madness and folly: I perceived that this also is vexation of spirit. |
| 中文 | 我又专心察明智慧，狂妄，和愚昧。乃知道也是捕风。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For in much wisdom is much grief: and he that increaseth knowledge increaseth sorrow. |
| 中文 | 因为多有智慧，就多有愁烦。加增知识的，就加增忧伤。 |
