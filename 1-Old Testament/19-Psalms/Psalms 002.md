# Psalms 2

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Why do the heathen rage, and the people imagine a vain thing? |
| 中文 | 外邦为什么争闹，万民为什么谋算虚妄的事。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The kings of the earth set themselves, and the rulers take counsel together, against the LORD, and against his anointed, saying, |
| 中文 | 世上的君王一齐起来，臣宰一同商议，要敌挡耶和华，并他的受膏者， |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let us break their bands asunder, and cast away their cords from us. |
| 中文 | 说，我们要挣开他们的捆绑，脱去他们的绳索。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that sitteth in the heavens shall laugh: the LORD shall have them in derision. |
| 中文 | 那坐在天上的必发笑。主必嗤笑他们。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then shall he speak unto them in his wrath, and vex them in his sore displeasure. |
| 中文 | 那时他要在怒中责备他们，在烈怒中惊吓他们， |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Yet have I set my king upon my holy hill of Zion. |
| 中文 | 说，我已立我的君在锡安我的圣山了。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will declare the decree: the LORD hath said unto me, Thou art my Son; this day have I begotten thee. |
| 中文 | 受膏者说，我要传圣旨。耶和华曾对我说，你是我的儿子，我今日生你。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ask of me, and I shall give thee the heathen for thine inheritance, and the uttermost parts of the earth for thy possession. |
| 中文 | 你求我，我就将列国赐你为基业，将地极赐你为田产。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt break them with a rod of iron; thou shalt dash them in pieces like a potter's vessel. |
| 中文 | 你必用铁杖打破他们。你必将他们如同窑匠的瓦器摔碎。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Be wise now therefore, O ye kings: be instructed, ye judges of the earth. |
| 中文 | 现在你们君王应当醒悟。你们世上的审判官该受管教。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Serve the LORD with fear, and rejoice with trembling. |
| 中文 | 当存畏惧事奉耶和华，又当存战兢而快乐。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Kiss the Son, lest he be angry, and ye perish from the way, when his wrath is kindled but a little. Blessed are all they that put their trust in him. |
| 中文 | 当以嘴亲子，恐怕他发怒，你们便在道中灭亡，因为他的怒气快要发作。凡投靠他的，都是有福的。 |
