# Job 17

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My breath is corrupt, my days are extinct, the graves are ready for me. |
| 中文 | 我的心灵消耗，我的日子灭尽。坟墓为我预备好了。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Are there not mockers with me? and doth not mine eye continue in their provocation? |
| 中文 | 真有戏笑我的在我这里，我眼常见他们惹动我。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Lay down now, put me in a surety with thee; who is he that will strike hands with me? |
| 中文 | 愿主拿凭据给我，自己为我作保。在你以外谁肯与我击掌呢。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thou hast hid their heart from understanding: therefore shalt thou not exalt them. |
| 中文 | 因你使他们心不明理，所以你必不高举他们。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that speaketh flattery to his friends, even the eyes of his children shall fail. |
| 中文 | 控告他的朋友，以朋友为可抢夺的，连他儿女的眼睛也要失明。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He hath made me also a byword of the people; and aforetime I was as a tabret. |
| 中文 | 神使我作了民中的笑谈。他们也吐唾沫在我脸上。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Mine eye also is dim by reason of sorrow, and all my members are as a shadow. |
| 中文 | 我的眼睛因忧愁昏花。我的百体好像影儿。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Upright men shall be astonied at this, and the innocent shall stir up himself against the hypocrite. |
| 中文 | 正直人因此必惊奇。无辜的人，要兴起攻击不敬虔之辈。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The righteous also shall hold on his way, and he that hath clean hands shall be stronger and stronger. |
| 中文 | 然而，义人要持守所行的道。手洁的人要力上加力。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But as for you all, do ye return, and come now: for I cannot find one wise man among you. |
| 中文 | 至于你们众人，可以再来辩论吧。你们中间，我找不着一个智慧人。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My days are past, my purposes are broken off, even the thoughts of my heart. |
| 中文 | 我的日子已经过了。我的谋算，我心所想望的已经断绝。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They change the night into day: the light is short because of darkness. |
| 中文 | 他们以黑夜为白昼，说，亮光近乎黑暗。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If I wait, the grave is mine house: I have made my bed in the darkness. |
| 中文 | 我若盼望阴间为我的房屋，若下榻在黑暗中， |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I have said to corruption, Thou art my father: to the worm, Thou art my mother, and my sister. |
| 中文 | 若对朽坏说，你是我的父。对虫说，你是我的母亲姊妹。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And where is now my hope? as for my hope, who shall see it? |
| 中文 | 这样，我的指望在那里呢。我所指望的谁能看见呢。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They shall go down to the bars of the pit, when our rest together is in the dust. |
| 中文 | 等到安息在尘土中，这指望必下到阴间的门闩那里了。 |
