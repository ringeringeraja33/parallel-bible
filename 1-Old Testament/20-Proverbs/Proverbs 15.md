# Proverbs 15

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A soft answer turneth away wrath: but grievous words stir up anger. |
| 中文 | 回答柔和，使怒消退。言语暴戾，触动怒气。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The tongue of the wise useth knowledge aright: but the mouth of fools poureth out foolishness. |
| 中文 | 智慧人的舌，善发知识。愚昧人的口，吐出愚昧。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The eyes of the LORD are in every place, beholding the evil and the good. |
| 中文 | 耶和华的眼目，无处不在。恶人善人，他都鉴察。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A wholesome tongue is a tree of life: but perverseness therein is a breach in the spirit. |
| 中文 | 温良的舌，是生命树。乖谬的嘴，使人心碎。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A fool despiseth his father's instruction: but he that regardeth reproof is prudent. |
| 中文 | 愚妄人藐视父亲的管教。领受责备的，得着见识。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | In the house of the righteous is much treasure: but in the revenues of the wicked is trouble. |
| 中文 | 义人家中，多有财宝。恶人得利，反受扰害。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The lips of the wise disperse knowledge: but the heart of the foolish doeth not so. |
| 中文 | 智慧人的嘴，播扬知识。愚昧人的心，并不如此。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The sacrifice of the wicked is an abomination to the LORD: but the prayer of the upright is his delight. |
| 中文 | 恶人献祭，为耶和华所憎恶。正直人祈祷，为他所喜悦。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The way of the wicked is an abomination unto the LORD: but he loveth him that followeth after righteousness. |
| 中文 | 恶人的道路，为耶和华所憎恶。追求公义的，为他所喜爱。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Correction is grievous unto him that forsaketh the way: and he that hateth reproof shall die. |
| 中文 | 舍弃正路的，必受严刑。恨恶责备的，必致死亡。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hell and destruction are before the LORD: how much more then the hearts of the children of men? |
| 中文 | 阴间和死亡，尚在耶和华眼前，何况世人的心呢。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A scorner loveth not one that reproveth him: neither will he go unto the wise. |
| 中文 | 亵慢人不爱受责备。他也不就近智慧人。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A merry heart maketh a cheerful countenance: but by sorrow of the heart the spirit is broken. |
| 中文 | 心中喜乐，面带笑容。心里忧愁，灵被损伤。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The heart of him that hath understanding seeketh knowledge: but the mouth of fools feedeth on foolishness. |
| 中文 | 聪明人心求知识。愚昧人口吃愚昧。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | All the days of the afflicted are evil: but he that is of a merry heart hath a continual feast. |
| 中文 | 困苦人的日子，都是愁苦。心中欢畅的，常享丰筵。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Better is little with the fear of the LORD than great treasure and trouble therewith. |
| 中文 | 少有财宝，敬畏耶和华，强如多有财宝，烦乱不安。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Better is a dinner of herbs where love is, than a stalled ox and hatred therewith. |
| 中文 | 吃素菜，彼此相爱，强如吃肥牛，彼此相恨。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A wrathful man stirreth up strife: but he that is slow to anger appeaseth strife. |
| 中文 | 暴怒的人，挑启争端。忍怒的人，止息分争。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The way of the slothful man is as an hedge of thorns: but the way of the righteous is made plain. |
| 中文 | 懒惰人的道，像荆棘的篱笆。正直人的路，是平坦的大道。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A wise son maketh a glad father: but a foolish man despiseth his mother. |
| 中文 | 智慧子使父亲喜乐。愚昧人藐视母亲。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Folly is joy to him that is destitute of wisdom: but a man of understanding walketh uprightly. |
| 中文 | 无知的人，以愚妄为乐。聪明的人，按正直而行。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Without counsel purposes are disappointed: but in the multitude of counsellors they are established. |
| 中文 | 不先商议，所谋无效。谋士众多，所谋乃成。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A man hath joy by the answer of his mouth: and a word spoken in due season, how good is it! |
| 中文 | 口善应对，自觉喜乐。话合其时，何等美好。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The way of life is above to the wise, that he may depart from hell beneath. |
| 中文 | 智慧人从生命的道上升，使他远离在下的阴间。 |

## 25

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD will destroy the house of the proud: but he will establish the border of the widow. |
| 中文 | 耶和华必拆毁骄傲人的家。却要立定寡妇的地界。 |

## 26

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The thoughts of the wicked are an abomination to the LORD: but the words of the pure are pleasant words. |
| 中文 | 恶谋为耶和华所憎恶。良言乃为纯净。 |

## 27

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that is greedy of gain troubleth his own house; but he that hateth gifts shall live. |
| 中文 | 贪恋财利的，扰害己家。恨恶贿赂的，必得存活。 |

## 28

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The heart of the righteous studieth to answer: but the mouth of the wicked poureth out evil things. |
| 中文 | 义人的心，思量如何回答。恶人的口，吐出恶言。 |

## 29

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD is far from the wicked: but he heareth the prayer of the righteous. |
| 中文 | 耶和华远离恶人。却听义人的祷告。 |

## 30

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The light of the eyes rejoiceth the heart: and a good report maketh the bones fat. |
| 中文 | 眼有光使心喜乐。好信息使骨滋润。 |

## 31

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The ear that heareth the reproof of life abideth among the wise. |
| 中文 | 听从生命责备的，必常在智慧人中。 |

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 弃绝管教的，轻看自己的生命。听从责备的，却得智慧。 |

## 33
