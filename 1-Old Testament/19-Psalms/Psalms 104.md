# Psalms 104

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Bless the LORD, O my soul. O LORD my God, thou art very great; thou art clothed with honour and majesty. |
| 中文 | 我的心哪，你要称颂耶和华。耶和华我的神阿，你为至大。你以尊荣威严为衣服。 |

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who coverest thyself with light as with a garment: who stretchest out the heavens like a curtain: |
| 中文 | 披上亮光，如披外袍，铺张穹苍，如铺幔子。 |

| Language | Content |

| 中文 | 在水中立楼阁的楝梁，用云彩为车辇，藉着风的翅膀而行。 |

| Language | Content |

| Language | Content |
