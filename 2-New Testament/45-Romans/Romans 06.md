# Romans 6

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | What shall we say then? Shall we continue in sin, that grace may abound? |
| 中文 | 这样，怎吗说呢。我们可以仍在罪中，叫恩典显多吗。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | God forbid. How shall we, that are dead to sin, live any longer therein? |
| 中文 | 断乎不可。我们在罪上死了的人，岂可仍在罪中活着呢。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Know ye not, that so many of us as were baptized into Jesus Christ were baptized into his death? |
| 中文 | 岂不知我们这受洗归入基督耶稣的人，是受洗归入他的死吗。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Therefore we are buried with him by baptism into death: that like as Christ was raised up from the dead by the glory of the Father, even so we also should walk in newness of life. |
| 中文 | 所已，我们藉着洗礼归入死，和他一同埋葬，原是叫我们一举一动有新生的样式，像基督藉着父的荣耀从死里复活一样。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For if we have been planted together in the likeness of his death, we shall be also in the likeness of his resurrection: |
| 中文 | 我们若在他死的形状上与他联合，也要在他复活的形状上与他联合。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Knowing this, that our old man is crucified with him, that the body of sin might be destroyed, that henceforth we should not serve sin. |
| 中文 | 因为知道我们的旧人和他同定十字架，使罪身灭绝，叫我们不再作罪的奴仆。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For he that is dead is freed from sin. |
| 中文 | 因为已死的人，是脱离了罪。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Now if we be dead with Christ, we believe that we shall also live with him: |
| 中文 | 我们若是与基督同死，就信必与他同活。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Knowing that Christ being raised from the dead dieth no more; death hath no more dominion over him. |
| 中文 | 因为知道基督既从死里复活，就不再死，死也不再作他的主了。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For in that he died, he died unto sin once: but in that he liveth, he liveth unto God. |
| 中文 | 他死是向罪死了，只有一次。他活是向神活着。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Likewise reckon ye also yourselves to be dead indeed unto sin, but alive unto God through Jesus Christ our Lord. |
| 中文 | 这样，你们向罪也当看自己是死的。向神在基督耶稣里却当看自己是活的。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let not sin therefore reign in your mortal body, that ye should obey it in the lusts thereof. |
| 中文 | 所以不要容罪在你们必死的身上作王，使你们顺从身子的私欲。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Neither yield ye your members as instruments of unrighteousness unto sin: but yield yourselves unto God, as those that are alive from the dead, and your members as instruments of righteousness unto God. |
| 中文 | 也不要将你们的肢体献给罪作不义的器具。倒要像从死里复活的人，将自己献给神。并将肢体作义的器具献给神。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For sin shall not have dominion over you: for ye are not under the law, but under grace. |
| 中文 | 罪必不能作你们的主。因为你们不在律法之下，乃在恩典之下。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | What then? shall we sin, because we are not under the law, but under grace? God forbid. |
| 中文 | 这却怎吗样呢。我们在恩典之下，不在律法之下，就可以犯罪吗。断乎不可。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Know ye not, that to whom ye yield yourselves servants to obey, his servants ye are to whom ye obey; whether of sin unto death, or of obedience unto righteousness? |
| 中文 | 岂不晓得你们献上自己作奴仆，顺从谁，就作谁的奴仆吗。或作罪的奴仆，以至于死。或作顺命的奴仆，以至成义。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But God be thanked, that ye were the servants of sin, but ye have obeyed from the heart that form of doctrine which was delivered you. |
| 中文 | 感谢神，因为你们从前虽然作罪的奴仆，现今却从心里顺服了所传给你们道理的模范。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Being then made free from sin, ye became the servants of righteousness. |
| 中文 | 你们既从罪里得了释放，就作了义的奴仆。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I speak after the manner of men because of the infirmity of your flesh: for as ye have yielded your members servants to uncleanness and to iniquity unto iniquity; even so now yield your members servants to righteousness unto holiness. |
| 中文 | 我因你们肉体的软弱，就照人的常话对你们说，你们从前怎样将肢体献给不洁不法作奴仆，以至于不法。现今也要照样将肢体献给义作奴仆，以至于成圣。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For when ye were the servants of sin, ye were free from righteousness. |
| 中文 | 因为你们作罪的奴仆的时候，就不被义约束了。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | What fruit had ye then in those things whereof ye are now ashamed? for the end of those things is death. |
| 中文 | 你们现今所看为羞耻的事，当日有什么果子呢。那些事的结局就是死。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But now being made free from sin, and become servants to God, ye have your fruit unto holiness, and the end everlasting life. |
| 中文 | 但现今你们既从罪里得了释放，作了神的奴仆，就有成圣的果子，那结局就是永生。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the wages of sin is death; but the gift of God is eternal life through Jesus Christ our Lord. |
| 中文 | 因为罪的工价乃是死。惟有神的恩赐，在我们的主基督耶稣里乃是永生。 |
