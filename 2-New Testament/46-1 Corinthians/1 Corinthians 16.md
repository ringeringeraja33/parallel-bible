# 1 Corinthians 16 / 哥林多前书 16

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Now concerning the collection for the saints, as I have given order to the churches of Galatia, even so do ye. |
| 中文 | 论到为圣徒捐钱，我从前怎样吩咐加拉太的众教会，你们也当怎样行。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Upon the first day of the week let every one of you lay by him in store, as God hath prospered him, that there be no gatherings when I come. |
| 中文 | 每逢七日的第一日，各人要照自己的进项抽出来留着。免得我来的时候现凑。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And when I come, whomsoever ye shall approve by your letters, them will I send to bring your liberality unto Jerusalem. |
| 中文 | 及至我来到了，你们写信举荐谁，我就打发他们，把你们的捐资送到耶路撒冷去。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And if it be meet that I go also, they shall go with me. |
| 中文 | 若我也该去，他们可以和我同去。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Now I will come unto you, when I shall pass through Macedonia: for I do pass through Macedonia. |
| 中文 | 我要从马其顿经过。既经过了，就要到你们那里去。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And it may be that I will abide, yea, and winter with you, that ye may bring me on my journey whithersoever I go. |
| 中文 | 或者和你们同住几时，或者也过冬。无论我往那里去，你们就可以给我送行。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For I will not see you now by the way; but I trust to tarry a while with you, if the Lord permit. |
| 中文 | 我如今不愿意路过见你们。主若许我，我就指望和你们同住几时。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But I will tarry at Ephesus until Pentecost. |
| 中文 | 但我要仍旧住在以弗所，直等到五旬节。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For a great door and effectual is opened unto me, and there are many adversaries. |
| 中文 | 因为有功效的门，为我开了，并且反对的人也多。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Now if Timotheus come, see that he may be with you without fear: for he worketh the work of the Lord, as I also do. |
| 中文 | 若是提摩太来到，你们要留心，叫他在你们那里无所惧怕。因为他劳力作主的工，像我一样。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let no man therefore despise him: but conduct him forth in peace, that he may come unto me: for I look for him with the brethren. |
| 中文 | 所以无论谁，都不可藐视他。只要送他平安前行，叫他到我这里来。因我指望他和弟兄们同来。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | As touching our brother Apollos, I greatly desired him to come unto you with the brethren: but his will was not at all to come at this time; but he will come when he shall have convenient time. |
| 中文 | 至于兄弟亚波罗，我再三的劝他，同弟兄们到你们那里去。但这时他决不愿意去。几时有了机会他必去。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Watch ye, stand fast in the faith, quit you like men, be strong. |
| 中文 | 你们务要儆醒，在真道上站立得稳，要作大丈夫，要刚强。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let all your things be done with charity. |
| 中文 | 凡你们所作的，都要凭爱心而作。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I beseech you, brethren, (ye know the house of Stephanas, that it is the firstfruits of Achaia, and that they have addicted themselves to the ministry of the saints,) |
| 中文 | 弟兄们，你们晓得司提反一家，是亚该亚初结的果子。并且他们专以服事圣徒为念。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | That ye submit yourselves unto such, and to every one that helpeth with us, and laboureth. |
| 中文 | 我劝你们顺服这样的人，并一切同工同劳的人。 |

| 中文 | 司提反，和福徒拿都，并亚该古，到这里来，我很喜欢。因为你们待我有不及之处，他们补上了。 |

| 中文 | 他们叫我和你们心里都快活。这样的人，你们务要敬重。 |
