# Proverbs 20 / 箴言 20

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Wine is a mocker, strong drink is raging: and whosoever is deceived thereby is not wise. |
| 中文 | 酒能使人亵慢，浓酒使人喧囔。凡因酒错误的，就无智慧。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The fear of a king is as the roaring of a lion: whoso provoketh him to anger sinneth against his own soul. |
| 中文 | 王的威吓，如同狮子吼叫。惹动他怒的，是自害己命。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | It is an honour for a man to cease from strife: but every fool will be meddling. |
| 中文 | 远离分争，是人的尊荣。愚妄人都爱争闹。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The sluggard will not plow by reason of the cold; therefore shall he beg in harvest, and have nothing. |
| 中文 | 懒惰人因冬寒不肯耕种。到收割的时候，他必讨饭，而无所得。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Counsel in the heart of man is like deep water; but a man of understanding will draw it out. |
| 中文 | 人心怀藏谋略，好像深水。惟明哲人，才能汲引出来。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Most men will proclaim every one his own goodness: but a faithful man who can find? |
| 中文 | 人多述说自己的仁慈。但忠信人谁能遇着呢。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The just man walketh in his integrity: his children are blessed after him. |
| 中文 | 行为纯正的义人，他的子孙，是有福的。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A king that sitteth in the throne of judgment scattereth away all evil with his eyes. |
| 中文 | 王坐在审判的位上，以眼目驱散诸恶。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who can say, I have made my heart clean, I am pure from my sin? |
| 中文 | 谁能说，我洁净了我的心。我脱净了我的罪。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Divers weights, and divers measures, both of them are alike abomination to the LORD. |
| 中文 | 两样的法码，两样的升斗，都为耶和华所憎恶。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Even a child is known by his doings, whether his work be pure, and whether it be right. |
| 中文 | 孩童的动作，是清洁，是正直，都显明他的本性。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The hearing ear, and the seeing eye, the LORD hath made even both of them. |
| 中文 | 能听的耳，能看的眼，都是耶和华所造的。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Love not sleep, lest thou come to poverty; open thine eyes, and thou shalt be satisfied with bread. |
| 中文 | 不要贪睡，免致贫穷。眼要睁开，你就吃饱。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | It is naught, it is naught, saith the buyer: but when he is gone his way, then he boasteth. |
| 中文 | 买物的说，不好，不好。及至买去，他便自夸。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | There is gold, and a multitude of rubies: but the lips of knowledge are a precious jewel. |
| 中文 | 有金子，和许多珍珠。（或作红宝石）惟有知识的嘴，乃为贵重的珍宝。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Take his garment that is surety for a stranger: and take a pledge of him for a strange woman. |
| 中文 | 谁为生人作保，就拿谁的衣服。谁为外人作保，谁就要承当。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 以虚谎而得的食物，人觉甘甜。但后来他的口，必充满尘沙。 |

## 18

| 中文 | 计谋都凭筹算立定。打仗要凭智谋。 |

| 中文 | 往来传舌的，泄漏密事。大张嘴的不可与他结交。 |
