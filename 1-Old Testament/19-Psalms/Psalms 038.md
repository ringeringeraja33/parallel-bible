# Psalms 38

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O LORD, rebuke me not in thy wrath: neither chasten me in thy hot displeasure. |
| 中文 | （大卫的记念诗）耶和华阿，求你不要在怒中责备我。不要在烈怒中惩罚我。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thine arrows stick fast in me, and thy hand presseth me sore. |
| 中文 | 因为你的箭射入我身，你的手压住我。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | There is no soundness in my flesh because of thine anger; neither is there any rest in my bones because of my sin. |
| 中文 | 因你的脑怒，我的肉无一完全。因我的罪过，我的骨头也不安宁。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For mine iniquities are gone over mine head: as an heavy burden they are too heavy for me. |
| 中文 | 我的罪孽高过我的头，如同重担叫我担当不起。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My wounds stink and are corrupt because of my foolishness. |
| 中文 | 因我的愚昧，我的伤发臭流脓。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I am troubled; I am bowed down greatly; I go mourning all the day long. |
| 中文 | 我疼痛，大大拳曲，终日哀痛。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For my loins are filled with a loathsome disease: and there is no soundness in my flesh. |
| 中文 | 我满腰是火。我的肉无一完全。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I am feeble and sore broken: I have roared by reason of the disquietness of my heart. |
| 中文 | 我被压伤，身体疲倦。因心里不安，我就唉哼。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Lord, all my desire is before thee; and my groaning is not hid from thee. |
| 中文 | 主阿，我的心愿都在你面前。我的叹息不向你隐瞒。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My heart panteth, my strength faileth me: as for the light of mine eyes, it also is gone from me. |
| 中文 | 我心跳动，我力衰微，连我眼中的光，也没有了。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My lovers and my friends stand aloof from my sore; and my kinsmen stand afar off. |
| 中文 | 我的良朋密友，因我的灾病，都躲在旁边站着。我的亲戚本家，也远远的站立。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They also that seek after my life lay snares for me: and they that seek my hurt speak mischievous things, and imagine deceits all the day long. |
| 中文 | 那寻索我命的，设下网罗。那想要害我的，口出恶言，终日思想诡计。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But I, as a deaf man, heard not; and I was as a dumb man that openeth not his mouth. |
| 中文 | 但我如聋子不听，像哑吧不开口。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thus I was as a man that heareth not, and in whose mouth are no reproofs. |
| 中文 | 我如不听见的人，口中没有回话。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For in thee, O LORD, do I hope: thou wilt hear, O Lord my God. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，我仰望你。主我的神阿，你必应允我。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For I said, Hear me, lest otherwise they should rejoice over me: when my foot slippeth, they magnify themselves against me. |
| 中文 | 我曾说，恐怕他们向我夸耀。我失脚的时候，他们向我夸大。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For I am ready to halt, and my sorrow is continually before me. |
| 中文 | 我几乎跌倒，我的痛苦常在我面前。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For I will declare mine iniquity; I will be sorry for my sin. |
| 中文 | 我要承认我的罪孽。我要因我的罪忧愁。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But mine enemies are lively, and they are strong: and they that hate me wrongfully are multiplied. |
| 中文 | 但我的仇敌又活泼又强壮。无理恨我的增多了。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They also that render evil for good are mine adversaries; because I follow the thing that good is. |
| 中文 | 以恶报善的与我作对，因我是追求良善。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Forsake me not, O LORD: O my God, be not far from me. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你不要撇弃我。我的神阿，求你不要远离我。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Make haste to help me, O Lord my salvation. |
| 中文 | 拯救我的主阿，求你快快帮助我。 |
