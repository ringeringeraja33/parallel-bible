# Deuteronomy 5 / 申命记 5

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And Moses called all Israel, and said unto them, Hear, O Israel, the statutes and judgments which I speak in your ears this day, that ye may learn them, and keep, and do them. |
| 中文 | 摩西将以色列众人召了来，对他们说，以色列人哪，我今日晓谕你们的律例典章，你们要听，可以学习，谨守遵行。 |

## 9

| --- | --- |

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 当照耶和华你神所吩咐的守安息日为圣日。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 六日要劳碌作你一切的工， |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But the seventh day is the sabbath of the LORD thy God: in it thou shalt not do any work, thou, nor thy son, nor thy daughter, nor thy manservant, nor thy maidservant, nor thine ox, nor thine ass, nor any of thy cattle, nor thy stranger that is within thy gates; that thy manservant and thy maidservant may rest as well as thou. |
| 中文 | 但第七日是向耶和华你神当守的安息日。这一日，你和你的儿女，仆婢，牛，驴，牲畜，并在你城里寄居的客旅，无论何工都不可作，使你的仆婢可以和你一样安息。 |
