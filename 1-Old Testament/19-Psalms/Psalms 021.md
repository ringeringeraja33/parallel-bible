# Psalms 21

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The king shall joy in thy strength, O LORD; and in thy salvation how greatly shall he rejoice! |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，王必因你的能力欢喜。因你的救恩，他的快乐何其大。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou hast given him his heart's desire, and hast not withholden the request of his lips. Selah. |
| 中文 | 他心里所愿的，你已经赐给他。他嘴唇所求的，你未尝不应允。（细拉） |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thou preventest him with the blessings of goodness: thou settest a crown of pure gold on his head. |
| 中文 | 你以美福迎接他，把精金的冠冕戴在他头上。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He asked life of thee, and thou gavest it him, even length of days for ever and ever. |
| 中文 | 他向你求寿，你便赐给他，就是日子长久，直到永远。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | His glory is great in thy salvation: honour and majesty hast thou laid upon him. |
| 中文 | 他因你的救恩大有荣耀。你又将尊荣威严加在他身上。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thou hast made him most blessed for ever: thou hast made him exceeding glad with thy countenance. |
| 中文 | 你使他有洪福，直到永远。又使他在你面前欢喜快乐。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the king trusteth in the LORD, and through the mercy of the most High he shall not be moved. |
| 中文 | 王依靠耶和华，因至高者的慈爱必不摇动。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thine hand shall find out all thine enemies: thy right hand shall find out those that hate thee. |
| 中文 | 你的手要搜出你的一切仇敌。你的右手要搜出那些恨你的人。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt make them as a fiery oven in the time of thine anger: the LORD shall swallow them up in his wrath, and the fire shall devour them. |
| 中文 | 你发怒的时候，要使他们如在炎热的火炉中。耶和华要在他的震怒中吞灭他们。那火要把他们烧尽了。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Their fruit shalt thou destroy from the earth, and their seed from among the children of men. |
| 中文 | 你必从世上灭绝他们的子孙，从人间灭绝他们的后裔。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For they intended evil against thee: they imagined a mischievous device, which they are not able to perform. |
| 中文 | 因为他们有意加害于你。他们想出计谋，却不能作成。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Therefore shalt thou make them turn their back, when thou shalt make ready thine arrows upon thy strings against the face of them. |
| 中文 | 你必使他们转背逃跑，向他们的脸搭箭在弦。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Be thou exalted, LORD, in thine own strength: so will we sing and praise thy power. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，愿你因自己的能力显为至高。这样，我们就唱诗，歌颂你的大能。 |
