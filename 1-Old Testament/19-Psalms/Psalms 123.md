# Psalms 123 / 诗篇 123

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Unto thee lift I up mine eyes, O thou that dwellest in the heavens. |
| 中文 | （上行之诗）坐在天上的主阿，我向你举目。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, as the eyes of servants look unto the hand of their masters, and as the eyes of a maiden unto the hand of her mistress; so our eyes wait upon the LORD our God, until that he have mercy upon us. |
| 中文 | 看哪，仆人的眼睛怎样望主人的手，使女的眼睛怎样望主母的手，我们的眼睛也照样望耶和华我们的神，直到他怜悯我们。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Have mercy upon us, O LORD, have mercy upon us: for we are exceedingly filled with contempt. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你怜悯我们，怜悯我们。因为我们被藐视，已到极处。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Our soul is exceedingly filled with the scorning of those that are at ease, and with the contempt of the proud. |
| 中文 | 我们被那些安逸人的讥诮，和骄傲人的藐视，已到极处。 |
