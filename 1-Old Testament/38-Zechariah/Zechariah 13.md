# Zechariah 13 / 撒迦利亚书 13

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | In that day there shall be a fountain opened to the house of David and to the inhabitants of Jerusalem for sin and for uncleanness. |
| 中文 | 那日，必给大卫家和耶路撒冷的居民，开一个泉源，洗除罪恶与污秽。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And it shall come to pass in that day, saith the LORD of hosts, that I will cut off the names of the idols out of the land, and they shall no more be remembered: and also I will cause the prophets and the unclean spirit to pass out of the land. |
| 中文 | 万军之耶和华说，那日，我必从地上除灭偶像的名，不再被人记念。也必使这地不再有假先知与污秽的灵。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And it shall come to pass, that when any shall yet prophesy, then his father and his mother that begat him shall say unto him, Thou shalt not live; for thou speakest lies in the name of the LORD: and his father and his mother that begat him shall thrust him through when he prophesieth. |
| 中文 | 若再有人说预言，生他的父母必对他说，你不得存活，因为你托耶和华的名说假预言。生他的父母在他说预言的时候，要将他刺透。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And it shall come to pass in that day, that the prophets shall be ashamed every one of his vision, when he hath prophesied; neither shall they wear a rough garment to deceive: |
| 中文 | 那日，凡作先知说预言的，必因他所论的异象羞愧，不再穿毛衣哄骗人。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But he shall say, I am no prophet, I am an husbandman; for man taught me to keep cattle from my youth. |
| 中文 | 他必说，我不是先知，我是耕地的。我从幼年作人的奴仆。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And one shall say unto him, What are these wounds in thine hands? Then he shall answer, Those with which I was wounded in the house of my friends. |
| 中文 | 必有人问他说，你两臂中间是什么伤呢。他必回答说，这是我在亲友家中所受的伤。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Awake, O sword, against my shepherd, and against the man that is my fellow, saith the LORD of hosts: smite the shepherd, and the sheep shall be scattered: and I will turn mine hand upon the little ones. |
| 中文 | 万军之耶和华说，刀剑哪，应当兴起，攻击我的牧人，和我的同伴。击打牧人，羊就分散，我必反手加在微小者的身上。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And it shall come to pass, that in all the land, saith the LORD, two parts therein shall be cut off and die; but the third shall be left therein. |
| 中文 | 耶和华说，这全地的人，三分之二，必被剪除而死，三分之一，仍必存留。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And I will bring the third part through the fire, and will refine them as silver is refined, and will try them as gold is tried: they shall call on my name, and I will hear them: I will say, It is my people: and they shall say, The LORD is my God. |
| 中文 | 我要使这三分之一经火，熬炼他们，如熬炼银子，试炼金子。他们必求告我的名，我必应允他们。我要说，这是我的子民。他们也要说，耶和华是我们的神。 |
