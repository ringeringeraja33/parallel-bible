# Job 35

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Elihu spake moreover, and said, |
| 中文 | 以利户又说， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thinkest thou this to be right, that thou saidst, My righteousness is more than God's? |
| 中文 | 你以为有理，或以为你的公义胜于神的公义， |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thou saidst, What advantage will it be unto thee? and, What profit shall I have, if I be cleansed from my sin? |
| 中文 | 才说这与我有什么益处。我不犯罪比犯罪有什么好处呢。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will answer thee, and thy companions with thee. |
| 中文 | 我要回答你，和在你这里的朋友。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Look unto the heavens, and see; and behold the clouds which are higher than thou. |
| 中文 | 你要向天观看，瞻望那高于你的穹苍。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If thou sinnest, what doest thou against him? or if thy transgressions be multiplied, what doest thou unto him? |
| 中文 | 你若犯罪，能使神受何害呢。你的过犯加增，能使神受何损呢。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If thou be righteous, what givest thou him? or what receiveth he of thine hand? |
| 中文 | 你若是公义，还能加增他什么呢。他从你手里还接受什么呢。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thy wickedness may hurt a man as thou art; and thy righteousness may profit the son of man. |
| 中文 | 你的过恶，或能害你这类的人。你的公义，或能叫世人得益处。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | By reason of the multitude of oppressions they make the oppressed to cry: they cry out by reason of the arm of the mighty. |
| 中文 | 人因多受欺压就哀求，因受能者的辖制（辖制原文作膀臂）便求救。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But none saith, Where is God my maker, who giveth songs in the night; |
| 中文 | 却无人说，造我的神在那里。他使人夜间歌唱。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who teacheth us more than the beasts of the earth, and maketh us wiser than the fowls of heaven? |
| 中文 | 教训我们胜于地上的走兽，使我们有聪明胜于空中的飞鸟。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | There they cry, but none giveth answer, because of the pride of evil men. |
| 中文 | 他们在那里，因恶人的骄傲呼求，却无人答应。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Surely God will not hear vanity, neither will the Almighty regard it. |
| 中文 | 虚妄的呼求，神必不垂听。全能者也必不眷顾。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Although thou sayest thou shalt not see him, yet judgment is before him; therefore trust thou in him. |
| 中文 | 何况你说，你不得见他。你的案件在他面前，你等候他吧。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But now, because it is not so, he hath visited in his anger; yet he knoweth it not in great extremity: |
| 中文 | 但如今因他未曾发怒降罚，也不甚理会狂傲， |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Therefore doth Job open his mouth in vain; he multiplieth words without knowledge. |
| 中文 | 所以约伯开口说虚妄的话，多发无知识的言语。 |
