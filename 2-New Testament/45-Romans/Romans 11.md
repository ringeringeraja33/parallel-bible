# Romans 11 / 罗马书 11

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I say then, Hath God cast away his people? God forbid. For I also am an Israelite, of the seed of Abraham, of the tribe of Benjamin. |
| 中文 | 我且说，神弃绝了他的百姓吗。断乎没有。因为我也是以色列人，亚伯拉罕的后裔，属便雅悯支派的。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | God hath not cast away his people which he foreknew. Wot ye not what the scripture saith of Elias? how he maketh intercession to God against Israel, saying, |
| 中文 | 神并每有弃绝他豫先所知道的百姓。你们岂不晓得经上论到以利亚是怎吗说的呢。他在神面前怎样控告以色列人，说， |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Lord, they have killed thy prophets, and digged down thine altars; and I am left alone, and they seek my life. |
| 中文 | 主阿，他们杀了你的先知，拆了你的祭坛，只剩下我一个人，他们还要寻索我的命。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But what saith the answer of God unto him? I have reserved to myself seven thousand men, who have not bowed the knee to the image of Baal. |
| 中文 | 神的回话是怎吗说的呢。他说，我为自己留下七千人，是未曾向巴力屈膝的。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Even so then at this present time also there is a remnant according to the election of grace. |
| 中文 | 如今也是这样，照着拣选的恩典还有所留的馀数。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And if by grace, then is it no more of works: otherwise grace is no more grace. But if it be of works, then is it no more grace: otherwise work is no more work. |
| 中文 | 既是出于恩典，就不在乎行为。不然，恩典就不是恩典了。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | What then? Israel hath not obtained that which he seeketh for; but the election hath obtained it, and the rest were blinded. |
| 中文 | 这是怎吗样呢。以色列人所求的，他们没有得着。惟有蒙拣选的人得着了，其馀的就成了顽梗不化的。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | (According as it is written, God hath given them the spirit of slumber, eyes that they should not see, and ears that they should not hear;) unto this day. |
| 中文 | 如经上所记，神给他们昏迷的心，眼睛不能看见，耳朵不能听见，直到今日。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And David saith, Let their table be made a snare, and a trap, and a stumblingblock, and a recompence unto them: |
| 中文 | 大卫也说，愿他们的筵席变为网罗，变为机槛，变为绊脚石，作他们的报应。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let their eyes be darkened, that they may not see, and bow down their back alway. |
| 中文 | 愿他们的眼睛昏蒙，不得看见。愿你时常弯下他们的腰。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I say then, Have they stumbled that they should fall? God forbid: but rather through their fall salvation is come unto the Gentiles, for to provoke them to jealousy. |
| 中文 | 我且说，他们失脚是要他们跌倒吗。断乎不是。反倒因他们的过失，救恩便临到外邦人，要激动他们发愤。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Now if the fall of them be the riches of the world, and the diminishing of them the riches of the Gentiles; how much more their fulness? |
| 中文 | 若他们的过失，为天下的富足，他们的缺乏，为外邦人的富足。何况他们的丰满呢。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For I speak to you Gentiles, inasmuch as I am the apostle of the Gentiles, I magnify mine office: |
| 中文 | 我对你们外邦人说这话。因我是外邦人的使徒，所以敬重我的职分。（敬重原文作荣耀） |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If by any means I may provoke to emulation them which are my flesh, and might save some of them. |
| 中文 | 或者可已激动我骨肉之亲发愤，好救他们一些人。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For if the casting away of them be the reconciling of the world, what shall the receiving of them be, but life from the dead? |
| 中文 | 若他们被丢弃，天下就得与神和好，他们被收纳，岂不是死而复生吗。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For if the firstfruit be holy, the lump is also holy: and if the root be holy, so are the branches. |
| 中文 | 所献的新面，若是圣洁，全团也就圣洁了。树根若是圣洁，树枝也就圣洁了。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 若有几根枝子被折下来，你这野橄榄得接在其中，一同得着橄榄根的肥汁。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Boast not against the branches. But if thou boast, thou bearest not the root, but the root thee. |
| 中文 | 你就不可向旧枝子夸口，若是夸口，当知道不是你托着根，乃是根托着你。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou wilt say then, The branches were broken off, that I might be graffed in. |
| 中文 | 你若说，那枝子被折下来，是特为叫我接上。 |

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For if God spared not the natural branches, take heed lest he also spare not thee. |
| 中文 | 神既不爱惜原来的枝子，也必不爱惜你。 |

| --- | --- |

| --- | --- |
