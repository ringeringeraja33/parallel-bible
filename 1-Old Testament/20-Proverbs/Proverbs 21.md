# Proverbs 21 / 箴言 21

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The king's heart is in the hand of the LORD, as the rivers of water: he turneth it whithersoever he will. |
| 中文 | 王的心在耶和华手中，好像陇沟的水，随意流转。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Every way of a man is right in his own eyes: but the LORD pondereth the hearts. |
| 中文 | 人所行的，在自己的眼中都看为正，惟有耶和华衡量人心。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | To do justice and judgment is more acceptable to the LORD than sacrifice. |
| 中文 | 行仁义公平，比献祭更蒙耶和华悦纳。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | An high look, and a proud heart, and the plowing of the wicked, is sin. |
| 中文 | 恶人发达，眼高心傲，这乃是罪。（发达原文是灯） |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The thoughts of the diligent tend only to plenteousness; but of every one that is hasty only to want. |
| 中文 | 殷勤筹画的，足致丰裕。行事急躁的，都必缺乏。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The getting of treasures by a lying tongue is a vanity tossed to and fro of them that seek death. |
| 中文 | 用诡诈之舌求财的，就是自己取死。所得之财，乃是吹来吹去的浮云。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The robbery of the wicked shall destroy them; because they refuse to do judgment. |
| 中文 | 恶人的强暴，必将自己扫除。因他们不肯按公平行事。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The way of man is froward and strange: but as for the pure, his work is right. |
| 中文 | 负罪之人的路，甚是弯曲。至于清洁的人，他所行的乃是正直。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | It is better to dwell in a corner of the housetop, than with a brawling woman in a wide house. |
| 中文 | 宁可住在房顶的角上，不在宽阔的房屋，与争吵的妇人同住。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The soul of the wicked desireth evil: his neighbour findeth no favour in his eyes. |
| 中文 | 恶人的心，乐人受祸。他眼并不怜恤邻舍。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When the scorner is punished, the simple is made wise: and when the wise is instructed, he receiveth knowledge. |
| 中文 | 亵慢的人受刑罚，愚蒙的人就得智慧。智慧人受训诲，便得知识。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The righteous man wisely considereth the house of the wicked: but God overthroweth the wicked for their wickedness. |
| 中文 | 义人思想恶人的家，知道恶人倾倒，必至灭亡。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Whoso stoppeth his ears at the cry of the poor, he also shall cry himself, but shall not be heard. |
| 中文 | 塞耳不听穷人哀求的，他将来呼吁也不蒙应允。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A gift in secret pacifieth anger: and a reward in the bosom strong wrath. |
| 中文 | 暗中送的礼物，挽回怒气。怀中搋的贿赂，止息暴怒。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | It is joy to the just to do judgment: but destruction shall be to the workers of iniquity. |
| 中文 | 秉公行义，使义人喜乐，使作孽的人败坏。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The man that wandereth out of the way of understanding shall remain in the congregation of the dead. |
| 中文 | 迷离通达道路的，必住在阴魂的会中。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that loveth pleasure shall be a poor man: he that loveth wine and oil shall not be rich. |
| 中文 | 爱宴乐的，必致穷乏。好酒爱膏油的，必不富足。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The wicked shall be a ransom for the righteous, and the transgressor for the upright. |
| 中文 | 恶人作了义人的赎价。奸诈人代替正直人。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | It is better to dwell in the wilderness, than with a contentious and an angry woman. |
| 中文 | 宁可住在旷野，不与争吵使气的妇人同住。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | There is treasure to be desired and oil in the dwelling of the wise; but a foolish man spendeth it up. |
| 中文 | 智慧人家中积蓄宝物膏油。愚昧人随得来随吞下。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that followeth after righteousness and mercy findeth life, righteousness, and honour. |
| 中文 | 追求公义仁慈的，就寻得生命，公义，和尊荣。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A wise man scaleth the city of the mighty, and casteth down the strength of the confidence thereof. |
| 中文 | 智慧人爬上勇士的城墙，倾覆他所倚靠的坚垒。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Whoso keepeth his mouth and his tongue keepeth his soul from troubles. |
| 中文 | 谨守口与舌的，就保守自己免受灾难。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Proud and haughty scorner is his name, who dealeth in proud wrath. |
| 中文 | 心骄气傲的人，名叫亵慢。他行事狂妄，都出于骄傲。 |

## 25

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The desire of the slothful killeth him; for his hands refuse to labour. |
| 中文 | 懒惰人的心愿，将他杀害，因为他手不肯作工。 |

## 26

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He coveteth greedily all the day long: but the righteous giveth and spareth not. |
| 中文 | 有终日贪得无厌的，义人施舍而不吝惜。 |

## 27

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The sacrifice of the wicked is abomination: how much more, when he bringeth it with a wicked mind? |
| 中文 | 恶人的祭物是可憎的，何况他存恶意来献呢。 |

## 28

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A false witness shall perish: but the man that heareth speaketh constantly. |
| 中文 | 作假见证的必灭亡，惟有听真情而言的，其言长存。 |

## 29

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A wicked man hardeneth his face: but as for the upright, he directeth his way. |
| 中文 | 恶人脸无羞耻，正直人行事坚定。 |

## 30

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | There is no wisdom nor understanding nor counsel against the LORD. |
| 中文 | 没有人能以智慧，聪明，谋略，敌挡耶和华。 |

## 31

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The horse is prepared against the day of battle: but safety is of the LORD. |
| 中文 | 马是为打仗之日豫备的。得胜乃在乎耶和华。 |
