# Psalms 64 / 诗篇 64

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hear my voice, O God, in my prayer: preserve my life from fear of the enemy. |
| 中文 | （大卫的诗，交与伶长）神阿，我哀叹的时候，求你听我的声音。求你保护我的性命，不受仇敌的惊恐。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hide me from the secret counsel of the wicked; from the insurrection of the workers of iniquity: |
| 中文 | 求你把我隐藏，使我脱离恶之人的暗谋，和作孽之人的扰乱。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who whet their tongue like a sword, and bend their bows to shoot their arrows, even bitter words: |
| 中文 | 他们磨舌如刀，发出苦毒的言语，好像比准了的箭。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | That they may shoot in secret at the perfect: suddenly do they shoot at him, and fear not. |
| 中文 | 要在暗地射完全人。他们忽然射他，并不惧怕。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They encourage themselves in an evil matter: they commune of laying snares privily; they say, Who shall see them? |
| 中文 | 他们彼此勉励设下恶计。他们商量暗设网罗，说，谁能看见。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They search out iniquities; they accomplish a diligent search: both the inward thought of every one of them, and the heart, is deep. |
| 中文 | 他们图谋奸恶，说，我们是极力图谋的。他们各人的意念心思是深的。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But God shall shoot at them with an arrow; suddenly shall they be wounded. |
| 中文 | 但神要射他们。他们忽然被箭射伤。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | So they shall make their own tongue to fall upon themselves: all that see them shall flee away. |
| 中文 | 他们必然绊跌，被自己的舌头所害。凡看见他们的，必都摇头。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And all men shall fear, and shall declare the work of God; for they shall wisely consider of his doing. |
| 中文 | 众人都要害怕，要传杨神的工作，并且明白他的作为。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The righteous shall be glad in the LORD, and shall trust in him; and all the upright in heart shall glory. |
| 中文 | 义人必因耶和华欢喜，并要投靠他。凡心里正直的人，都要夸口。 |
