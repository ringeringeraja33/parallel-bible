# Psalms 72

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Give the king thy judgments, O God, and thy righteousness unto the king's son. |
| 中文 | （所罗门的诗）神阿，求你将判断的权柄赐给王，将公义赐给王的儿子。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He shall judge thy people with righteousness, and thy poor with judgment. |
| 中文 | 他要按公义审判你的民，按公平审判你的困苦人。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The mountains shall bring peace to the people, and the little hills, by righteousness. |
| 中文 | 大山小山，都要因公义使民得享平安。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He shall judge the poor of the people, he shall save the children of the needy, and shall break in pieces the oppressor. |
| 中文 | 他必为民中的困苦人伸冤，拯救穷乏之辈，压碎那欺压人的。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They shall fear thee as long as the sun and moon endure, throughout all generations. |
| 中文 | 太阳还存，月亮还在，人要敬畏你，直到万代。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He shall come down like rain upon the mown grass: as showers that water the earth. |
| 中文 | 他必降临，像雨降在已割的草地上，如甘霖滋润田地。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | In his days shall the righteous flourish; and abundance of peace so long as the moon endureth. |
| 中文 | 在他的日子义人要发旺，大有平安，好像月亮长存。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He shall have dominion also from sea to sea, and from the river unto the ends of the earth. |
| 中文 | 他要执掌权柄，从这海直到那海，从大河直到地极。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They that dwell in the wilderness shall bow before him; and his enemies shall lick the dust. |
| 中文 | 住在旷野的，必在他面前下拜。他的仇敌，必要舔土。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The kings of Tarshish and of the isles shall bring presents: the kings of Sheba and Seba shall offer gifts. |
| 中文 | 他施和海岛的王要进贡。示巴和西巴的王要献礼物。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Yea, all kings shall fall down before him: all nations shall serve him. |
| 中文 | 诸王都要叩拜他，万国都要事奉他。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For he shall deliver the needy when he crieth; the poor also, and him that hath no helper. |
| 中文 | 因为穷乏人呼求的时候，他要搭救，没有人帮助的困苦人，他也要搭救。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He shall spare the poor and needy, and shall save the souls of the needy. |
| 中文 | 他要怜恤贫寒和穷乏的人，拯救穷苦人的性命。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He shall redeem their soul from deceit and violence: and precious shall their blood be in his sight. |
| 中文 | 他要救赎他们脱离欺压和强暴。他们的血在他眼中看为宝贵。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And he shall live, and to him shall be given of the gold of Sheba: prayer also shall be made for him continually; and daily shall he be praised. |
| 中文 | 他们要存活。示巴的金子要奉给他。人要常常为他祷告，终日称颂他。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | There shall be an handful of corn in the earth upon the top of the mountains; the fruit thereof shall shake like Lebanon: and they of the city shall flourish like grass of the earth. |
| 中文 | 在地的山顶上，五谷必然茂盛。（五谷必然茂盛或作有一把五谷）所结的谷实，要响动如黎巴嫩的树林。城里的人，要发旺如地上的草。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | His name shall endure for ever: his name shall be continued as long as the sun: and men shall be blessed in him: all nations shall call him blessed. |
| 中文 | 他的名要存到永远，要留传如日之久。人要因他蒙福。万国要称他有福。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Blessed be the LORD God, the God of Israel, who only doeth wondrous things. |
| 中文 | 独行奇事的耶和华以色列的神，是应当称颂的。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And blessed be his glorious name for ever: and let the whole earth be filled with his glory; Amen, and Amen. |
| 中文 | 他荣耀的名，也当称颂，直到永远。愿他的荣耀，充满全地。阿们，阿们。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The prayers of David the son of Jesse are ended. |
| 中文 | 耶西的儿子大卫的祈祷完毕。 |
