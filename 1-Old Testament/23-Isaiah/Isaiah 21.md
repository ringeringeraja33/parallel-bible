# Isaiah 21

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The burden of the desert of the sea. As whirlwinds in the south pass through; so it cometh from the desert, from a terrible land. |
| 中文 | 论海旁旷野的默示。有仇敌从旷野，从可怕之地而来，好像南方的旋风，猛然扫过。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A grievous vision is declared unto me; the treacherous dealer dealeth treacherously, and the spoiler spoileth. Go up, O Elam: besiege, O Media; all the sighing thereof have I made to cease. |
| 中文 | 令人凄惨的异象，已默示于我。诡诈的行诡诈，毁灭的行毁灭。以拦哪，你要上去。玛代阿，你要围困。主说，我使一切叹息止住。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Therefore are my loins filled with pain: pangs have taken hold upon me, as the pangs of a woman that travaileth: I was bowed down at the hearing of it; I was dismayed at the seeing of it. |
| 中文 | 所以我满腰疼痛。痛苦将我抓住，好像产难的妇人一样。我疼痛甚至不能听。我惊惶甚至不能看。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My heart panted, fearfulness affrighted me: the night of my pleasure hath he turned into fear unto me. |
| 中文 | 我心慌张，惊恐威吓我，我所羡慕的黄昏，变为我的战兢。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Prepare the table, watch in the watchtower, eat, drink: arise, ye princes, and anoint the shield. |
| 中文 | 他们摆设筵席，派人守望，又吃又喝。首领阿，你们起来，用油抹盾牌。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thus hath the LORD said unto me, Go, set a watchman, let him declare what he seeth. |
| 中文 | 主对我如此说，你去设立守望的，使他将所看见的述说。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And he saw a chariot with a couple of horsemen, a chariot of asses, and a chariot of camels; and he hearkened diligently with much heed: |
| 中文 | 他看见军队，就是骑马的一对一对的来，又看见驴队，骆驼队，就要侧耳细听。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And he cried, A lion: My lord, I stand continually upon the watchtower in the daytime, and I am set in my ward whole nights: |
| 中文 | 他像狮子吼叫，说，主阿，我白日常站在望楼上，整夜立在我守望所。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And, behold, here cometh a chariot of men, with a couple of horsemen. And he answered and said, Babylon is fallen, is fallen; and all the graven images of her gods he hath broken unto the ground. |
| 中文 | 看哪，有一队军兵骑着马，一对一对的来。他就说，巴比伦倾倒了。倾倒了，他一切雕刻的神像，都打碎于地。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O my threshing, and the corn of my floor: that which I have heard of the LORD of hosts, the God of Israel, have I declared unto you. |
| 中文 | 我被打的禾稼，我场上的谷阿，我从万军之耶和华以色列的神那里所听见的，都告诉你们了。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The burden of Dumah. He calleth to me out of Seir, Watchman, what of the night? Watchman, what of the night? |
| 中文 | 论度玛的默示。有人声从西珥呼问我，说，守望的阿，夜里如何。守望的阿，夜里如何。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The watchman said, The morning cometh, and also the night: if ye will enquire, enquire ye: return, come. |
| 中文 | 守望的说，早晨将到，黑夜也来。你们若要问，就可以问，可以回头再来。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The burden upon Arabia. In the forest in Arabia shall ye lodge, O ye travelling companies of Dedanim. |
| 中文 | 论阿拉伯的默示。底但结伴的客旅阿，你们必在阿拉伯的树林中住宿。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The inhabitants of the land of Tema brought water to him that was thirsty, they prevented with their bread him that fled. |
| 中文 | 提玛地的居民拿水来，送给口渴的，拿饼来迎接逃避的。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For they fled from the swords, from the drawn sword, and from the bent bow, and from the grievousness of war. |
| 中文 | 因为他们逃避刀剑，和出了鞘的刀，并上了弦的弓，与刀兵的重灾。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thus hath the LORD said unto me, Within a year, according to the years of an hireling, and all the glory of Kedar shall fail: |
| 中文 | 主对我这样说，一年之内，照雇工的年数，基达的一切荣耀必归于无有。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the residue of the number of archers, the mighty men of the children of Kedar, shall be diminished: for the LORD God of Israel hath spoken it. |
| 中文 | 弓箭手所馀剩的，就是基达人的勇士，必然稀少。因为这是耶和华以色列的神说的。 |
