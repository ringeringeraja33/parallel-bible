# 1 Peter 5

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The elders which are among you I exhort, who am also an elder, and a witness of the sufferings of Christ, and also a partaker of the glory that shall be revealed: |
| 中文 | 我这作长老，作基督受苦的见证，同享后来所要显现之荣耀的，劝你们中间与我同作长老的人。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Feed the flock of God which is among you, taking the oversight thereof, not by constraint, but willingly; not for filthy lucre, but of a ready mind; |
| 中文 | 务要牧养在你们中间神的群羊，按着神旨意照管他们。不是出于勉强，乃是出于甘心。也不是因为贪财，乃是出于乐意。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Neither as being lords over God's heritage, but being ensamples to the flock. |
| 中文 | 也不是辖制所托付你们的，乃是作群羊的榜样。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And when the chief Shepherd shall appear, ye shall receive a crown of glory that fadeth not away. |
| 中文 | 到了牧长显现的时候，你们必得那永不衰残的荣耀冠冕。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Likewise, ye younger, submit yourselves unto the elder. Yea, all of you be subject one to another, and be clothed with humility: for God resisteth the proud, and giveth grace to the humble. |
| 中文 | 你们年幼的，也要顺服年长的。就是你们众人，也都要以谦卑束腰，彼此顺服。因为神阻挡骄傲的人，赐恩给谦卑的人。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Humble yourselves therefore under the mighty hand of God, that he may exalt you in due time: |
| 中文 | 所以你们要自卑，服在神大能的手下，到了时候他必叫你们升高。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Casting all your care upon him; for he careth for you. |
| 中文 | 你们要将一切的忧虑卸给神，因为他顾念你们。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Be sober, be vigilant; because your adversary the devil, as a roaring lion, walketh about, seeking whom he may devour: |
| 中文 | 务要谨守，儆醒。因为你们的仇敌魔鬼，如同吼叫的狮子，遍地游行，寻梢可吞吃的人。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Whom resist stedfast in the faith, knowing that the same afflictions are accomplished in your brethren that are in the world. |
| 中文 | 你们要用坚固的信心抵挡他，因为知道你们在世上的众弟兄，也是经历这样的苦难。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But the God of all grace, who hath called us unto his eternal glory by Christ Jesus, after that ye have suffered a while, make you perfect, stablish, strengthen, settle you. |
| 中文 | 那赐诸般恩典的神，曾在基督里召你们，得亨他永远的荣耀，等你们暂受苦难之后，必要亲自成全你们，坚固你们，赐力量给你们。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | To him be glory and dominion for ever and ever. Amen. |
| 中文 | 愿权能归给他，直到永永远远。阿们。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | By Silvanus, a faithful brother unto you, as I suppose, I have written briefly, exhorting, and testifying that this is the true grace of God wherein ye stand. |
| 中文 | 我略略的写了这信，托我所看为忠心的兄弟西拉转交你们，劝勉你们，又证明这恩是神的真恩。你们务要在这恩上站立得住。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The church that is at Babylon, elected together with you, saluteth you; and so doth Marcus my son. |
| 中文 | 在巴比伦与你们同蒙拣选的教会问你们安。我儿子马可也问你们安。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Greet ye one another with a kiss of charity. Peace be with you all that are in Christ Jesus. Amen. |
| 中文 | 你们要用爱心彼此亲嘴问安。愿平安归与你们凡在基督里的人。 |
