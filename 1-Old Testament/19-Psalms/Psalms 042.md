# Psalms 42

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | As the hart panteth after the water brooks, so panteth my soul after thee, O God. |
| 中文 | （可拉后裔的训悔诗，交给伶长）神阿，我的心切慕你，如鹿切慕溪水。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My soul thirsteth for God, for the living God: when shall I come and appear before God? |
| 中文 | 我的心渴想神，就是永生神。我几时得朝见神呢。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My tears have been my meat day and night, while they continually say unto me, Where is thy God? |
| 中文 | 我昼夜以眼泪当饮食。人不住的对我说，你的神在那里呢。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When I remember these things, I pour out my soul in me: for I had gone with the multitude, I went with them to the house of God, with the voice of joy and praise, with a multitude that kept holyday. |
| 中文 | 我从前与众人同往，用欢呼称赞的声音，领他们到神的殿里，大家守节。我追想这些事，我的心极其悲伤。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Why art thou cast down, O my soul? and why art thou disquieted in me? hope thou in God: for I shall yet praise him for the help of his countenance. |
| 中文 | 我的心哪，你为何忧闷，为何在我里面烦躁。因当仰望神。因他笑脸帮助我，我还要称赞他。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O my God, my soul is cast down within me: therefore will I remember thee from the land of Jordan, and of the Hermonites, from the hill Mizar. |
| 中文 | 我的神阿，我的心在我里面忧闷。所以我从约旦地，从黑门岭，从米萨山，记念你。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Deep calleth unto deep at the noise of thy waterspouts: all thy waves and thy billows are gone over me. |
| 中文 | 你的瀑布发声，深渊就与深渊响应。你的波浪洪涛漫过我身。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Yet the LORD will command his lovingkindness in the day time, and in the night his song shall be with me, and my prayer unto the God of my life. |
| 中文 | 白昼耶和华必向我施慈爱。黑夜我要歌颂祷告赐我生命的神。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will say unto God my rock, Why hast thou forgotten me? why go I mourning because of the oppression of the enemy? |
| 中文 | 我要对神我的磐石说，你为何忘记我呢。我为何因仇敌的欺压时常哀痛呢。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | As with a sword in my bones, mine enemies reproach me; while they say daily unto me, Where is thy God? |
| 中文 | 我的仇敌辱骂我，好像打碎我的骨头，不住的对我说，你的神在那里呢。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Why art thou cast down, O my soul? and why art thou disquieted within me? hope thou in God: for I shall yet praise him, who is the health of my countenance, and my God. |
| 中文 | 我的心哪，你为何忧闷，为何在我里面烦躁。应当仰望神。因我还要称赞他。他是我脸上的光荣，（原文作帮助），是我的神。 |
