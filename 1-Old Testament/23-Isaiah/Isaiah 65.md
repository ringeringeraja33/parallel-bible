# Isaiah 65 / 以赛亚书 65

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I am sought of them that asked not for me; I am found of them that sought me not: I said, Behold me, behold me, unto a nation that was not called by my name. |
| 中文 | 素来没有访问我的，现在求问我。没有寻梢我的，我叫他们遇见。没有称为我名下的，我对他们说，我在这里，我在这里。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I have spread out my hands all the day unto a rebellious people, which walketh in a way that was not good, after their own thoughts; |
| 中文 | 我整天伸手招呼那悖逆的百姓，他们随自己的意念行不善之道。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A people that provoketh me to anger continually to my face; that sacrificeth in gardens, and burneth incense upon altars of brick; |
| 中文 | 这百姓时常当面惹我发怒，在园中献祭，在坛（原文作砖）上烧香。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Which remain among the graves, and lodge in the monuments, which eat swine's flesh, and broth of abominable things is in their vessels; |
| 中文 | 在坟墓间坐着，在隐密处住宿，吃猪肉，他们器皿中有可憎之物作的汤。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Which say, Stand by thyself, come not near to me; for I am holier than thou. These are a smoke in my nose, a fire that burneth all the day. |
| 中文 | 且对人说，你站开吧，不要挨近我，因为我比你圣洁。主说，这些人是我鼻中的烟，是整天烧着的火。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, it is written before me: I will not keep silence, but will recompense, even recompense into their bosom, |
| 中文 | 看哪，这都写在我面前，我必不静默，必施行报应，必将你们的罪孽，和你们列祖的罪孽，就是在山上烧香，在冈上亵渎我的罪孽， |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Your iniquities, and the iniquities of your fathers together, saith the LORD, which have burned incense upon the mountains, and blasphemed me upon the hills: therefore will I measure their former work into their bosom. |
| 中文 | 一同报应在他们后人怀中。我先要把他们所行的量给他们。这是耶和华说的。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thus saith the LORD, As the new wine is found in the cluster, and one saith, Destroy it not; for a blessing is in it: so will I do for my servants' sakes, that I may not destroy them all. |
| 中文 | 耶和华如此说，葡萄中寻得新酒，人就说，不要毁坏，因为福在其中。我因我仆人的缘故也必照样而行，不将他们全然毁灭。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And I will bring forth a seed out of Jacob, and out of Judah an inheritor of my mountains: and mine elect shall inherit it, and my servants shall dwell there. |
| 中文 | 我必从雅各中领出后裔，从犹大中领出承受我众山的。我的选民必承受，我的仆人要在那里居住。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And Sharon shall be a fold of flocks, and the valley of Achor a place for the herds to lie down in, for my people that have sought me. |
| 中文 | 沙仑平原必成为羊群的圈，亚割古必成为牛群躺卧之处。都为寻求我的民所得。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But ye are they that forsake the LORD, that forget my holy mountain, that prepare a table for that troop, and that furnish the drink offering unto that number. |
| 中文 | 但你们这些离弃耶和华，忘记我的圣山，给时运摆筵席，（原文作桌子）给天命盛满调和酒的。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Therefore will I number you to the sword, and ye shall all bow down to the slaughter: because when I called, ye did not answer; when I spake, ye did not hear; but did evil before mine eyes, and did choose that wherein I delighted not. |
| 中文 | 我要命定你们归在刀下，都必屈身被杀，因为我呼唤，你们没有答应。我说话，你们没有听从。反倒行我眼中看为恶的，拣选我所不喜悦的。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Therefore thus saith the Lord GOD, Behold, my servants shall eat, but ye shall be hungry: behold, my servants shall drink, but ye shall be thirsty: behold, my servants shall rejoice, but ye shall be ashamed: |
| 中文 | 所以主耶和华如此说，我的仆人必得吃，你们却饥饿。我的仆人必得喝，你们却乾渴。我的仆人必欢喜，你们却蒙羞。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, my servants shall sing for joy of heart, but ye shall cry for sorrow of heart, and shall howl for vexation of spirit. |
| 中文 | 我的仆人因心中高兴欢呼，你们却因心中忧愁哀哭，又因心里忧伤哀号。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And ye shall leave your name for a curse unto my chosen: for the Lord GOD shall slay thee, and call his servants by another name: |
| 中文 | 你们必留下自己的名，为我选民指着赌咒。主耶和华必杀你们，另起别名称呼他的仆人。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | That he who blesseth himself in the earth shall bless himself in the God of truth; and he that sweareth in the earth shall swear by the God of truth; because the former troubles are forgotten, and because they are hid from mine eyes. |
| 中文 | 这样，在地上为自己求福的，必凭真实的神求福。在地上起誓的，必指真实的神起誓。因为从前的患难已经忘记，也从我眼前隐藏了。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For, behold, I create new heavens and a new earth: and the former shall not be remembered, nor come into mind. |
| 中文 | 看哪，我造新天新地，从前的事不再被记念，也不再追想。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But be ye glad and rejoice for ever in that which I create: for, behold, I create Jerusalem a rejoicing, and her people a joy. |
| 中文 | 你们当因我所造的永远欢喜快乐。因我造耶路撒冷为人所喜，造其中的居民为人所乐。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And I will rejoice in Jerusalem, and joy in my people: and the voice of weeping shall be no more heard in her, nor the voice of crying. |
| 中文 | 我必因耶路撒冷欢喜，因我的百姓快乐。其中必不再听见哭泣的声音，和哀号的声音。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | There shall be no more thence an infant of days, nor an old man that hath not filled his days: for the child shall die an hundred years old; but the sinner being an hundred years old shall be accursed. |
| 中文 | 其中必没有数日夭亡的婴孩，也没有寿数不满的老者。因为百岁死的仍算孩童，有百岁死的罪人算被咒诅。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And they shall build houses, and inhabit them; and they shall plant vineyards, and eat the fruit of them. |
| 中文 | 他们要建造房屋，自己居住。栽种葡萄园，吃其中的果子。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They shall not build, and another inhabit; they shall not plant, and another eat: for as the days of a tree are the days of my people, and mine elect shall long enjoy the work of their hands. |
| 中文 | 他们建造的，别人不得住。他们栽种的，别人不得吃。因为我民的日子必像树木的日子。我选民亲手劳碌得来的必长久享用。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They shall not labour in vain, nor bring forth for trouble; for they are the seed of the blessed of the LORD, and their offspring with them. |
| 中文 | 他们必不徒然劳碌，所生产的，也不遭灾害。因为都是蒙耶和华赐福的后裔，他们的子孙也是如此。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And it shall come to pass, that before they call, I will answer; and while they are yet speaking, I will hear. |
| 中文 | 他们尚未求告，我就应允。正说话的时候，我就垂听。 |

## 25

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The wolf and the lamb shall feed together, and the lion shall eat straw like the bullock: and dust shall be the serpent's meat. They shall not hurt nor destroy in all my holy mountain, saith the LORD. |
| 中文 | 豺狼必与羊羔同食，狮子必吃草与牛一样。尘土必作蛇的食物。在我圣山的遍处，这一切都不伤人不害物，这是耶和华说的。 |
