# Isaiah 33 / 以赛亚书 33

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Woe to thee that spoilest, and thou wast not spoiled; and dealest treacherously, and they dealt not treacherously with thee! when thou shalt cease to spoil, thou shalt be spoiled; and when thou shalt make an end to deal treacherously, they shall deal treacherously with thee. |
| 中文 | 祸哉，你这毁灭人的，自己倒不被毁灭。行事诡诈的，人倒不以诡诈待你。你毁灭吧休了，自己必被毁灭。你行完了诡诈，人必以诡诈待你。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O LORD, be gracious unto us; we have waited for thee: be thou their arm every morning, our salvation also in the time of trouble. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你施恩于我们。我们等候你。求你每早晨作我们的膀臂。遭难的时候，为我们的拯救。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | At the noise of the tumult the people fled; at the lifting up of thyself the nations were scattered. |
| 中文 | 喧囔的响声一发，众民奔逃。你一兴起，列国四散。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And your spoil shall be gathered like the gathering of the caterpiller: as the running to and fro of locusts shall he run upon them. |
| 中文 | 你们所掳的，必被敛尽，好像蚂蚱吃（原文作敛）尽禾稼。人要蹦在其上，好像蝗虫一样。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD is exalted; for he dwelleth on high: he hath filled Zion with judgment and righteousness. |
| 中文 | 耶和华被尊崇，因他居在高处。他以公平公义充满锡安。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And wisdom and knowledge shall be the stability of thy times, and strength of salvation: the fear of the LORD is his treasure. |
| 中文 | 你一生一世必得安稳，有丰盛的救恩，并智慧，和知识。你以敬畏耶和华为至宝。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, their valiant ones shall cry without: the ambassadors of peace shall weep bitterly. |
| 中文 | 看哪，他们的豪杰在外头哀号。求和的使臣，痛痛哭泣。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The highways lie waste, the wayfaring man ceaseth: he hath broken the covenant, he hath despised the cities, he regardeth no man. |
| 中文 | 大路荒凉，行人止息。敌人背约，藐视城邑，不顾人民。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The earth mourneth and languisheth: Lebanon is ashamed and hewn down: Sharon is like a wilderness; and Bashan and Carmel shake off their fruits. |
| 中文 | 地上悲哀衰残。黎巴嫩羞愧枯乾。沙仑像旷野。巴珊和迦密的树林凋残。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Now will I rise, saith the LORD; now will I be exalted; now will I lift up myself. |
| 中文 | 耶和华说，现在我要起来。我要兴起。我要勃然而兴。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ye shall conceive chaff, ye shall bring forth stubble: your breath, as fire, shall devour you. |
| 中文 | 你们要怀的是糠？？。要生的是碎秸。你们的气就是吞灭自己的火。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the people shall be as the burnings of lime: as thorns cut up shall they be burned in the fire. |
| 中文 | 列邦必像已烧的石灰，已割的荆棘，在火中焚烧。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hear, ye that are far off, what I have done; and, ye that are near, acknowledge my might. |
| 中文 | 你们远方的人，当听我所行的，你们近处的人，当承认我的大能。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The sinners in Zion are afraid; fearfulness hath surprised the hypocrites. Who among us shall dwell with the devouring fire? who among us shall dwell with everlasting burnings? |
| 中文 | 锡安中的罪人都惧怕。不敬虔的人被战兢抓住。我们中间谁能与吞灭的火同住。我们中间谁能与永火同住呢。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that walketh righteously, and speaketh uprightly; he that despiseth the gain of oppressions, that shaketh his hands from holding of bribes, that stoppeth his ears from hearing of blood, and shutteth his eyes from seeing evil; |
| 中文 | 行事公义，说话正直，憎恶欺压的财利，摆手不受贿胳，塞耳不听流血的话，闭眼不看邪恶事的， |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He shall dwell on high: his place of defence shall be the munitions of rocks: bread shall be given him; his waters shall be sure. |
| 中文 | 他必居高处。他的保障是磐石的坚垒。他的粮必不缺乏，（原文作赐给）他的水必不断绝。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thine eyes shall see the king in his beauty: they shall behold the land that is very far off. |
| 中文 | 你的眼必见王的荣美，必见辽阔之地。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thine heart shall meditate terror. Where is the scribe? where is the receiver? where is he that counted the towers? |
| 中文 | 你的心必思想那惊吓的事，自问说，记数目的在那里呢。平贡银的在那里呢。数戍楼的在那里呢。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt not see a fierce people, a people of a deeper speech than thou canst perceive; of a stammering tongue, that thou canst not understand. |
| 中文 | 你必不见那强暴的民，就是说话深奥，你不能明白，言语呢喃，你不能懂得的。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Look upon Zion, the city of our solemnities: thine eyes shall see Jerusalem a quiet habitation, a tabernacle that shall not be taken down; not one of the stakes thereof shall ever be removed, neither shall any of the cords thereof be broken. |
| 中文 | 你要看锡安我们守圣节的城。你的眼必见耶路撒冷为安静的居所，为不挪移的帐幕，橛子永不拔出，绳索一根也不折断。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But there the glorious LORD will be unto us a place of broad rivers and streams; wherein shall go no galley with oars, neither shall gallant ship pass thereby. |
| 中文 | 在那里耶和华必显威严与我们同在，当作江河宽阔之地。其中必没有荡桨摇橹的船来往，也没有威武的船经过。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 因为耶和华是审判我们的，耶和华是给我们设律法的，耶和华是我们的王。他必拯救我们。 |
