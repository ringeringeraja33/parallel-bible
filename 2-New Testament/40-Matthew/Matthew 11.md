# Matthew 11 / 马太福音 11

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And it came to pass, when Jesus had made an end of commanding his twelve disciples, he departed thence to teach and to preach in their cities. |
| 中文 | 耶稣吩咐完了十二个门徒，就离开那里，往各城去传道教训人。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Now when John had heard in the prison the works of Christ, he sent two of his disciples, |
| 中文 | 约翰在监里听见基督所作的事，就打发两个门徒去， |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And said unto him, Art thou he that should come, or do we look for another? |
| 中文 | 问他说，那将要来的是你吗，还是我们等候别人呢。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Jesus answered and said unto them, Go and shew John again those things which ye do hear and see: |
| 中文 | 耶稣回答说，你们去，把所听见所看见的事告诉约翰。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The blind receive their sight, and the lame walk, the lepers are cleansed, and the deaf hear, the dead are raised up, and the poor have the gospel preached to them. |
| 中文 | 就是瞎子看见，瘸子行走，长大麻疯的洁净，聋子听见。死人复活，穷人有福音传给他们。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And blessed is he, whosoever shall not be offended in me. |
| 中文 | 凡不因我跌倒的，就有福了。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And as they departed, Jesus began to say unto the multitudes concerning John, What went ye out into the wilderness to see? A reed shaken with the wind? |
| 中文 | 他们走的时候，耶稣就对众人讲论约翰说，你们从前出到旷野，是要看什么呢，要看风吹动的芦苇吗。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But what went ye out for to see? A man clothed in soft raiment? behold, they that wear soft clothing are in kings' houses. |
| 中文 | 你们出去，到底是要看什么，要看穿细软衣服的人吗，那穿细软衣服的人，是在王宫里。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But what went ye out for to see? A prophet? yea, I say unto you, and more than a prophet. |
| 中文 | 你们出去，究竟是为什么，是要看先知吗。我告诉你们，是的，他比先知大多了。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For this is he, of whom it is written, Behold, I send my messenger before thy face, which shall prepare thy way before thee. |
| 中文 | 经上记着说，我要差遣我的使者在你面前，豫备道路。所说的就是这个人。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Verily I say unto you, Among them that are born of women there hath not risen a greater than John the Baptist: notwithstanding he that is least in the kingdom of heaven is greater than he. |
| 中文 | 我实在告诉你们，凡妇人所生的，没有一个兴起来大过施洗约翰的。然而天国里最小的，比他还大。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And from the days of John the Baptist until now the kingdom of heaven suffereth violence, and the violent take it by force. |
| 中文 | 从施洗约翰的时候到如今，天国是努力进入的，努力的人就得着了。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For all the prophets and the law prophesied until John. |
| 中文 | 因为众先知和律法说预言，到约翰为止。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And if ye will receive it, this is Elias, which was for to come. |
| 中文 | 你们若肯领受，这人就是应当来的以利亚。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that hath ears to hear, let him hear. |
| 中文 | 有耳可听的，就应当听。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But whereunto shall I liken this generation? It is like unto children sitting in the markets, and calling unto their fellows, |
| 中文 | 我可用什么比这世代呢。好像孩童坐在街市上，招呼同伴，说， |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And saying, We have piped unto you, and ye have not danced; we have mourned unto you, and ye have not lamented. |
| 中文 | 我们向你们吹笛，你们不跳舞。我们向你们举哀，你们不捶胸。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For John came neither eating nor drinking, and they say, He hath a devil. |
| 中文 | 约翰来了，也不吃，也不喝，人就说他是被鬼附着的。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The Son of man came eating and drinking, and they say, Behold a man gluttonous, and a winebibber, a friend of publicans and sinners. But wisdom is justified of her children. |
| 中文 | 人子来了，也吃，也喝，人又说他是贪食好酒的人，是税吏罪人的朋友。但智慧之子，总以智慧为是。（有古卷作但智慧在行为上就显为是） |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 耶稣在诸城中行了许多异能，那些城的人终不悔改，就在那时候责备他们说， |

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
