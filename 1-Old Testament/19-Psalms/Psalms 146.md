# Psalms 146

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Praise ye the LORD. Praise the LORD, O my soul. |
| 中文 | 你们要赞美耶和华。我的心哪，你要赞美耶和华。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | While I live will I praise the LORD: I will sing praises unto my God while I have any being. |
| 中文 | 我一生要赞美耶和华。我还活的时候要歌颂我的神。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Put not your trust in princes, nor in the son of man, in whom there is no help. |
| 中文 | 你们不要倚靠君王，不要倚靠世人。他一点不能帮助。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | His breath goeth forth, he returneth to his earth; in that very day his thoughts perish. |
| 中文 | 他的气一断，就归回尘土。他所打算的，当日就消灭了。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Happy is he that hath the God of Jacob for his help, whose hope is in the LORD his God: |
| 中文 | 以雅各的神为帮助，仰望耶和华他神的，这人便为有福。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Which made heaven, and earth, the sea, and all that therein is: which keepeth truth for ever: |
| 中文 | 耶和华造天，地，海，和其中的万物。他守诚实，直到永远。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Which executeth judgment for the oppressed: which giveth food to the hungry. The LORD looseth the prisoners: |
| 中文 | 他为受屈的伸冤，赐食物与饥饿的。耶和华释放被囚的。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD openeth the eyes of the blind: the LORD raiseth them that are bowed down: the LORD loveth the righteous: |
| 中文 | 耶和华开了瞎子的眼睛。耶和华扶起被压下的人。耶和华喜爱义人。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD preserveth the strangers; he relieveth the fatherless and widow: but the way of the wicked he turneth upside down. |
| 中文 | 耶和华保护寄居的，扶持孤儿和寡妇，却使恶人的道路弯曲。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD shall reign for ever, even thy God, O Zion, unto all generations. Praise ye the LORD. |
| 中文 | 耶和华要作王，直到永远。锡安哪，你的神要作王，直到万代。你们要赞美耶和华。 |
