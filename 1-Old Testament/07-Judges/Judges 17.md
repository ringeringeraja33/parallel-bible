# Judges 17 / 士师记 17

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And there was a man of mount Ephraim, whose name was Micah. |
| 中文 | 以法莲山地有一个人名叫米迦。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And he said unto his mother, The eleven hundred shekels of silver that were taken from thee, about which thou cursedst, and spakest of also in mine ears, behold, the silver is with me; I took it. And his mother said, Blessed be thou of the LORD, my son. |
| 中文 | 他对母亲说，你那一千一百舍客勒银子被人拿去，你因此咒诅，并且告诉了我。看哪。这银子在我这里，是我拿去了。他母亲说，我儿阿，愿耶和华赐福与你。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And when he had restored the eleven hundred shekels of silver to his mother, his mother said, I had wholly dedicated the silver unto the LORD from my hand for my son, to make a graven image and a molten image: now therefore I will restore it unto thee. |
| 中文 | 米迦就把这一千一百舍客勒银子还他母亲。他母亲说，我分出这银子来为你献给耶和华，好雕刻一个像，铸成一个像。现在我还是交给你。 |
