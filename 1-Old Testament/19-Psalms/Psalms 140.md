# Psalms 140 / 诗篇 140

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Deliver me, O LORD, from the evil man: preserve me from the violent man; |
| 中文 | （大卫的诗，交与伶长）耶和华阿，求你拯救我脱离凶恶的人，保护我脱离强暴的人。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Which imagine mischiefs in their heart; continually are they gathered together for war. |
| 中文 | 他们心中图谋奸恶，常常聚集要争战。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They have sharpened their tongues like a serpent; adders' poison is under their lips. Selah. |
| 中文 | 他们使舌头尖利如蛇，嘴里有虺蛇的毒气。（细拉） |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Keep me, O LORD, from the hands of the wicked; preserve me from the violent man; who have purposed to overthrow my goings. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你拯救我脱离恶人的手，保护我脱离强暴的人。他们图谋推我跌倒。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The proud have hid a snare for me, and cords; they have spread a net by the wayside; they have set gins for me. Selah. |
| 中文 | 骄傲人为我暗设网罗和绳索。他们在路旁铺下网，设下圈套。（细拉） |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I said unto the LORD, Thou art my God: hear the voice of my supplications, O LORD. |
| 中文 | 我曾对耶和华说，你是我的神。耶和华阿，求你留心听我恳求的声音。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O GOD the Lord, the strength of my salvation, thou hast covered my head in the day of battle. |
| 中文 | 主耶和华，我救恩的力量阿，在争战的日子，你遮蔽了我的头。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Grant not, O LORD, the desires of the wicked: further not his wicked device; lest they exalt themselves. Selah. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你不要遂恶人的心愿。不要成就他们的计谋，恐怕他们自高。（细拉） |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | As for the head of those that compass me about, let the mischief of their own lips cover them. |
| 中文 | 至于那些昂首围困我的人，愿他们嘴唇的奸恶陷害（原文是遮蔽）自己。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let burning coals fall upon them: let them be cast into the fire; into deep pits, that they rise not up again. |
| 中文 | 愿火炭落在他们身上。愿他们被丢在火中，抛在深坑里，不能再起来。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let not an evil speaker be established in the earth: evil shall hunt the violent man to overthrow him. |
| 中文 | 说恶言的人，在地上必坚立不住。祸患必猎取强暴的人，将他打倒。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I know that the LORD will maintain the cause of the afflicted, and the right of the poor. |
| 中文 | 我知道耶和华必为困苦人伸冤，必为穷乏人辨屈。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Surely the righteous shall give thanks unto thy name: the upright shall dwell in thy presence. |
| 中文 | 义人必要称赞你的名。正直人必住在你面前。 |
