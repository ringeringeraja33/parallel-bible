# Psalms 92 / 诗篇 92

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | IT IS A GOOD THING TO GIVE THANKS UNTO THE LORD, AND TO SING PRAISES UNTO THY NAME, O MOST HIGH: |
| 中文 | （安息日的诗歌）称谢耶和华，歌颂你至高者的名。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | To shew forth thy lovingkindness in the morning, and thy faithfulness every night, |
| 中文 | 用十弦的乐器和瑟，用琴弹幽雅的声音， |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Upon an instrument of ten strings, and upon the psaltery; upon the harp with a solemn sound. |
| 中文 | 早晨传杨你的慈爱，每夜传杨你的信实，这本为美事。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thou, LORD, hast made me glad through thy work: I will triumph in the works of thy hands. |
| 中文 | 因你耶和华藉着你的作为，叫我高兴。我要因你手的工作欢呼。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O LORD, how great are thy works! and thy thoughts are very deep. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，你的工作何其大，你的心思极其深。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A brutish man knoweth not; neither doth a fool understand this. |
| 中文 | 畜类人不晓得，愚顽人也不明白。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When the wicked spring as the grass, and when all the workers of iniquity do flourish; it is that they shall be destroyed for ever: |
| 中文 | 恶人茂盛如草，一切作孽之人发旺的时候，正是他们要灭亡，直到永远。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But thou, LORD, art most high for evermore. |
| 中文 | 惟你耶和华是至高，直到永远。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For, lo, thine enemies, O LORD, for, lo, thine enemies shall perish; all the workers of iniquity shall be scattered. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，你的仇敌都要灭亡，一切作孽的也要离散。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But my horn shalt thou exalt like the horn of an unicorn: I shall be anointed with fresh oil. |
| 中文 | 你却高举了我的角，如野牛的角。我是被新油膏了的。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Mine eye also shall see my desire on mine enemies, and mine ears shall hear my desire of the wicked that rise up against me. |
| 中文 | 我眼睛看见仇敌遭报，我耳朵听见那些起来攻击我的恶人受罚。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The righteous shall flourish like the palm tree: he shall grow like a cedar in Lebanon. |
| 中文 | 义人要发旺如棕树，生长如黎巴嫩的香柏树。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Those that be planted in the house of the LORD shall flourish in the courts of our God. |
| 中文 | 他们栽于耶和华的殿中，发旺在我们神的院里。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They shall still bring forth fruit in old age; they shall be fat and flourishing; |
| 中文 | 他们年老的时候，仍要结果子。要满了汁浆而常发青。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | To shew that the LORD is upright: he is my rock, and there is no unrighteousness in him. |
| 中文 | 好显明耶和华是正直的，他是我的磐石，在他毫无不义。 |
