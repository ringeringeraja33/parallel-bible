# Psalms 77 / 诗篇 77

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I cried unto God with my voice, even unto God with my voice; and he gave ear unto me. |
| 中文 | （亚萨的诗，照耶杜顿的作法，交与伶长）我要向神发声呼求。我向神发声，他必留心听我。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | In the day of my trouble I sought the Lord: my sore ran in the night, and ceased not: my soul refused to be comforted. |
| 中文 | 我在患难之日寻求主。我在夜间不住的举手祷告。我的心不肯受安慰。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I remembered God, and was troubled: I complained, and my spirit was overwhelmed. Selah. |
| 中文 | 我想念神，就烦躁不安。我沉吟悲伤，心便发昏。（细拉） |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou holdest mine eyes waking: I am so troubled that I cannot speak. |
| 中文 | 你叫我不能闭眼。我烦乱不安，甚至不能说话。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I have considered the days of old, the years of ancient times. |
| 中文 | 我追想古时之日，上古之年。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I call to remembrance my song in the night: I commune with mine own heart: and my spirit made diligent search. |
| 中文 | 我想起我夜间的歌曲，扪心自问。我心里也仔细省察。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Will the Lord cast off for ever? and will he be favourable no more? |
| 中文 | 难道主要永远丢弃我，不再施恩吗。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Is his mercy clean gone for ever? doth his promise fail for evermore? |
| 中文 | 难道他的慈爱永远穷尽，他的应许世世废弃吗。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hath God forgotten to be gracious? hath he in anger shut up his tender mercies? Selah. |
| 中文 | 难道神忘记开恩。因发怒就止住他的慈悲吗。（细拉） |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And I said, This is my infirmity: but I will remember the years of the right hand of the most High. |
| 中文 | 我便说，这是我的懦弱。但我要追念至高者显出右手之年代。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will remember the works of the LORD: surely I will remember thy wonders of old. |
| 中文 | 我要题说耶和华所行的。我要记念你古时的奇事。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will meditate also of all thy work, and talk of thy doings. |
| 中文 | 我也要思想你的经营，默念你的作为。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thy way, O God, is in the sanctuary: who is so great a God as our God? |
| 中文 | 神阿，你的作为是洁净的。有何神大如神呢。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou art the God that doest wonders: thou hast declared thy strength among the people. |
| 中文 | 你是行奇事的神。你曾在列邦中彰显你的能力。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou hast with thine arm redeemed thy people, the sons of Jacob and Joseph. Selah. |
| 中文 | 你曾用你的膀臂赎了你的民，就是雅各和约瑟的子孙。（细拉） |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The waters saw thee, O God, the waters saw thee; they were afraid: the depths also were troubled. |
| 中文 | 神阿，诸水见你，一见就都惊惶。深渊也都战抖。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The clouds poured out water: the skies sent out a sound: thine arrows also went abroad. |
| 中文 | 云中倒出水来。天空发出响声。你的箭也飞行四方。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The voice of thy thunder was in the heaven: the lightnings lightened the world: the earth trembled and shook. |
| 中文 | 你的雷声在旋风中。电光照亮世界。大地战抖震动。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thy way is in the sea, and thy path in the great waters, and thy footsteps are not known. |
| 中文 | 你的道在海中，你的路在大水中，你的脚踪无人知道。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou leddest thy people like a flock by the hand of Moses and Aaron. |
| 中文 | 你曾藉摩西和亚伦的手，引导你的百姓，好像羊群一般。 |
