# Psalms 36 / 诗篇 36

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The transgression of the wicked saith within my heart, that there is no fear of God before his eyes. |
| 中文 | （耶和华的仆人大卫的诗，交与伶长）恶人的罪过，在他心里说，我眼中不怕神。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For he flattereth himself in his own eyes, until his iniquity be found to be hateful. |
| 中文 | 他自夸自媚，以为他的罪孽终不显露，不被恨恶。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The words of his mouth are iniquity and deceit: he hath left off to be wise, and to do good. |
| 中文 | 他口中的言语，尽是罪孽诡诈。他与智慧善行，已经断绝。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He deviseth mischief upon his bed; he setteth himself in a way that is not good; he abhorreth not evil. |
| 中文 | 他在床上图谋罪孽，定意行不善的道，不憎恶恶事。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thy mercy, O LORD, is in the heavens; and thy faithfulness reacheth unto the clouds. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，你的慈爱，上及诸天。你的信实，达到穹苍。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thy righteousness is like the great mountains; thy judgments are a great deep: O LORD, thou preservest man and beast. |
| 中文 | 你的公义，好像高山。你的判断，如同深渊。耶和华阿，人民牲畜，你都救护。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | How excellent is thy lovingkindness, O God! therefore the children of men put their trust under the shadow of thy wings. |
| 中文 | 神阿，你的慈爱，何其宝贵。世上投靠在你翅膀的荫下。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They shall be abundantly satisfied with the fatness of thy house; and thou shalt make them drink of the river of thy pleasures. |
| 中文 | 他们必因你殿里的肥甘，得以饱足。你也必叫他们喝你乐河的水 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For with thee is the fountain of life: in thy light shall we see light. |
| 中文 | 因为在你那里，有生命的源头。在你的光中，我们必得见光 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O continue thy lovingkindness unto them that know thee; and thy righteousness to the upright in heart. |
| 中文 | 愿你常施慈爱给认识你的人。常以公义待心里正直的人。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let not the foot of pride come against me, and let not the hand of the wicked remove me. |
| 中文 | 不容骄傲人的脚踏我，不容凶恶人的手赶逐我。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | There are the workers of iniquity fallen: they are cast down, and shall not be able to rise. |
| 中文 | 在那里作孽的人，已经仆倒。他们被推倒，不能再起来。 |
