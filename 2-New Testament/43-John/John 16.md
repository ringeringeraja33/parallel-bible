# John 16

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | These things have I spoken unto you, that ye should not be offended. |
| 中文 | 我已将这些事告诉你们，使你们不至于跌倒。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They shall put you out of the synagogues: yea, the time cometh, that whosoever killeth you will think that he doeth God service. |
| 中文 | 人要把你们赶出会堂。并且时候将到，凡杀你们的，就以为是事奉神。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And these things will they do unto you, because they have not known the Father, nor me. |
| 中文 | 他们这样行，是因未曾认识父，也未曾认识我。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But these things have I told you, that when the time shall come, ye may remember that I told you of them. And these things I said not unto you at the beginning, because I was with you. |
| 中文 | 我将这些事告诉你们，是叫你们到了时候，可以想起我对你们说过了。我起先没有将这事告诉你们，因为我与你们同在。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But now I go my way to him that sent me; and none of you asketh me, Whither goest thou? |
| 中文 | 现今我往差我来的父那里去。你们中间并没有人问我，你往那里去。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But because I have said these things unto you, sorrow hath filled your heart. |
| 中文 | 只因我将这事情告诉你们，你们就满心忧愁。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Nevertheless I tell you the truth; It is expedient for you that I go away: for if I go not away, the Comforter will not come unto you; but if I depart, I will send him unto you. |
| 中文 | 然而我将真情告诉你们。我去是与你们有益的。我若不去，保惠师就不到你们这里来。我若去，就差他来。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And when he is come, he will reprove the world of sin, and of righteousness, and of judgment: |
| 中文 | 他既来了，就要叫世人为罪，为义，为审判，自己责备自己。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Of sin, because they believe not on me; |
| 中文 | 为罪，是因他们不信我。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Of righteousness, because I go to my Father, and ye see me no more; |
| 中文 | 为义，是因我往父那里去，你们就不再见我。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Of judgment, because the prince of this world is judged. |
| 中文 | 为审判，是因这世界的王受了审判。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I have yet many things to say unto you, but ye cannot bear them now. |
| 中文 | 我还有好些事要告诉你们，但你们现在担当不了（或作不能领会）。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Howbeit when he, the Spirit of truth, is come, he will guide you into all truth: for he shall not speak of himself; but whatsoever he shall hear, that shall he speak: and he will shew you things to come. |
| 中文 | 只等真理的圣灵来了，他要引导你们明白（原文作进入）一切的真理。因为他不是凭自己说的，乃是把他所听见的都说出来。并要把将来的事告诉你们。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He shall glorify me: for he shall receive of mine, and shall shew it unto you. |
| 中文 | 他要荣耀我。因为他要将受于我的，告诉你们。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | All things that the Father hath are mine: therefore said I, that he shall take of mine, and shall shew it unto you. |
| 中文 | 凡父所有的，都是我的，所以我说，他要将受于我的，告诉你们。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A little while, and ye shall not see me: and again, a little while, and ye shall see me, because I go to the Father. |
| 中文 | 等不多时，你们就不得见我。再等不多时，你们还要见我。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then said some of his disciples among themselves, What is this that he saith unto us, A little while, and ye shall not see me: and again, a little while, and ye shall see me: and, Because I go to the Father? |
| 中文 | 有几个门徒就彼此说，他对我们说，等不多时，你们就不得见我。再等不多时，你们还要见我。又说，因我往父那里去。这是什么意思呢。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They said therefore, What is this that he saith, A little while? we cannot tell what he saith. |
| 中文 | 门徒彼此说，他说等不多时，到底是什么意思呢。我们不明白他所说的话。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Now Jesus knew that they were desirous to ask him, and said unto them, Do ye enquire among yourselves of that I said, A little while, and ye shall not see me: and again, a little while, and ye shall see me? |
| 中文 | 耶稣看出他们要问他，就说，我说等不多时，你们就不得见我，再等不多时，你们还要见我。你们为这话彼此相问吗。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Verily, verily, I say unto you, That ye shall weep and lament, but the world shall rejoice: and ye shall be sorrowful, but your sorrow shall be turned into joy. |
| 中文 | 我实实在在的告诉你们，你们将要痛哭，哀号，世人倒要喜乐。你们将要忧愁，然而你们的忧愁，要变为喜乐。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A woman when she is in travail hath sorrow, because her hour is come: but as soon as she is delivered of the child, she remembereth no more the anguish, for joy that a man is born into the world. |
| 中文 | 妇人生产的时候，就忧愁，因为他的时候到了。既生了孩子，就不再记念那苦楚，因为欢喜世上生了一个人。 |

## 22

| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 你们现在也是忧愁。但我要再见你们，你们的心就喜乐了。这喜乐，也没有人能夺去。 |
