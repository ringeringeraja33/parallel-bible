# Psalms 28 / 诗篇 28

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Unto thee will I cry, O LORD my rock; be not silent to me: lest, if thou be silent to me, I become like them that go down into the pit. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，我要求告你。我的磐石阿，不要向我缄默。倘若你向我闭口，我就如将死的人一样。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hear the voice of my supplications, when I cry unto thee, when I lift up my hands toward thy holy oracle. |
| 中文 | 我呼求你，向你至圣所举手的时候，求你垂听我恳求的声音。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Draw me not away with the wicked, and with the workers of iniquity, which speak peace to their neighbours, but mischief is in their hearts. |
| 中文 | 不要把我和恶人，并作孽的，一同除掉。他们与邻舍说和平话，心里却是奸恶。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Give them according to their deeds, and according to the wickedness of their endeavours: give them after the work of their hands; render to them their desert. |
| 中文 | 愿你按着他们所作的，并他们所行的恶事待他们。愿你照着他们手所作的待他们。将他们所应得的报应加给他们。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Because they regard not the works of the LORD, nor the operation of his hands, he shall destroy them, and not build them up. |
| 中文 | 他们既然不留心耶和华所行的，和他手所作的，他就必毁坏他们，不建立他们。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Blessed be the LORD, because he hath heard the voice of my supplications. |
| 中文 | 耶和华是应当称颂的，因为他听了我恳求的声音。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD is my strength and my shield; my heart trusted in him, and I am helped: therefore my heart greatly rejoiceth; and with my song will I praise him. |
| 中文 | 耶和华是我的力量，是我的盾牌。我心里倚靠他，就得帮助。所以我心中欢乐。我必用诗歌颂赞他。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD is their strength, and he is the saving strength of his anointed. |
| 中文 | 耶和华是他百姓的力量，又是他受膏者得救的保障。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Save thy people, and bless thine inheritance: feed them also, and lift them up for ever. |
| 中文 | 求你拯救你的百姓，赐福给你的产业，牧养他们，扶持他们，直到永远。 |
