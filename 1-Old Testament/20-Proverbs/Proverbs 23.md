# Proverbs 23

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When thou sittest to eat with a ruler, consider diligently what is before thee: |
| 中文 | 你若与官长坐席，要留意在你面前的是谁。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And put a knife to thy throat, if thou be a man given to appetite. |
| 中文 | 你若是贪食的，就当拿刀放在喉咙上。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Be not desirous of his dainties: for they are deceitful meat. |
| 中文 | 不可贪恋他的美食，因为是哄人的食物。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Labour not to be rich: cease from thine own wisdom. |
| 中文 | 不要劳碌求富。休仗自己的聪明。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Wilt thou set thine eyes upon that which is not? for riches certainly make themselves wings; they fly away as an eagle toward heaven. |
| 中文 | 你岂要定睛在虚无的钱财上吗。因钱财必长翅膀，如鹰向天飞去。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Eat thou not the bread of him that hath an evil eye, neither desire thou his dainty meats: |
| 中文 | 不要吃恶眼人的饭。也不要贪他的美味。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For as he thinketh in his heart, so is he: Eat and drink, saith he to thee; but his heart is not with thee. |
| 中文 | 因为他心怎样思量，他为人就是怎样。他虽对你说，请吃，请喝。他的心却与你向背。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The morsel which thou hast eaten shalt thou vomit up, and lose thy sweet words. |
| 中文 | 你所吃的那点食物，必吐出来。你所说的甘美言语，也必落空。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Speak not in the ears of a fool: for he will despise the wisdom of thy words. |
| 中文 | 你不要说话给愚昧人听。因他必藐视你智慧的言语。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Remove not the old landmark; and enter not into the fields of the fatherless: |
| 中文 | 不可挪移古时的地界。也不可侵入孤儿的田地。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For their redeemer is mighty; he shall plead their cause with thee. |
| 中文 | 因为他们的救赎主，大有能力。他必向你为他们辨屈。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Apply thine heart unto instruction, and thine ears to the words of knowledge. |
| 中文 | 你要留心领受训诲。侧耳听从知识的言语。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Withhold not correction from the child: for if thou beatest him with the rod, he shall not die. |
| 中文 | 不可不管教孩童，你用杖打他，他必不至于死。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt beat him with the rod, and shalt deliver his soul from hell. |
| 中文 | 你要用杖打他，就可以救他的灵魂免下阴间。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My son, if thine heart be wise, my heart shall rejoice, even mine. |
| 中文 | 我儿你心若存智慧，我的心也甚欢喜。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Yea, my reins shall rejoice, when thy lips speak right things. |
| 中文 | 你的嘴若说正直话，我的心肠也必快乐。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let not thine heart envy sinners: but be thou in the fear of the LORD all the day long. |
| 中文 | 你心中不要嫉妒罪人。只要终日敬畏耶和华。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For surely there is an end; and thine expectation shall not be cut off. |
| 中文 | 因为至终必有善报。你的指望也不至断绝。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hear thou, my son, and be wise, and guide thine heart in the way. |
| 中文 | 我儿，你当听，当存智慧，好在正道上引导你的心。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Be not among winebibbers; among riotous eaters of flesh: |
| 中文 | 好饮酒的，好吃肉的，不要与他们来往。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the drunkard and the glutton shall come to poverty: and drowsiness shall clothe a man with rags. |
| 中文 | 因为好酒贪食的，必至贫穷。好睡觉的，必穿破烂衣服。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hearken unto thy father that begat thee, and despise not thy mother when she is old. |
| 中文 | 你要听从生你的父亲。你母亲老了，也不可藐视他。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Buy the truth, and sell it not; also wisdom, and instruction, and understanding. |
| 中文 | 你当买真理。就是智慧，训诲，和聪明，也都不可卖。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The father of the righteous shall greatly rejoice: and he that begetteth a wise child shall have joy of him. |
| 中文 | 义人的父亲，必大得快乐。人生智慧的儿子，必因他欢喜。 |

## 25

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thy father and thy mother shall be glad, and she that bare thee shall rejoice. |
| 中文 | 你要使父母欢喜。使生你的快乐。 |

## 26

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My son, give me thine heart, and let thine eyes observe my ways. |
| 中文 | 我儿，要将你的心归我。你的眼目，也要喜悦我的道路。 |

## 27

| Language | Content |
