# Leviticus 22 / 利未记 22

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the LORD spake unto Moses, saying, |
| 中文 | 耶和华对摩西说， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Speak unto Aaron and to his sons, that they separate themselves from the holy things of the children of Israel, and that they profane not my holy name in those things which they hallow unto me: I am the LORD. |
| 中文 | 你吩咐亚伦和他子孙说，要远离以色列人所分别为圣，归给我的圣物，免得亵渎我的圣名。我是耶和华。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Say unto them, Whosoever he be of all your seed among your generations, that goeth unto the holy things, which the children of Israel hallow unto the LORD, having his uncleanness upon him, that soul shall be cut off from my presence: I am the LORD. |
| 中文 | 你要对他们说，你们世世代代的后裔，凡身上有污秽，亲近以色列人所分别为圣，归耶和华圣物的，那人必在我面前剪除。我是耶和华。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | What man soever of the seed of Aaron is a leper, or hath a running issue; he shall not eat of the holy things, until he be clean. And whoso toucheth any thing that is unclean by the dead, or a man whose seed goeth from him; |
| 中文 | 亚伦的后裔，凡长大麻疯的，或是有漏症的，不可吃圣物，直等他洁净了。无论谁摸那因死尸不洁净的物（物或作人），或是遗精的人， |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Or whosoever toucheth any creeping thing, whereby he may be made unclean, or a man of whom he may take uncleanness, whatsoever uncleanness he hath; |
| 中文 | 或是摸什么使他不洁净的爬物，或是摸那使他不洁净的人（不拘那人有什么不洁净）， |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The soul which hath touched any such shall be unclean until even, and shall not eat of the holy things, unless he wash his flesh with water. |
| 中文 | 摸了这些人，物的，必不洁净到晚上，若不用水洗身，就不可吃圣物。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And when the sun is down, he shall be clean, and shall afterward eat of the holy things; because it is his food. |
| 中文 | 日落的时候，他就洁净了，然后可以吃圣物，因为这是他的食物。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | That which dieth of itself, or is torn with beasts, he shall not eat to defile himself therewith: I am the LORD. |
| 中文 | 自死的或是被野兽撕裂的，他不可吃，因此污秽自己。我是耶和华。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They shall therefore keep mine ordinance, lest they bear sin for it, and die therefore, if they profane it: I the LORD do sanctify them. |
| 中文 | 所以他们要守我所吩咐的，免得轻忽了，因此担罪而死。我是叫他们成圣的耶和华。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | There shall no stranger eat of the holy thing: a sojourner of the priest, or an hired servant, shall not eat of the holy thing. |
| 中文 | 凡外人不可吃圣物，寄居在祭司家的，或是雇工人，都不可吃圣物。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But if the priest buy any soul with his money, he shall eat of it, and he that is born in his house: they shall eat of his meat. |
| 中文 | 倘若祭司买人，是他的钱买的，那人就可以吃圣物，生在他家的人也可以吃。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If the priest's daughter also be married unto a stranger, she may not eat of an offering of the holy things. |
| 中文 | 祭司的女儿若嫁外人，就不可吃举祭的圣物。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But if the priest's daughter be a widow, or divorced, and have no child, and is returned unto her father's house, as in her youth, she shall eat of her father's meat: but there shall no stranger eat thereof. |
| 中文 | 但祭司的女儿若是寡妇，或是被休的，没有孩子，又归回父家，与她青年一样，就可以吃她父亲的食物，只是外人不可吃。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And if a man eat of the holy thing unwittingly, then he shall put the fifth part thereof unto it, and shall give it unto the priest with the holy thing. |
| 中文 | 若有人误吃了圣物，要照圣物的原数加上五分之一交给祭司。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And they shall not profane the holy things of the children of Israel, which they offer unto the LORD; |
| 中文 | 祭司不可亵渎以色列人所献给耶和华的圣物， |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Or suffer them to bear the iniquity of trespass, when they eat their holy things: for I the LORD do sanctify them. |
| 中文 | 免得他们在吃圣物上自取罪孽，因为我是叫他们成圣的耶和华。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the LORD spake unto Moses, saying, |
| 中文 | 耶和华对摩西说， |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Speak unto Aaron, and to his sons, and unto all the children of Israel, and say unto them, Whatsoever he be of the house of Israel, or of the strangers in Israel, that will offer his oblation for all his vows, and for all his freewill offerings, which they will offer unto the LORD for a burnt offering; |
| 中文 | 你晓谕亚伦和他子孙，并以色列众人说，以色列家中的人，或在以色列中寄居的，凡献供物，无论是所许的愿，是甘心献的，就是献给耶和华作燔祭的， |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ye shall offer at your own will a male without blemish, of the beeves, of the sheep, or of the goats. |
| 中文 | 要将没有残疾的公牛，或是绵羊，或是山羊献上，如此方蒙悦纳。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But whatsoever hath a blemish, that shall ye not offer: for it shall not be acceptable for you. |
| 中文 | 凡有残疾的，你们不可献上，因为这不蒙悦纳。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And whosoever offereth a sacrifice of peace offerings unto the LORD to accomplish his vow, or a freewill offering in beeves or sheep, it shall be perfect to be accepted; there shall be no blemish therein. |
| 中文 | 凡从牛群或是羊群中，将平安祭献给耶和华，为要还特许的愿，或是作甘心献的，所献的必纯全无残疾的才蒙悦纳。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Blind, or broken, or maimed, or having a wen, or scurvy, or scabbed, ye shall not offer these unto the LORD, nor make an offering by fire of them upon the altar unto the LORD. |
| 中文 | 瞎眼的，折伤的，残废的，有瘤子的，长癣的，长疥的都不可献给耶和华，也不可在坛上作为火祭献给耶和华。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Either a bullock or a lamb that hath any thing superfluous or lacking in his parts, that mayest thou offer for a freewill offering; but for a vow it shall not be accepted. |
| 中文 | 无论是公牛是绵羊羔，若肢体有馀的，或是缺少的，只可作甘心祭献上，用以还愿，却不蒙悦纳。 |

## 24

| 中文 | 肾子损伤的，或是压碎的，或是破裂的，或是骟了的，不可献给耶和华，在你们的地上也不可这样行。 |
