# 1 Peter 2 / 彼得前书 2

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Wherefore laying aside all malice, and all guile, and hypocrisies, and envies, and all evil speakings, |
| 中文 | 所以你们既除去一切的恶毒（或作阴毒），诡诈，并假善，嫉妒，和一切毁谤的话， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | As newborn babes, desire the sincere milk of the word, that ye may grow thereby: |
| 中文 | 就要爱慕那纯净的灵奶，像才生的婴孩爱慕奶一样，叫你们因此渐长，以致得救。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If so be ye have tasted that the Lord is gracious. |
| 中文 | 你们若尝过主恩的滋味，就必如此 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | To whom coming, as unto a living stone, disallowed indeed of men, but chosen of God, and precious, |
| 中文 | 主乃活石。固然是被人所弃的，却是被神所拣选所宝贵的。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ye also, as lively stones, are built up a spiritual house, an holy priesthood, to offer up spiritual sacrifices, acceptable to God by Jesus Christ. |
| 中文 | 你们来到主面前，也就像活石，被建造成为灵宫，作圣洁的祭司，藉着耶稣基督奉献神所悦纳的灵祭。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Wherefore also it is contained in the scripture, Behold, I lay in Sion a chief corner stone, elect, precious: and he that believeth on him shall not be confounded. |
| 中文 | 因为经上说，看哪，我把所拣选所宝贵的房角石，安放在锡安。信靠他的人，必不至于羞愧。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Unto you therefore which believe he is precious: but unto them which be disobedient, the stone which the builders disallowed, the same is made the head of the corner, |
| 中文 | 所以他在你们信的人就为宝贵，在那不信的人有话说，匠人所弃的石头，已作了房角的头块石头。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And a stone of stumbling, and a rock of offence, even to them which stumble at the word, being disobedient: whereunto also they were appointed. |
| 中文 | 又说，作了绊脚的石头，跌人的磐石。他们既不顺从，就在道理上绊跌。（或作他们绊跌都因不顺从道理）他们这样绊跌也是豫定的。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But ye are a chosen generation, a royal priesthood, an holy nation, a peculiar people; that ye should shew forth the praises of him who hath called you out of darkness into his marvellous light: |
| 中文 | 惟有你们是被拣选的族类，是有君尊的祭司，是圣洁的国度，是属神的子民，要叫你们宣扬那召你们出黑暗入奇妙光明者的美德。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Which in time past were not a people, but are now the people of God: which had not obtained mercy, but now have obtained mercy. |
| 中文 | 你们从前算不得子民，现在却作了神的子民。从前未曾蒙怜恤，现在却蒙了怜恤。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Dearly beloved, I beseech you as strangers and pilgrims, abstain from fleshly lusts, which war against the soul; |
| 中文 | 亲爱的弟兄阿你们是客旅，是寄居的。我劝你们要禁戒肉体的私欲。这私欲是与灵魂争战的。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Having your conversation honest among the Gentiles: that, whereas they speak against you as evildoers, they may by your good works, which they shall behold, glorify God in the day of visitation. |
| 中文 | 你们在外邦人中，应当品行端正，叫那些毁谤你们是作恶的，因看见你们的好行为，便在鉴察的日子（鉴察或作眷顾），归荣耀给神。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Submit yourselves to every ordinance of man for the Lord's sake: whether it be to the king, as supreme; |
| 中文 | 你们为主的缘故，要顺服人的一切制度，或是在上的君王， |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Or unto governors, as unto them that are sent by him for the punishment of evildoers, and for the praise of them that do well. |
| 中文 | 或是君王所派罚恶赏善的臣宰。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For so is the will of God, that with well doing ye may put to silence the ignorance of foolish men: |
| 中文 | 因为神的旨意原是要你们行善，可以堵住那糊涂无知人的口。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | As free, and not using your liberty for a cloke of maliciousness, but as the servants of God. |
| 中文 | 你们虽是自由的，却不可藉着自由遮盖恶毒（或作阴毒），总要作神的仆人。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Honour all men. Love the brotherhood. Fear God. Honour the king. |
| 中文 | 务要尊敬众人。亲爱教中的弟兄。敬畏神。尊敬君王。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Servants, be subject to your masters with all fear; not only to the good and gentle, but also to the froward. |
| 中文 | 你们作仆人的，凡事要存敬畏的心顺服主人。不但顺服那善良温和的，就是那乖僻的也要顺服。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For this is thankworthy, if a man for conscience toward God endure grief, suffering wrongfully. |
| 中文 | 倘若人为叫良心对得住神，就忍受冤屈的苦楚，这是可喜爱的。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For what glory is it, if, when ye be buffeted for your faults, ye shall take it patiently? but if, when ye do well, and suffer for it, ye take it patiently, this is acceptable with God. |
| 中文 | 你们若因犯罪受责打，能忍耐，有什么可夸的呢。但你们若因行善受苦，若能忍耐，这在神看是可喜爱的。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For even hereunto were ye called: because Christ also suffered for us, leaving us an example, that ye should follow his steps: |
| 中文 | 你们蒙恩原是为此。因基督也为你们受过苦，给你们留下榜样，叫你们跟随他的脚踪行。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who did no sin, neither was guile found in his mouth: |
| 中文 | 他并没有犯罪，口里也没有诡诈。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who, when he was reviled, reviled not again; when he suffered, he threatened not; but committed himself to him that judgeth righteously: |
| 中文 | 他被骂不还口。受害不说威吓的话。只将自己交托那按公义审判人的主。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who his own self bare our sins in his own body on the tree, that we, being dead to sins, should live unto righteousness: by whose stripes ye were healed. |
| 中文 | 他被挂在木头上亲身担当了我们的罪，使我们既然在罪上死，就得以在义上活。因他受的鞭伤，你们便得医治。 |

## 25

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For ye were as sheep going astray; but are now returned unto the Shepherd and Bishop of your souls. |
| 中文 | 你们从前好像迷路的羊。如今却归到你们灵魂的牧人监督了。 |
