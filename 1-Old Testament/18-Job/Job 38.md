# Job 38 / 约伯记 38

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then the LORD answered Job out of the whirlwind, and said, |

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who is this that darkeneth counsel by words without knowledge? |
| 中文 | 谁用无知的言语，使我的旨意暗昧不明。 |

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Gird up now thy loins like a man; for I will demand of thee, and answer thou me. |
| 中文 | 你要如勇士束腰。我问你，你可以指示我。 |

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
