# Matthew 15 / 马太福音 15

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 那时有法利赛人和文士，从耶路撒冷来见耶稣说， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Why do thy disciples transgress the tradition of the elders? for they wash not their hands when they eat bread. |
| 中文 | 你的门徒为什么犯古人的遗传呢。因为吃饭的时候，他们不洗手。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But he answered and said unto them, Why do ye also transgress the commandment of God by your tradition? |
| 中文 | 耶稣回答说，你们为什么因着你们的遗传，犯神的诫命呢。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For God commanded, saying, Honour thy father and mother: and, He that curseth father or mother, let him die the death. |
| 中文 | 神说，当孝敬父母。又说，咒骂父母的，必治死他。 |

## 5

| --- | --- |

| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 他就可以不孝敬父母。这就是你们藉着遗传，废了神的诫命。 |
