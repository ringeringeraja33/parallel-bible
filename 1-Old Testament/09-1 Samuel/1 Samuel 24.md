# 1 Samuel 24

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And it came to pass, when Saul was returned from following the Philistines, that it was told him, saying, Behold, David is in the wilderness of Engedi. |
| 中文 | 扫罗追赶非利士人回来，有人告诉他说，大卫在隐基底的旷野。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then Saul took three thousand chosen men out of all Israel, and went to seek David and his men upon the rocks of the wild goats. |
| 中文 | 扫罗就从以色列人中挑选三千精兵，率领他们往野羊的磐石去，寻索大卫和跟随他的人。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And he came to the sheepcotes by the way, where was a cave; and Saul went in to cover his feet: and David and his men remained in the sides of the cave. |
| 中文 | 到了路旁的羊圈，在那里有洞，扫罗进去大解。大卫和跟随他的人正藏在洞里的深处。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the men of David said unto him, Behold the day of which the LORD said unto thee, Behold, I will deliver thine enemy into thine hand, that thou mayest do to him as it shall seem good unto thee. Then David arose, and cut off the skirt of Saul's robe privily. |
| 中文 | 跟随的人对大卫说，耶和华曾应许你说，我要将你的仇敌交在你手里，你可以任意待他。如今时候到了。大卫就起来，悄悄地割下扫罗外袍的衣襟。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And it came to pass afterward, that David's heart smote him, because he had cut off Saul's skirt. |
| 中文 | 随后大卫心中自责，因为割下扫罗的衣襟。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And he said unto his men, The LORD forbid that I should do this thing unto my master, the LORD's anointed, to stretch forth mine hand against him, seeing he is the anointed of the LORD. |
| 中文 | 对跟随他的人说，我的主乃是耶和华的受膏者，我在耶和华面前万不敢伸手害他，因他是耶和华的受膏者。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | So David stayed his servants with these words, and suffered them not to rise against Saul. But Saul rose up out of the cave, and went on his way. |
| 中文 | 大卫用这话拦住跟随他的人，不容他们起来害扫罗。扫罗起来，从洞里出去行路。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | David also arose afterward, and went out of the cave, and cried after Saul, saying, My lord the king. And when Saul looked behind him, David stooped with his face to the earth, and bowed himself. |
| 中文 | 随后大卫也起来，从洞里出去，呼叫扫罗说，我主，我王。扫罗回头观看，大卫就屈身，脸伏于地下拜。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And David said to Saul, Wherefore hearest thou men's words, saying, Behold, David seeketh thy hurt? |
| 中文 | 大卫对扫罗说，你为何听信人的谗言，说大卫想要害你呢。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, this day thine eyes have seen how that the LORD had delivered thee to day into mine hand in the cave: and some bade me kill thee: but mine eye spared thee; and I said, I will not put forth mine hand against my lord; for he is the LORD's anointed. |
| 中文 | 今日你亲眼看见在洞中，耶和华将你交在我手里。有人叫我杀你，我却爱惜你，说，我不敢伸手害我的主，因为他是耶和华的受膏者。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Moreover, my father, see, yea, see the skirt of thy robe in my hand: for in that I cut off the skirt of thy robe, and killed thee not, know thou and see that there is neither evil nor transgression in mine hand, and I have not sinned against thee; yet thou huntest my soul to take it. |
| 中文 | 我父阿，看看你外袍的衣襟在我手中。我割下你的衣襟，没有杀你。你由此可以知道我没有恶意叛逆你。你虽然猎取我的命，我却没有得罪你。 |
