# Psalms 51 / 诗篇 51

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Have mercy upon me, O God, according to thy lovingkindness: according unto the multitude of thy tender mercies blot out my transgressions. |
| 中文 | （大卫与拔示巴同室以候，先知拿单来见他。他作这诗，交与伶长）神阿，求你按你的慈爱怜恤我，按你丰盛的慈悲涂抹我的过犯。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Wash me throughly from mine iniquity, and cleanse me from my sin. |
| 中文 | 求你将我的罪孽洗除净尽并洁除我的罪。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For I acknowledge my transgressions: and my sin is ever before me. |
| 中文 | 因为我知道我的过犯我的罪常在我面前 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Against thee, thee only, have I sinned, and done this evil in thy sight: that thou mightest be justified when thou speakest, and be clear when thou judgest. |
| 中文 | 我向你犯罪，惟独得罪了你，在你眼前行了这恶，以致你责备我的时候，显为公义。判断我的时候，显为清正。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, I was shapen in iniquity; and in sin did my mother conceive me. |
| 中文 | 我是在罪孽里生的。在我母胎的时候，就有了罪。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, thou desirest truth in the inward parts: and in the hidden part thou shalt make me to know wisdom. |
| 中文 | 你所洗爱的，是内里诚实。你在我隐密处，必使我得智慧。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Purge me with hyssop, and I shall be clean: wash me, and I shall be whiter than snow. |
| 中文 | 求你用牛膝草洁净我，我就乾净，求你洗涤我，我就比雪更白。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Make me to hear joy and gladness; that the bones which thou hast broken may rejoice. |
| 中文 | 求你使我得听欢喜快乐的声音，使你所压伤的骨头，可以踊跃。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hide thy face from my sins, and blot out all mine iniquities. |
| 中文 | 求你掩面不看我的罪，涂抹我一切的罪孽。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Create in me a clean heart, O God; and renew a right spirit within me. |
| 中文 | 神阿，求你为我造清洁的心，使我里面重新有正直的灵。（正直或作坚定）。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Cast me not away from thy presence; and take not thy holy spirit from me. |
| 中文 | 不要丢弃我，使我离开你的面。不要从我收回你的圣灵。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Restore unto me the joy of thy salvation; and uphold me with thy free spirit. |
| 中文 | 求你使我仍得救恩之乐，赐我乐意的灵扶持我。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then will I teach transgressors thy ways; and sinners shall be converted unto thee. |
| 中文 | 我就把你的道指教有过犯的人。罪人必归顺你。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Deliver me from bloodguiltiness, O God, thou God of my salvation: and my tongue shall sing aloud of thy righteousness. |
| 中文 | 神阿，你是拯救我的神。求你救我脱离流人血的罪。我的舌头就高声歌唱你的公义。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O Lord, open thou my lips; and my mouth shall shew forth thy praise. |
| 中文 | 主阿，求你使我的嘴唇张开，我的口便传扬赞美你的话。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thou desirest not sacrifice; else would I give it: thou delightest not in burnt offering. |
| 中文 | 你本不喜爱祭物。若喜爱，我就献上。燔祭你也不喜悦。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The sacrifices of God are a broken spirit: a broken and a contrite heart, O God, thou wilt not despise. |
| 中文 | 神所要的祭，就是忧伤的灵。神阿，忧伤痛悔的心，你必不轻看。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Do good in thy good pleasure unto Zion: build thou the walls of Jerusalem. |
| 中文 | 求你随你的美意善待锡安，建造耶路撒冷的城墙。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then shalt thou be pleased with the sacrifices of righteousness, with burnt offering and whole burnt offering: then shall they offer bullocks upon thine altar. |
| 中文 | 那时，你必喜爱公义的祭，和燔祭并全牲的燔祭。那时，人必将公牛献在你坛上。 |
