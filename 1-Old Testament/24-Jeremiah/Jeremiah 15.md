# Jeremiah 15 / 耶利米书 15

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then said the LORD unto me, Though Moses and Samuel stood before me, yet my mind could not be toward this people: cast them out of my sight, and let them go forth. |
| 中文 | 耶和华对我说，虽有摩西和撒母耳站在我面前代求，我的心也不顾惜这百姓。你将他们从我眼前赶出，叫他们去吧。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And it shall come to pass, if they say unto thee, Whither shall we go forth? then thou shalt tell them, Thus saith the LORD; Such as are for death, to death; and such as are for the sword, to the sword; and such as are for the famine, to the famine; and such as are for the captivity, to the captivity. |
| 中文 | 他们问你说，我们往哪里去呢。你便告诉他们，耶和华如此说，定为死亡的，必致死亡。定为刀杀的，必交刀杀。定为饥荒的，必遭饥荒。定为掳掠的，必被掳掠。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And I will appoint over them four kinds, saith the LORD: the sword to slay, and the dogs to tear, and the fowls of the heaven, and the beasts of the earth, to devour and destroy. |
| 中文 | 耶和华说，我命定四样害他们，就是刀剑杀戮，狗类撕裂，空中的飞鸟，和地上的野兽吞吃毁灭。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And I will cause them to be removed into all kingdoms of the earth, because of Manasseh the son of Hezekiah king of Judah, for that which he did in Jerusalem. |
| 中文 | 又必使他们在天下万国中抛来抛去，都因犹大王希西家的儿子玛拿西在耶路撒冷所行的事。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For who shall have pity upon thee, O Jerusalem? or who shall bemoan thee? or who shall go aside to ask how thou doest? |
| 中文 | 耶路撒冷阿，谁可怜你呢。谁为你悲伤呢。谁转身问你的安呢。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou hast forsaken me, saith the LORD, thou art gone backward: therefore will I stretch out my hand against thee, and destroy thee; I am weary with repenting. |
| 中文 | 耶和华说，你弃绝了我，转身退后。因此我伸手攻击你，毁坏你。我后悔甚不耐烦。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And I will fan them with a fan in the gates of the land; I will bereave them of children, I will destroy my people, since they return not from their ways. |
| 中文 | 我在境内各城门口（或作我在这地边界的关口），用簸箕簸了我的百姓，使他们丧掉儿女。我毁灭他们，他们仍不转离所行的道。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Their widows are increased to me above the sand of the seas: I have brought upon them against the mother of the young men a spoiler at noonday: I have caused him to fall upon it suddenly, and terrors upon the city. |
| 中文 | 他们的寡妇在我面前比海沙更多。我使灭命的午间来，攻击少年人的母亲，使痛苦惊吓忽然临到她身上。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | She that hath borne seven languisheth: she hath given up the ghost; her sun is gone down while it was yet day: she hath been ashamed and confounded: and the residue of them will I deliver to the sword before their enemies, saith the LORD. |
| 中文 | 生过七子的妇人力衰气绝。尚在白昼，日头忽落。她抱愧蒙羞。其馀的人，我必在他们敌人跟前，交与刀剑。这是耶和华说的。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Woe is me, my mother, that thou hast borne me a man of strife and a man of contention to the whole earth! I have neither lent on usury, nor men have lent to me on usury; yet every one of them doth curse me. |
| 中文 | 我的母亲哪，我有祸了。因你生我作为遍地相争相竞的人。我素来没有借贷与人，人也没有借贷与我，人人却都咒骂我。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD said, Verily it shall be well with thy remnant; verily I will cause the enemy to entreat thee well in the time of evil and in the time of affliction. |
| 中文 | 耶和华说，我必要坚固你，使你得好处。灾祸苦难临到的时候，我必要使仇敌央求你。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Shall iron break the northern iron and the steel? |
| 中文 | 人岂能将铜与铁，就是北方的铁折断呢。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thy substance and thy treasures will I give to the spoil without price, and that for all thy sins, even in all thy borders. |
| 中文 | 我必因你在四境之内所犯的一切罪，把你的货物财宝当掠物，白白地交给仇敌。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And I will make thee to pass with thine enemies into a land which thou knowest not: for a fire is kindled in mine anger, which shall burn upon you. |
| 中文 | 我也必使仇敌带这掠物到你所不认识的地去，因我怒中起的火要将你们焚烧。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O LORD, thou knowest: remember me, and visit me, and revenge me of my persecutors; take me not away in thy longsuffering: know that for thy sake I have suffered rebuke. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，你是知道的。求你记念我，眷顾我，向逼迫我的人为我报仇。不要向他们忍怒取我的命，要知道我为你的缘故受了凌辱。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thy words were found, and I did eat them; and thy word was unto me the joy and rejoicing of mine heart: for I am called by thy name, O LORD God of hosts. |
| 中文 | 耶和华万军之神阿，我得着你的言语就当食物吃了。你的言语是我心中的欢喜快乐，因我是称为你名下的人。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I sat not in the assembly of the mockers, nor rejoiced; I sat alone because of thy hand: for thou hast filled me with indignation. |
| 中文 | 我没有坐在宴乐人的会中，也没有欢乐。我因你的感动（原文作手）独自静坐，因你使我满心愤恨。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Why is my pain perpetual, and my wound incurable, which refuseth to be healed? wilt thou be altogether unto me as a liar, and as waters that fail? |
| 中文 | 我的痛苦为何长久不止呢。我的伤痕为何无法医治，不能痊愈呢。难道你待我有诡诈，像流乾的河道吗。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Therefore thus saith the LORD, If thou return, then will I bring thee again, and thou shalt stand before me: and if thou take forth the precious from the vile, thou shalt be as my mouth: let them return unto thee; but return not thou unto them. |
| 中文 | 耶和华如此说，你若归回，我就将你再带来，使你站在我面前。你若将宝贵的和下贱的分别出来，你就可以当作我的口。他们必归向你，你却不可归向他们。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And I will make thee unto this people a fenced brasen wall: and they shall fight against thee, but they shall not prevail against thee: for I am with thee to save thee and to deliver thee, saith the LORD. |
| 中文 | 我必使你向这百姓成为坚固的铜墙。他们必攻击你，却不能胜你。因我与你同在，要拯救你，搭救你。这是耶和华说的。 |

## 21

| 中文 | 我必搭救你脱离恶人的手，救赎你脱离强暴人的手。 |
