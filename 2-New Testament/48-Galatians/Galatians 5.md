# Galatians 5 / 加拉太书 5

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Stand fast therefore in the liberty wherewith Christ hath made us free, and be not entangled again with the yoke of bondage. |
| 中文 | 基督释放了我们，叫我们得自由，所以要站立得稳，不要再被奴仆的轭挟制。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, I Paul say unto you, that if ye be circumcised, Christ shall profit you nothing. |
| 中文 | 我保罗告诉你们，若受割礼，基督就与你们无益了。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For I testify again to every man that is circumcised, that he is a debtor to do the whole law. |
| 中文 | 我再指着凡受割礼的人确实的说，他是欠着行全律法的债。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Christ is become of no effect unto you, whosoever of you are justified by the law; ye are fallen from grace. |
| 中文 | 你们这要靠律法称义的，是与基督隔绝，从恩典中坠落了。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For we through the Spirit wait for the hope of righteousness by faith. |
| 中文 | 我们靠着圣灵，凭着信心，等候所盼望的义。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For in Jesus Christ neither circumcision availeth any thing, nor uncircumcision; but faith which worketh by love. |
| 中文 | 原来在基督耶稣里，受割礼不受割礼，全无功效。惟独使人生发仁爱的信心，才有功效。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ye did run well; who did hinder you that ye should not obey the truth? |
| 中文 | 你们向来跑得好。有谁拦阻你们，叫你们不顺从真理呢。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | This persuasion cometh not of him that calleth you. |
| 中文 | 这样的劝导，不是出于那召你们的。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A little leaven leaveneth the whole lump. |
| 中文 | 一点面酵能使全团都发起来。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I have confidence in you through the Lord, that ye will be none otherwise minded: but he that troubleth you shall bear his judgment, whosoever he be. |
| 中文 | 我在主里很信你们必不怀别样的心，但搅扰你们的，无论是谁，必担当他的罪名。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And I, brethren, if I yet preach circumcision, why do I yet suffer persecution? then is the offence of the cross ceased. |
| 中文 | 弟兄们，我若仍旧传割礼，为什么还受逼迫呢。若是这样，那十字架讨厌的地方就没有了。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I would they were even cut off which trouble you. |
| 中文 | 恨不得那搅乱你们的人，把自己割绝了。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For, brethren, ye have been called unto liberty; only use not liberty for an occasion to the flesh, but by love serve one another. |
| 中文 | 弟兄们，你们蒙召，是要得自由。只是不可将你们的自由当作放纵情欲的机会。总要用爱心互相服事。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For all the law is fulfilled in one word, even in this; Thou shalt love thy neighbour as thyself. |
| 中文 | 因为全律法都包在爱人如己这一句话之内了。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But if ye bite and devour one another, take heed that ye be not consumed one of another. |
| 中文 | 你们要谨慎。若相咬相吞，只怕要彼此消灭了。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | This I say then, Walk in the Spirit, and ye shall not fulfil the lust of the flesh. |
| 中文 | 我说，你们当顺着圣灵而行，就不放纵肉体的情欲了。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the flesh lusteth against the Spirit, and the Spirit against the flesh: and these are contrary the one to the other: so that ye cannot do the things that ye would. |
| 中文 | 因为情欲和圣灵相争，圣灵和情欲相争。这两个是彼此相敌，使你们不能作所愿意作的。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But if ye be led of the Spirit, ye are not under the law. |
| 中文 | 但你们若被圣灵引导，就不在律法以下。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Now the works of the flesh are manifest, which are these; Adultery, fornication, uncleanness, lasciviousness, |
| 中文 | 情欲的事，都是显而易见的。就如奸淫，污秽，邪荡， |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Idolatry, witchcraft, hatred, variance, emulations, wrath, strife, seditions, heresies, |
| 中文 | 拜偶像，邪术，仇恨，争竞，忌恨，恼怒，结党，纷争，异端， |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Envyings, murders, drunkenness, revellings, and such like: of the which I tell you before, as I have also told you in time past, that they which do such things shall not inherit the kingdom of God. |
| 中文 | 嫉妒，（有古卷在此有凶杀二字）醉酒，荒宴等类，我从前告诉你们，现在又告诉你们，行这样事的人，必不能承受神的国。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, longsuffering, gentleness, goodness, faith, |
| 中文 | 圣灵所结的果子，就是仁爱，喜乐，和平，忍耐，恩慈，良善，信实， |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Meekness, temperance: against such there is no law. |
| 中文 | 温柔，节制。这样的事，没有律法禁止。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And they that are Christ's have crucified the flesh with the affections and lusts. |
| 中文 | 凡属基督耶稣的人，是已经把肉体，连肉体的邪情私欲，同钉在十字架上了。 |

## 25

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If we live in the Spirit, let us also walk in the Spirit. |
| 中文 | 我们若是靠圣灵得生，就当靠圣灵行事。 |

## 26

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let us not be desirous of vain glory, provoking one another, envying one another. |
| 中文 | 不要贪图虚名，彼此惹气，互相嫉妒。 |
