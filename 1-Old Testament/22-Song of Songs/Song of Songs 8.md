# Song of Songs 8

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O that thou wert as my brother, that sucked the breasts of my mother! when I should find thee without, I would kiss thee; yea, I should not be despised. |
| 中文 | 巴不得你像我的兄弟，像吃我母亲奶的兄弟。我在外头遇见你就与你亲嘴，谁也不轻看我。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I would lead thee, and bring thee into my mother's house, who would instruct me: I would cause thee to drink of spiced wine of the juice of my pomegranate. |
| 中文 | 我必引导你，领你进我母亲的家。我可以领受教训，也就使你喝石榴汁酿的香酒。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | His left hand should be under my head, and his right hand should embrace me. |
| 中文 | 他的左手必在我头下，他的右手必将我抱住。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I charge you, O daughters of Jerusalem, that ye stir not up, nor awake my love, until he please. |
| 中文 | 耶路撒冷的众女子阿，我嘱咐你们，不要惊动，不要叫醒我所亲爱的，等他自己情愿（不要叫醒云云或作不要激动爱情等它自发）。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who is this that cometh up from the wilderness, leaning upon her beloved? I raised thee up under the apple tree: there thy mother brought thee forth: there she brought thee forth that bare thee. |
| 中文 | 那靠着良人从旷野上来的，是谁呢。我在苹果树下叫醒你。你母亲在那里为你劬劳，生养你的在那里为你劬劳。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Set me as a seal upon thine heart, as a seal upon thine arm: for love is strong as death; jealousy is cruel as the grave: the coals thereof are coals of fire, which hath a most vehement flame. |
| 中文 | 求你将我放在心上如印记，带在你臂上如戳记。因为爱情如死之坚强。嫉恨如阴间之残忍。所发的电光，是火焰的电光，是耶和华的烈焰。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Many waters cannot quench love, neither can the floods drown it: if a man would give all the substance of his house for love, it would utterly be contemned. |
| 中文 | 爱情众水不能息灭，大水也不能淹没。若有人拿家中所有的财宝要换爱情，就全被藐视。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | We have a little sister, and she hath no breasts: what shall we do for our sister in the day when she shall be spoken for? |
| 中文 | 我们有一小妹，她的两乳尚未长成。人来提亲的日子，我们当为她怎样办理。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If she be a wall, we will build upon her a palace of silver: and if she be a door, we will inclose her with boards of cedar. |
| 中文 | 她若是墙，我们要在其上建造银塔。她若是门，我们要用香柏木板围护她。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I am a wall, and my breasts like towers: then was I in his eyes as one that found favour. |
| 中文 | 我是墙，我两乳像其上的楼。那时，我在他眼中像得平安的人。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Solomon had a vineyard at Baalhamon; he let out the vineyard unto keepers; every one for the fruit thereof was to bring a thousand pieces of silver. |
| 中文 | 所罗门在巴力哈们有一葡萄园。他将这葡萄园交给看守的人，为其中的果子，必交一千舍客勒银子。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My vineyard, which is mine, is before me: thou, O Solomon, must have a thousand, and those that keep the fruit thereof two hundred. |
| 中文 | 我自己的葡萄园在我面前。所罗门哪，一千舍客勒归你，二百舍客勒归看守果子的人。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou that dwellest in the gardens, the companions hearken to thy voice: cause me to hear it. |
| 中文 | 你这住在园中的，同伴都要听你的声音，求你使我也得听见。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Make haste, my beloved, and be thou like to a roe or to a young hart upon the mountains of spices. |
| 中文 | 我的良人哪，求你快来。如羚羊或小鹿在香草山上。 |
