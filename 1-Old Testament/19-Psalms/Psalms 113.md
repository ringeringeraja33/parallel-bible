# Psalms 113 / 诗篇 113

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Praise ye the LORD. Praise, O ye servants of the LORD, praise the name of the LORD. |
| 中文 | 你们要赞美耶和华。耶和华的仆人哪，你们要赞美，赞美耶和华的名。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Blessed be the name of the LORD from this time forth and for evermore. |
| 中文 | 耶和华的名，是应当称颂的，从今时直到永远。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | From the rising of the sun unto the going down of the same the LORD's name is to be praised. |
| 中文 | 从日出之地，到日落之处，耶和华的名是应当赞美的。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD is high above all nations, and his glory above the heavens. |
| 中文 | 耶和华超乎万民之上。他的荣耀高过诸天。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who is like unto the LORD our God, who dwelleth on high, |
| 中文 | 谁像耶和华我们的神呢。他坐在至高之处， |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who humbleth himself to behold the things that are in heaven, and in the earth! |
| 中文 | 自己谦卑，观看天上地下的事。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He raiseth up the poor out of the dust, and lifteth the needy out of the dunghill; |
| 中文 | 他从灰尘里抬举贫寒人，从粪堆中提拔穷乏人。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | That he may set him with princes, even with the princes of his people. |
| 中文 | 使他们与王子同坐，就是与本国的王子同坐。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He maketh the barren woman to keep house, and to be a joyful mother of children. Praise ye the LORD. |
| 中文 | 他使不能生育的妇人安居家中，为多子的乐母。你们要赞美耶和华。 |
