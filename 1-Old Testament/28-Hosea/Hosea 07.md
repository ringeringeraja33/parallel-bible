# Hosea 7

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When I would have healed Israel, then the iniquity of Ephraim was discovered, and the wickedness of Samaria: for they commit falsehood; and the thief cometh in, and the troop of robbers spoileth without. |
| 中文 | 我想医治以色列的时候，以法莲的罪孽，和撒玛利亚的罪恶，就显露出来。他们行事虚谎，内有贼人入室偷窃，外有强盗成群骚扰。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And they consider not in their hearts that I remember all their wickedness: now their own doings have beset them about; they are before my face. |
| 中文 | 他们心里并不思想我记念他们的一切恶，他们所行的现在缠绕他们，都在我面前。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They make the king glad with their wickedness, and the princes with their lies. |
| 中文 | 他们行恶使君王欢喜，说谎使首领喜乐。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They are all adulterers, as an oven heated by the baker, who ceaseth from raising after he hath kneaded the dough, until it be leavened. |
| 中文 | 他们都是行淫的，像火炉被烤饼的烧热，从抟面到发面的时候，暂不使火着旺。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | In the day of our king the princes have made him sick with bottles of wine; he stretched out his hand with scorners. |
| 中文 | 在我们王宴乐的日子，首领因酒的烈性成病。王与亵慢人拉手。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For they have made ready their heart like an oven, whiles they lie in wait: their baker sleepeth all the night; in the morning it burneth as a flaming fire. |
| 中文 | 首领埋伏的时候，心中热如火炉，就如烤饼的整夜睡卧，到了早晨火气炎炎。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They are all hot as an oven, and have devoured their judges; all their kings are fallen: there is none among them that calleth unto me. |
| 中文 | 众民也热如火炉，烧灭他们的官长。他们的君王都仆倒而死。他们中间无一人求告我。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ephraim, he hath mixed himself among the people; Ephraim is a cake not turned. |
| 中文 | 以法莲与列邦人搀杂。以法莲是没有翻过的饼。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Strangers have devoured his strength, and he knoweth it not: yea, gray hairs are here and there upon him, yet he knoweth not. |
| 中文 | 外邦人吞吃他劳力得来的，他却不知道，头发斑白，他也不觉得。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the pride of Israel testifieth to his face: and they do not return to the LORD their God, nor seek him for all this. |
| 中文 | 以色列的骄傲当面见证自己，虽遭遇这一切，他们仍不归向耶和华他们的神，也不寻求他。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ephraim also is like a silly dove without heart: they call to Egypt, they go to Assyria. |
| 中文 | 以法莲好像鸽子愚蠢无知。他们求告埃及，投奔亚述。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When they shall go, I will spread my net upon them; I will bring them down as the fowls of the heaven; I will chastise them, as their congregation hath heard. |
| 中文 | 他们去的时候，我必将我的网撒在他们身上，我要打下他们，如同空中的鸟。我必按他们会众所听见的惩罚他们。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Woe unto them! for they have fled from me: destruction unto them! because they have transgressed against me: though I have redeemed them, yet they have spoken lies against me. |
| 中文 | 他们因离弃我，必定有祸，因违背我，必被毁灭。我虽要救赎他们，他们却向我说谎。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And they have not cried unto me with their heart, when they howled upon their beds: they assemble themselves for corn and wine, and they rebel against me. |
| 中文 | 他们并不诚心哀求我，乃在床上呼号。他们为求五谷新酒聚集，仍然悖逆我。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Though I have bound and strengthened their arms, yet do they imagine mischief against me. |
| 中文 | 我虽教导他们，坚固他们的膀臂，他们竟图谋抗拒我。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They return, but not to the most High: they are like a deceitful bow: their princes shall fall by the sword for the rage of their tongue: this shall be their derision in the land of Egypt. |
| 中文 | 他们归向，却不归向至上者。他们如同翻背的弓。他们的首领必因舌头的狂傲倒在刀下，这在埃及地必作人的讥笑。 |
