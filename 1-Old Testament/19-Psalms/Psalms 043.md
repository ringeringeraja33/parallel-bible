# Psalms 43

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Judge me, O God, and plead my cause against an ungodly nation: O deliver me from the deceitful and unjust man. |
| 中文 | 神阿，求你伸我的冤，向不虔诚的国，为我辨屈。求你救我脱离诡诈不义的人。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thou art the God of my strength: why dost thou cast me off? why go I mourning because of the oppression of the enemy? |
| 中文 | 因为你是赐我力量的神，为何丢弃我呢。我为何因仇敌的欺压时常哀痛呢。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O send out thy light and thy truth: let them lead me; let them bring me unto thy holy hill, and to thy tabernacles. |
| 中文 | 求你发出你的亮光和真实，好引导我，带我到你的圣山，到你的居所。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then will I go unto the altar of God, unto God my exceeding joy: yea, upon the harp will I praise thee, O God my God. |
| 中文 | 我就走到神的祭坛，到我最喜乐的神那里。神阿，我的神，我要弹琴称赞你。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Why art thou cast down, O my soul? and why art thou disquieted within me? hope in God: for I shall yet praise him, who is the health of my countenance, and my God. |
| 中文 | 我的心哪，你为何忧闷，为何在我里面烦躁。因当仰望神。因我还要称赞他。他是我脸上的光荣，（原文作帮助）是我的神。 |
