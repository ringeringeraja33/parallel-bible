# Hebrews 3

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Wherefore, holy brethren, partakers of the heavenly calling, consider the Apostle and High Priest of our profession, Christ Jesus; |
| 中文 | 同蒙天召的圣洁弟兄阿，你们应当思想，我们所认为使者，为大祭司的耶稣。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who was faithful to him that appointed him, as also Moses was faithful in all his house. |
| 中文 | 他为那设立他的尽忠，如同摩西在神的全家尽忠一样。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For this man was counted worthy of more glory than Moses, inasmuch as he who hath builded the house hath more honour than the house. |
| 中文 | 他比摩西算是更配多得荣耀，好像建造房屋的比房屋更尊荣。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For every house is builded by some man; but he that built all things is God. |
| 中文 | 因为房屋都必有人建造。但建造万物的就是神。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And Moses verily was faithful in all his house, as a servant, for a testimony of those things which were to be spoken after; |
| 中文 | 摩西为仆人，在神的全家诚然尽忠，为要证明将来必传说的事。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But Christ as a son over his own house; whose house are we, if we hold fast the confidence and the rejoicing of the hope firm unto the end. |
| 中文 | 但基督为儿子，治理神的家。我们若将可夸的盼望和胆量，坚持到底，便是他的家了。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Wherefore (as the Holy Ghost saith, To day if ye will hear his voice, |
| 中文 | 圣灵有话说，你们今日若听他的话， |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Harden not your hearts, as in the provocation, in the day of temptation in the wilderness: |
| 中文 | 就不可硬着心，像在旷野惹他发怒，试探他的时候一样。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When your fathers tempted me, proved me, and saw my works forty years. |
| 中文 | 在那里，你们的祖宗试我探我，并且观看我的作为，有四十年之久。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Wherefore I was grieved with that generation, and said, They do alway err in their heart; and they have not known my ways. |
| 中文 | 所以我厌烦那世代的人，说，他们心里常常迷糊，竟不晓得我的作为。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | So I sware in my wrath, They shall not enter into my rest.) |
| 中文 | 我就在怒中起誓说，他们断不可进入我的安息。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Take heed, brethren, lest there be in any of you an evil heart of unbelief, in departing from the living God. |
| 中文 | 弟兄们，你们要谨慎，免得你们中间，或有人存着不信的恶心，把永生神离弃了。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But exhort one another daily, while it is called To day; lest any of you be hardened through the deceitfulness of sin. |
| 中文 | 总要趁着还有今日，天天彼此相劝，免得你们中间，有人被罪迷惑，心里就刚硬了。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 我们若将起初确实的信心，坚持到底，就在基督里有分了。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |

| 中文 | 经上说，你们今日若听他的话，就不可硬着心，像惹他发怒的日子一样。 |

## 16
