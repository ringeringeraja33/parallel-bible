# Psalms 5

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Give ear to my words, O LORD, consider my meditation. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你留心听我的言语，顾念我的心思。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hearken unto the voice of my cry, my King, and my God: for unto thee will I pray. |
| 中文 | 我的王我的神阿，求你垂听我呼求的声音。因为我向你祈祷。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My voice shalt thou hear in the morning, O LORD; in the morning will I direct my prayer unto thee, and will look up. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，早晨你必听我的声音。早晨我必向你陈明我的心意，并要儆醒。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thou art not a God that hath pleasure in wickedness: neither shall evil dwell with thee. |
| 中文 | 因为你不是喜悦恶事的神。恶人不能与你同居。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The foolish shall not stand in thy sight: thou hatest all workers of iniquity. |
| 中文 | 狂傲人不能站在你眼前。凡作孽的，都是你所恨恶的。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt destroy them that speak leasing: the LORD will abhor the bloody and deceitful man. |
| 中文 | 说谎言的，你必灭绝。好流人血弄诡诈的，都为耶和华所憎恶。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But as for me, I will come into thy house in the multitude of thy mercy: and in thy fear will I worship toward thy holy temple. |
| 中文 | 至于我，我必凭你丰盛的慈爱进入你的居所。我必存敬畏你的心向你的圣殿下拜。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Lead me, O LORD, in thy righteousness because of mine enemies; make thy way straight before my face. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你因我的仇敌，凭你的公义，引领我。使你的道路在我面前正直。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For there is no faithfulness in their mouth; their inward part is very wickedness; their throat is an open sepulchre; they flatter with their tongue. |
| 中文 | 因为他们的口中没有诚实。他们的心里满有邪恶。他们的喉咙，是敞开的坟墓。他们用舌头谄媚人。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Destroy thou them, O God; let them fall by their own counsels; cast them out in the multitude of their transgressions; for they have rebelled against thee. |
| 中文 | 神阿，求你定他们的罪。愿他们因自己的计谋跌倒。愿你在他们许多的过犯中，把他们逐出。因为他们背叛了你。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But let all those that put their trust in thee rejoice: let them ever shout for joy, because thou defendest them: let them also that love thy name be joyful in thee. |
| 中文 | 凡投靠你的，愿他们喜乐，时常欢呼，因为你护庇他们。又愿那爱你名的人，都靠你欢欣。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thou, LORD, wilt bless the righteous; with favour wilt thou compass him as with a shield. |
| 中文 | 因为你必赐福与义人。耶和华阿，你必用恩惠如同盾牌四面护卫他。 |
