# Job 12 / 约伯记 12

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And Job answered and said, |
| 中文 | 约伯回答说， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | No doubt but ye are the people, and wisdom shall die with you. |
| 中文 | 你们真是子民哪，你们死亡，智慧也就灭没了。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But I have understanding as well as you; I am not inferior to you: yea, who knoweth not such things as these? |
| 中文 | 但我也有聪明，与你们一样，并非不及你们。你们所说的，谁不知道呢。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I am as one mocked of his neighbour, who calleth upon God, and he answereth him: the just upright man is laughed to scorn. |
| 中文 | 我这求告神，蒙他应允的人，竟成了朋友所讥笑的。公义完全人，竟受了人的讥笑。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that is ready to slip with his feet is as a lamp despised in the thought of him that is at ease. |
| 中文 | 安逸的人，心里藐视灾祸。这灾祸常常等待滑脚的人。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The tabernacles of robbers prosper, and they that provoke God are secure; into whose hand God bringeth abundantly. |
| 中文 | 强盗的帐棚兴旺，惹神的人稳固，神多将财物送到他们手中。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But ask now the beasts, and they shall teach thee; and the fowls of the air, and they shall tell thee: |
| 中文 | 你且问走兽，走兽必指教你。又问空中的飞鸟，飞鸟必告诉你。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Or speak to the earth, and it shall teach thee: and the fishes of the sea shall declare unto thee. |
| 中文 | 或与地说话，地必指教你。海中的鱼也必向你说明。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who knoweth not in all these that the hand of the LORD hath wrought this? |
| 中文 | 看这一切，谁不知道是耶和华的手做成的呢。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | In whose hand is the soul of every living thing, and the breath of all mankind. |
| 中文 | 凡活物的生命，和人类的气息，都在他手中。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Doth not the ear try words? and the mouth taste his meat? |
| 中文 | 耳朵岂不试验言语，正如上膛尝食物吗。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | With the ancient is wisdom; and in length of days understanding. |
| 中文 | 年老的有智慧，寿高的有知识。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | With him is wisdom and strength, he hath counsel and understanding. |
| 中文 | 在神有智慧和能力，他有谋略和知识。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, he breaketh down, and it cannot be built again: he shutteth up a man, and there can be no opening. |
| 中文 | 他拆毁的，就不能再建造。他捆住人，便不得开释。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, he withholdeth the waters, and they dry up: also he sendeth them out, and they overturn the earth. |
| 中文 | 他把水留住，水便枯乾。他再发出水来，水就翻地。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | With him is strength and wisdom: the deceived and the deceiver are his. |
| 中文 | 在他有能力和智慧。被诱惑的，与诱惑人的，都是属他。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He leadeth counsellors away spoiled, and maketh the judges fools. |
| 中文 | 他把谋士剥衣掳去，又使审判官变成愚人。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 他放松君王的绑，又用带子捆他们的腰。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 他把祭司剥衣掳去，又使有能的人倾败。 |

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| --- | --- |

| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 他们无光，在黑暗中摸索，又使他们东倒西歪，像醉酒的人一样。 |
