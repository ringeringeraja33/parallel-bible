# Psalms 111 / 诗篇 111

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Praise ye the LORD. I will praise the LORD with my whole heart, in the assembly of the upright, and in the congregation. |
| 中文 | 你们要赞美耶和华。我要在正直人的大会中，并公会中，一心称谢耶和华。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The works of the LORD are great, sought out of all them that have pleasure therein. |
| 中文 | 耶和华的作为本为大。凡喜爱的都必考察。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | His work is honourable and glorious: and his righteousness endureth for ever. |
| 中文 | 他所行的是尊荣和威严。他的公义存到永远。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He hath made his wonderful works to be remembered: the LORD is gracious and full of compassion. |
| 中文 | 他行了奇事，使人记念。耶和华有恩惠，有怜悯。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He hath given meat unto them that fear him: he will ever be mindful of his covenant. |
| 中文 | 他赐粮食给敬畏他的人。他必永远记念他的约。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He hath shewed his people the power of his works, that he may give them the heritage of the heathen. |
| 中文 | 他向百姓显出大能的作为，把外邦的地赐给他们为业。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The works of his hands are verity and judgment; all his commandments are sure. |
| 中文 | 他手所行的，是诚实公平。他的训词都是确实的。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They stand fast for ever and ever, and are done in truth and uprightness. |
| 中文 | 是永永远远坚定的，是按诚实正直设立的。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He sent redemption unto his people: he hath commanded his covenant for ever: holy and reverend is his name. |
| 中文 | 他向百姓施行救赎，命定他的约，直到永远。他的名圣而可畏。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The fear of the LORD is the beginning of wisdom: a good understanding have all they that do his commandments: his praise endureth for ever. |
| 中文 | 敬畏耶和华是智慧得开端。凡遵行。他命令的，便是聪明人。耶和华是永远当赞美的。 |
