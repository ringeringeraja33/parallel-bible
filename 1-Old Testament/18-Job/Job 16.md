# Job 16

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then Job answered and said, |
| 中文 | 约伯回答说， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I have heard many such things: miserable comforters are ye all. |
| 中文 | 这样的话我听了许多。你们安慰人，反叫人愁烦。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Shall vain words have an end? or what emboldeneth thee that thou answerest? |
| 中文 | 虚空的言语有穷尽吗。有什么话惹动你回答呢。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I also could speak as ye do: if your soul were in my soul's stead, I could heap up words against you, and shake mine head at you. |
| 中文 | 我也能说你们那样的话。你们若处在我的境遇，我也会联络言语攻击你们，又能向你们摇头。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But I would strengthen you with my mouth, and the moving of my lips should asswage your grief. |
| 中文 | 但我必用口坚固你们，用嘴消解你们的忧愁。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Though I speak, my grief is not asswaged: and though I forbear, what am I eased? |
| 中文 | 我虽说话，忧愁仍不得消解。我虽停住不说，忧愁就离开我吗。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But now he hath made me weary: thou hast made desolate all my company. |
| 中文 | 但现在神使我困倦，使亲友远离我。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And thou hast filled me with wrinkles, which is a witness against me: and my leanness rising up in me beareth witness to my face. |
| 中文 | 又抓住我，作见证攻击我。我身体的枯瘦，也当面见证我的不是。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He teareth me in his wrath, who hateth me: he gnasheth upon me with his teeth; mine enemy sharpeneth his eyes upon me. |
| 中文 | 主发怒撕裂我，逼迫我，向我切齿。我的敌人怒目看我。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They have gaped upon me with their mouth; they have smitten me upon the cheek reproachfully; they have gathered themselves together against me. |
| 中文 | 他们向我开口，打我的脸羞辱我，聚会攻击我。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | God hath delivered me to the ungodly, and turned me over into the hands of the wicked. |
| 中文 | 神把我交给不敬虔的人，把我扔到恶人的手中。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I was at ease, but he hath broken me asunder: he hath also taken me by my neck, and shaken me to pieces, and set me up for his mark. |
| 中文 | 我素来安逸，他折断我，掐住我的颈项，把我摔碎。又立我为他的箭靶子。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | His archers compass me round about, he cleaveth my reins asunder, and doth not spare; he poureth out my gall upon the ground. |
| 中文 | 他的弓箭手四面围绕我。他破裂我的肺腑，并不留情，把我的胆倾倒在地上， |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He breaketh me with breach upon breach, he runneth upon me like a giant. |
| 中文 | 将我破裂又破裂，如同勇士向我直闯。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I have sewed sackcloth upon my skin, and defiled my horn in the dust. |
| 中文 | 我缝麻布在我皮肤上，把我的角放在尘土中。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My face is foul with weeping, and on my eyelids is the shadow of death; |
| 中文 | 我的脸因哭泣发紫，在我的眼皮上有死荫。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Not for any injustice in mine hands: also my prayer is pure. |
| 中文 | 我的手中却无强暴。我的祈祷也是清洁。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O earth, cover not thou my blood, and let my cry have no place. |
| 中文 | 地阿，不要遮盖我的血。不要阻挡我的哀求。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Also now, behold, my witness is in heaven, and my record is on high. |
| 中文 | 现今，在天有我的见证，在上有我的中保。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My friends scorn me: but mine eye poureth out tears unto God. |
| 中文 | 我的朋友讥诮我，我却向神眼泪汪汪。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O that one might plead for a man with God, as a man pleadeth for his neighbour! |
| 中文 | 愿人得与神辩白，如同人与朋友辩白一样。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When a few years are come, then I shall go the way whence I shall not return. |
| 中文 | 因为再过几年，我必走那往而不返之路。 |
