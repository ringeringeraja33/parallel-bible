# Job 4 / 约伯记 4

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then Eliphaz the Temanite answered and said, |
| 中文 | 毯螅人以利法回答说， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If we assay to commune with thee, wilt thou be grieved? but who can withhold himself from speaking? |
| 中文 | 人若想与你说话，你就厌烦吗。但谁能忍住不说呢。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, thou hast instructed many, and thou hast strengthened the weak hands. |
| 中文 | 你素来教导许多的人，又坚固软弱的手。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thy words have upholden him that was falling, and thou hast strengthened the feeble knees. |
| 中文 | 你的言语曾扶助那将要跌倒的人。你又使软弱的膝稳固。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But now it is come upon thee, and thou faintest; it toucheth thee, and thou art troubled. |
| 中文 | 但现在祸患临到你，你就昏迷，挨近你，你便惊惶。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Is not this thy fear, thy confidence, thy hope, and the uprightness of thy ways? |
| 中文 | 你的倚靠不是在你敬畏神吗。你的盼望不是在你行事纯正吗。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Remember, I pray thee, who ever perished, being innocent? or where were the righteous cut off? |
| 中文 | 请你追想，无辜的人有谁灭亡。正直的人在何处剪除。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Even as I have seen, they that plow iniquity, and sow wickedness, reap the same. |
| 中文 | 按我所见，耕罪孽，种毒害的人，都照样收割。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | By the blast of God they perish, and by the breath of his nostrils are they consumed. |
| 中文 | 神一出气，他们就灭亡。神一发怒，他们就消没。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The roaring of the lion, and the voice of the fierce lion, and the teeth of the young lions, are broken. |
| 中文 | 狮子的吼叫，和猛狮的声音，尽都止息。少壮狮子的牙齿，也都敲掉。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The old lion perisheth for lack of prey, and the stout lion's whelps are scattered abroad. |
| 中文 | 老狮子因绝食而死。母狮之子也都离散。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Now a thing was secretly brought to me, and mine ear received a little thereof. |
| 中文 | 我暗暗地得了默示。我耳朵也听其细微的声音。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 在思念夜中，异象之间，世人沉睡的时候。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 恐惧，战兢临到我身，使我百骨打战。 |

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 有灵从我面前经过，我身上的毫毛直立。 |

| 中文 | 必死的人岂能比神公义吗。人岂能比造他的主洁净吗。 |

| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 主不信靠他的臣仆，并且指他的使者为愚昧。 |
