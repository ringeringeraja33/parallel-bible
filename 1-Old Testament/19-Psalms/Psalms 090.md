# Psalms 90 / 诗篇 90

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Lord, thou hast been our dwelling place in all generations. |
| 中文 | （神人摩西的祈祷）主阿，你世世代代作我们的居所。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Before the mountains were brought forth, or ever thou hadst formed the earth and the world, even from everlasting to everlasting, thou art God. |
| 中文 | 诸山未曾生出，地与世界你未曾造成，从亘古到永远，你是神。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou turnest man to destruction; and sayest, Return, ye children of men. |
| 中文 | 你使人归于尘土，说，你们世人要归回。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For a thousand years in thy sight are but as yesterday when it is past, and as a watch in the night. |
| 中文 | 在你看来，千年如已过的昨日，又如夜间的一更。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou carriest them away as with a flood; they are as a sleep: in the morning they are like grass which groweth up. |
| 中文 | 你教他们如水冲去。他们如睡一觉。早晨他们如生长的草。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | In the morning it flourisheth, and groweth up; in the evening it is cut down, and withereth. |
| 中文 | 早晨发芽生长，晚上割下枯乾。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For we are consumed by thine anger, and by thy wrath are we troubled. |
| 中文 | 我们因你的怒气而消灭，因你的忿怒而惊惶。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou hast set our iniquities before thee, our secret sins in the light of thy countenance. |
| 中文 | 你将我们的罪孽摆在你面前，将我们的隐恶摆在你面光之中。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For all our days are passed away in thy wrath: we spend our years as a tale that is told. |
| 中文 | 我们经过的日子，都在你震怒之下。我们度尽的年岁好像一声叹息。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The days of our years are threescore years and ten; and if by reason of strength they be fourscore years, yet is their strength labour and sorrow; for it is soon cut off, and we fly away. |
| 中文 | 我们一生的年日是七十岁。若是强壮可到八十岁。但其中所矜夸的，不过是劳苦愁烦。转眼成空，我们便如飞而去。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who knoweth the power of thine anger? even according to thy fear, so is thy wrath. |
| 中文 | 谁晓得你怒气的权势，谁按着你该受的敬畏晓得你的忿怒呢。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | So teach us to number our days, that we may apply our hearts unto wisdom. |
| 中文 | 求你指教我们怎样数算自己的日子，好叫我们得着智慧的心。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Return, O LORD, how long? and let it repent thee concerning thy servants. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，我们要等到几时呢。求你转回，为你的仆人后悔。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O satisfy us early with thy mercy; that we may rejoice and be glad all our days. |
| 中文 | 求你使我们早早饱得你的慈爱，好叫我们一生一世欢呼喜乐。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Make us glad according to the days wherein thou hast afflicted us, and the years wherein we have seen evil. |
| 中文 | 求你照着你使我们受苦的日子，和我们遭难的年岁，叫我们喜乐。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let thy work appear unto thy servants, and thy glory unto their children. |
| 中文 | 愿你的作为向你仆人显现。愿你的荣耀向他们子孙显明。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And let the beauty of the LORD our God be upon us: and establish thou the work of our hands upon us; yea, the work of our hands establish thou it. |
| 中文 | 愿主我们神的荣美，归与我们身上。愿你坚立我们手所作的工。我们手所作的工，愿你坚立。 |
