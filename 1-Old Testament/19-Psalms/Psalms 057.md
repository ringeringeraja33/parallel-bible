# Psalms 57 / 诗篇 57

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Be merciful unto me, O God, be merciful unto me: for my soul trusteth in thee: yea, in the shadow of thy wings will I make my refuge, until these calamities be overpast. |
| 中文 | （大卫逃避扫罗，藏在洞里。那时他作这金诗，交与伶长，调用休要毁坏）神阿，求你怜悯我，怜悯我。因为我的心投靠你。我要投靠在你翅膀的荫下，等到灾害过去。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will cry unto God most high; unto God that performeth all things for me. |
| 中文 | 我要求告至高的神，就是为我成全诸事的神。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He shall send from heaven, and save me from the reproach of him that would swallow me up. Selah. God shall send forth his mercy and his truth. |
| 中文 | 那要吞我的人辱骂我的时候，神从天上必施恩救我。也必向我发出慈爱和诚实。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My soul is among lions: and I lie even among them that are set on fire, even the sons of men, whose teeth are spears and arrows, and their tongue a sharp sword. |
| 中文 | 我的性命在狮子中间。我躺卧在性如烈火的世人当中。他们的牙齿是枪，箭。他们的舌头是快刀。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Be thou exalted, O God, above the heavens; let thy glory be above all the earth. |
| 中文 | 神阿，愿你崇高，过于诸天。愿你的荣耀，高过全地。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They have prepared a net for my steps; my soul is bowed down: they have digged a pit before me, into the midst whereof they are fallen themselves. Selah. |
| 中文 | 他们为我的脚设下网罗，压制我的心。他们在我面前挖了坑，自己反掉在其中。（细拉） |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My heart is fixed, O God, my heart is fixed: I will sing and give praise. |
| 中文 | 神阿，我心坚定，我心坚定。我要唱诗，我要歌颂。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Awake up, my glory; awake, psaltery and harp: I myself will awake early. |
| 中文 | 我的灵阿，（原文作荣耀）你当醒起，琴瑟阿，你们当醒起。我自己要极早醒起。 |

| 中文 | 主阿，我要在万民中称谢你。在列邦中歌颂你。 |
