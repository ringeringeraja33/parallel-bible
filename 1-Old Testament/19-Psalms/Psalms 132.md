# Psalms 132 / 诗篇 132

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Lord, remember David, and all his afflictions: |
| 中文 | （上行之诗）耶和华阿，求你记念大卫所受的一切苦难。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | How he sware unto the LORD, and vowed unto the mighty God of Jacob; |
| 中文 | 他怎样向耶和华起誓，向雅各的大能者许愿， |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Surely I will not come into the tabernacle of my house, nor go up into my bed; |
| 中文 | 说，我必不进我的帐幕，也不上我的床榻。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will not give sleep to mine eyes, or slumber to mine eyelids, |
| 中文 | 我不容我的眼睛睡觉，也不容我的眼目打盹。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Until I find out a place for the LORD, an habitation for the mighty God of Jacob. |
| 中文 | 直等我为耶和华寻得所在，为雅各的大能者寻得居所。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Lo, we heard of it at Ephratah: we found it in the fields of the wood. |
| 中文 | 我们听说约柜在以法他，我们在基列耶琳就寻见了。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | We will go into his tabernacles: we will worship at his footstool. |
| 中文 | 我们要进他的居所，在他脚凳前下拜。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Arise, O LORD, into thy rest; thou, and the ark of thy strength. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你兴起，和你有能力的约柜同入安息之所。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let thy priests be clothed with righteousness; and let thy saints shout for joy. |
| 中文 | 愿你的祭司披上公义。愿你的圣民欢呼。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thy servant David's sake turn not away the face of thine anointed. |
| 中文 | 求你因你仆人大卫的缘故，不要厌弃你的受膏者。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD hath sworn in truth unto David; he will not turn from it; Of the fruit of thy body will I set upon thy throne. |
| 中文 | 耶和华向大卫，凭诚实起了誓，必不反覆，说，我要使你所生的坐在你的宝座上。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If thy children will keep my covenant and my testimony that I shall teach them, their children shall also sit upon thy throne for evermore. |
| 中文 | 你的众子若守我的约，和我所教训他们的法度，他们的子孙，必永远坐在你的宝座上。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the LORD hath chosen Zion; he hath desired it for his habitation. |
| 中文 | 因为耶和华拣选了锡安，愿意当作自己的居所， |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | This is my rest for ever: here will I dwell; for I have desired it. |
| 中文 | 说，这是我永远安息之所。我要住在这里，因为是我所愿意的。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will abundantly bless her provision: I will satisfy her poor with bread. |
| 中文 | 我要使其中的粮食丰满，使其中的穷人饱足。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will also clothe her priests with salvation: and her saints shall shout aloud for joy. |
| 中文 | 我要使祭司披上救恩，圣民大声欢呼。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | There will I make the horn of David to bud: I have ordained a lamp for mine anointed. |
| 中文 | 我要叫大卫的角，在那里发生。我为我的受膏者，预备明灯。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | His enemies will I clothe with shame: but upon himself shall his crown flourish. |
| 中文 | 我要使他的仇敌披上羞耻。但他的冠冕，要在头上发光。 |
