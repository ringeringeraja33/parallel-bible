# Psalms 95

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O come, let us sing unto the LORD: let us make a joyful noise to the rock of our salvation. |
| 中文 | 来阿，我们要向耶和华歌唱，向拯救我们的磐石欢呼。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let us come before his presence with thanksgiving, and make a joyful noise unto him with psalms. |
| 中文 | 我们要来感谢他，用诗歌向他欢呼。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the LORD is a great God, and a great King above all gods. |
| 中文 | 因耶和华为大神，为大王，超乎万神之上。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | In his hand are the deep places of the earth: the strength of the hills is his also. |
| 中文 | 地的深处在他手中。山的高峰也属他。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The sea is his, and he made it: and his hands formed the dry land. |
| 中文 | 海洋属他。是他造的。旱地也是他手造成的。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O come, let us worship and bow down: let us kneel before the LORD our maker. |
| 中文 | 来阿，我们要屈身敬拜，在造我们的耶和华面前跪下。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For he is our God; and we are the people of his pasture, and the sheep of his hand. To day if ye will hear his voice, |
| 中文 | 因为他是我们的神。我们是他草场的羊，是他手下的民。惟愿你们今天听他的话。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Harden not your heart, as in the provocation, and as in the day of temptation in the wilderness: |
| 中文 | 你们不可硬着心，像当日在米利巴，就是在旷野的玛撒。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When your fathers tempted me, proved me, and saw my work. |
| 中文 | 那时你们的祖宗试我探我，并且观看我的作为。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Forty years long was I grieved with this generation, and said, It is a people that do err in their heart, and they have not known my ways: |
| 中文 | 四十年之久，我厌烦那世代，说，这是心里迷糊的百姓，竟不晓得我的作为。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Unto whom I sware in my wrath that they should not enter into my rest. |
| 中文 | 所以我在怒中起誓，说，他们断不可进入我的安息。 |
