# 3 John 1

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The elder unto the wellbeloved Gaius, whom I love in the truth. |
| 中文 | 作长老的写信给亲爱的该犹，就是我诚心所爱的。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Beloved, I wish above all things that thou mayest prosper and be in health, even as thy soul prospereth. |
| 中文 | 亲爱的兄弟阿，我愿你凡事兴盛，身体健壮，如你的灵魂兴盛一样。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For I rejoiced greatly, when the brethren came and testified of the truth that is in thee, even as thou walkest in the truth. |
| 中文 | 有弟兄来证明你心里存的真理，正如你按真理而行，我就甚喜乐。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I have no greater joy than to hear that my children walk in truth. |
| 中文 | 我听见我的儿女们按真理而行，我的喜乐就没有比这个大的。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Beloved, thou doest faithfully whatsoever thou doest to the brethren, and to strangers; |
| 中文 | 亲爱的弟兄阿，凡你向作客旅之弟兄所行的，都是忠心的； |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Which have borne witness of thy charity before the church: whom if thou bring forward on their journey after a godly sort, thou shalt do well: |
| 中文 | 他们在教会面前证明了你的爱。你若配得过神，帮住他们往前行，这就好了。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Because that for his name's sake they went forth, taking nothing of the Gentiles. |
| 中文 | 因他们是为主的名（原文作那名）出外，对于外邦人一无所取。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | We therefore ought to receive such, that we might be fellowhelpers to the truth. |
| 中文 | 所以我们应该接待这样的人，叫我们与他们一同为真理作工。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I wrote unto the church: but Diotrephes, who loveth to have the preeminence among them, receiveth us not. |
| 中文 | 我曾略略的写信给教会。但那在教会中好为首的丢特腓不接待我们。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Wherefore, if I come, I will remember his deeds which he doeth, prating against us with malicious words: and not content therewith, neither doth he himself receive the brethren, and forbiddeth them that would, and casteth them out of the church. |
| 中文 | 所以我若去，必要题说他所行的事。就是他用恶言妄论我们。还不以此为足，他自己不接待弟兄，有人愿意接待，他也禁止，并且将接待弟兄的人赶出教会。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Beloved, follow not that which is evil, but that which is good. He that doeth good is of God: but he that doeth evil hath not seen God. |
| 中文 | 亲爱的兄弟阿，不要效法恶，只要效法善。行善的属乎神。行恶的未曾见过神。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Demetrius hath good report of all men, and of the truth itself: yea, and we also bear record; and ye know that our record is true. |
| 中文 | 低米丢行善有众人给他作见证。又有真理给他作见证。就是我们也给他作见证。你也知道我们的见证是真的。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I had many things to write, but I will not with ink and pen write unto thee: |
| 中文 | 我原有许多事要写给你，却不愿意用笔墨写给你。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But I trust I shall shortly see thee, and we shall speak face to face. |
| 中文 | 但盼望快快的见你，我们就当面谈论。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Peace be to thee. Our friends salute thee. Greet the friends by name. |
| 中文 | 愿你平安。众位朋友都问你安。请你替我按着姓名问众位朋友安。 |
