# Psalms 131 / 诗篇 131

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Lord, my heart is not haughty, nor mine eyes lofty: neither do I exercise myself in great matters, or in things too high for me. |
| 中文 | （大卫上行之诗）耶和华阿，我的心不狂傲，我的眼不高大。重大和测不透的事，我也不敢行。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Surely I have behaved and quieted myself, as a child that is weaned of his mother: my soul is even as a weaned child. |
| 中文 | 我的心平稳安静，好像断过奶的孩子在他母亲的怀中。我的心在我里面真像断过奶的孩子。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let Israel hope in the LORD from henceforth and for ever. |
| 中文 | 以色列阿，你当仰望耶和华，从今时直到永远。 |
