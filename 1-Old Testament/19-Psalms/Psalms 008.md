# Psalms 8

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O LORD, our Lord, how excellent is thy name in all the earth! who hast set thy glory above the heavens. |
| 中文 | 耶和华我们的主阿，你的名在全地何其美。你将你的荣耀彰显于天。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Out of the mouth of babes and sucklings hast thou ordained strength because of thine enemies, that thou mightest still the enemy and the avenger. |
| 中文 | 你因敌人的缘故，从婴孩和吃奶的口中，建立了能力，使仇敌和报仇的，闭口无言。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When I consider thy heavens, the work of thy fingers, the moon and the stars, which thou hast ordained; |
| 中文 | 我观看你指头所造的天，并你所陈设的月亮星宿， |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | What is man, that thou art mindful of him? and the son of man, that thou visitest him? |
| 中文 | 便说，人算什么，你竟顾念他。世人算什么，你竟眷顾他。 |

| Language | Content |

| 中文 | 你叫他比天使微小一点，并赐他荣耀尊贵为冠冕。 |
