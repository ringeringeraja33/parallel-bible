# Exodus 27

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And thou shalt make an altar of shittim wood, five cubits long, and five cubits broad; the altar shall be foursquare: and the height thereof shall be three cubits. |
| 中文 | 你要用皂荚木作坛。这坛要四方的，长五肘，宽五肘，高三肘。 |

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 要在坛的四拐角上作四个角，与坛接连一块，用铜把坛包裹。 |
