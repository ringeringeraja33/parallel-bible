# Psalms 70 / 诗篇 70

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | MAKE HASTE, O GOD, TO DELIVER ME; MAKE HASTE TO HELP ME, O LORD. |
| 中文 | （大卫的记念诗，交与伶长）神阿，求你快快搭救我。耶和华阿，求你速速帮助我。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let them be ashamed and confounded that seek after my soul: let them be turned backward, and put to confusion, that desire my hurt. |
| 中文 | 愿那些寻索我命的，抱愧蒙羞。愿那些喜悦我遭害的，退后受辱。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let them be turned back for a reward of their shame that say, Aha, aha. |
| 中文 | 愿那些对我说，阿哈，阿哈的，因羞愧退后。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let all those that seek thee rejoice and be glad in thee: and let such as love thy salvation say continually, Let God be magnified. |
| 中文 | 愿一切寻求你的，因你高兴欢喜。愿那些喜爱你救恩的常说，当尊神为大。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But I am poor and needy: make haste unto me, O God: thou art my help and my deliverer; O LORD, make no tarrying. |
| 中文 | 但我是困苦穷乏的。神阿，求你速速到我这里来。你是帮助我的，搭救我的。耶和华阿，求你不要耽延。 |
