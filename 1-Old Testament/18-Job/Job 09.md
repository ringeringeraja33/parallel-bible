# Job 9

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then Job answered and said, |
| 中文 | 约伯回答说， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I know it is so of a truth: but how should man be just with God? |
| 中文 | 我真知道是这样。但人在神面前怎能成为义呢。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If he will contend with him, he cannot answer him one of a thousand. |
| 中文 | 若愿意与他争辩，千中之一也不能回答。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He is wise in heart, and mighty in strength: who hath hardened himself against him, and hath prospered? |
| 中文 | 他心里有智慧，且大有能力。谁向神刚硬而得亨通呢。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Which removeth the mountains, and they know not: which overturneth them in his anger. |
| 中文 | 他发怒，把山翻倒挪移，山并不知觉。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Which shaketh the earth out of her place, and the pillars thereof tremble. |
| 中文 | 他使地震动，离其本位，地的柱子就摇撼。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Which commandeth the sun, and it riseth not; and sealeth up the stars. |
| 中文 | 他吩咐日头不出来，就不出来，又封闭众星。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Which alone spreadeth out the heavens, and treadeth upon the waves of the sea. |
| 中文 | 他独自铺张苍天，步行在海浪之上。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Which maketh Arcturus, Orion, and Pleiades, and the chambers of the south. |
| 中文 | 他造北斗，叁星，昴星，并南方的密宫。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Which doeth great things past finding out; yea, and wonders without number. |
| 中文 | 他行大事，不可测度，行奇事，不可胜数。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Lo, he goeth by me, and I see him not: he passeth on also, but I perceive him not. |
| 中文 | 他从我旁边经过，我却不看见。他在我面前行走，我倒不知觉。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, he taketh away, who can hinder him? who will say unto him, What doest thou? |
| 中文 | 他夺取，谁能阻挡。谁敢问他，你做什么。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If God will not withdraw his anger, the proud helpers do stoop under him. |
| 中文 | 神必不收回他的怒气。扶助拉哈伯的，屈身在他以下。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | How much less shall I answer him, and choose out my words to reason with him? |
| 中文 | 既是这样，我怎敢回答他，怎敢选择言语与他辩论呢。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Whom, though I were righteous, yet would I not answer, but I would make supplication to my judge. |
| 中文 | 我虽有义，也不回答他。只要向那审判我的恳求。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If I had called, and he had answered me; yet would I not believe that he had hearkened unto my voice. |
| 中文 | 我若呼吁，他应允我。我仍不信他真听我的声音。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For he breaketh me with a tempest, and multiplieth my wounds without cause. |
| 中文 | 他用暴风折断我，无故地加增我的损伤。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He will not suffer me to take my breath, but filleth me with bitterness. |
| 中文 | 我就是喘一口气，他都不容，倒使我满心苦恼。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If I speak of strength, lo, he is strong: and if of judgment, who shall set me a time to plead? |
| 中文 | 若论力量，他真有能力。若论审判，他说谁能将我传来呢。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If I justify myself, mine own mouth shall condemn me: if I say, I am perfect, it shall also prove me perverse. |
| 中文 | 我虽有义，自己的口要定我为有罪。我虽完全，我口必显我为弯曲。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Though I were perfect, yet would I not know my soul: I would despise my life. |
| 中文 | 我本完全，不顾自己。我厌恶我的性命。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | This is one thing, therefore I said it, He destroyeth the perfect and the wicked. |
| 中文 | 善恶无分，都是一样。所以我说，完全人和恶人，他都灭绝。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If the scourge slay suddenly, he will laugh at the trial of the innocent. |
| 中文 | 若忽然遭杀害之祸，他必戏笑无辜的人遇难。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 世界交在恶人手中。蒙蔽世界审判官的脸，若不是他，是谁呢。 |

## 25

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Now my days are swifter than a post: they flee away, they see no good. |
| 中文 | 我的日子比跑信的更快，急速过去，不见福乐。 |

## 26

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They are passed away as the swift ships: as the eagle that hasteth to the prey. |
| 中文 | 我的日子过去如快船，如急落抓食的鹰。 |

## 27

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| --- | --- |
