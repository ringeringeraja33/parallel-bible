# Psalms 147 / 诗篇 147

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Praise ye the LORD: for it is good to sing praises unto our God; for it is pleasant; and praise is comely. |
| 中文 | 你们要赞美耶和华，因歌颂我们的神为善为美。赞美的话是合宜的。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD doth build up Jerusalem: he gathereth together the outcasts of Israel. |
| 中文 | 耶和华建造耶路撒冷，聚集以色列中被赶散的人。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He healeth the broken in heart, and bindeth up their wounds. |
| 中文 | 他医好伤心的人，裹好他们的伤处。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He telleth the number of the stars; he calleth them all by their names. |
| 中文 | 他数点星宿的数目，一一称它的名。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Great is our Lord, and of great power: his understanding is infinite. |
| 中文 | 我们的主为大，最有能力。他的智慧无法测度。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD lifteth up the meek: he casteth the wicked down to the ground. |
| 中文 | 耶和华扶持谦卑人，将恶人倾覆于地。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Sing unto the LORD with thanksgiving; sing praise upon the harp unto our God: |
| 中文 | 你们要以感谢向耶和华歌唱，用琴向我们的神歌颂。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who covereth the heaven with clouds, who prepareth rain for the earth, who maketh grass to grow upon the mountains. |
| 中文 | 他用云遮天，为地降雨，使草生长在山上。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He giveth to the beast his food, and to the young ravens which cry. |
| 中文 | 他赐食给走兽，和啼叫的小乌鸦。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He delighteth not in the strength of the horse: he taketh not pleasure in the legs of a man. |
| 中文 | 他不喜悦马的力大，不喜爱人的腿快。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD taketh pleasure in them that fear him, in those that hope in his mercy. |
| 中文 | 耶和华喜爱敬畏他和盼望他慈爱的人。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Praise the LORD, O Jerusalem; praise thy God, O Zion. |
| 中文 | 耶路撒冷阿，你要颂赞耶和华。锡安哪，你要赞美你的神。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For he hath strengthened the bars of thy gates; he hath blessed thy children within thee. |
| 中文 | 因为他坚固了你的门闩，赐福给你中间的儿女。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He maketh peace in thy borders, and filleth thee with the finest of the wheat. |
| 中文 | 他使你境内平安。用上好的麦子使你满足。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He sendeth forth his commandment upon earth: his word runneth very swiftly. |
| 中文 | 他发命在地。他的话颁行最快。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He giveth snow like wool: he scattereth the hoarfrost like ashes. |
| 中文 | 他降雪如羊毛，撒霜如炉灰。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He casteth forth his ice like morsels: who can stand before his cold? |
| 中文 | 他掷下冰雹如碎渣。他发出寒冷，谁能当得起呢。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He sendeth out his word, and melteth them: he causeth his wind to blow, and the waters flow. |
| 中文 | 他一出令，这些就都消化。他使风刮起，水便流动。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He sheweth his word unto Jacob, his statutes and his judgments unto Israel. |
| 中文 | 他将他的道指示雅各，将他的律例典章指示以色列。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He hath not dealt so with any nation: and as for his judgments, they have not known them. Praise ye the LORD. |
| 中文 | 别国他都没有这样待过。至于他的典章，他们向来没有知道。你们要赞美耶和华。 |
