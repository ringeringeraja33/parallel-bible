# Hebrews 11 / 希伯来书 11

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Now faith is the substance of things hoped for, the evidence of things not seen. |
| 中文 | 信就是所望之事的实底，是未见之事的确据。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For by it the elders obtained a good report. |
| 中文 | 古人在这信上得了美好的证据。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Through faith we understand that the worlds were framed by the word of God, so that things which are seen were not made of things which do appear. |
| 中文 | 我们因着信，就知道诸世界是藉神话造成的。这样，所看见的，并不是从显然之物造出来的。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | By faith Abel offered unto God a more excellent sacrifice than Cain, by which he obtained witness that he was righteous, God testifying of his gifts: and by it he being dead yet speaketh. |
| 中文 | 亚伯因着献祭与神，比该隐所献的更美，因此便得了称义的见证，就是神指他礼物作的见证。他虽然死了，却因这信仍旧说话。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | By faith Enoch was translated that he should not see death; and was not found, because God had translated him: for before his translation he had this testimony, that he pleased God. |
| 中文 | 以诺因着信被接去，不至于见死。人也找不着他，因为神已经把他接去了。只是他被接去以先，已经得了神喜悦他的明证。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But without faith it is impossible to please him: for he that cometh to God must believe that he is, and that he is a rewarder of them that diligently seek him. |
| 中文 | 人非有信就不能得神的喜悦。因为到神面前来的人，必须信有神，且信他赏赐那寻求他的人。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | By faith Noah, being warned of God of things not seen as yet, moved with fear, prepared an ark to the saving of his house; by the which he condemned the world, and became heir of the righteousness which is by faith. |
| 中文 | 挪亚因着信，既蒙神指示他未见的事，动了敬畏的心，豫备了一只方舟，使他全家得救。因此就定了那世代的罪，自己也承受了那从信而来的义。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | By faith Abraham, when he was called to go out into a place which he should after receive for an inheritance, obeyed; and he went out, not knowing whither he went. |
| 中文 | 亚伯拉罕因着信，蒙召的时候，就遵命出去，往将来要得为业的地方去。出去的时候，还不知往那里去。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | By faith he sojourned in the land of promise, as in a strange country, dwelling in tabernacles with Isaac and Jacob, the heirs with him of the same promise: |
| 中文 | 他因着信，就在所应许之地作客，好像在异地居住帐棚，与那同蒙一个应许的以撒，雅各一样。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For he looked for a city which hath foundations, whose builder and maker is God. |
| 中文 | 因为他等候那座有根基的城，就是神所经营所建造的。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Through faith also Sara herself received strength to conceive seed, and was delivered of a child when she was past age, because she judged him faithful who had promised. |
| 中文 | 因着信，连撒拉自己，虽然过了生育的岁数，还能怀孕。因他以为那应许他的是可信的。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 所以从一个彷佛已死的人就生出子孙，如同天上的星那样众多，海边的沙那样无数。 |

## 13
