# Job 15

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then answered Eliphaz the Temanite, and said, |
| 中文 | 毯螅人以利法回答说， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Should a wise man utter vain knowledge, and fill his belly with the east wind? |
| 中文 | 智慧人岂可用虚空的知识回答，用东风充满肚腹呢。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Should he reason with unprofitable talk? or with speeches wherewith he can do no good? |
| 中文 | 他岂可用无益的话，和无济于事的言语，理论呢。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Yea, thou castest off fear, and restrainest prayer before God. |
| 中文 | 你是废弃敬畏的意，在神面前阻止敬虔的心。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thy mouth uttereth thine iniquity, and thou choosest the tongue of the crafty. |
| 中文 | 你的罪孽指教你的口。你选用诡诈人的舌头。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thine own mouth condemneth thee, and not I: yea, thine own lips testify against thee. |
| 中文 | 你自己的口定你有罪，并非是我。你自己的嘴，见证你的不是。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Art thou the first man that was born? or wast thou made before the hills? |
| 中文 | 你岂是头一个被生的人吗。你受造在诸山之先吗。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hast thou heard the secret of God? and dost thou restrain wisdom to thyself? |
| 中文 | 你曾听见神的密旨吗。你还将智慧独自得尽吗。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | What knowest thou, that we know not? what understandest thou, which is not in us? |
| 中文 | 你知道什么是我们不知道的呢。你明白什么是我们不明白的呢。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | With us are both the grayheaded and very aged men, much elder than thy father. |
| 中文 | 我们这里有白发的，和年纪老迈的，比你父亲还老。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Are the consolations of God small with thee? is there any secret thing with thee? |
| 中文 | 神用温和的话安慰你，你以为太小吗。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Why doth thine heart carry thee away? and what do thy eyes wink at, |
| 中文 | 你的心为何将你逼去。你的眼为何冒出火星。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | That thou turnest thy spirit against God, and lettest such words go out of thy mouth? |
| 中文 | 使你的灵反对神，也任你的口发这言语。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | What is man, that he should be clean? and he which is born of a woman, that he should be righteous? |
| 中文 | 人是什么，竟算为洁净呢。妇人所生的是什么，竟算为义呢。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, he putteth no trust in his saints; yea, the heavens are not clean in his sight. |
| 中文 | 神不信靠他的众圣者。在他眼前，天也不洁净。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | How much more abominable and filthy is man, which drinketh iniquity like water? |
| 中文 | 何况那污秽可憎，喝罪孽如水的世人呢。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will shew thee, hear me; and that which I have seen I will declare; |
| 中文 | 我指示你，你要听。我要述说所看见的。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Which wise men have told from their fathers, and have not hid it: |
| 中文 | 就是智慧人从列祖所受，传说而不隐瞒的。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Unto whom alone the earth was given, and no stranger passed among them. |
| 中文 | （这地惟独赐给他们，并没有外人从他们中间经过）。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The wicked man travaileth with pain all his days, and the number of years is hidden to the oppressor. |
| 中文 | 恶人一生之日劬劳痛苦，强暴人一生的年数也是如此。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A dreadful sound is in his ears: in prosperity the destroyer shall come upon him. |
| 中文 | 惊吓的声音常在他耳中。在平安时，抢夺的必临到他那里。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He believeth not that he shall return out of darkness, and he is waited for of the sword. |
| 中文 | 他不信自己能从黑暗中转回，他被刀剑等候。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He wandereth abroad for bread, saying, Where is it? he knoweth that the day of darkness is ready at his hand. |
| 中文 | 他漂流在外求食，说，那里有食物呢。他知道黑暗的日子，在他手边预备好了。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Trouble and anguish shall make him afraid; they shall prevail against him, as a king ready to the battle. |
| 中文 | 急难困苦叫他害怕，而且胜了他，好像君王预备上阵一样。 |

## 25

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For he stretcheth out his hand against God, and strengtheneth himself against the Almighty. |
| 中文 | 他伸手攻击神，以骄傲攻击全能者。 |

## 26

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He runneth upon him, even on his neck, upon the thick bosses of his bucklers: |
| 中文 | 挺着颈项，用盾牌的厚凸面，向全能者直闯。 |

## 27

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Because he covereth his face with his fatness, and maketh collops of fat on his flanks. |
| 中文 | 是因他的脸蒙上脂油，腰积成肥肉。 |

## 28

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And he dwelleth in desolate cities, and in houses which no man inhabiteth, which are ready to become heaps. |
| 中文 | 他曾住在荒凉城邑，无人居住，将成乱堆的房屋。 |

## 29

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He shall not be rich, neither shall his substance continue, neither shall he prolong the perfection thereof upon the earth. |
| 中文 | 他不得富足，财物不得常存，产业在地上也不加增。 |

## 30

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He shall not depart out of darkness; the flame shall dry up his branches, and by the breath of his mouth shall he go away. |
| 中文 | 他不得出离黑暗。火焰要将他的枝子烧乾。因神口中的气，他要灭亡（灭亡原文作走去）。 |

## 31

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let not him that is deceived trust in vanity: for vanity shall be his recompence. |
| 中文 | 他不用倚靠虚假欺哄自己，因虚假必成为他的报应。 |

## 32

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | It shall be accomplished before his time, and his branch shall not be green. |
| 中文 | 他的日期未到之先，这事必成就。他的枝子不得青绿。 |

## 33

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He shall shake off his unripe grape as the vine, and shall cast off his flower as the olive. |
| 中文 | 他必像葡萄树的葡萄，未熟而落。又像橄榄树的花，一开而谢。 |

## 34

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the congregation of hypocrites shall be desolate, and fire shall consume the tabernacles of bribery. |
| 中文 | 原来不敬虔之辈必无生育。受贿赂之人的帐棚必被火烧。 |

## 35

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They conceive mischief, and bring forth vanity, and their belly prepareth deceit. |
| 中文 | 他们所怀的是毒害，所生的是罪孽，心里所豫备的是诡诈。 |
