# 1 Corinthians 9

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Am I not an apostle? am I not free? have I not seen Jesus Christ our Lord? are not ye my work in the Lord? |
| 中文 | 我不是自由的吗。我不是使徒吗。我不是见过我们的主耶稣吗。你们不是我在主里面所作之工吗。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If I be not an apostle unto others, yet doubtless I am to you: for the seal of mine apostleship are ye in the Lord. |
| 中文 | 假若在别人我不是使徒，在你们我总是使徒。因为你们在主里正是我作使徒的印证 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Mine answer to them that do examine me is this, |
| 中文 | 我对那磐问我的人，就是这样分诉。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Have we not power to eat and to drink? |
| 中文 | 难道我们没有权柄靠福音吃喝吗。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Have we not power to lead about a sister, a wife, as well as other apostles, and as the brethren of the Lord, and Cephas? |
| 中文 | 难道我们没有权柄娶信主的姊妹为妻，带着一同往来，彷佛其馀的使徒，和主的弟兄，并矶法一样吗。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Or I only and Barnabas, have not we power to forbear working? |
| 中文 | 独有我与巴拿巴没有权柄不作工吗。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who goeth a warfare any time at his own charges? who planteth a vineyard, and eateth not of the fruit thereof? or who feedeth a flock, and eateth not of the milk of the flock? |
| 中文 | 有谁当兵，自备粮饷呢。有谁栽葡萄园，不吃园里的果子呢。有谁牧养牛羊，不吃牛羊的奶呢。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Say I these things as a man? or saith not the law the same also? |
| 中文 | 我说这话，岂是照人的意见。律法不也是这样说吗。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For it is written in the law of Moses, Thou shalt not muzzle the mouth of the ox that treadeth out the corn. Doth God take care for oxen? |
| 中文 | 就如摩西的律法记着说，牛在场上踹谷的时候，不可笼住它的嘴。难道神所挂念的是牛吗。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Or saith he it altogether for our sakes? For our sakes, no doubt, this is written: that he that ploweth should plow in hope; and that he that thresheth in hope should be partaker of his hope. |
| 中文 | 不全是为我们说的吗。分明是为我们说的。因为耕种的当存着指望去耕种。打场的也当存得粮的指望去打场。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If we have sown unto you spiritual things, is it a great thing if we shall reap your carnal things? |
| 中文 | 我们若把属灵的种子撒在你们中间，就是从你们收割奉养肉身之物，这还算大事吗。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If others be partakers of this power over you, are not we rather? Nevertheless we have not used this power; but suffer all things, lest we should hinder the gospel of Christ. |
| 中文 | 若别人在你们身上有这权柄，何况我们呢。然而我们没有用过这权柄，倒凡事忍受，免得基督的福音被阻隔。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Do ye not know that they which minister about holy things live of the things of the temple? and they which wait at the altar are partakers with the altar? |
| 中文 | 你们岂不知为圣事劳碌的，就吃殿中的物吗。伺候祭坛的，就分领坛上的物吗。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Even so hath the Lord ordained that they which preach the gospel should live of the gospel. |
| 中文 | 主也这样命定，叫传福音的靠福音养生。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But I have used none of these things: neither have I written these things, that it should be so done unto me: for it were better for me to die, than that any man should make my glorying void. |
| 中文 | 但这权柄我全没有用过。我写这话，并非要你们这样待我。因为我宁可死，也不叫人使我所夸的落了空。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For though I preach the gospel, I have nothing to glory of: for necessity is laid upon me; yea, woe is unto me, if I preach not the gospel! |
| 中文 | 我传福音原没有可夸的。因为我是不得已的。若不传福音，我便有祸了。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For if I do this thing willingly, I have a reward: but if against my will, a dispensation of the gospel is committed unto me. |
| 中文 | 我若甘心作这事，就有赏赐。若不甘心，责任却已经托付我了。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | What is my reward then? Verily that, when I preach the gospel, I may make the gospel of Christ without charge, that I abuse not my power in the gospel. |
| 中文 | 既是这样，我的赏赐是什么呢。就是我传福音的时候，叫人不花钱得福音，免得用尽我传福音的权柄。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For though I be free from all men, yet have I made myself servant unto all, that I might gain the more. |
| 中文 | 我虽是自由的，无人辖管，然而我甘心作了众人的仆人，为要多得人。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And unto the Jews I became as a Jew, that I might gain the Jews; to them that are under the law, as under the law, that I might gain them that are under the law; |
| 中文 | 向犹太人，我就作犹太人，为要得犹太人。向律法以下的人，我虽不在律法以下，还是作律法以下的人，为要得律法以下的人。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | To them that are without law, as without law, (being not without law to God, but under the law to Christ,) that I might gain them that are without law. |
| 中文 | 向没有律法的人，我就作没有律法的人，为要得没有律法的人。其实我在神面前，不是没有律法，在基督面前，正在律法之下。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | To the weak became I as weak, that I might gain the weak: I am made all things to all men, that I might by all means save some. |
| 中文 | 向软弱的人，我就作软弱的人，为要得软弱的人。向什么样的人，我就作什么样的人。无论如何，总要救些人。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And this I do for the gospel's sake, that I might be partaker thereof with you. |
| 中文 | 凡我所行的，都是为福音的缘故，为要与人同的这福音的好处。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Know ye not that they which run in a race run all, but one receiveth the prize? So run, that ye may obtain. |
| 中文 | 岂不知在场上赛跑的都跑，但得奖赏的只有一人。你们也当这样跑，好叫你们得着奖赏。 |

## 25

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And every man that striveth for the mastery is temperate in all things. Now they do it to obtain a corruptible crown; but we an incorruptible. |
| 中文 | 凡较力争胜的，诸事都有节制。他们不过是要得能坏的冠冕。我们却是要得不能坏的冠冕。 |

## 26

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I therefore so run, not as uncertainly; so fight I, not as one that beateth the air: |
| 中文 | 所以我奔跑，不像无定向的。我斗拳，不像打空气的。 |

## 27

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But I keep under my body, and bring it into subjection: lest that by any means, when I have preached to others, I myself should be a castaway. |
| 中文 | 我是攻克己身，叫身服我。恐怕我传福音给别人，自己反被弃绝了。 |
