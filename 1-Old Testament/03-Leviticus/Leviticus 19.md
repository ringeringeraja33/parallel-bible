# Leviticus 19 / 利未记 19

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the LORD spake unto Moses, saying, |
| 中文 | 耶和华对摩西说， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Speak unto all the congregation of the children of Israel, and say unto them, Ye shall be holy: for I the LORD your God am holy. |
| 中文 | 你晓谕以色列全会众说，你们要圣洁，因为我耶和华你们的神是圣洁的。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ye shall fear every man his mother, and his father, and keep my sabbaths: I am the LORD your God. |
| 中文 | 你们各人都当孝敬父母，也要守我的安息日。我是耶和华你们的神。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Turn ye not unto idols, nor make to yourselves molten gods: I am the LORD your God. |
| 中文 | 你们不可偏向虚无的神，也不可为自己铸造神像。我是耶和华你们的神。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And if ye offer a sacrifice of peace offerings unto the LORD, ye shall offer it at your own will. |
| 中文 | 你们献平安祭给耶和华的时候，要献得可蒙悦纳。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | It shall be eaten the same day ye offer it, and on the morrow: and if ought remain until the third day, it shall be burnt in the fire. |
| 中文 | 这祭物要在献的那一天和第二天吃，若有剩到第三天的，就必用火焚烧。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And if it be eaten at all on the third day, it is abominable; it shall not be accepted. |
| 中文 | 第三天若再吃，这就为可憎恶的，必不蒙悦纳。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Therefore every one that eateth it shall bear his iniquity, because he hath profaned the hallowed thing of the LORD: and that soul shall be cut off from among his people. |
| 中文 | 凡吃的人必担当他的罪孽，因为他亵渎了耶和华的圣物，那人必从民中剪除。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And when ye reap the harvest of your land, thou shalt not wholly reap the corners of thy field, neither shalt thou gather the gleanings of thy harvest. |
| 中文 | 在你们的地收割庄稼，不可割尽田角，也不可拾取所遗落的。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And thou shalt not glean thy vineyard, neither shalt thou gather every grape of thy vineyard; thou shalt leave them for the poor and stranger: I am the LORD your God. |
| 中文 | 不可摘尽葡萄园的果子，也不可拾取葡萄园所掉的果子，要留给穷人和寄居的。我是耶和华你们的神。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ye shall not steal, neither deal falsely, neither lie one to another. |
| 中文 | 你们不可偷盗，不可欺骗，也不可彼此说谎。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And ye shall not swear by my name falsely, neither shalt thou profane the name of thy God: I am the LORD. |
| 中文 | 不可指着我的名起假誓，亵渎你神的名。我是耶和华。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt not defraud thy neighbour, neither rob him: the wages of him that is hired shall not abide with thee all night until the morning. |
| 中文 | 不可欺压你的邻舍，也不可抢夺他的物。雇工人的工价，不可在你那里过夜，留到早晨。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt not curse the deaf, nor put a stumblingblock before the blind, but shalt fear thy God: I am the LORD. |
| 中文 | 不可咒骂聋子，也不可将绊脚石放在瞎子面前，只要敬畏你的神。我是耶和华。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ye shall do no unrighteousness in judgment: thou shalt not respect the person of the poor, nor honor the person of the mighty: but in righteousness shalt thou judge thy neighbour. |
| 中文 | 你们施行审判，不可行不义，不可偏护穷人，也不可重看有势力的人，只要按着公义审判你的邻舍。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt not go up and down as a talebearer among thy people: neither shalt thou stand against the blood of thy neighbour: I am the LORD. |
| 中文 | 不可在民中往来搬弄是非，也不可与邻舍为敌，置之于死（原文作流他的血）。我是耶和华。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt not hate thy brother in thine heart: thou shalt in any wise rebuke thy neighbour, and not suffer sin upon him. |
| 中文 | 不可心里恨你的弟兄，总要指摘你的邻舍，免得因他担罪。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt not avenge, nor bear any grudge against the children of thy people, but thou shalt love thy neighbour as thyself: I am the LORD. |
| 中文 | 不可报仇，也不可埋怨你本国的子民，却要爱人如己。我是耶和华。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ye shall keep my statutes. Thou shalt not let thy cattle gender with a diverse kind: thou shalt not sow thy field with mingled seed: neither shall a garment mingled of linen and woollen come upon thee. |
| 中文 | 你们要守我的律例。不可叫你的牲畜与异类配合，不可用两样搀杂的种种你的地，也不可用两样搀杂的料作衣服穿在身上。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And whosoever lieth carnally with a woman, that is a bondmaid, betrothed to an husband, and not at all redeemed, nor freedom given her; she shall be scourged; they shall not be put to death, because she was not free. |
| 中文 | 婢女许配了丈夫，还没有被赎得释放，人若与她行淫，二人要受刑罚，却不把他们治死，因为婢女还没有得自由。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And he shall bring his trespass offering unto the LORD, unto the door of the tabernacle of the congregation, even a ram for a trespass offering. |
| 中文 | 那人要把赎愆祭，就是一只公绵羊牵到会幕门口，耶和华面前。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the priest shall make an atonement for him with the ram of the trespass offering before the LORD for his sin which he hath done: and the sin which he hath done shall be forgiven him. |
| 中文 | 祭司要用赎愆祭的羊在耶和华面前赎他所犯的罪，他的罪就必蒙赦免。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And when ye shall come into the land, and shall have planted all manner of trees for food, then ye shall count the fruit thereof as uncircumcised: three years shall it be as uncircumcised unto you: it shall not be eaten of. |
| 中文 | 你们到了迦南地，栽种各样结果子的树木，就要以所结的果子如未受割礼的一样。三年之久，你们要以这些果子，如未受割礼的，是不可吃的。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But in the fourth year all the fruit thereof shall be holy to praise the LORD withal. |
| 中文 | 但第四年所结的果子全要成为圣，用以赞美耶和华。 |

## 25

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And in the fifth year shall ye eat of the fruit thereof, that it may yield unto you the increase thereof: I am the LORD your God. |
| 中文 | 第五年，你们要吃那树上的果子，好叫树给你们结果子更多。我是耶和华你们的神。 |

## 26

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ye shall not eat any thing with the blood: neither shall ye use enchantment, nor observe times. |
| 中文 | 你们不可吃带血的物，不可用法术，也不可观兆。 |

## 27

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ye shall not round the corners of your heads, neither shalt thou mar the corners of thy beard. |
| 中文 | 头的周围（周围或作两鬓）不可剃，胡须的周围也不可损坏。 |

## 28

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ye shall not make any cuttings in your flesh for the dead, nor print any marks upon you: I am the LORD. |
| 中文 | 不可为死人用刀划身，也不可在身上刺花纹。我是耶和华。 |

## 29

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Do not prostitute thy daughter, to cause her to be a whore; lest the land fall to whoredom, and the land become full of wickedness. |
| 中文 | 不可辱没你的女儿，使她为娼妓，恐怕地上的人专向淫乱，地就满了大恶。 |

## 30

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ye shall keep my sabbaths, and reverence my sanctuary: I am the LORD. |
| 中文 | 你们要守我的安息日，敬我的圣所。我是耶和华。 |

## 31

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Regard not them that have familiar spirits, neither seek after wizards, to be defiled by them: I am the LORD your God. |
| 中文 | 不可偏向那些交鬼的和行巫术的，不可求问他们，以致被他们玷污了。我是耶和华你们的神。 |

## 32

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou shalt rise up before the hoary head, and honour the face of the old man, and fear thy God: I am the LORD. |
| 中文 | 在白发的人面前，你要站起来，也要尊敬老人，又要敬畏你的神。我是耶和华。 |

## 33

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And if a stranger sojourn with thee in your land, ye shall not vex him. |
| 中文 | 若有外人在你们国中和你同居，就不可欺负他。 |

## 34

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But the stranger that dwelleth with you shall be unto you as one born among you, and thou shalt love him as thyself; for ye were strangers in the land of Egypt: I am the LORD your God. |
| 中文 | 和你们同居的外人，你们要看他如本地人一样，并要爱他如己，因为你们在埃及地也作过寄居的。我是耶和华你们的神。 |

## 35

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ye shall do no unrighteousness in judgment, in meteyard, in weight, or in measure. |
| 中文 | 你们施行审判，不可行不义，在尺，秤，升，斗上也是如此。 |

## 36

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Just balances, just weights, a just ephah, and a just hin, shall ye have: I am the LORD your God, which brought you out of the land of Egypt. |
| 中文 | 要用公道天平，公道法码，公道升斗，公道秤。我是耶和华你们的神，曾把你们从埃及地领出来的。 |

## 37

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Therefore shall ye observe all my statutes, and all my judgments, and do them: I am the LORD. |
| 中文 | 你们要谨守遵行我一切的律例典章。我是耶和华。 |
