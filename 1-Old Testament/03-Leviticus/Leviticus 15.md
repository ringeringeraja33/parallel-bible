# Leviticus 15 / 利未记 15

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the LORD spake unto Moses and to Aaron, saying, |
| 中文 | 耶和华对摩西，亚伦说， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Speak unto the children of Israel, and say unto them, When any man hath a running issue out of his flesh, because of his issue he is unclean. |
| 中文 | 你们晓谕以色列人说，人若身患漏症，他因这漏症就不洁净了。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And this shall be his uncleanness in his issue: whether his flesh run with his issue, or his flesh be stopped from his issue, it is his uncleanness. |
| 中文 | 他患漏症，无论是下流的，是止住的，都是不洁净。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Every bed, whereon he lieth that hath the issue, is unclean: and every thing, whereon he sitteth, shall be unclean. |
| 中文 | 他所躺的床都为不洁净，所坐的物也为不洁净。 |
