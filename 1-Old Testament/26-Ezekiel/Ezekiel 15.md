# Ezekiel 15

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the word of the LORD came unto me, saying, |
| 中文 | 耶和华的话临到我说， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Son of man, What is the vine tree more than any tree, or than a branch which is among the trees of the forest? |
| 中文 | 人子阿，葡萄树比别样树有什么强处。葡萄枝比众树枝有什么好处。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Shall wood be taken thereof to do any work? or will men take a pin of it to hang any vessel thereon? |
| 中文 | 其上可以取木料作什么工用，可以取来作钉子挂什么器皿吗。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, it is cast into the fire for fuel; the fire devoureth both the ends of it, and the midst of it is burned. Is it meet for any work? |
| 中文 | 看哪，已经抛在火中当作柴烧，火既烧了两头，中间也被烧了，还有益于工用吗。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, when it was whole, it was meet for no work: how much less shall it be meet yet for any work, when the fire hath devoured it, and it is burned? |
| 中文 | 完全的时候尚且不合乎什么工用，何况被火烧坏，还能合乎什么工用吗。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Therefore thus saith the Lord GOD; As the vine tree among the trees of the forest, which I have given to the fire for fuel, so will I give the inhabitants of Jerusalem. |
| 中文 | 所以，主耶和华如此说，众树以内的葡萄树，我怎样使它在火中当柴，也必照样待耶路撒冷的居民。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And I will set my face against them; they shall go out from one fire, and another fire shall devour them; and ye shall know that I am the LORD, when I set my face against them. |
| 中文 | 我必向他们变脸。他们虽从火中出来，火却要烧灭他们。我向他们变脸的时候，你们就知道我是耶和华。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And I will make the land desolate, because they have committed a trespass, saith the Lord GOD. |
| 中文 | 我必使地土荒凉，因为他们行事干犯我。这是主耶和华说的。 |
