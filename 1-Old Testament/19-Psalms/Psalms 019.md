# Psalms 19

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The heavens declare the glory of God; and the firmament sheweth his handywork. |
| 中文 | 诸天述说神的荣耀，穹苍传扬他的手段。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Day unto day uttereth speech, and night unto night sheweth knowledge. |
| 中文 | 这日到那日发出言语。这夜到那夜传出知识。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | There is no speech nor language, where their voice is not heard. |
| 中文 | 无言无语，也无声音可听。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Their line is gone out through all the earth, and their words to the end of the world. In them hath he set a tabernacle for the sun, |
| 中文 | 他的量带通遍天下，他的言语传到地极。神在其间为太阳安设帐幕。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Which is as a bridegroom coming out of his chamber, and rejoiceth as a strong man to run a race. |
| 中文 | 太阳如同新郎出洞房，又如勇士欢然奔路。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | His going forth is from the end of the heaven, and his circuit unto the ends of it: and there is nothing hid from the heat thereof. |
| 中文 | 他从天这边出来，绕到天那边。没有一物被隐藏不得他的热气。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The law of the LORD is perfect, converting the soul: the testimony of the LORD is sure, making wise the simple. |
| 中文 | 耶和华的律法全备，能苏醒人心。耶和华的法度确定，能使愚人有智慧。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The statutes of the LORD are right, rejoicing the heart: the commandment of the LORD is pure, enlightening the eyes. |
| 中文 | 耶和华的训词正直，能快活人心。耶和华的命令清洁，能明亮人的眼目。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The fear of the LORD is clean, enduring for ever: the judgments of the LORD are true and righteous altogether. |
| 中文 | 耶和华的道理洁净，存到永远。耶和华的典章真实，全然公义。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | More to be desired are they than gold, yea, than much fine gold: sweeter also than honey and the honeycomb. |
| 中文 | 都比金子可羡慕，且比极多的精金可羡慕。比蜜甘甜，且比蜂房下滴的蜜甘甜。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Moreover by them is thy servant warned: and in keeping of them there is great reward. |
| 中文 | 况且你的仆人因此受警戒。守着这些便有大赏。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who can understand his errors? cleanse thou me from secret faults. |
| 中文 | 谁能知道自己的错失呢。愿你赦免我隐而未现的过错。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Keep back thy servant also from presumptuous sins; let them not have dominion over me: then shall I be upright, and I shall be innocent from the great transgression. |
| 中文 | 求你拦阻仆人，不犯任意妄为的罪。不容这罪辖制我。我便完全，免犯大罪。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let the words of my mouth, and the meditation of my heart, be acceptable in thy sight, O LORD, my strength, and my redeemer. |
| 中文 | 耶和华我的磐石，我的救赎主阿，愿我口中的言语，心中的意念，在你面前蒙悦纳。 |
