# Psalms 133 / 诗篇 133

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, how good and how pleasant it is for brethren to dwell together in unity! |
| 中文 | （大卫上行之诗）看哪，弟兄和睦同居，是何等地善，何等地美。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | It is like the precious ointment upon the head, that ran down upon the beard, even Aaron's beard: that went down to the skirts of his garments; |
| 中文 | 这好比那贵重的油，浇在亚伦的头上，流到胡须，又流到他的衣襟。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | As the dew of Hermon, and as the dew that descended upon the mountains of Zion: for there the LORD commanded the blessing, even life for evermore. |
| 中文 | 又好比黑门的甘露，降在锡安山。因为在那里有耶和华所命定的福，就是永远的生命。 |
