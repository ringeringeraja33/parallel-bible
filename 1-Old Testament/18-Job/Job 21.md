# Job 21 / 约伯记 21

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But Job answered and said, |
| 中文 | 约伯回答说， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hear diligently my speech, and let this be your consolations. |
| 中文 | 你们要细听我的言语，就算是你们安慰我。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Suffer me that I may speak; and after that I have spoken, mock on. |
| 中文 | 请宽容我，我又要说话。说了以后，任凭你们嗤笑吧。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | As for me, is my complaint to man? and if it were so, why should not my spirit be troubled? |
| 中文 | 我岂是向人诉冤，为何不焦急呢。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Mark me, and be astonished, and lay your hand upon your mouth. |
| 中文 | 你们要看着我而惊奇，用手捂口。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Even when I remember I am afraid, and trembling taketh hold on my flesh. |
| 中文 | 我每逢思想，心就惊惶，浑身战兢。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Wherefore do the wicked live, become old, yea, are mighty in power? |
| 中文 | 恶人为何存活，享大寿数，势力强盛呢。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Their seed is established in their sight with them, and their offspring before their eyes. |
| 中文 | 他们眼见儿孙，和他们一同坚立。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Their houses are safe from fear, neither is the rod of God upon them. |
| 中文 | 他们的家宅平安无惧。神的杖也不加在他们身上。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Their bull gendereth, and faileth not; their cow calveth, and casteth not her calf. |
| 中文 | 他们的公牛孳生而不断绝。母牛下犊而不掉胎。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They send forth their little ones like a flock, and their children dance. |
| 中文 | 他们打发小孩子出去，多如羊群。他们的儿女踊跃跳舞。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They take the timbrel and harp, and rejoice at the sound of the organ. |
| 中文 | 他们随着琴鼓歌唱，又因箫声欢喜。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They spend their days in wealth, and in a moment go down to the grave. |
| 中文 | 他们度日诸事亨通，转眼下入阴间。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Therefore they say unto God, Depart from us; for we desire not the knowledge of thy ways. |
| 中文 | 他们对神说，离开我们吧。我们不愿晓得你的道。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | What is the Almighty, that we should serve him? and what profit should we have, if we pray unto him? |
| 中文 | 全能者是谁，我们何必事奉他呢。求告他有什么益处呢。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Lo, their good is not in their hand: the counsel of the wicked is far from me. |
| 中文 | 看哪，他们亨通不在乎自己。恶人所谋定的离我好远。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | How oft is the candle of the wicked put out! and how oft cometh their destruction upon them! God distributeth sorrows in his anger. |
| 中文 | 恶人的灯何尝熄灭。患难何尝临到他们呢。神何尝发怒，向他们分散灾祸呢。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They are as stubble before the wind, and as chaff that the storm carrieth away. |
| 中文 | 他们何尝像风前的碎秸，如暴风刮去的糠秕呢。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | God layeth up his iniquity for his children: he rewardeth him, and he shall know it. |
| 中文 | 你们说，神为恶人的儿女积蓄罪孽。我说，不如本人受报，好使他亲自知道。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | His eyes shall see his destruction, and he shall drink of the wrath of the Almighty. |
| 中文 | 愿他亲眼看见自己败亡，亲自饮全能者的忿怒。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For what pleasure hath he in his house after him, when the number of his months is cut off in the midst? |
| 中文 | 他的岁月既尽，他还顾他本家吗。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Shall any teach God knowledge? seeing he judgeth those that are high. |
| 中文 | 神既审判那在高位的，谁能将知识教训他呢。 |

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 有人至死身体强壮，尽得平靖安逸。 |

| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 他的奶桶充满，他的骨髓滋润。 |

| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 有人至死心中痛苦，终身未尝福乐的滋味。 |
