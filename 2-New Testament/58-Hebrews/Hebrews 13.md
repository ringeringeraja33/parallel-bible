# Hebrews 13 / 希伯来书 13

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let brotherly love continue. |
| 中文 | 你们务要存弟兄相爱的心。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Be not forgetful to entertain strangers: for thereby some have entertained angels unawares. |
| 中文 | 不可忘记用爱心接待客旅。因为曾有接待客旅的，不知不觉就接待了天使。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Remember them that are in bonds, as bound with them; and them which suffer adversity, as being yourselves also in the body. |
| 中文 | 你们要记念被捆绑的人，好像与他们同受捆绑，也要记念遭苦害的人，想到自己也在肉身之内。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Marriage is honourable in all, and the bed undefiled: but whoremongers and adulterers God will judge. |
| 中文 | 婚姻，人人都当尊重，床也不可污秽。因为苟合行淫的人神必要审判。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let your conversation be without covetousness; and be content with such things as ye have: for he hath said, I will never leave thee, nor forsake thee. |
| 中文 | 你们存心不可贪爱钱财。要以自己所有的为足。因为主曾说，我总不撇下你，也不丢去你。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | So that we may boldly say, The Lord is my helper, and I will not fear what man shall do unto me. |
| 中文 | 所以我们可以放胆说，主是帮助我的，我必不惧怕。人能把我怎吗样呢。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Remember them which have the rule over you, who have spoken unto you the word of God: whose faith follow, considering the end of their conversation. |
| 中文 | 从前引导你们，传神之道给你们的人，你们要想念他们，效法他们的信心，留心看他们为人的结局。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Jesus Christ the same yesterday, and to day, and for ever. |
| 中文 | 耶稣基督，昨日今日一直到永远是一样的。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Be not carried about with divers and strange doctrines. For it is a good thing that the heart be established with grace; not with meats, which have not profited them that have been occupied therein. |
| 中文 | 你们不要被那诸般怪异的教训勾引了去。因为人心靠恩得坚固才是好的。并不是靠饮食。那在饮食上专心的，从来没有得着益处。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | We have an altar, whereof they have no right to eat which serve the tabernacle. |
| 中文 | 我们有一祭坛，上面的祭物是那些在帐幕中供职的人不可同吃的。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the bodies of those beasts, whose blood is brought into the sanctuary by the high priest for sin, are burned without the camp. |
| 中文 | 原来牲畜的血，被大祭司带入圣所作赎罪祭，牲畜的身子，被烧在营外。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Wherefore Jesus also, that he might sanctify the people with his own blood, suffered without the gate. |
| 中文 | 所以耶稣，要用自己的血叫百姓成圣，也就在城门外受苦。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let us go forth therefore unto him without the camp, bearing his reproach. |
| 中文 | 这样，我们也当出到营外就了他去，忍受他所受的凌辱。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For here have we no continuing city, but we seek one to come. |
| 中文 | 我们在这里本没有常存的城，乃是寻求那将来的城。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | By him therefore let us offer the sacrifice of praise to God continually, that is, the fruit of our lips giving thanks to his name. |
| 中文 | 我们应当靠着耶稣，常常以颂赞为祭献给神，这就是那承认主名之人嘴唇的果子。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But to do good and to communicate forget not: for with such sacrifices God is well pleased. |
| 中文 | 只是不可忘记行善，和捐输的事。因为这样的祭，是神所喜悦的。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Obey them that have the rule over you, and submit yourselves: for they watch for your souls, as they that must give account, that they may do it with joy, and not with grief: for that is unprofitable for you. |
| 中文 | 你们要依从那些引导你们的，且要顺服。因他们为你们的灵魂时刻儆醒，好像那将来交账的人。你们要使他们交的时候有快乐，不至忧愁。若忧愁就与你们无益了。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Pray for us: for we trust we have a good conscience, in all things willing to live honestly. |
| 中文 | 请你们为我们祷告。因我们自觉良心无亏，愿意凡事按正道而行。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But I beseech you the rather to do this, that I may be restored to you the sooner. |
| 中文 | 我更求你们为我祷告，使我快些回到你们那里去。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Now the God of peace, that brought again from the dead our Lord Jesus, that great shepherd of the sheep, through the blood of the everlasting covenant, |
| 中文 | 但愿赐平安的神，就是那凭永约之血使群羊的大牧人我主耶稣，从死里复活的神， |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
