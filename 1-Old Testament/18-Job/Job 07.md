# Job 7 / 约伯记 7

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Is there not an appointed time to man upon earth? are not his days also like the days of an hireling? |
| 中文 | 人在世上岂无争战吗。他的日子不像雇工人的日子吗。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | As a servant earnestly desireth the shadow, and as an hireling looketh for the reward of his work: |
| 中文 | 像奴仆切慕黑影，像雇工人盼望工价。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | So am I made to possess months of vanity, and wearisome nights are appointed to me. |
| 中文 | 我也照样经过困苦的日月，夜间的疲乏为我而定。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When I lie down, I say, When shall I arise, and the night be gone? and I am full of tossings to and fro unto the dawning of the day. |
| 中文 | 我躺卧的时候便说，我何时起来，黑夜就过去呢。我尽是反来覆去，直到天亮。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My flesh is clothed with worms and clods of dust; my skin is broken, and become loathsome. |
| 中文 | 我的肉体以虫子和尘土为衣。我的皮肤才收了口又重新破裂。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My days are swifter than a weaver's shuttle, and are spent without hope. |
| 中文 | 我的日子比梭更快，都消耗在无指望之中。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O remember that my life is wind: mine eye shall no more see good. |
| 中文 | 求你想念我，我的生命不过是一口气。我的眼睛必不再见福乐。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The eye of him that hath seen me shall see me no more: thine eyes are upon me, and I am not. |
| 中文 | 观看我的人，他的眼必不再见我。你的眼目要看我，我却不在了。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | As the cloud is consumed and vanisheth away: so he that goeth down to the grave shall come up no more. |
| 中文 | 云彩消散而过。照样，人下阴间也不再上来。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He shall return no more to his house, neither shall his place know him any more. |
| 中文 | 他不再回自己的家，故土也不再认识他。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Therefore I will not refrain my mouth; I will speak in the anguish of my spirit; I will complain in the bitterness of my soul. |
| 中文 | 我不禁止我口。我灵愁苦，要发出言语。我心苦恼，要吐露哀情。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Am I a sea, or a whale, that thou settest a watch over me? |
| 中文 | 我对神说，我岂是洋海，岂是大鱼，你竟防守我呢。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When I say, My bed shall comfort me, my couch shall ease my complaint; |
| 中文 | 若说，我的床必安慰我，我的榻必解释我的苦情。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then thou scarest me with dreams, and terrifiest me through visions: |
| 中文 | 你就用梦惊骇我，用异象恐吓我。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | So that my soul chooseth strangling, and death rather than my life. |
| 中文 | 甚至我宁肯噎死，宁肯死亡，胜似留我这一身的骨头。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I loathe it; I would not live alway: let me alone; for my days are vanity. |
| 中文 | 我厌弃性命，不愿永活。你任凭我吧，因我的日子都是虚空。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | What is man, that thou shouldest magnify him? and that thou shouldest set thine heart upon him? |
| 中文 | 人算什么，你竟看他为大，将他放在心上， |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And that thou shouldest visit him every morning, and try him every moment? |
| 中文 | 每早晨鉴察他，时刻试验他。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | How long wilt thou not depart from me, nor let me alone till I swallow down my spittle? |
| 中文 | 你到何时才转眼不看我，才任凭我咽下唾沫呢。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I have sinned; what shall I do unto thee, O thou preserver of men? why hast thou set me as a mark against thee, so that I am a burden to myself? |
| 中文 | 鉴察人的主阿，我若有罪，于你何妨。为何以我当你的箭靶子，使我厌弃自己的性命。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And why dost thou not pardon my transgression, and take away mine iniquity? for now shall I sleep in the dust; and thou shalt seek me in the morning, but I shall not be. |
| 中文 | 为何不赦免我的过犯，除掉我的罪孽。我现今要躺卧在尘土中。你要殷勤地寻梢我，我却不在了。 |
