# Psalms 75 / 诗篇 75

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Unto thee, O God, do we give thanks, unto thee do we give thanks: for that thy name is near thy wondrous works declare. |
| 中文 | （亚萨的诗歌，交与伶长，调用休要毁坏）神阿，我们称谢你，我们称谢你，因为你的名相近，人都述说你奇妙的作为。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When I shall receive the congregation I will judge uprightly. |
| 中文 | 我到了所定的日期，必按正直施行审判。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The earth and all the inhabitants thereof are dissolved: I bear up the pillars of it. Selah. |
| 中文 | 地和其上的居民，都消化了。我曾立了地的柱子。（细拉） |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I said unto the fools, Deal not foolishly: and to the wicked, Lift not up the horn: |
| 中文 | 我对狂傲人说，不要行事狂傲。对凶恶的人说，不要举角。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Lift not up your horn on high: speak not with a stiff neck. |
| 中文 | 不要把你们的角高举。不要挺着颈项说话。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For promotion cometh neither from the east, nor from the west, nor from the south. |
| 中文 | 因为高举非从东，非从西，也非从南而来。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But God is the judge: he putteth down one, and setteth up another. |
| 中文 | 惟有神断定。他使这人降卑，使那人升高。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For in the hand of the LORD there is a cup, and the wine is red; it is full of mixture; and he poureth out of the same: but the dregs thereof, all the wicked of the earth shall wring them out, and drink them. |
| 中文 | 耶和华手里有杯。其中的酒起沫。杯内满了搀杂的酒。他倒出来。地上的恶人必都喝这酒的渣滓，而且喝尽。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But I will declare for ever; I will sing praises to the God of Jacob. |
| 中文 | 但我要宣扬，直到永远。我要歌颂雅各的神。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | All the horns of the wicked also will I cut off; but the horns of the righteous shall be exalted. |
| 中文 | 恶人一切的角，我要砍断。惟有义人的角，必被高举。 |
