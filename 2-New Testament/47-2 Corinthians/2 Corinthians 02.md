# 2 Corinthians 2 / 哥林多后书 2

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But I determined this with myself, that I would not come again to you in heaviness. |
| 中文 | 我自己定了主意，再到你们那里去，必须大家没有忧愁。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For if I make you sorry, who is he then that maketh me glad, but the same which is made sorry by me? |
| 中文 | 倘若我叫你们忧愁，除了我叫那忧愁的人以外，谁能叫我快乐呢。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And I wrote this same unto you, lest, when I came, I should have sorrow from them of whom I ought to rejoice; having confidence in you all, that my joy is the joy of you all. |
| 中文 | 我曾把这事写给你们，恐怕我到的时候，应该叫我快乐的那些人，反倒叫我忧愁。我也深信，你们众人都以我的快乐为自己的快乐。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For out of much affliction and anguish of heart I wrote unto you with many tears; not that ye should be grieved, but that ye might know the love which I have more abundantly unto you. |
| 中文 | 我先前心里难过痛苦，多多的流泪，写信给你们。不是叫你们忧愁，乃是叫你们知道我格外的疼爱你们。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But if any have caused grief, he hath not grieved me, but in part: that I may not overcharge you all. |
| 中文 | 若有叫人忧愁的，他不但叫我忧愁，也是叫你们众人有几分忧愁，我说几分，恐怕说得太重。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Sufficient to such a man is this punishment, which was inflicted of many. |
| 中文 | 这样的人，受了众人的责罚，也就够了。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 倒不如赦免他，安慰他，免得他忧愁太过，甚至沉沦了。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 所以我劝你们，要向他显出坚定不移的爱心来。 |

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 为此我先前也写信给你们，要试验你们，看你们凡事顺从不顺从。 |

## 10

| 中文 | 你们赦免谁，我也赦免谁，我若有所赦免的，是在基督面前为你们赦免的。 |
