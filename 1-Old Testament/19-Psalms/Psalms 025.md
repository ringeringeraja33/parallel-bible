# Psalms 25

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Unto thee, O LORD, do I lift up my soul. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，我的心仰望你。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O my God, I trust in thee: let me not be ashamed, let not mine enemies triumph over me. |
| 中文 | 我的神阿，我素来倚靠你。求你不要叫我羞愧。不要叫我的仇敌向我夸胜。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Yea, let none that wait on thee be ashamed: let them be ashamed which transgress without cause. |
| 中文 | 凡等候你的必不羞愧。惟有那无故行奸诈的，必要羞愧。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Shew me thy ways, O LORD; teach me thy paths. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你将你的道指示我，将你的路教训我。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Lead me in thy truth, and teach me: for thou art the God of my salvation; on thee do I wait all the day. |
| 中文 | 求你以你的真理引导我，教训我。因为你是救我的神。我终日等候你。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Remember, O LORD, thy tender mercies and thy lovingkindnesses; for they have been ever of old. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你记念你的怜悯和慈爱。因为这是亘古以来所常有的。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Remember not the sins of my youth, nor my transgressions: according to thy mercy remember thou me for thy goodness' sake, O LORD. |
| 中文 | 求你不要记念我幼年的罪愆，和我的过犯。耶和华阿，求你因你的恩惠，按你的慈爱记念我。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Good and upright is the LORD: therefore will he teach sinners in the way. |
| 中文 | 耶和华是良善正直的。所以他必指示罪人走正路。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The meek will he guide in judgment: and the meek will he teach his way. |
| 中文 | 他必按公平引领谦卑人，将他的道教训他们。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | All the paths of the LORD are mercy and truth unto such as keep his covenant and his testimonies. |
| 中文 | 凡遵守他的约和他法度的人，耶和华都以慈爱诚实待他。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thy name's sake, O LORD, pardon mine iniquity; for it is great. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你因你的名赦免我的罪，因为我的罪重大。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | What man is he that feareth the LORD? him shall he teach in the way that he shall choose. |
| 中文 | 谁敬畏耶和华，耶和华必指示他当选择的道路。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | His soul shall dwell at ease; and his seed shall inherit the earth. |
| 中文 | 他必安然居住。他的后裔必承受地土。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The secret of the LORD is with them that fear him; and he will shew them his covenant. |
| 中文 | 耶和华与敬畏他的人亲密。他必将自己的约指示他们。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 我的眼目时常仰望耶和华。因为他必将我的脚从网里拉出来。 |
