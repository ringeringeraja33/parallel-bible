# Psalms 26

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Judge me, O LORD; for I have walked in mine integrity: I have trusted also in the LORD; therefore I shall not slide. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你为我伸冤，因我向来行事纯全，我又倚靠耶和华并不摇动。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Examine me, O LORD, and prove me; try my reins and my heart. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你察看我，试验我，熬炼我的肺腑心肠。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thy lovingkindness is before mine eyes: and I have walked in thy truth. |
| 中文 | 因为你的慈爱常在我眼前。我也按你的真理而行。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I have not sat with vain persons, neither will I go in with dissemblers. |
| 中文 | 我没有和虚谎人同坐。也不与瞒哄人同群。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I have hated the congregation of evil doers; and will not sit with the wicked. |
| 中文 | 我恨恶恶人的会，必不与恶人同坐。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will wash mine hands in innocency: so will I compass thine altar, O LORD: |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，我要洗手表明无辜，才环绕你的祭坛。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | That I may publish with the voice of thanksgiving, and tell of all thy wondrous works. |
| 中文 | 我好发称谢的声音。也要述说你一切奇妙的作为。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | LORD, I have loved the habitation of thy house, and the place where thine honour dwelleth. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，我喜爱你所住的殿，和你显荣耀的居所。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Gather not my soul with sinners, nor my life with bloody men: |
| 中文 | 不要把我的灵魂和罪人一同除掉，不要把我的性命和流人血的一同除掉。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | In whose hands is mischief, and their right hand is full of bribes. |
| 中文 | 他们的手中有奸恶，右手满有贿赂。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But as for me, I will walk in mine integrity: redeem me, and be merciful unto me. |
| 中文 | 至于我，却要行事纯全。求你救赎我，怜恤我。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My foot standeth in an even place: in the congregations will I bless the LORD. |
| 中文 | 我的脚站在平坦地方。在众会中我要称颂耶和华。 |
