# Isaiah 1

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The vision of Isaiah the son of Amoz, which he saw concerning Judah and Jerusalem in the days of Uzziah, Jotham, Ahaz, and Hezekiah, kings of Judah. |
| 中文 | 当乌西雅，约坦，亚哈斯，希西家，作犹大王的时候，亚摩斯的儿子以赛亚得默示，论到犹大和耶路撒冷。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hear, O heavens, and give ear, O earth: for the LORD hath spoken, I have nourished and brought up children, and they have rebelled against me. |
| 中文 | 天哪，要听，地阿，侧耳而听。因为耶和华说，我养育儿女，将他们养大，他们竟悖逆我。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The ox knoweth his owner, and the ass his master's crib: but Israel doth not know, my people doth not consider. |
| 中文 | 牛认识主人，驴认识主人得的槽。以色列却不认识，我的民却不留意。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ah sinful nation, a people laden with iniquity, a seed of evildoers, children that are corrupters: they have forsaken the LORD, they have provoked the Holy One of Israel unto anger, they are gone away backward. |
| 中文 | ？？，犯罪的国民，担着罪孽的百姓，行恶的种类，败坏的儿女。他们离弃耶和华，藐视以色列的圣者，与他生疏，往后退步。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Why should ye be stricken any more? ye will revolt more and more: the whole head is sick, and the whole heart faint. |
| 中文 | 你们为什么屡次悖逆，还要受责打吗。你们已经满头疼痛，全心发昏。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | From the sole of the foot even unto the head there is no soundness in it; but wounds, and bruises, and putrifying sores: they have not been closed, neither bound up, neither mollified with ointment. |
| 中文 | 从脚掌到头顶，没有一处完全的。竟是伤口，青肿，与新打的伤痕。都没有收口，没有缠裹，也没有用膏滋润。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Your country is desolate, your cities are burned with fire: your land, strangers devour it in your presence, and it is desolate, as overthrown by strangers. |
| 中文 | 你们的地土已经荒凉。你们的城邑被火焚毁。你们的田地，在你们的眼前为外邦人所侵吞，既被外邦人倾覆，就成为荒凉。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the daughter of Zion is left as a cottage in a vineyard, as a lodge in a garden of cucumbers, as a besieged city. |
| 中文 | 仅存锡安城（城原文作女子），好像葡萄园的草棚，瓜田的茅屋，被围困的城邑。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Except the LORD of hosts had left unto us a very small remnant, we should have been as Sodom, and we should have been like unto Gomorrah. |
| 中文 | 若不是万军之耶和华给我们稍留馀种，我们早以像所多玛，蛾摩拉的样子了。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hear the word of the LORD, ye rulers of Sodom; give ear unto the law of our God, ye people of Gomorrah. |
| 中文 | 你们这所多玛的官长阿，要听耶和华的话。你们这蛾摩拉的百姓阿，要侧耳听我们的神的训诲。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | To what purpose is the multitude of your sacrifices unto me? saith the LORD: I am full of the burnt offerings of rams, and the fat of fed beasts; and I delight not in the blood of bullocks, or of lambs, or of he goats. |
| 中文 | 耶和华说，你们所献的许多祭物，与我何益呢。公绵羊的燔祭，和肥畜的脂油，我已经彀了。公牛的血，羊羔的血，公山羊的血，我都不喜悦。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When ye come to appear before me, who hath required this at your hand, to tread my courts? |
| 中文 | 你们来朝见我，谁向你们讨这些，使你们践踏我的院宇呢。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Bring no more vain oblations; incense is an abomination unto me; the new moons and sabbaths, the calling of assemblies, I cannot away with; it is iniquity, even the solemn meeting. |
| 中文 | 你们不要再献虚浮的供物。香品是我所憎恶的。月朔，和安息日，并宣召的大会，也是我所憎恶的。作罪孽，又守严肃会，我也不能容忍。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Your new moons and your appointed feasts my soul hateth: they are a trouble unto me; I am weary to bear them. |
| 中文 | 你们的月朔，和节期，我心里恨恶，我都以为麻烦。我担当，便不耐烦。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And when ye spread forth your hands, I will hide mine eyes from you: yea, when ye make many prayers, I will not hear: your hands are full of blood. |
| 中文 | 你们举手祷告，我必遮眼不看。就是你们多多的祈祷，我也不听。你们的手都满了杀人的血。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Wash you, make you clean; put away the evil of your doings from before mine eyes; cease to do evil; |
| 中文 | 你们要洗濯，自洁。从我眼前除掉你们的恶行。要止住作恶， |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Learn to do well; seek judgment, relieve the oppressed, judge the fatherless, plead for the widow. |
| 中文 | 学习行善。寻求公平，解救受欺压的，给孤儿伸冤，为寡妇辨屈。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Come now, and let us reason together, saith the LORD: though your sins be as scarlet, they shall be as white as snow; though they be red like crimson, they shall be as wool. |
| 中文 | 耶和华说，你们来，我们彼此辩论。你们的罪虽像朱红，必变成雪白。虽红如丹颜，必白如羊毛。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If ye be willing and obedient, ye shall eat the good of the land: |
| 中文 | 你们若甘心听从，必吃地上的美物。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But if ye refuse and rebel, ye shall be devoured with the sword: for the mouth of the LORD hath spoken it. |
| 中文 | 若不听从，反倒悖逆，必被刀剑吞灭。这是耶和华亲口说的。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | How is the faithful city become an harlot! it was full of judgment; righteousness lodged in it; but now murderers. |
| 中文 | 可叹忠信的城，变为妓女。从前充满了公平，公义居住在其中，现今却有凶手居住。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thy silver is become dross, thy wine mixed with water: |
| 中文 | 你的银子，变为渣滓，你的酒，用水搀对。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thy princes are rebellious, and companions of thieves: every one loveth gifts, and followeth after rewards: they judge not the fatherless, neither doth the cause of the widow come unto them. |
| 中文 | 你的官长居心悖逆，与盗贼作伴。各都喜爱贿赂，追求赃私。他们不为孤儿伸冤，寡妇的案件，也不得呈到他们面前。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Therefore saith the LORD, the LORD of hosts, the mighty One of Israel, Ah, I will ease me of mine adversaries, and avenge me of mine enemies: |
| 中文 | 因此主万军之耶和华以色列的大能者说，我要向我的对头雪恨，向我的敌人报仇。 |

## 25

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And I will turn my hand upon thee, and purely purge away thy dross, and take away all thy tin: |
| 中文 | 我必反手加在你身上，炼尽你的渣滓，除尽你的杂质。 |

## 26

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And I will restore thy judges as at the first, and thy counsellors as at the beginning: afterward thou shalt be called, The city of righteousness, the faithful city. |
| 中文 | 我也必复还你的审判官，像起初一样，复还你的谋士，像起先一般。然后你必称为公义之城，忠信之邑。 |

## 27

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Zion shall be redeemed with judgment, and her converts with righteousness. |
| 中文 | 锡安必因公平得蒙救赎，其中归正的人，必因公义得蒙救赎。 |

## 28

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the destruction of the transgressors and of the sinners shall be together, and they that forsake the LORD shall be consumed. |
| 中文 | 但悖逆的，和犯罪的，必一同败亡，离弃耶和华的，必致消灭。 |

## 29

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For they shall be ashamed of the oaks which ye have desired, and ye shall be confounded for the gardens that ye have chosen. |
| 中文 | 那等人必因你们所喜爱的橡树抱愧，你们必因所选择的园子蒙羞。 |

## 30

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For ye shall be as an oak whose leaf fadeth, and as a garden that hath no water. |
| 中文 | 因为你们必如叶子枯乾的橡树，好像无水浇灌的园子。 |

## 31

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the strong shall be as tow, and the maker of it as a spark, and they shall both burn together, and none shall quench them. |
| 中文 | 有权势的必如麻瓤，他的工作，好像火星，都要一同焚毁，无人扑灭。 |
