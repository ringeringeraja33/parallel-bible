# Psalms 99

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD reigneth; let the people tremble: he sitteth between the cherubims; let the earth be moved. |
| 中文 | 耶和华作王，万民当战抖。他坐在二基路伯上，地当动摇。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD is great in Zion; and he is high above all the people. |
| 中文 | 耶和华在锡安为大。他超乎万民之上。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let them praise thy great and terrible name; for it is holy. |
| 中文 | 他们当称赞他大而可畏的名。他本为圣。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The king's strength also loveth judgment; thou dost establish equity, thou executest judgment and righteousness in Jacob. |
| 中文 | 王有能力喜爱公平，竖立公正，在雅各中施行公平和公义。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Exalt ye the LORD our God, and worship at his footstool; for he is holy. |
| 中文 | 你们当尊崇耶和华我们的神，在他脚凳前下拜。他本为圣。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Moses and Aaron among his priests, and Samuel among them that call upon his name; they called upon the LORD, and he answered them. |
| 中文 | 在他的祭司中有摩西和亚伦，在求告他名的人中有撒母耳，他们求告耶和华，他就应允他们。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He spake unto them in the cloudy pillar: they kept his testimonies, and the ordinance that he gave them. |
| 中文 | 他在云柱中对他们说话。他们遵守他的法度，和他所赐给他们的律例。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou answeredst them, O LORD our God: thou wast a God that forgavest them, though thou tookest vengeance of their inventions. |
| 中文 | 耶和华我们的神阿，你应允他们。你是赦免他们的神，却按他们所行的报应他们。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Exalt the LORD our God, and worship at his holy hill; for the LORD our God is holy. |
| 中文 | 你们要尊崇耶和华我们的神，在他的圣山下拜。因为耶和华我们的神本为圣。 |
