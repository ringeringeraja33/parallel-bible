# Psalms 3 / 诗篇 3

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Lord, how are they increased that trouble me! many are they that rise up against me. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，我的敌人何其加增。有许多人起来攻击我。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Many there be which say of my soul, There is no help for him in God. Selah. |
| 中文 | 有许多人议论我说，他得不着神的帮助。（细拉） |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But thou, O LORD, art a shield for me; my glory, and the lifter up of mine head. |
| 中文 | 但你耶和华是我四围的盾牌。是我的荣耀，又是叫我抬起头来的。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I cried unto the LORD with my voice, and he heard me out of his holy hill. Selah. |
| 中文 | 我用我的声音求告耶和华，他就从他的圣山上应允我。（细拉） |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I laid me down and slept; I awaked; for the LORD sustained me. |
| 中文 | 我躺下睡觉。我醒着。耶和华都保佑我。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will not be afraid of ten thousands of people, that have set themselves against me round about. |
| 中文 | 虽有成万的百姓来周围攻击我，我也不怕。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Arise, O LORD; save me, O my God: for thou hast smitten all mine enemies upon the cheek bone; thou hast broken the teeth of the ungodly. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你起来。我的神阿，求你救我。因为你打了我一切仇敌的腮骨，敲碎了恶人的牙齿。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Salvation belongeth unto the LORD: thy blessing is upon thy people. Selah. |
| 中文 | 救恩属乎耶和华。愿你赐福给你的百姓。（细拉） |
