# Jeremiah 1

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The words of Jeremiah the son of Hilkiah, of the priests that were in Anathoth in the land of Benjamin: |
| 中文 | 便雅悯地亚拿突城的祭司中，希勒家的儿子耶利米的话记在下面。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | To whom the word of the LORD came in the days of Josiah the son of Amon king of Judah, in the thirteenth year of his reign. |
| 中文 | 犹大王亚们的儿子约西亚在位十三年，耶和华的话临到耶利米。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | It came also in the days of Jehoiakim the son of Josiah king of Judah, unto the end of the eleventh year of Zedekiah the son of Josiah king of Judah, unto the carrying away of Jerusalem captive in the fifth month. |
| 中文 | 从犹大王约西亚的儿子约雅敬在位的时候，直到犹大王约西亚的儿子西底家在位的末年，就是十一年五月间耶路撒冷人被掳的时候，耶和华的话也常临到耶利米。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then the word of the LORD came unto me, saying, |
| 中文 | 耶利米说，耶和华的话临到我说， |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Before I formed thee in the belly I knew thee; and before thou camest forth out of the womb I sanctified thee, and I ordained thee a prophet unto the nations. |
| 中文 | 我未将你造在腹中，我已晓得你。你未出母胎，我已分别你为圣。我已派你作列国的先知。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then said I, Ah, Lord GOD! behold, I cannot speak: for I am a child. |
| 中文 | 我就说，主耶和华阿，我不知怎样说，因为我是年幼的。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But the LORD said unto me, Say not, I am a child: for thou shalt go to all that I shall send thee, and whatsoever I command thee thou shalt speak. |
| 中文 | 耶和华对我说，你不要说我是年幼的，因为我差遣你到谁那里去，你都要去。我吩咐你说什么话，你都要说。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Be not afraid of their faces: for I am with thee to deliver thee, saith the LORD. |
| 中文 | 你不要惧怕他们，因为我与你同在，要拯救你。这是耶和华说的。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then the LORD put forth his hand, and touched my mouth. And the LORD said unto me, Behold, I have put my words in thy mouth. |
| 中文 | 于是耶和华伸手按我的口，对我说，我已将当说的话传给你。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | See, I have this day set thee over the nations and over the kingdoms, to root out, and to pull down, and to destroy, and to throw down, to build, and to plant. |
| 中文 | 看哪，我今日立你在列邦列国之上，为要施行拔出，拆毁，毁坏，倾覆，又要建立，栽植。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Moreover the word of the LORD came unto me, saying, Jeremiah, what seest thou? And I said, I see a rod of an almond tree. |
| 中文 | 耶和华的话又临到我说，耶利米，你看见什么。我说，我看见一根杏树枝。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then said the LORD unto me, Thou hast well seen: for I will hasten my word to perform it. |
| 中文 | 耶和华对我说，你看得不错。因为我留意保守我的话，使得成就。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the word of the LORD came unto me the second time, saying, What seest thou? And I said, I see a seething pot; and the face thereof is toward the north. |
| 中文 | 耶和华的话第二次临到我说，你看见什么。我说，我看见一个烧开的锅，从北而倾。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then the LORD said unto me, Out of the north an evil shall break forth upon all the inhabitants of the land. |
| 中文 | 耶和华对我说，必有灾祸从北方发出，临到这地的一切居民。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For, lo, I will call all the families of the kingdoms of the north, saith the LORD; and they shall come, and they shall set every one his throne at the entering of the gates of Jerusalem, and against all the walls thereof round about, and against all the cities of Judah. |
| 中文 | 耶和华说，看哪，我要召北方列国的众族。他们要来，各安座位在耶路撒冷的城门口，周围攻击城墙，又要攻击犹大的一切城邑。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And I will utter my judgments against them touching all their wickedness, who have forsaken me, and have burned incense unto other gods, and worshipped the works of their own hands. |
| 中文 | 至于这民的一切恶，就是离弃我，向别神烧香，跪拜自己手所造的，我要发出我的判语，攻击他们。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou therefore gird up thy loins, and arise, and speak unto them all that I command thee: be not dismayed at their faces, lest I confound thee before them. |
| 中文 | 所以你当束腰，起来将我所吩咐你的一切话告诉他们。不要因他们惊惶，免得我使你在他们面前惊惶。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For, behold, I have made thee this day a defenced city, and an iron pillar, and brasen walls against the whole land, against the kings of Judah, against the princes thereof, against the priests thereof, and against the people of the land. |
| 中文 | 看哪，我今日使你成为坚城，铁柱，铜墙，与全地和犹大的君王，首领，祭司，并地上的众民反对。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And they shall fight against thee; but they shall not prevail against thee; for I am with thee, saith the LORD, to deliver thee. |
| 中文 | 他们要攻击你，却不能胜你。因为我与你同在，要拯救你。这是耶和华说的。 |
