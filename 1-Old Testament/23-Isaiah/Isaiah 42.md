# Isaiah 42

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold my servant, whom I uphold; mine elect, in whom my soul delighteth; I have put my spirit upon him: he shall bring forth judgment to the Gentiles. |
| 中文 | 看哪，我的仆人，我所扶持，所拣选，心里所喜悦的，我已将我的灵赐给他，他必将公理传给外邦。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He shall not cry, nor lift up, nor cause his voice to be heard in the street. |
| 中文 | 他不喧囔，不扬声，也不使街上听见他的声音。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A bruised reed shall he not break, and the smoking flax shall he not quench: he shall bring forth judgment unto truth. |
| 中文 | 压伤的芦苇，他不折断。将残的灯火，他不吹灭。他凭真实将公理传开。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He shall not fail nor be discouraged, till he have set judgment in the earth: and the isles shall wait for his law. |
| 中文 | 他不灰心，也不丧胆，直到他在地上设立公理。海岛都等候他的训诲。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thus saith God the LORD, he that created the heavens, and stretched them out; he that spread forth the earth, and that which cometh out of it; he that giveth breath unto the people upon it, and spirit to them that walk therein: |
| 中文 | 创造诸天，铺张穹苍，将地和地所出的一并铺开，赐气息给地上的众人，又赐灵性给行在其上之人的神之和华，他如此说， |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I the LORD have called thee in righteousness, and will hold thine hand, and will keep thee, and give thee for a covenant of the people, for a light of the Gentiles; |
| 中文 | 我耶和华凭公义召你，必搀扶你的手，保守你，使你作众民的中保，（中保原文作约）作外邦人的光， |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | To open the blind eyes, to bring out the prisoners from the prison, and them that sit in darkness out of the prison house. |
| 中文 | 开瞎子的眼，领被囚的出牢狱，领坐黑暗的出监牢。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I am the LORD: that is my name: and my glory will I not give to another, neither my praise to graven images. |
| 中文 | 我是耶和华，这是我的名。我必不将我的荣耀归给假神，也不将我的称赞归给雕刻的偶像。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, the former things are come to pass, and new things do I declare: before they spring forth I tell you of them. |
| 中文 | 看哪，先前的事已经成就，现在我将新事说明，这事未发以前，我就说给你们听。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Sing unto the LORD a new song, and his praise from the end of the earth, ye that go down to the sea, and all that is therein; the isles, and the inhabitants thereof. |
| 中文 | 航海的，和海中所有的，海岛，和其上的居民，都当向耶和华唱新歌，从地极赞美他。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let the wilderness and the cities thereof lift up their voice, the villages that Kedar doth inhabit: let the inhabitants of the rock sing, let them shout from the top of the mountains. |
| 中文 | 旷野和其中的城邑，并基达人居住的村庄，都当扬声。西拉的居民当欢呼，在山顶上呐喊。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let them give glory unto the LORD, and declare his praise in the islands. |
| 中文 | 他们当将荣耀归给耶和华，在海岛中传扬他的颂赞。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD shall go forth as a mighty man, he shall stir up jealousy like a man of war: he shall cry, yea, roar; he shall prevail against his enemies. |
| 中文 | 耶和华必像勇士出去，必像战士激动热心，要喊叫，大声呐喊，要用大力攻击仇敌。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I have long time holden my peace; I have been still, and refrained myself: now will I cry like a travailing woman; I will destroy and devour at once. |
| 中文 | 我许久闭口不言，静默不语，现在我要喊叫像产难的妇人，我要急气而喘哮。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will make waste mountains and hills, and dry up all their herbs; and I will make the rivers islands, and I will dry up the pools. |
| 中文 | 我要使大山小冈变为荒场，使其上的花草都枯乾，我要使江河变为洲岛，使水池都乾涸。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And I will bring the blind by a way that they knew not; I will lead them in paths that they have not known: I will make darkness light before them, and crooked things straight. These things will I do unto them, and not forsake them. |
| 中文 | 我要引瞎子行不认识的道，领他们走不知道的路。在他们面前使黑暗变为光明，使弯曲变为平直。这些事我都要行，并不离弃他们。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They shall be turned back, they shall be greatly ashamed, that trust in graven images, that say to the molten images, Ye are our gods. |
| 中文 | 倚靠雕刻的偶像，对铸造的偶像说，你是我们的神，这等人要退后，全然蒙羞。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hear, ye deaf; and look, ye blind, that ye may see. |
| 中文 | 你们这耳聋的听吧。你们这眼瞎的看吧。使你们能看见。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who is blind, but my servant? or deaf, as my messenger that I sent? who is blind as he that is perfect, and blind as the LORD's servant? |
| 中文 | 谁比我的仆人眼瞎呢，谁比我差遣的使者耳聋呢，谁瞎眼像那与我和好的，谁瞎眼像耶和华的仆人呢。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Seeing many things, but thou observest not; opening the ears, but he heareth not. |
| 中文 | 你看见许多事却不领会。耳朵开通却不听见。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD is well pleased for his righteousness' sake; he will magnify the law, and make it honourable. |
| 中文 | 耶和华因自己公义的缘故，喜欢使律法（或作训诲）为大，为尊。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But this is a people robbed and spoiled; they are all of them snared in holes, and they are hid in prison houses: they are for a prey, and none delivereth; for a spoil, and none saith, Restore. |
| 中文 | 但这百姓是被抢被夺的，都牢笼在坑中，隐藏在狱里，他们作掠物，无人拯救，作掳物，无人说交还。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who among you will give ear to this? who will hearken and hear for the time to come? |
| 中文 | 你们中间谁肯侧耳听此，谁肯留心而听，以防将来呢。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who gave Jacob for a spoil, and Israel to the robbers? did not the LORD, he against whom we have sinned? for they would not walk in his ways, neither were they obedient unto his law. |
| 中文 | 谁将雅各交出当作掳物，将以色列交给抢夺的呢，岂不是耶和华吗，就是我们所得罪的那位。他们不肯遵行他的道，也不听从他的训诲。 |

## 25

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Therefore he hath poured upon him the fury of his anger, and the strength of battle: and it hath set him on fire round about, yet he knew not; and it burned him, yet he laid it not to heart. |
| 中文 | 所以他将猛烈的怒气，和争战的勇力，倾倒在以色列的身上。在他四围如火着起，他还不知道，烧着他，他也不介意。 |
