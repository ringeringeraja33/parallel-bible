# Psalms 130 / 诗篇 130

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Out of the depths have I cried unto thee, O LORD. |
| 中文 | （上行之诗）耶和华阿，我从深处向你求告。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Lord, hear my voice: let thine ears be attentive to the voice of my supplications. |
| 中文 | 主阿，求你听我的声音。愿你侧耳听我恳求的声音。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If thou, LORD, shouldest mark iniquities, O Lord, who shall stand? |
| 中文 | 主耶和华阿，你若究察罪孽，谁能站得住呢。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But there is forgiveness with thee, that thou mayest be feared. |
| 中文 | 但在你有赦免之恩，要叫人敬畏你。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I wait for the LORD, my soul doth wait, and in his word do I hope. |
| 中文 | 我等候耶和华，我的心等候。我也仰望他的话。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My soul waiteth for the Lord more than they that watch for the morning: I say, more than they that watch for the morning. |
| 中文 | 我的心等候主，胜于守夜的，等候天亮，胜于守夜的，等候天亮。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let Israel hope in the LORD: for with the LORD there is mercy, and with him is plenteous redemption. |
| 中文 | 以色列阿，你当仰望耶和华。因他有慈爱，有丰盛的救恩。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And he shall redeem Israel from all his iniquities. |
| 中文 | 他必救赎以色列脱离一切的罪孽。 |
