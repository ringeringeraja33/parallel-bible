# Psalms 61

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hear my cry, O God; attend unto my prayer. |
| 中文 | （大卫的诗，交与伶长，用丝弦的乐器）神阿，求你听我的呼求，侧耳听我的祷告。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | From the end of the earth will I cry unto thee, when my heart is overwhelmed: lead me to the rock that is higher than I. |
| 中文 | 我心里发昏的时候，我要从地极求告你。求你领我到那比我更高的磐石。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thou hast been a shelter for me, and a strong tower from the enemy. |
| 中文 | 因为你作过我的避难所，作过我的坚固台，脱离仇敌。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will abide in thy tabernacle for ever: I will trust in the covert of thy wings. Selah. |
| 中文 | 我要永远住在你的帐幕里。我要投靠在你翅膀下的隐密处。（细拉） |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thou, O God, hast heard my vows: thou hast given me the heritage of those that fear thy name. |
| 中文 | 神阿，你原是听了我所许的愿。你将产业赐给敬畏你名的人。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou wilt prolong the king's life: and his years as many generations. |
| 中文 | 你要加添王的寿数。他的年岁必存到世世。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He shall abide before God for ever: O prepare mercy and truth, which may preserve him. |
| 中文 | 他必永远坐在神面前。愿你豫备慈爱和诚实保佑他。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | So will I sing praise unto thy name for ever, that I may daily perform my vows. |
| 中文 | 这样，我要歌颂你的名直到永远，好天天还我所许的愿。 |
