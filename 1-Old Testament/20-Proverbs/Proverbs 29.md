# Proverbs 29

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He, that being often reproved hardeneth his neck, shall suddenly be destroyed, and that without remedy. |
| 中文 | 人屡次受责罚，仍然硬着颈项，他必顷刻败坏，无法可治。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When the righteous are in authority, the people rejoice: but when the wicked beareth rule, the people mourn. |
| 中文 | 义人增多，民就喜乐。恶人掌权，民就叹息。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Whoso loveth wisdom rejoiceth his father: but he that keepeth company with harlots spendeth his substance. |
| 中文 | 爱慕智慧的，使父亲喜乐。与妓女结交的，却浪费钱财。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The king by judgment establisheth the land: but he that receiveth gifts overthroweth it. |
| 中文 | 王藉公平，使国坚定。索要贿赂，使国倾败。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A man that flattereth his neighbour spreadeth a net for his feet. |
| 中文 | 谄媚邻舍的，就是设网罗绊他的脚。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | In the transgression of an evil man there is a snare: but the righteous doth sing and rejoice. |
| 中文 | 恶人犯罪，自陷网罗。惟独义人，欢呼喜乐。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The righteous considereth the cause of the poor: but the wicked regardeth not to know it. |
| 中文 | 义人知道查明穷人的案。恶人没有聪明，就不得而知。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Scornful men bring a city into a snare: but wise men turn away wrath. |
| 中文 | 亵慢人煽惑通城。智慧人止息众怒。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If a wise man contendeth with a foolish man, whether he rage or laugh, there is no rest. |
| 中文 | 智慧人与愚妄人相争，或怒，或笑，总不能使他止息。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The bloodthirsty hate the upright: but the just seek his soul. |
| 中文 | 好流人血的，恨恶完全人。索取正直人的性命。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A fool uttereth all his mind: but a wise man keepeth it in till afterwards. |
| 中文 | 愚妄人怒气全发。智慧人忍气含怒。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If a ruler hearken to lies, all his servants are wicked. |
| 中文 | 君王若听谎言，他一切臣仆都是奸恶。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The poor and the deceitful man meet together: the LORD lighteneth both their eyes. |
| 中文 | 贫穷人，强暴人，在世相遇，他们的眼目，都蒙耶和华光照。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The king that faithfully judgeth the poor, his throne shall be established for ever. |
| 中文 | 君王凭诚实判断穷人，他的国位，必永远坚立。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The rod and reproof give wisdom: but a child left to himself bringeth his mother to shame. |
| 中文 | 杖打和责备，能加增智慧。放纵的儿子，使母亲羞愧。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When the wicked are multiplied, transgression increaseth: but the righteous shall see their fall. |
| 中文 | 恶人加多，过犯也加多。义人必看见他们跌倒。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Correct thy son, and he shall give thee rest; yea, he shall give delight unto thy soul. |
| 中文 | 管教你的儿子，他就使你得安息，也必使你心里喜乐。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Where there is no vision, the people perish: but he that keepeth the law, happy is he. |
| 中文 | 没有异象，（或作默示）民就放肆。惟遵守律法的，便为有福。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A servant will not be corrected by words: for though he understand he will not answer. |
| 中文 | 只用言语，仆人不肯受管教，他虽然明白，也不留意。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Seest thou a man that is hasty in his words? there is more hope of a fool than of him. |
| 中文 | 你见言语急躁的人吗。愚昧人比他更有指望。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that delicately bringeth up his servant from a child shall have him become his son at the length. |
| 中文 | 人将仆人从小娇养，这仆人终久必成了他的儿子。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | An angry man stirreth up strife, and a furious man aboundeth in transgression. |
| 中文 | 好气的人，挑启争端。暴怒的人，多多犯罪。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A man's pride shall bring him low: but honour shall uphold the humble in spirit. |
| 中文 | 人的高傲，必使他卑下。心里谦逊的，必得尊荣。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Whoso is partner with a thief hateth his own soul: he heareth cursing, and bewrayeth it not. |
| 中文 | 人与盗贼分赃，是恨恶自己的性命。他听见叫人发誓的声音，却不言语。 |

## 25

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The fear of man bringeth a snare: but whoso putteth his trust in the LORD shall be safe. |
| 中文 | 惧怕人的陷入网罗。惟有倚靠耶和华的，必得安稳。 |

## 26

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Many seek the ruler's favour; but every man's judgment cometh from the LORD. |
| 中文 | 求王恩的人多。定人事乃在耶和华。 |

## 27

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | An unjust man is an abomination to the just: and he that is upright in the way is abomination to the wicked. |
| 中文 | 为非作歹的，被义人憎嫌。行事正直的，被恶人憎恶。 |
