# Leviticus 7

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Likewise this is the law of the trespass offering: it is most holy. |
| 中文 | 赎愆祭的条例乃是如此，这祭是至圣的。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | In the place where they kill the burnt offering shall they kill the trespass offering: and the blood thereof shall he sprinkle round about upon the altar. |
| 中文 | 人在那里宰燔祭牲，也要在那里宰赎愆祭牲，其血，祭司要洒在坛的周围。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And he shall offer of it all the fat thereof; the rump, and the fat that covereth the inwards, |
| 中文 | 又要将肥尾巴和盖脏的脂油， |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the two kidneys, and the fat that is on them, which is by the flanks, and the caul that is above the liver, with the kidneys, it shall he take away: |
| 中文 | 两个腰子和腰子上的脂油，就是靠腰两旁的脂油，并肝上的网子和腰子，一概取下。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the priest shall burn them upon the altar for an offering made by fire unto the LORD: it is a trespass offering. |
| 中文 | 祭司要在坛上焚烧，为献给耶和华的火祭，是赎愆祭。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Every male among the priests shall eat thereof: it shall be eaten in the holy place: it is most holy. |
| 中文 | 祭司中的男丁都可以吃这祭物，要在圣处吃，是至圣的。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | As the sin offering is, so is the trespass offering: there is one law for them: the priest that maketh atonement therewith shall have it. |
| 中文 | 赎罪祭怎样，赎愆祭也是怎样，两个祭是一个条例。献赎愆祭赎罪的祭司要得这祭物。 |

## 8

| --- | --- |
| English | And the priest that offereth any man's burnt offering, even the priest shall have to himself the skin of the burnt offering which he hath offered. |
| 中文 | 献燔祭的祭司，无论为谁奉献，要亲自得他所献那燔祭牲的皮。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And all the meat offering that is baken in the oven, and all that is dressed in the fryingpan, and in the pan, shall be the priest's that offereth it. |
| 中文 | 凡在炉中烤的素祭和煎盘中作的，并铁鏊上作的，都要归那献祭的祭司。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 凡素祭，无论是油调和的是乾的，都要归亚伦的子孙，大家均分。 |

| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 第三天若吃了平安祭的肉，这祭必不蒙悦纳，人所献的也不算为祭，反为可憎嫌的，吃这祭肉的，就必担当他的罪孽。 |
