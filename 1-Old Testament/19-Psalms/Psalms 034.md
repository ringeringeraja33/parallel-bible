# Psalms 34

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will bless the LORD at all times: his praise shall continually be in my mouth. |
| 中文 | 我要时时称颂耶和华。赞美他的话必常在我口中。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My soul shall make her boast in the LORD: the humble shall hear thereof, and be glad. |
| 中文 | 我的心必因耶和华夸耀。谦卑人听见，就要喜乐。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O magnify the LORD with me, and let us exalt his name together. |
| 中文 | 你们和我当称耶和华为大，一同高举他的名。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I sought the LORD, and he heard me, and delivered me from all my fears. |
| 中文 | 我曾寻求耶和华，他就应允我，救我脱离了一切的恐惧。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They looked unto him, and were lightened: and their faces were not ashamed. |
| 中文 | 凡仰望他的，便有光荣。他们的脸，必不蒙羞。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | This poor man cried, and the LORD heard him, and saved him out of all his troubles. |
| 中文 | 我这困苦人呼求，耶和华便垂听，救我脱离一切患难。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The angel of the LORD encampeth round about them that fear him, and delivereth them. |
| 中文 | 耶和华的使者，在敬畏他的人四围安营，搭救他们。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O taste and see that the LORD is good: blessed is the man that trusteth in him. |
| 中文 | 你们要尝尝主恩的滋味，便知道他是美善。投靠他的人有福了。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O fear the LORD, ye his saints: for there is no want to them that fear him. |
| 中文 | 耶和华的圣民哪，你们当敬畏他，因敬畏他的一无所缺。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The young lions do lack, and suffer hunger: but they that seek the LORD shall not want any good thing. |
| 中文 | 少壮狮子，还缺食忍饿。但寻求耶和华的。什么好处都不缺。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Come, ye children, hearken unto me: I will teach you the fear of the LORD. |
| 中文 | 众弟子阿，你们当来听我的话。我要将敬畏耶和华的道教训你们。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | What man is he that desireth life, and loveth many days, that he may see good? |
| 中文 | 有何人喜好存活，爱慕长寿，得享美福。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Keep thy tongue from evil, and thy lips from speaking guile. |
| 中文 | 就要禁止舌头不出恶言，嘴唇不说诡诈的话。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Depart from evil, and do good; seek peace, and pursue it. |
| 中文 | 要离恶行善，寻求和睦，一心追赶。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The eyes of the LORD are upon the righteous, and his ears are open unto their cry. |
| 中文 | 耶和华的眼目，看顾义人，他的耳朵，听他们的呼求。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The face of the LORD is against them that do evil, to cut off the remembrance of them from the earth. |
| 中文 | 耶和华向行恶的人变脸，要从世上除灭他们的名号。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The righteous cry, and the LORD heareth, and delivereth them out of all their troubles. |
| 中文 | 义人呼求，耶和华听见了，便救他们脱离一切患难。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD is nigh unto them that are of a broken heart; and saveth such as be of a contrite spirit. |
| 中文 | 耶和华靠近伤心的人，拯救灵性痛悔的人。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Many are the afflictions of the righteous: but the LORD delivereth him out of them all. |
| 中文 | 义人多有苦难。但耶和华救他脱离这一切。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He keepeth all his bones: not one of them is broken. |
| 中文 | 又要保全他一身的骨头，连一根也不折断。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Evil shall slay the wicked: and they that hate the righteous shall be desolate. |
| 中文 | 恶必害死恶人。恨恶义人的，必被定罪。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD redeemeth the soul of his servants: and none of them that trust in him shall be desolate. |
| 中文 | 耶和华救赎他仆人的灵魂。凡投靠他的，必不至定罪。 |
