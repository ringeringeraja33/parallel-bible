# Ephesians 5 / 以弗所书 5

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Be ye therefore followers of God, as dear children; |
| 中文 | 所以你们该效法神，好像蒙慈爱的儿女一样。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And walk in love, as Christ also hath loved us, and hath given himself for us an offering and a sacrifice to God for a sweetsmelling savour. |
| 中文 | 也要凭爱心行事，正如基督爱我们，为我们舍了自己，当作馨香的供物，和祭物，献与神。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But fornication, and all uncleanness, or covetousness, let it not be once named among you, as becometh saints; |
| 中文 | 至于淫乱，并一切污秽，或是贪婪，在你们中间连题都不可，方合圣徒的体统。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Neither filthiness, nor foolish talking, nor jesting, which are not convenient: but rather giving of thanks. |
| 中文 | 淫词，妄语，和戏笑的话，都不相宜，总要说感谢的话。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For this ye know, that no whoremonger, nor unclean person, nor covetous man, who is an idolater, hath any inheritance in the kingdom of Christ and of God. |
| 中文 | 因为你们确实的知道，无论是淫乱的，是污秽的，是有贪心的，在基督和神的国里，都是无分的。有贪心的，就与拜偶像的一样。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let no man deceive you with vain words: for because of these things cometh the wrath of God upon the children of disobedience. |
| 中文 | 不要被人虚浮的话欺哄。因这些事，神的忿怒必临到那悖逆之子。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Be not ye therefore partakers with them. |
| 中文 | 所以你们不要与他们同夥。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For ye were sometimes darkness, but now are ye light in the Lord: walk as children of light: |
| 中文 | 从前你们是暗昧的，但如今在主里面是光明的，行事为人就当像光明的子女。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | (For the fruit of the Spirit is in all goodness and righteousness and truth;) |
| 中文 | 光明所结的果子，就是一切良善，公义，诚实。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Proving what is acceptable unto the Lord. |
| 中文 | 总要察验何为主所喜悦的事。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And have no fellowship with the unfruitful works of darkness, but rather reprove them. |
| 中文 | 那暗昧无益的事，不要与人同行，倒要责备行这事的人 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For it is a shame even to speak of those things which are done of them in secret. |
| 中文 | 因为他们暗中所行的，就是题起来，也是可耻的。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But all things that are reproved are made manifest by the light: for whatsoever doth make manifest is light. |
| 中文 | 凡事受了责备，就被光显明出来。因为一切能显明的，就是光。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Wherefore he saith, Awake thou that sleepest, and arise from the dead, and Christ shall give thee light. |
| 中文 | 所以主说，你这睡着的人，当醒过来，从死里复活，基督就要光照你了。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | See then that ye walk circumspectly, not as fools, but as wise, |
| 中文 | 你们要谨慎行事，不要像愚昧人，当像智慧人。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Redeeming the time, because the days are evil. |
| 中文 | 要爱惜光阴，因为现今的世代邪恶。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Wherefore be ye not unwise, but understanding what the will of the Lord is. |
| 中文 | 不要作糊涂人，要明白主的旨意如何。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And be not drunk with wine, wherein is excess; but be filled with the Spirit; |
| 中文 | 不要醉酒，酒能使人放荡，乃要被圣灵充满。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Speaking to yourselves in psalms and hymns and spiritual songs, singing and making melody in your heart to the Lord; |
| 中文 | 当用诗章，颂词，灵歌，彼此对说，口唱心和的赞美主。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Giving thanks always for all things unto God and the Father in the name of our Lord Jesus Christ; |
| 中文 | 凡事要奉我们主耶稣基督的名，常常感谢父神。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Submitting yourselves one to another in the fear of God. |
| 中文 | 又当存敬畏基督的心，彼此顺服。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Wives, submit yourselves unto your own husbands, as unto the Lord. |
| 中文 | 你们作妻子的，当顺服自己的丈夫，如同顺服主。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the husband is the head of the wife, even as Christ is the head of the church: and he is the saviour of the body. |
| 中文 | 因为丈夫是妻子的头，如同基督是教会的头。他又是教会全体的救主。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Therefore as the church is subject unto Christ, so let the wives be to their own husbands in every thing. |
| 中文 | 教会怎样顺服基督，妻子也要怎样凡事顺服丈夫。 |

## 25

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Husbands, love your wives, even as Christ also loved the church, and gave himself for it; |
| 中文 | 你们作丈夫的，要爱你们的妻子，正如基督爱教会，为教会舍己。 |

## 26

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | That he might sanctify and cleanse it with the washing of water by the word, |
| 中文 | 要用水藉着道，把教会洗净，成为圣洁， |

## 27

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | That he might present it to himself a glorious church, not having spot, or wrinkle, or any such thing; but that it should be holy and without blemish. |
| 中文 | 可以献给自己，作个荣耀的教会，毫无玷污皱纹等类的病，乃是圣洁没有瑕疵的。 |

## 28

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | So ought men to love their wives as their own bodies. He that loveth his wife loveth himself. |
| 中文 | 丈夫也当照样爱妻子，如同爱自己的身子。爱妻子，便是爱自己了。 |

## 29

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For no man ever yet hated his own flesh; but nourisheth and cherisheth it, even as the Lord the church: |
| 中文 | 从来没有人恨恶自己的身子，总是保养顾惜，正像基督待教会一样。 |

## 30

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For we are members of his body, of his flesh, and of his bones. |
| 中文 | 因我们是他身上的肢体。（有古卷在此有就是他的骨他的肉）。 |

## 31

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For this cause shall a man leave his father and mother, and shall be joined unto his wife, and they two shall be one flesh. |
| 中文 | 为这个缘故，人要离开父母，与妻子连合，二人成为一体。 |

## 32

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | This is a great mystery: but I speak concerning Christ and the church. |
| 中文 | 这是极大的奥秘，但我是指着基督和教会说的。 |

## 33

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Nevertheless let every one of you in particular so love his wife even as himself; and the wife see that she reverence her husband. |
| 中文 | 然而你们各人都当爱妻子，如同爱自己一样。妻子也当敬重他的丈夫。 |
