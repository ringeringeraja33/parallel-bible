# Song of Songs 1

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The song of songs, which is Solomon's. |
| 中文 | 所罗门的歌，是歌中的雅歌。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let him kiss me with the kisses of his mouth: for thy love is better than wine. |
| 中文 | 愿他用口与我亲嘴。因你的爱情比酒更美。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Because of the savour of thy good ointments thy name is as ointment poured forth, therefore do the virgins love thee. |
| 中文 | 你的膏油馨香。你的名如同倒出来的香膏，所以众童女都爱你。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Draw me, we will run after thee: the king hath brought me into his chambers: we will be glad and rejoice in thee, we will remember thy love more than wine: the upright love thee. |
| 中文 | 愿你吸引我，我们就快跑跟随你。王带我进了内室，我们必因你欢喜快乐。我们要称赞你的爱情，胜似称赞美酒。他们爱你是理所当然的。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I am black, but comely, O ye daughters of Jerusalem, as the tents of Kedar, as the curtains of Solomon. |
| 中文 | 耶路撒冷的众女子阿，我虽然黑，却是秀美，如同基达的帐棚，好像所罗门的幔子。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Look not upon me, because I am black, because the sun hath looked upon me: my mother's children were angry with me; they made me the keeper of the vineyards; but mine own vineyard have I not kept. |
| 中文 | 不要因日头把我晒黑了，就轻看我。我同母的弟兄向我发怒，他们使我看守葡萄园，我自己的葡萄园却没有看守。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Tell me, O thou whom my soul loveth, where thou feedest, where thou makest thy flock to rest at noon: for why should I be as one that turneth aside by the flocks of thy companions? |
| 中文 | 我心所爱的阿，求你告诉我，你在何处牧羊，晌午在何处使羊歇卧。我何必在你同伴的羊群旁边，好像蒙着脸的人呢。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If thou know not, O thou fairest among women, go thy way forth by the footsteps of the flock, and feed thy kids beside the shepherds' tents. |
| 中文 | 你这女子中极美丽的，你若不知道，只管跟随羊群的脚踪去，把你的山羊羔牧放在牧人帐棚的旁边。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I have compared thee, O my love, to a company of horses in Pharaoh's chariots. |
| 中文 | 我的佳偶，我将你比法老车上套的骏马。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thy cheeks are comely with rows of jewels, thy neck with chains of gold. |
| 中文 | 你的两腮因发辫而秀美，你的颈项因珠串而华丽。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 我们要为你编上金辫，镶上银钉。 |

## 12

| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 王正坐席的时候，我的哪哒香膏发出香味。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 我以我的良人为一袋没药，常在我怀中。 |
