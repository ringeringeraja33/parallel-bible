# Proverbs 19 / 箴言 19

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Better is the poor that walketh in his integrity, than he that is perverse in his lips, and is a fool. |
| 中文 | 行为纯正的贫穷人，胜过乖谬愚妄的富足人。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Also, that the soul be without knowledge, it is not good; and he that hasteth with his feet sinneth. |
| 中文 | 心无知识的，乃为不善。脚步急快的难免犯罪。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The foolishness of man perverteth his way: and his heart fretteth against the LORD. |
| 中文 | 人的愚昧，倾败他的道。他的心也抱怨耶和华。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Wealth maketh many friends; but the poor is separated from his neighbour. |
| 中文 | 财物使朋友增多。但穷人朋友远离。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A false witness shall not be unpunished, and he that speaketh lies shall not escape. |
| 中文 | 作假见证的，必不免受罚。吐出谎言的，终不能逃脱。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Many will intreat the favour of the prince: and every man is a friend to him that giveth gifts. |
| 中文 | 好施散的，有多人求他的恩情。爱送礼的，人都为他的朋友。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | All the brethren of the poor do hate him: how much more do his friends go far from him? he pursueth them with words, yet they are wanting to him. |
| 中文 | 贫穷人弟兄都恨他，何况他的朋友，更远离他。他用言语追随，他们却走了。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that getteth wisdom loveth his own soul: he that keepeth understanding shall find good. |
| 中文 | 得着智慧的，爱惜生命，保守聪明的，必得好处。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A false witness shall not be unpunished, and he that speaketh lies shall perish. |
| 中文 | 作假见证的，不免受罚。吐出谎言的，也必灭亡。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Delight is not seemly for a fool; much less for a servant to have rule over princes. |
| 中文 | 愚昧人宴乐度日，是不合宜的，何况仆人管辖王子呢。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The discretion of a man deferreth his anger; and it is his glory to pass over a transgression. |
| 中文 | 人有见识，就不轻易发怒。宽恕人的过失，便是自己的荣耀。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The king's wrath is as the roaring of a lion; but his favour is as dew upon the grass. |
| 中文 | 王的忿怒，好像狮子吼叫。他的恩典，却如草上的甘露。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A foolish son is the calamity of his father: and the contentions of a wife are a continual dropping. |
| 中文 | 愚昧的儿子，是父亲的祸患。妻子的争吵，如雨连连滴漏。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | House and riches are the inheritance of fathers: and a prudent wife is from the LORD. |
| 中文 | 房屋钱财。是祖宗所遗留的。惟有贤慧的妻，是耶和华所赐的。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Slothfulness casteth into a deep sleep; and an idle soul shall suffer hunger. |
| 中文 | 懒惰使人沉睡。懈怠的人，必受饥饿。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that keepeth the commandment keepeth his own soul; but he that despiseth his ways shall die. |
| 中文 | 谨守诫命的，保全生命。轻忽己路的，必致死亡。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that hath pity upon the poor lendeth unto the LORD; and that which he hath given will he pay him again. |
| 中文 | 怜悯贫穷的，就是借给耶和华。他的善行，耶和华必偿还。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Chasten thy son while there is hope, and let not thy soul spare for his crying. |
| 中文 | 趁有指望，管教你的儿子。你的心不可任他死亡。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A man of great wrath shall suffer punishment: for if thou deliver him, yet thou must do it again. |
| 中文 | 暴怒的人，必受刑罚。你若救他，必须再救。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hear counsel, and receive instruction, that thou mayest be wise in thy latter end. |
| 中文 | 你要听劝教，受训诲，使你终久有智慧。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | There are many devices in a man's heart; nevertheless the counsel of the LORD, that shall stand. |
| 中文 | 人心多有计谋。惟有耶和华的筹算，才能立定。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The desire of a man is his kindness: and a poor man is better than a liar. |
| 中文 | 施行仁慈的，令人爱慕。穷人强如说谎言的。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 敬畏耶和华的，得着生命。他必恒久知足，不遭祸患。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 懒惰人放手在盘子里。就是向口撤回，他也不肯。 |

## 25

| Language | Content |

| 中文 | 鞭打亵慢人，愚蒙人必长见识。责备明哲人，他就明白知识。 |
