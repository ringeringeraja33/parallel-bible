# Proverbs 28

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The wicked flee when no man pursueth: but the righteous are bold as a lion. |
| 中文 | 恶人虽无人追赶也逃跑，义人却胆壮像狮子。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the transgression of a land many are the princes thereof: but by a man of understanding and knowledge the state thereof shall be prolonged. |
| 中文 | 邦国因有罪过，君王就多更换。因有聪明知识的人，国必长存。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A poor man that oppresseth the poor is like a sweeping rain which leaveth no food. |
| 中文 | 穷人欺压贫民，好像暴雨冲没粮食。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They that forsake the law praise the wicked: but such as keep the law contend with them. |
| 中文 | 违弃律法的，夸奖恶人。遵守律法的，却与恶人相争。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Evil men understand not judgment: but they that seek the LORD understand all things. |
| 中文 | 坏人不明白公义。惟有寻求耶和华的，无不明白。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Better is the poor that walketh in his uprightness, than he that is perverse in his ways, though he be rich. |
| 中文 | 行为纯正的穷乏人，胜过行事乖僻的富足人。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Whoso keepeth the law is a wise son: but he that is a companion of riotous men shameth his father. |
| 中文 | 谨守律法的是智慧之子。与贪食人作伴的，却羞辱其父。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that by usury and unjust gain increaseth his substance, he shall gather it for him that will pity the poor. |
| 中文 | 人以厚利加增财物，是给那怜悯穷人者积蓄的。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that turneth away his ear from hearing the law, even his prayer shall be abomination. |
| 中文 | 转耳不听律法的，他的祈祷也为可憎。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Whoso causeth the righteous to go astray in an evil way, he shall fall himself into his own pit: but the upright shall have good things in possession. |
| 中文 | 诱惑正直人行恶道的，必掉在自己的坑里。惟有完全人，必承受福分。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The rich man is wise in his own conceit; but the poor that hath understanding searcheth him out. |
| 中文 | 富足人自以为有智慧。但聪明的贫穷人，能将他查透。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When righteous men do rejoice, there is great glory: but when the wicked rise, a man is hidden. |
| 中文 | 义人得志有大荣耀。恶人兴起人就躲藏。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that covereth his sins shall not prosper: but whoso confesseth and forsaketh them shall have mercy. |
| 中文 | 遮掩自己罪过的，必不享通。承认离弃罪过的，必蒙怜恤。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Happy is the man that feareth alway: but he that hardeneth his heart shall fall into mischief. |
| 中文 | 常存敬畏的，便为有福。心存刚硬的，必陷在祸患里。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | As a roaring lion, and a ranging bear; so is a wicked ruler over the poor people. |
| 中文 | 暴虐的君王辖制贫民。好像吼叫的狮子，觅食的熊。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The prince that wanteth understanding is also a great oppressor: but he that hateth covetousness shall prolong his days. |
| 中文 | 无知的君多行暴虐。以贪财为可恨的，必年长日久。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A man that doeth violence to the blood of any person shall flee to the pit; let no man stay him. |
| 中文 | 背负流人血之罪的，必往坑里奔跑，谁也不可拦阻他。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Whoso walketh uprightly shall be saved: but he that is perverse in his ways shall fall at once. |
| 中文 | 行动正直的，必蒙拯救。行事弯曲的，立时跌倒。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that tilleth his land shall have plenty of bread: but he that followeth after vain persons shall have poverty enough. |
| 中文 | 耕种自己田地的，必得饱食。追随虚浮的，足受穷乏。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A faithful man shall abound with blessings: but he that maketh haste to be rich shall not be innocent. |
| 中文 | 诚实人必多得福，想要急速发财的，不免受罚。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | To have respect of persons is not good: for for a piece of bread that man will transgress. |
| 中文 | 看人的情面，乃为不好。人因一块饼枉法，也为不好。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that hasteth to be rich hath an evil eye, and considereth not that poverty shall come upon him. |
| 中文 | 人有恶眼想要急速发财，却不知穷乏必临到他身。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that rebuketh a man afterwards shall find more favour than he that flattereth with the tongue. |
| 中文 | 责备人的，后来蒙人喜悦，多于那用舌头谄媚人的。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Whoso robbeth his father or his mother, and saith, It is no transgression; the same is the companion of a destroyer. |
| 中文 | 偷窃父母的，说，这不是罪。此人就是与强盗同类。 |

## 25

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that is of a proud heart stirreth up strife: but he that putteth his trust in the LORD shall be made fat. |
| 中文 | 心中贪婪的，挑起争端。倚靠耶和华的，必得丰裕。 |

## 26

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that trusteth in his own heart is a fool: but whoso walketh wisely, he shall be delivered. |
| 中文 | 心中自是的，便是愚昧人。凭智慧行事的，必蒙拯救。 |

## 27

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that giveth unto the poor shall not lack: but he that hideth his eyes shall have many a curse. |
| 中文 | 周济贫穷的，不致缺乏。佯为不见的，必多受咒诅。 |

## 28

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When the wicked rise, men hide themselves: but when they perish, the righteous increase. |
| 中文 | 恶人兴起，人就躲藏。恶人败亡，义人增多。 |
