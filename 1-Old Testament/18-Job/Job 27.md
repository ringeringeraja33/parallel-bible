# Job 27

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Moreover Job continued his parable, and said, |
| 中文 | 约伯接着说， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | As God liveth, who hath taken away my judgment; and the Almighty, who hath vexed my soul; |
| 中文 | 神夺去我的理，全能者使我心中愁苦。我指着永生的神起誓。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | All the while my breath is in me, and the spirit of God is in my nostrils; |
| 中文 | 我的生命尚在我里面，神所赐呼吸之气，仍在我的鼻孔内。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My lips shall not speak wickedness, nor my tongue utter deceit. |
| 中文 | 我的嘴决不说非义之言，我的舌也不说诡诈之语。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | God forbid that I should justify you: till I die I will not remove mine integrity from me. |
| 中文 | 我断不以你们为是，我至死必不以自己为不正。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My righteousness I hold fast, and will not let it go: my heart shall not reproach me so long as I live. |
| 中文 | 我持定我的义，必不放松。在世的日子，我心必不责备我。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let mine enemy be as the wicked, and he that riseth up against me as the unrighteous. |
| 中文 | 愿我的仇敌如恶人一样。愿那起来攻击我的，如不义之人一般。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For what is the hope of the hypocrite, though he hath gained, when God taketh away his soul? |
| 中文 | 不敬虔的人虽然得利，神夺取其命的时候，还有什么指望呢。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Will God hear his cry when trouble cometh upon him? |
| 中文 | 患难临到他，神岂能听他的呼求。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Will he delight himself in the Almighty? will he always call upon God? |
| 中文 | 他岂以全能者为乐，随时求告神呢。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will teach you by the hand of God: that which is with the Almighty will I not conceal. |
| 中文 | 神的作为，我要指教你们。全能者所行的，我也不隐瞒。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, all ye yourselves have seen it; why then are ye thus altogether vain? |
| 中文 | 你们自己也都见过，为何全然变为虚妄呢。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | This is the portion of a wicked man with God, and the heritage of oppressors, which they shall receive of the Almighty. |
| 中文 | 神为恶人所定的分，强暴人从全能者所得的报（报原文作产业）乃是这样。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If his children be multiplied, it is for the sword: and his offspring shall not be satisfied with bread. |
| 中文 | 倘或他的儿女增多，还是被刀所杀。他的子孙必不得饱食。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Those that remain of him shall be buried in death: and his widows shall not weep. |
| 中文 | 他所遗留的人必死而埋葬，他的寡妇也不哀哭。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Though he heap up silver as the dust, and prepare raiment as the clay; |
| 中文 | 他虽积蓄银子如尘沙，预备衣服如泥土。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He may prepare it, but the just shall put it on, and the innocent shall divide the silver. |
| 中文 | 他只管预备，义人却要穿上。他的银子，无辜的人要分取。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He buildeth his house as a moth, and as a booth that the keeper maketh. |
| 中文 | 他建造房屋如虫做窝，又如守望者所搭的棚。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The rich man shall lie down, but he shall not be gathered: he openeth his eyes, and he is not. |
| 中文 | 他虽富足躺卧，却不得收殓，转眼之间就不在了。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Terrors take hold on him as waters, a tempest stealeth him away in the night. |
| 中文 | 惊恐如波涛将他追上。暴风在夜间将他刮去。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The east wind carrieth him away, and he departeth: and as a storm hurleth him out of his place. |
| 中文 | 东风把他飘去，又刮他离开本处。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For God shall cast upon him, and not spare: he would fain flee out of his hand. |
| 中文 | 神要向他射箭，并不留情。他恨不得逃脱神的手。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Men shall clap their hands at him, and shall hiss him out of his place. |
| 中文 | 人要向他拍掌，并要发叱声，使他离开本处。 |
