# 2 Timothy 3

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | This know also, that in the last days perilous times shall come. |
| 中文 | 你该知道，末世必有危险的日子来到。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For men shall be lovers of their own selves, covetous, boasters, proud, blasphemers, disobedient to parents, unthankful, unholy, |
| 中文 | 因为那时人要专顾自己，贪爱钱财，自夸，狂傲，谤？？，违背父母，忘恩负义，心不圣洁， |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Without natural affection, trucebreakers, false accusers, incontinent, fierce, despisers of those that are good, |
| 中文 | 无亲情，不解怨，好说才言，不能自约，性情凶暴，不爱良善， |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Traitors, heady, highminded, lovers of pleasures more than lovers of God; |
| 中文 | 卖主卖友，任意妄为，自高自大，爱宴乐不爱神。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Having a form of godliness, but denying the power thereof: from such turn away. |
| 中文 | 有敬虔的外貌，却背了敬虔的实意。这等人你要躲开。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For of this sort are they which creep into houses, and lead captive silly women laden with sins, led away with divers lusts, |
| 中文 | 那偷进人家，牢笼无知妇女的，正是这等人。这些妇女担负罪恶，被各样的私欲引诱。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ever learning, and never able to come to the knowledge of the truth. |
| 中文 | 常常学习，终久不能明白真道。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Now as Jannes and Jambres withstood Moses, so do these also resist the truth: men of corrupt minds, reprobate concerning the faith. |
| 中文 | 从前雅尼和佯庇怎样敌挡摩西，这等人也怎样敌挡真道。他们的心地坏了，在真道上是可废弃的。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But they shall proceed no further: for their folly shall be manifest unto all men, as theirs also was. |
| 中文 | 然而他们不能再这样敌挡，因为他们的愚昧，必在众人面前显露出来，像那二人一样。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But thou hast fully known my doctrine, manner of life, purpose, faith, longsuffering, charity, patience, |
| 中文 | 但你已经服从了我的教训，品行，志向，信心，宽容，爱心，忍耐， |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Persecutions, afflictions, which came unto me at Antioch, at Iconium, at Lystra; what persecutions I endured: but out of them all the Lord delivered me. |
| 中文 | 以及我在安提阿，以哥念，路司得，所遭遇的逼迫，苦难。我所忍受是何等的逼迫。但从这一切苦难中主都把我救出来了。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Yea, and all that will live godly in Christ Jesus shall suffer persecution. |
| 中文 | 不但如此，凡立志在基督耶稣里敬虔度日的，也都要受逼迫。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But evil men and seducers shall wax worse and worse, deceiving, and being deceived. |
| 中文 | 只是作恶的，和迷惑人的，必越久越恶，他欺哄人也被人欺哄。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But continue thou in the things which thou hast learned and hast been assured of, knowing of whom thou hast learned them; |
| 中文 | 但你所学习的，所确信的，要存在心里。因为你知道是跟谁学的。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And that from a child thou hast known the holy scriptures, which are able to make thee wise unto salvation through faith which is in Christ Jesus. |
| 中文 | 并且知道你是从小明白圣经。这圣经能使你因信基督耶稣有得救的智慧。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | All scripture is given by inspiration of God, and is profitable for doctrine, for reproof, for correction, for instruction in righteousness: |
| 中文 | 圣经都是神所默示的，（或作凡神默示的圣经）于教训，督责，使人归正，教导人学义，都是有益的。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | That the man of God may be perfect, throughly furnished unto all good works. |
| 中文 | 叫属神的人得以完全，豫备行各样的善事。 |
