# Psalms 148 / 诗篇 148

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Praise ye the LORD. Praise ye the LORD from the heavens: praise him in the heights. |
| 中文 | 你们要赞美耶和华，从天上赞美耶和华，在高处赞美他。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Praise ye him, all his angels: praise ye him, all his hosts. |
| 中文 | 他的众使者都要赞美他。他的诸军都要赞美他。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Praise ye him, sun and moon: praise him, all ye stars of light. |
| 中文 | 日头月亮，你们要赞美他。放光的星宿，你们都要赞美他。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Praise him, ye heavens of heavens, and ye waters that be above the heavens. |
| 中文 | 天上的天，和天上的水，你们都要赞美他。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let them praise the name of the LORD: for he commanded, and they were created. |
| 中文 | 愿这些都赞美耶和华的名。因他一吩咐便都造成。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He hath also stablished them for ever and ever: he hath made a decree which shall not pass. |
| 中文 | 他将这些立定，直到永永远远。他定了命，不能废去（或作越过）。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Praise the LORD from the earth, ye dragons, and all deeps: |
| 中文 | 所有在地上的，大鱼和一切深洋， |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Fire, and hail; snow, and vapours; stormy wind fulfilling his word: |
| 中文 | 火与冰雹，雪和雾气，成就他命的狂风， |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Mountains, and all hills; fruitful trees, and all cedars: |
| 中文 | 大山和小山，结果的树木，和一切香柏树， |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Beasts, and all cattle; creeping things, and flying fowl: |
| 中文 | 野兽和一切牲畜，昆虫和飞鸟， |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Kings of the earth, and all people; princes, and all judges of the earth: |
| 中文 | 世上的君王和万民，首领和世上一切审判官， |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Both young men, and maidens; old men, and children: |
| 中文 | 少年人和处女，老年人和孩童，都当赞美耶和华。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let them praise the name of the LORD: for his name alone is excellent; his glory is above the earth and heaven. |
| 中文 | 愿这些都赞美耶和华的名。因为独有他的名被尊崇。他的荣耀在天地之上。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He also exalteth the horn of his people, the praise of all his saints; even of the children of Israel, a people near unto him. Praise ye the LORD. |
| 中文 | 他将他百姓的角高举，因此他（因此他或作他使）一切圣民以色列人，就是与他相近的百姓，都赞美他。你们要赞美耶和华。 |
