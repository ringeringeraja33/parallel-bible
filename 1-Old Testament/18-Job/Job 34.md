# Job 34

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Furthermore Elihu answered and said, |
| 中文 | 以利户又说， |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hear my words, O ye wise men; and give ear unto me, ye that have knowledge. |
| 中文 | 你们智慧人，要听我的话。有知识的人，要留心听我说。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the ear trieth words, as the mouth tasteth meat. |
| 中文 | 因为耳朵试验话语，好像上膛尝食物。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let us choose to us judgment: let us know among ourselves what is good. |
| 中文 | 我们当选择何为是，彼此知道何为善。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For Job hath said, I am righteous: and God hath taken away my judgment. |
| 中文 | 约伯曾说，我是公义，神夺去我的理。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Should I lie against my right? my wound is incurable without transgression. |
| 中文 | 我虽有理，还算为说谎言的。我虽无过，受的伤还不能医治。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 谁像约伯，喝讥诮如同喝水呢。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 他与作孽的结伴，和恶人同行。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For he hath said, It profiteth a man nothing that he should delight himself with God. |
| 中文 | 他说，人以神为乐，总是无益。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Therefore hearken unto me ye men of understanding: far be it from God, that he should do wickedness; and from the Almighty, that he should commit iniquity. |
| 中文 | 所以你们明理的人，要听我的话。神断旁不至行恶，全能者断不至作孽。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the work of a man shall he render unto him, and cause every man to find according to his ways. |
| 中文 | 他必按人所作的报应人，使各人照所行的得报。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Yea, surely God will not do wickedly, neither will the Almighty pervert judgment. |
| 中文 | 神必不作恶，全能者也不偏离公平。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who hath given him a charge over the earth? or who hath disposed the whole world? |
| 中文 | 谁派他治理地，安定全世界呢。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If he set his heart upon man, if he gather unto himself his spirit and his breath; |
| 中文 | 他若专心为己，将灵和气收归自己。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | All flesh shall perish together, and man shall turn again unto dust. |
| 中文 | 凡有血气的就必一同死亡，世人必仍归尘土。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If now thou hast understanding, hear this: hearken to the voice of my words. |
| 中文 | 你若明理，就当听我的话，留心听我言语的声音。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Shall even he that hateth right govern? and wilt thou condemn him that is most just? |
| 中文 | 难道恨恶公平的可以掌权吗。那有公义的，有大能的，岂可定他有罪吗。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Is it fit to say to a king, Thou art wicked? and to princes, Ye are ungodly? |
| 中文 | 他对君王说，你是鄙陋的。对贵臣说，你是邪恶的。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | How much less to him that accepteth not the persons of princes, nor regardeth the rich more than the poor? for they all are the work of his hands. |
| 中文 | 他待王子不徇情面，也不看重富足的过于贫穷的，因为都是他手所造。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | In a moment shall they die, and the people shall be troubled at midnight, and pass away: and the mighty shall be taken away without hand. |
| 中文 | 在转眼之间，半夜之中，他们就死亡。百姓被震动而去世。有权力的被夺去非借人手。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For his eyes are upon the ways of man, and he seeth all his goings. |
| 中文 | 神注目观看人的道路，看明人的脚步。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | There is no darkness, nor shadow of death, where the workers of iniquity may hide themselves. |
| 中文 | 没有黑暗，阴翳能给作孽的藏身。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For he will not lay upon man more than right; that he should enter into judgment with God. |
| 中文 | 神审判人，不必使人到他面前再三鉴察。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 他用难测之法，打破有能力的人，设立别人代替他们。 |

## 25

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Therefore he knoweth their works, and he overturneth them in the night, so that they are destroyed. |
| 中文 | 他原知道他们的行为，使他们在夜间倾倒灭亡。 |

## 26

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 他在众人眼前击打他们，如同击打恶人一样。 |

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Because they turned back from him, and would not consider any of his ways: |
| 中文 | 因为他们偏行不跟从他，也不留心他的道， |

## 28

| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 甚至使贫穷人的哀声，达到他那里。他也听了困苦人的哀声。 |
