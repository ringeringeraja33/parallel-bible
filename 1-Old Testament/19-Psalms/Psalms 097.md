# Psalms 97 / 诗篇 97

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD reigneth; let the earth rejoice; let the multitude of isles be glad thereof. |
| 中文 | 耶和华作王。愿地快乐，愿众海岛欢喜。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Clouds and darkness are round about him: righteousness and judgment are the habitation of his throne. |
| 中文 | 密云和幽暗，在他的四围。公义和公平，是他宝座的根基。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A fire goeth before him, and burneth up his enemies round about. |
| 中文 | 有烈火在他前头行，烧灭他四围的敌人。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | His lightnings enlightened the world: the earth saw, and trembled. |
| 中文 | 他的闪电光照世界。大地看见便震动。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The hills melted like wax at the presence of the LORD, at the presence of the Lord of the whole earth. |
| 中文 | 诸山见耶和华的面，就是全地之主的面，便消化如腊。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The heavens declare his righteousness, and all the people see his glory. |
| 中文 | 诸天表明他的公义，万民看见他的荣耀。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Confounded be all they that serve graven images, that boast themselves of idols: worship him, all ye gods. |
| 中文 | 愿一切事奉雕刻的偶像，靠虚无之神自夸的，都蒙羞愧。万神哪，你们都当拜他。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Zion heard, and was glad; and the daughters of Judah rejoiced because of thy judgments, O LORD. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，锡安听见你的判断，就欢喜，犹大的城邑原文作女子，也都快乐。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For thou, LORD, art high above all the earth: thou art exalted far above all gods. |
| 中文 | 因为你耶和华至高超乎全地。你被尊崇，远超万神之上。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ye that love the LORD, hate evil: he preserveth the souls of his saints; he delivereth them out of the hand of the wicked. |
| 中文 | 你们爱耶和华的，都当恨恶罪恶。他保护圣民的性命，搭救他们脱离恶人的手。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Light is sown for the righteous, and gladness for the upright in heart. |
| 中文 | 散布亮光，是为义人。预备喜乐，是为正直人。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Rejoice in the LORD, ye righteous; and give thanks at the remembrance of his holiness. |
| 中文 | 你们义人当靠耶和华欢喜，称谢他可记念的圣名。 |
