# Psalms 149 / 诗篇 149

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Praise ye the LORD. Sing unto the LORD a new song, and his praise in the congregation of saints. |
| 中文 | 你们要赞美耶和华，向耶和华唱新歌，在圣民的会中赞美他。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let Israel rejoice in him that made him: let the children of Zion be joyful in their King. |
| 中文 | 愿以色列因造他的主欢喜。愿锡安的民，因他们的王快乐。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let them praise his name in the dance: let them sing praises unto him with the timbrel and harp. |
| 中文 | 愿他们跳舞赞美他的名，击鼓弹琴歌颂他。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the LORD taketh pleasure in his people: he will beautify the meek with salvation. |
| 中文 | 因为耶和华喜爱他的百姓。他要用救恩当作谦卑人的妆饰。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let the saints be joyful in glory: let them sing aloud upon their beds. |
| 中文 | 愿圣民因所得的荣耀高兴。愿他们在床上欢呼。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let the high praises of God be in their mouth, and a two-edged sword in their hand; |
| 中文 | 愿他们口中称赞神为高，手里有两刃的刀。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | To execute vengeance upon the heathen, and punishments upon the people; |
| 中文 | 为要报复列邦，刑罚万民。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | To bind their kings with chains, and their nobles with fetters of iron; |
| 中文 | 要用链子捆他们的君王，用铁镣锁他们的大臣。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | To execute upon them the judgment written: this honour have all his saints. Praise ye the LORD. |
| 中文 | 要在他们身上施行所记录的审判。他的圣民都有这荣耀。你们要赞美耶和华。 |
