# Psalms 59

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Deliver me from mine enemies, O my God: defend me from them that rise up against me. |
| 中文 | （扫罗打发人窥探大卫的房屋，要杀他。那时大卫用这金诗，交与伶长，调用休要毁坏）我的神阿，求你救我脱离仇敌，把我安置在高处，得脱那些起来攻击我的人。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Deliver me from the workers of iniquity, and save me from bloody men. |
| 中文 | 求你救我脱离作孽的人，和喜爱流人血的人。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For, lo, they lie in wait for my soul: the mighty are gathered against me; not for my transgression, nor for my sin, O LORD. |
| 中文 | 因为他们埋伏，要害我的命，有能力的人聚集来攻击我。耶和华阿，这不是为我的过犯，也不是为我的罪愆。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They run and prepare themselves without my fault: awake to help me, and behold. |
| 中文 | 我虽然无过，他们豫备整齐，跑来攻击我。求你兴起鉴察帮助我。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou therefore, O LORD God of hosts, the God of Israel, awake to visit all the heathen: be not merciful to any wicked transgressors. Selah. |
| 中文 | 万军之神耶和华以色列的神阿，求你兴起，惩治万邦。不要怜悯行诡诈的恶人。（细拉） |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They return at evening: they make a noise like a dog, and go round about the city. |
| 中文 | 他们晚上转回，叫号如狗，围城绕行。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, they belch out with their mouth: swords are in their lips: for who, say they, doth hear? |
| 中文 | 他们口中喷吐恶言，嘴里有刀，他们说有谁听见。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But thou, O LORD, shalt laugh at them; thou shalt have all the heathen in derision. |
| 中文 | 但你耶和华必笑话他们。你要嗤笑万邦。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Because of his strength will I wait upon thee: for God is my defence. |
| 中文 | 我的力量阿，我必仰望你。因为神是我的高台。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The God of my mercy shall prevent me: God shall let me see my desire upon mine enemies. |
| 中文 | 我的神要以慈爱迎接我。神要叫我看见我仇敌遭报。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Slay them not, lest my people forget: scatter them by thy power; and bring them down, O Lord our shield. |
| 中文 | 不要杀他们，恐怕我的民忘记。主阿，你是我们的盾牌。求你用你的能力使他们四散，且降为卑。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the sin of their mouth and the words of their lips let them even be taken in their pride: and for cursing and lying which they speak. |
| 中文 | 因他们口中的罪，和嘴里的言语，并咒骂虚谎的话，愿他们在骄傲之中被缠住了。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Consume them in wrath, consume them, that they may not be: and let them know that God ruleth in Jacob unto the ends of the earth. Selah. |
| 中文 | 求你发怒，使他们消灭，以至归于无有，叫他们知道神在雅各中间掌权，直到地极。（细拉） |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And at evening let them return; and let them make a noise like a dog, and go round about the city. |
| 中文 | 到了晚上，任凭他们转回。任凭他们叫号如狗，围城绕行。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let them wander up and down for meat, and grudge if they be not satisfied. |
| 中文 | 他们必走来走去，寻梢食物。若不得饱，就终夜在外。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But I will sing of thy power; yea, I will sing aloud of thy mercy in the morning: for thou hast been my defence and refuge in the day of my trouble. |
| 中文 | 但我要歌颂你的力量，早晨要高唱你的慈爱。因为你作过我的高台。在我急难的日子，作过我的避难所。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Unto thee, O my strength, will I sing: for God is my defence, and the God of my mercy. |
| 中文 | 我的力量阿，我要歌颂你。因为神是我的高台，是赐恩与我的神。 |
