# Isaiah 44

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Yet now hear, O Jacob my servant; and Israel, whom I have chosen: |
| 中文 | 我的仆人雅各，我所拣选的以色列阿，现在你当听。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thus saith the LORD that made thee, and formed thee from the womb, which will help thee; Fear not, O Jacob, my servant; and thou, Jesurun, whom I have chosen. |
| 中文 | 造作你，又从你出胎造就你，并要帮助你的耶和华如此说，我的仆人雅各，我所拣选的耶书仑哪，不要害怕。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For I will pour water upon him that is thirsty, and floods upon the dry ground: I will pour my spirit upon thy seed, and my blessing upon thine offspring: |
| 中文 | 因为我要将水浇灌口渴的人，将河浇灌乾旱之地。我要将我的灵浇灌你的后裔，将我的福浇灌你的子孙。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And they shall spring up as among the grass, as willows by the water courses. |
| 中文 | 他们要发生在草中，像溪水旁的柳树。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | One shall say, I am the LORD's; and another shall call himself by the name of Jacob; and another shall subscribe with his hand unto the LORD, and surname himself by the name of Israel. |
| 中文 | 这个要说，我是属耶和华的。那个要以雅各的名自称。又一个要亲手写归耶和华的（或作在手上写归耶和华）并自称为以色列。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thus saith the LORD the King of Israel, and his redeemer the LORD of hosts; I am the first, and I am the last; and beside me there is no God. |
| 中文 | 耶和华以色列的君，以色列的救赎主万军之耶和华如此说，我是首先的，我是末后的，除我以外，再没有真神。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And who, as I, shall call, and shall declare it, and set it in order for me, since I appointed the ancient people? and the things that are coming, and shall come, let them shew unto them. |
| 中文 | 自从我设立古时的民，谁能像我宣告，并且指明，又为自己陈说呢。让他将未来的事，和必成的事说明。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Fear ye not, neither be afraid: have not I told thee from that time, and have declared it? ye are even my witnesses. Is there a God beside me? yea, there is no God; I know not any. |
| 中文 | 你们不要恐惧，也不要害怕。我岂不是从上古就指示说明你们吗，并且你们是我的见证，除我以外，岂有真神吗，诚然没有磐石，我不知道一个。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They that make a graven image are all of them vanity; and their delectable things shall not profit; and they are their own witnesses; they see not, nor know; that they may be ashamed. |
| 中文 | 制造雕刻偶像的，尽都虚空。他们所喜悦的，都无益处。他们的见证，无所看见，无所知晓，他们便觉羞愧。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who hath formed a god, or molten a graven image that is profitable for nothing? |
| 中文 | 谁制造神像，铸造无益的偶像。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, all his fellows shall be ashamed: and the workmen, they are of men: let them all be gathered together, let them stand up; yet they shall fear, and they shall be ashamed together. |
| 中文 | 看哪，他们的同伴都必羞愧，工匠也不过是人。任他们聚会，任他们站立，都必惧怕，一同羞愧。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The smith with the tongs both worketh in the coals, and fashioneth it with hammers, and worketh it with the strength of his arms: yea, he is hungry, and his strength faileth: he drinketh no water, and is faint. |
| 中文 | 铁匠把铁在火炭中烧热，用槌打铁器，用他有力的臂膀锤成。他饥饿而无力，不喝水而发倦。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The carpenter stretcheth out his rule; he marketh it out with a line; he fitteth it with planes, and he marketh it out with the compass, and maketh it after the figure of a man, according to the beauty of a man; that it may remain in the house. |
| 中文 | 木匠拉线，用笔划出样子。用刨子刨成形状，用圆尺划了模样，仿照人的体态，作成人形，好住在房屋中。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He heweth him down cedars, and taketh the cypress and the oak, which he strengtheneth for himself among the trees of the forest: he planteth an ash, and the rain doth nourish it. |
| 中文 | 他砍伐香柏树，又取柞（或作青桐）树和橡树，在树林中选定了一棵。他栽种松树得雨长养， |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then shall it be for a man to burn: for he will take thereof, and warm himself; yea, he kindleth it, and baketh bread; yea, he maketh a god, and worshippeth it; he maketh it a graven image, and falleth down thereto. |
| 中文 | 这树，人可用以烧火，他自己取些烤火，又烧着烤饼。而且作神像跪拜，作雕刻的偶像向他叩拜。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He burneth part thereof in the fire; with part thereof he eateth flesh; he roasteth roast, and is satisfied: yea, he warmeth himself, and saith, Aha, I am warm, I have seen the fire: |
| 中文 | 他把一分烧在火中，把一分烤肉吃饱。自己烤火说，阿哈，我暖和了，我见火了。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the residue thereof he maketh a god, even his graven image: he falleth down unto it, and worshippeth it, and prayeth unto it, and saith, Deliver me; for thou art my god. |
| 中文 | 他用剩下的作了一神，就是雕刻的偶像，他向这偶像俯伏叩拜，祷告他说，求你拯救我，因你是我的神。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They have not known nor understood: for he hath shut their eyes, that they cannot see; and their hearts, that they cannot understand. |
| 中文 | 他们不知道，也不思想，因为耶和华闭住他们的眼不能看见，塞住他们的心不能明白。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And none considereth in his heart, neither is there knowledge nor understanding to say, I have burned part of it in the fire; yea, also I have baked bread upon the coals thereof; I have roasted flesh, and eaten it: and shall I make the residue thereof an abomination? shall I fall down to the stock of a tree? |
| 中文 | 谁心里也不醒悟，也没有知识，没有聪明，能说，我曾拿一分在火中烧了，在炭上烤过饼，我也烤过肉吃，这剩下的，我岂要作可憎的物吗。我岂可向木？？子叩拜呢。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He feedeth on ashes: a deceived heart hath turned him aside, that he cannot deliver his soul, nor say, Is there not a lie in my right hand? |
| 中文 | 他以灰为食，心中昏迷，使他偏邪，他不能自救，也不能说，我右手中岂不是有虚谎吗。 |

## 21

| 中文 | 雅各以色列阿，你是我的仆人，要记念这些事。以色列阿，你是我的仆人，我造就你必不忘记你。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 我涂抹了你的过犯，像厚云消散。我涂抹了你的罪恶，如薄云灭没。你当归向我，因我救赎了你。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Sing, O ye heavens; for the LORD hath done it: shout, ye lower parts of the earth: break forth into singing, ye mountains, O forest, and every tree therein: for the LORD hath redeemed Jacob, and glorified himself in Israel. |
| 中文 | 诸天哪，应当歌唱，因为耶和华作成这事。地的深处阿，应当欢呼，众山应当发声歌唱，树林和其中所有的树，都当如此，因为耶和华救赎了雅各，并要因以色列荣耀自己。 |

## 24

## 25

## 26

| --- | --- |
