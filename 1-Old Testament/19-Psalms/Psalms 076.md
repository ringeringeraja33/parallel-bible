# Psalms 76

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | In Judah is God known: his name is great in Israel. |
| 中文 | （亚萨的诗歌，交与伶长，用丝弦的乐器）在犹大神为人所认识。在以色列他的名为大。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | In Salem also is his tabernacle, and his dwelling place in Zion. |
| 中文 | 在撒冷有他的帐幕，在锡安有他的居所。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | There brake he the arrows of the bow, the shield, and the sword, and the battle. Selah. |
| 中文 | 他在那里折断弓上的火箭，并盾牌，刀剑，和争战的兵器。（细拉） |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou art more glorious and excellent than the mountains of prey. |
| 中文 | 你从有野食之山而来，有光华和荣美。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The stouthearted are spoiled, they have slept their sleep: and none of the men of might have found their hands. |
| 中文 | 心中勇敢的人，都被抢夺。他们睡了长觉。没有一个英雄能措手。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | At thy rebuke, O God of Jacob, both the chariot and horse are cast into a dead sleep. |
| 中文 | 雅各的神阿，你的斥责一发坐车的，骑马的，都沉睡了。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou, even thou, art to be feared: and who may stand in thy sight when once thou art angry? |
| 中文 | 惟独你是可畏的。你怒气一发，谁能在你面前删得住呢。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou didst cause judgment to be heard from heaven; the earth feared, and was still, |
| 中文 | 你从天上使人听判断。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | When God arose to judgment, to save all the meek of the earth. Selah. |
| 中文 | 神起来施行审判，要救地上一切谦卑的人。那时地就惧怕而静默。（细拉） |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Surely the wrath of man shall praise thee: the remainder of wrath shalt thou restrain. |
| 中文 | 人的忿怒，要成全你的荣美。人的馀怒，你要禁止。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Vow, and pay unto the LORD your God: let all that be round about him bring presents unto him that ought to be feared. |
| 中文 | 你们许愿，当向耶和华你们的神还愿。在他四面的人，都当拿贡物献给那可畏的主。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He shall cut off the spirit of princes: he is terrible to the kings of the earth. |
| 中文 | 他要挫折王子的骄气。他向地上的君王，显威可畏。 |
