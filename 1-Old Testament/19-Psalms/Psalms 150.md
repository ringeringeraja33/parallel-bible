# Psalms 150 / 诗篇 150

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Praise ye the LORD. Praise God in his sanctuary: praise him in the firmament of his power. |
| 中文 | 你们要赞美耶和华，在神的圣所赞美他，在他显能力的穹苍赞美他。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Praise him for his mighty acts: praise him according to his excellent greatness. |
| 中文 | 要因他大能的作为赞美他，按着他极美的大德赞美他。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Praise him with the sound of the trumpet: praise him with the psaltery and harp. |
| 中文 | 要用角声赞美他，鼓瑟弹琴赞美他。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Praise him with the timbrel and dance: praise him with stringed instruments and organs. |
| 中文 | 击鼓跳舞赞美他，用丝弦的乐器，和箫的声音赞美他。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Praise him upon the loud cymbals: praise him upon the high sounding cymbals. |
| 中文 | 用大响的钹赞美他，用高声的钹赞美他。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let every thing that hath breath praise the LORD. Praise ye the LORD. |
| 中文 | 凡有气息的，都要赞美耶和华。你们要赞美耶和华。 |
