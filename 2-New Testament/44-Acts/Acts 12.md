## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And because he saw it pleased the Jews, he proceeded further to take Peter also. (Then were the days of unleavened bread.) |
| 中文 | 他见犹太人喜欢这事，又去捉拿彼得。那时正是除酵的日子。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And when he had apprehended him, he put him in prison, and delivered him to four quaternions of soldiers to keep him; intending after Easter to bring him forth to the people. |
| 中文 | 希律拿了彼得收在监里，交付四班兵丁看守，每班四个人，意思要在逾越节后，把他提出来，当着百姓办他 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Peter therefore was kept in prison: but prayer was made without ceasing of the church unto God for him. |
| 中文 | 于是彼得被囚在监里。教会却为他切切的祷告神。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And when Herod would have brought him forth, the same night Peter was sleeping between two soldiers, bound with two chains: and the keepers before the door kept the prison. |
| 中文 | 希律将要提他出来的前一夜，彼得被两条铁链锁着，睡在两个兵丁当中。看守的人也在门外看守。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And, behold, the angel of the Lord came upon him, and a light shined in the prison: and he smote Peter on the side, and raised him up, saying, Arise up quickly. And his chains fell off from his hands. |
| 中文 | 忽然有主的一个使者，站在旁边，屋里有光照耀。天使拍彼得的肋旁，拍醒了他，说，快快起来。那铁链就从他手上脱落下来。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 天使对他说，束上带子。穿上鞋。他就那样作。天使又说，披上外衣跟着我来。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And he went out, and followed him; and wist not that it was true which was done by the angel; but thought he saw a vision. |
| 中文 | 彼得就出来跟着他，不知道天使所作是真的，只当见了异象。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 过了第一层，第二层监牢，就来到临街的铁门。那门自己开了。他们出来，走过一条街，天使便离开他去了。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
