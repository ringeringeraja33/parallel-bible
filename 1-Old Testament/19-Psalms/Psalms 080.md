# Psalms 80 / 诗篇 80

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Give ear, O Shepherd of Israel, thou that leadest Joseph like a flock; thou that dwellest between the cherubims, shine forth. |
| 中文 | （亚萨的诗，交与伶长，调用为证的百合花）领约瑟如领羊群的以色列的牧者阿，求你留心听。坐在二基路伯上的阿，求你发出光来。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Before Ephraim and Benjamin and Manasseh stir up thy strength, and come and save us. |
| 中文 | 在以法莲便雅悯玛拿西前面施展你的大能，来救我们。 |

## 3
