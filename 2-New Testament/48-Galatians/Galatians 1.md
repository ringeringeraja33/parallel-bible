# Galatians 1

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Paul, an apostle, (not of men, neither by man, but by Jesus Christ, and God the Father, who raised him from the dead;) |
| 中文 | 作使徒的保罗，（不是由于人，也不是藉着人，乃是藉着耶稣基督，与叫他从死里复活的父神） |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And all the brethren which are with me, unto the churches of Galatia: |
| 中文 | 和一切与我同在的众弟兄，写信给加拉太的各教会。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Grace be to you and peace from God the Father, and from our Lord Jesus Christ, |
| 中文 | 愿恩惠平安，从父神与我们的主耶稣基督，归与你们。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who gave himself for our sins, that he might deliver us from this present evil world, according to the will of God and our Father: |
| 中文 | 基督照我们父神的旨意为我们的罪舍己，要救我们脱离这罪恶的世代。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | To whom be glory for ever and ever. Amen. |
| 中文 | 但愿荣耀归于神直到永永远远。阿们。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I marvel that ye are so soon removed from him that called you into the grace of Christ unto another gospel: |
| 中文 | 我希奇你们这吗快离开那藉着基督之恩召你们的，去从别的福音。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Which is not another; but there be some that trouble you, and would pervert the gospel of Christ. |
| 中文 | 那并不是福音不过有些人搅扰你们，要把基督的福音更改了。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But though we, or an angel from heaven, preach any other gospel unto you than that which we have preached unto you, let him be accursed. |
| 中文 | 但无论是我们，是天上来的使者，若传福音给你们，与我们所传给你们的不同，他就应当被咒诅。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | As we said before, so say I now again, If any man preach any other gospel unto you than that ye have received, let him be accursed. |
| 中文 | 我们已经说了，现在又说，若有人传福音给你们，与你们所领受的不同，他就应当被咒诅。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For do I now persuade men, or God? or do I seek to please men? for if I yet pleased men, I should not be the servant of Christ. |
| 中文 | 我现在是要得人的心呢，还是要得神的心呢。我岂是讨人的喜欢吗。若仍旧讨人的喜欢，我就不是基督的仆人了。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But I certify you, brethren, that the gospel which was preached of me is not after man. |
| 中文 | 弟兄们，我告诉你们，我素来所传的福音，不是出于人的意思。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For I neither received it of man, neither was I taught it, but by the revelation of Jesus Christ. |
| 中文 | 因为我不是从人领受的，也不是人教导我的，乃是从耶稣基督启示来的。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For ye have heard of my conversation in time past in the Jews' religion, how that beyond measure I persecuted the church of God, and wasted it: |
| 中文 | 你们听见我从前在犹太教中所行的事，怎样极力逼迫残害神的教会。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And profited in the Jews' religion above many my equals in mine own nation, being more exceedingly zealous of the traditions of my fathers. |
| 中文 | 我又在犹太教中，比我本国许多同岁的人更有长进，为我祖宗的遗传更加热心。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But when it pleased God, who separated me from my mother's womb, and called me by his grace, |
| 中文 | 然而那把我从母腹里分别出来，又施恩召我的神， |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | To reveal his Son in me, that I might preach him among the heathen; immediately I conferred not with flesh and blood: |
| 中文 | 既然乐意将他儿子启示在我心里，叫我把他传在外邦人中，我就没有与属血气的人商量， |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Neither went I up to Jerusalem to them which were apostles before me; but I went into Arabia, and returned again unto Damascus. |
| 中文 | 也没有上耶路撒冷去，见那些比我先作使徒的。惟独往阿拉伯去。后又回到大马士革。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then after three years I went up to Jerusalem to see Peter, and abode with him fifteen days. |
| 中文 | 过了三年，才上耶路撒冷去见叽法，和他同住了十五天。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But other of the apostles saw I none, save James the Lord's brother. |
| 中文 | 至于别的使徒，除了主的兄弟雅各，我都没有看见。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Now the things which I write unto you, behold, before God, I lie not. |
| 中文 | 我写给你们的，不是谎话，这是我在神面前说的。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Afterwards I came into the regions of Syria and Cilicia; |
| 中文 | 以后我到了叙利亚和基利家境内。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And was unknown by face unto the churches of Judaea which were in Christ: |
| 中文 | 那时，犹太信基督的各教会都没有见过我的面。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But they had heard only, That he which persecuted us in times past now preacheth the faith which once he destroyed. |
| 中文 | 不过听说，那从前逼迫我们的，现在传扬他原先所残害的真道。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And they glorified God in me. |
| 中文 | 他们就为我的缘故，归荣耀给神。 |
