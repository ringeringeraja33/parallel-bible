# Malachi 4 / 玛拉基书 4

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For, behold, the day cometh, that shall burn as an oven; and all the proud, yea, and all that do wickedly, shall be stubble: and the day that cometh shall burn them up, saith the LORD of hosts, that it shall leave them neither root nor branch. |
| 中文 | 万军之耶和华说，那日临近，势如烧着的火炉。凡狂傲的和行恶的必如碎秸。在那日必被烧尽，根本枝条一无存留。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But unto you that fear my name shall the Sun of righteousness arise with healing in his wings; and ye shall go forth, and grow up as calves of the stall. |
| 中文 | 但向你们敬畏我名的人，必有公义的日头出现。其光线（原文作翅膀）有医治之能。你们必出来跳跃，如圈里的肥犊。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And ye shall tread down the wicked; for they shall be ashes under the soles of your feet in the day that I shall do this, saith the LORD of hosts. |
| 中文 | 你们必践踏恶人。在我所定的日子，他们必如灰尘在你们脚掌之下。这是万军之耶和华说的。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Remember ye the law of Moses my servant, which I commanded unto him in Horeb for all Israel, with the statutes and judgments. |
| 中文 | 你们当记念我仆人摩西的律法，就是我在何烈山，为以色列众人所吩咐他的律例典章。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, I will send you Elijah the prophet before the coming of the great and dreadful day of the LORD: |
| 中文 | 看哪，耶和华大而可畏之日未到以前，我必差遣先知以利亚到你们那里去。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And he shall turn the heart of the fathers to the children, and the heart of the children to their fathers, lest I come and smite the earth with a curse. |
| 中文 | 他必使父亲的心转向儿女，儿女的心转向父亲，免得我来咒诅遍地。 |
