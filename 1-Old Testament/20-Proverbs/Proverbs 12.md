# Proverbs 12 / 箴言 12

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Whoso loveth instruction loveth knowledge: but he that hateth reproof is brutish. |
| 中文 | 喜爱管教的，就是喜爱知识。恨恶责备的，却是畜类。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A good man obtaineth favour of the LORD: but a man of wicked devices will he condemn. |
| 中文 | 善人必蒙耶和华的嗯惠。设诡计的人，耶和华必定他得罪。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A man shall not be established by wickedness: but the root of the righteous shall not be moved. |
| 中文 | 人靠恶行不能坚立。义人的根，必不动摇。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A virtuous woman is a crown to her husband: but she that maketh ashamed is as rottenness in his bones. |
| 中文 | 才德的妇人，是丈夫的冠冕。贻羞的妇人，如同朽烂在他丈夫的骨中。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The thoughts of the righteous are right: but the counsels of the wicked are deceit. |
| 中文 | 义人的思念是公平。恶人的计谋是诡诈。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The words of the wicked are to lie in wait for blood: but the mouth of the upright shall deliver them. |
| 中文 | 恶人的言论，是埋伏流人的血。正直人的口，必拯救人。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The wicked are overthrown, and are not: but the house of the righteous shall stand. |
| 中文 | 恶人倾覆，归于无有。义人的家，必站得住。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A man shall be commended according to his wisdom: but he that is of a perverse heart shall be despised. |
| 中文 | 人必按自己的智慧被称赞。心中乖谬的，必被藐视。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that is despised, and hath a servant, is better than he that honoureth himself, and lacketh bread. |
| 中文 | 被人轻贱，却有仆人，强如自尊，缺少食物。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A righteous man regardeth the life of his beast: but the tender mercies of the wicked are cruel. |
| 中文 | 义人顾惜他牲畜的命。恶人的怜悯，也是残忍。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that tilleth his land shall be satisfied with bread: but he that followeth vain persons is void of understanding. |
| 中文 | 耕种自己田地的，必得饱食。追随虚浮的，却是无知。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The wicked desireth the net of evil men: but the root of the righteous yieldeth fruit. |
| 中文 | 恶人想得坏人的网罗。义人的根，得以结实。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The wicked is snared by the transgression of his lips: but the just shall come out of trouble. |
| 中文 | 恶人嘴中的过错，是自己的网罗。但义人必脱离患难。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A man shall be satisfied with good by the fruit of his mouth: and the recompence of a man's hands shall be rendered unto him. |
| 中文 | 人因口所结的果子，必饱得美福。人手所作的，必为自己的报应。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The way of a fool is right in his own eyes: but he that hearkeneth unto counsel is wise. |
| 中文 | 愚妄人所行的，在自己的眼中看为正直。惟智慧人，肯听人的劝教。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A fool's wrath is presently known: but a prudent man covereth shame. |
| 中文 | 愚妄人的恼怒，立时显露。通达人能忍辱藏羞。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He that speaketh truth sheweth forth righteousness: but a false witness deceit. |
| 中文 | 说出真话的，显明公义。作假见证的，显出诡诈。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | There is that speaketh like the piercings of a sword: but the tongue of the wise is health. |
| 中文 | 说话浮躁的，如刀刺人。智慧人的舌头，却为医人的良药。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The lip of truth shall be established for ever: but a lying tongue is but for a moment. |
| 中文 | 口吐真言，永远坚立。舌说谎话，只存片时。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Deceit is in the heart of them that imagine evil: but to the counsellors of peace is joy. |
| 中文 | 图谋恶事的，心存诡诈。劝人和睦的，便得喜乐。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | There shall no evil happen to the just: but the wicked shall be filled with mischief. |
| 中文 | 义人不遭灾害恶人满受祸患。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Lying lips are abomination to the LORD: but they that deal truly are his delight. |
| 中文 | 说谎言的嘴，为耶和华所憎恶。行事诚实的，为他所喜悦。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A prudent man concealeth knowledge: but the heart of fools proclaimeth foolishness. |
| 中文 | 通达人隐藏知识。愚昧人的心，彰显愚昧。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The hand of the diligent shall bear rule: but the slothful shall be under tribute. |
| 中文 | 殷勤人的手必掌权。懒惰的人必服苦。 |

## 25

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Heaviness in the heart of man maketh it stoop: but a good word maketh it glad. |
| 中文 | 人心忧虑，屈而不伸。一句良言，使心欢乐。 |

## 26

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The righteous is more excellent than his neighbour: but the way of the wicked seduceth them. |
| 中文 | 义人引导他的邻舍。恶人的道，叫人失迷。 |

## 27

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The slothful man roasteth not that which he took in hunting: but the substance of a diligent man is precious. |
| 中文 | 懒惰的人，不烤打猎所得的。殷勤的人，却得宝贵的财物。 |

## 28

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | In the way of righteousness is life: and in the pathway thereof there is no death. |
| 中文 | 在公义的道上有生命。其路之中，并无死亡。 |
