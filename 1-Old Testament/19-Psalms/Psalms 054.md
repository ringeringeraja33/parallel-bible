# Psalms 54

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Save me, O God, by thy name, and judge me by thy strength. |
| 中文 | （西弗人来对扫罗说，大卫岂不是在我们那里藏身吗。那时大卫作这训诲诗，交与伶长调用丝的乐器）。神阿，求你以你的名救我，凭你的大能为我伸冤。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hear my prayer, O God; give ear to the words of my mouth. |
| 中文 | 神阿，求你听我的祷告，留心听我口中的言语。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For strangers are risen up against me, and oppressors seek after my soul: they have not set God before them. Selah. |
| 中文 | 因为外人起来攻击我，强暴人寻索我的命。他们眼中没有神。（细拉） |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Behold, God is mine helper: the Lord is with them that uphold my soul. |
| 中文 | 神是帮助我的，是扶持我命的。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He shall reward evil unto mine enemies: cut them off in thy truth. |
| 中文 | 他要报应我仇敌所行的恶。求你凭你的诚实灭绝他们。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will freely sacrifice unto thee: I will praise thy name, O LORD; for it is good. |
| 中文 | 我要把甘心祭献给你。耶和华阿，我要称赞你的名。这名本为美好。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For he hath delivered me out of all trouble: and mine eye hath seen his desire upon mine enemies. |
| 中文 | 他从一切的急难中，把我救出来。我的眼睛也看见了我仇敌遭报。 |
