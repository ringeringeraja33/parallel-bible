# Psalms 22

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My God, my God, why hast thou forsaken me? why art thou so far from helping me, and from the words of my roaring? |
| 中文 | 我的神，我的神，为什么离弃我。为什么远离不救我，不听我唉哼的言语。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | O my God, I cry in the day time, but thou hearest not; and in the night season, and am not silent. |
| 中文 | 我的神阿，我白日呼求，你不应允。夜间呼求，必不住声。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But thou art holy, O thou that inhabitest the praises of Israel. |
| 中文 | 但你是圣洁的，是用以色列的赞美为宝座的。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Our fathers trusted in thee: they trusted, and thou didst deliver them. |
| 中文 | 我们的祖宗倚靠你。他们倚靠你，你便解救他们。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They cried unto thee, and were delivered: they trusted in thee, and were not confounded. |
| 中文 | 他们哀求你，便蒙解救。他们倚靠你，就不羞愧。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But I am a worm, and no man; a reproach of men, and despised of the people. |
| 中文 | 但我是虫，不是人。被众人羞辱，被百姓藐视。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | All they that see me laugh me to scorn: they shoot out the lip, they shake the head, saying, |
| 中文 | 凡看见我的都嗤笑我。他们撇嘴摇头，说， |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He trusted on the LORD that he would deliver him: let him deliver him, seeing he delighted in him. |
| 中文 | 他把自己交托耶和华，耶和华可以救他吧。耶和华既喜悦他，可以搭救他吧。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But thou art he that took me out of the womb: thou didst make me hope when I was upon my mother's breasts. |
| 中文 | 但你是叫我出母腹的。我在母怀里，你就使我有倚靠的心。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I was cast upon thee from the womb: thou art my God from my mother's belly. |
| 中文 | 我自出母胎就被交在你手里。从我母亲生我，你就是我的神。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Be not far from me; for trouble is near; for there is none to help. |
| 中文 | 求你不要远离我。因为急难临近了，没有人帮助我。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Many bulls have compassed me: strong bulls of Bashan have beset me round. |
| 中文 | 有许多公牛围绕我。巴珊大力的公牛四面困住我。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They gaped upon me with their mouths, as a ravening and a roaring lion. |
| 中文 | 他们向我张口，好像抓撕吼叫的狮子。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I am poured out like water, and all my bones are out of joint: my heart is like wax; it is melted in the midst of my bowels. |
| 中文 | 我如水被倒出来。我的骨头都脱了节。我心在我里面如腊熔化。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My strength is dried up like a potsherd; and my tongue cleaveth to my jaws; and thou hast brought me into the dust of death. |
| 中文 | 我的精力枯乾，如同瓦片。我的舌头贴在我牙床上。你将我安置在死地的尘土中。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For dogs have compassed me: the assembly of the wicked have inclosed me: they pierced my hands and my feet. |
| 中文 | 犬类围着我。恶党环绕我。他们扎了我的手，我的脚。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I may tell all my bones: they look and stare upon me. |
| 中文 | 我的骨头，我都能数过。他们瞪着眼看我。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They part my garments among them, and cast lots upon my vesture. |
| 中文 | 他们分我的外衣，为我的里衣拈阄。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | But be not thou far from me, O LORD: O my strength, haste thee to help me. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，求你不要远离我。我的救主阿，求你快来帮助我。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Deliver my soul from the sword; my darling from the power of the dog. |
| 中文 | 求你救我的灵魂脱离刀剑，救我的生命脱离犬类。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Save me from the lion's mouth: for thou hast heard me from the horns of the unicorns. |
| 中文 | 救我脱离狮子的口。你已经应允我，使我脱离野牛的角。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will declare thy name unto my brethren: in the midst of the congregation will I praise thee. |
| 中文 | 我要将你的名传与我的弟兄。在会中我要赞美你。 |

## 23

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Ye that fear the LORD, praise him; all ye the seed of Jacob, glorify him; and fear him, all ye the seed of Israel. |
| 中文 | 你们敬畏耶和华的人，要赞美他。雅各的后裔，都要荣耀他。以色列的后裔，都要惧怕他。 |

## 24

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For he hath not despised nor abhorred the affliction of the afflicted; neither hath he hid his face from him; but when he cried unto him, he heard. |
| 中文 | 因为他没有藐视憎恶受苦的人。也没有向他掩面。那受苦之人呼吁的时候，他就垂听。 |

## 25

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My praise shall be of thee in the great congregation: I will pay my vows before them that fear him. |
| 中文 | 我在大会中赞美你的话，是从你而来的。我要在敬畏耶和华的人面前还我的愿。 |

## 26

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The meek shall eat and be satisfied: they shall praise the LORD that seek him: your heart shall live for ever. |
| 中文 | 谦卑的人必吃得饱足。寻求耶和华的人必赞美他。愿你们的心永远活着。 |

## 27

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | All the ends of the world shall remember and turn unto the LORD: and all the kindreds of the nations shall worship before thee. |
| 中文 | 地的四极，都要想念耶和华，并且归顺他。列国的万族，都要在你面前敬拜。 |

## 28

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the kingdom is the LORD's: and he is the governor among the nations. |
| 中文 | 因为国权是耶和华的。他是管理万国的。 |

## 29

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | All they that be fat upon earth shall eat and worship: all they that go down to the dust shall bow before him: and none can keep alive his own soul. |
| 中文 | 地上一切丰肥的人，必吃喝而敬拜。凡下到尘土中不能存活自己性命的人，都要在他面前下拜。 |

## 30

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | A seed shall serve him; it shall be accounted to the Lord for a generation. |
| 中文 | 他必有后裔事奉他。主所行的事，必传与后代。 |

## 31

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They shall come, and shall declare his righteousness unto a people that shall be born, that he hath done this. |
| 中文 | 他们必来把他的公义传给将要生的民，言明这事是他所行的。 |
