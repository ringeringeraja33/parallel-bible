# Job 37

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | At this also my heart trembleth, and is moved out of his place. |
| 中文 | 因此我心战兢，从原处移动。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hear attentively the noise of his voice, and the sound that goeth out of his mouth. |
| 中文 | 听阿，神轰轰的声音，是他口中所发的响声。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He directeth it under the whole heaven, and his lightning unto the ends of the earth. |
| 中文 | 他发响声震遍天下，发电光闪到地极。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | After it a voice roareth: he thundereth with the voice of his excellency; and he will not stay them when his voice is heard. |
| 中文 | 随后人听见有雷声轰轰，大发威严，雷电接连不断。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | God thundereth marvellously with his voice; great things doeth he, which we cannot comprehend. |
| 中文 | 神发出奇妙的雷声，他行大事，我们不能测透。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For he saith to the snow, Be thou on the earth; likewise to the small rain, and to the great rain of his strength. |
| 中文 | 他对雪说，要降在地上，对大雨和暴雨也是这样说。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He sealeth up the hand of every man; that all men may know his work. |
| 中文 | 他封住各人的手，叫所造的万人，都晓得他的作为。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Then the beasts go into dens, and remain in their places. |
| 中文 | 百兽进入穴中，卧在洞内。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Out of the south cometh the whirlwind: and cold out of the north. |
| 中文 | 暴风出于南宫。寒冷出于北方。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | By the breath of God frost is given: and the breadth of the waters is straitened. |
| 中文 | 神嘘气成冰。宽阔之水也都凝结。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Also by watering he wearieth the thick cloud: he scattereth his bright cloud: |
| 中文 | 他使密云盛满水气，布散电光之云。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And it is turned round about by his counsels: that they may do whatsoever he commandeth them upon the face of the world in the earth. |
| 中文 | 这云是藉他的指引游行旋转，得以在全地面上，行他一切所吩咐的， |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He causeth it to come, whether for correction, or for his land, or for mercy. |
| 中文 | 或为责罚，或为润地，或为施行慈爱。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Hearken unto this, O Job: stand still, and consider the wondrous works of God. |
| 中文 | 约伯阿，你要留心听，要站立思想神奇妙的作为。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 神如何吩咐这些，如何使云中的电光照耀，你知道吗。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Dost thou know the balancings of the clouds, the wondrous works of him which is perfect in knowledge? |
| 中文 | 云彩如何浮于空中，那知识全备者奇妙的作为，你知道吗。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | How thy garments are warm, when he quieteth the earth by the south wind? |
| 中文 | 南风使地寂静，你的衣服就如火热，你知道吗。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 你岂能与神同铺穹苍吗。这穹苍坚硬，如同铸成的镜子。 |

## 19

| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 我们愚昧不能陈说。请你指教我们该对他说什么话。 |

| 中文 | 人岂可说，我愿与他说话。岂有人自愿灭亡吗。 |

| 中文 | 现在有云遮蔽，人不得见穹苍的光亮。但风吹过，天又发晴。 |

| 中文 | 金光出于北方，在神那里有可怕的威严。 |
