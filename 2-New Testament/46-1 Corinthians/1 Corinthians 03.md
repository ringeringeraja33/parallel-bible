# 1 Corinthians 3 / 哥林多前书 3

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And I, brethren, could not speak unto you as unto spiritual, but as unto carnal, even as unto babes in Christ. |
| 中文 | 弟兄们，我从前对你们说话，不能把你们当作属灵的，只得把你们当作属肉体，在基督里为婴孩的。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I have fed you with milk, and not with meat: for hitherto ye were not able to bear it, neither yet now are ye able. |
| 中文 | 我是用奶喂你们，没有用饭喂你们。那时你们不能吃，就是如今还是不能。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For ye are yet carnal: for whereas there is among you envying, and strife, and divisions, are ye not carnal, and walk as men? |
| 中文 | 你们仍是属肉体的。因为在你们中间有嫉妒分争，这岂不是属乎肉体，照着世人的样子行吗。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For while one saith, I am of Paul; and another, I am of Apollos; are ye not carnal? |
| 中文 | 有说，我是属保罗的。有说，我是属亚波罗的。这岂不是你们和世人一样吗。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Who then is Paul, and who is Apollos, but ministers by whom ye believed, even as the Lord gave to every man? |
| 中文 | 亚波罗算什么。保罗算什么。无非是执事，照主所赐给他们各人的，引导你们相信。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I have planted, Apollos watered; but God gave the increase. |
| 中文 | 我栽种了，亚波罗浇灌了。惟有神叫他生长。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | So then neither is he that planteth any thing, neither he that watereth; but God that giveth the increase. |
| 中文 | 可见栽种的算不得什么，浇灌的也算不得什么。只在那叫他生长的神。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Now he that planteth and he that watereth are one: and every man shall receive his own reward according to his own labour. |
| 中文 | 栽种的和浇灌的都是一样。但将来各人要照自己的工夫，得自己的赏赐。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For we are labourers together with God: ye are God's husbandry, ye are God's building. |
| 中文 | 因为我们是与神同工的。你们是神所耕种的田地，所建造的房屋。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | According to the grace of God which is given unto me, as a wise masterbuilder, I have laid the foundation, and another buildeth thereon. But let every man take heed how he buildeth thereupon. |
| 中文 | 我照神所给我的恩，好像一个聪明的工头，立好了根基，有别人在上面建造。只是各人要谨慎怎样在上面建造。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For other foundation can no man lay than that is laid, which is Jesus Christ. |
| 中文 | 因为那已经立好的根基，就是耶稣基督，此外没有人能立别的根基。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Now if any man build upon this foundation gold, silver, precious stones, wood, hay, stubble; |
| 中文 | 若有人用金，银，宝石，草木，禾楷，在这根基上建造。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Every man's work shall be made manifest: for the day shall declare it, because it shall be revealed by fire; and the fire shall try every man's work of what sort it is. |
| 中文 | 各人的工程必然显露。因为那日子要将他表明出来，有火发现。这火要试验各人的工程怎样。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If any man's work abide which he hath built thereupon, he shall receive a reward. |
| 中文 | 人在那根基上所建造的工程，若存得住，他就要得赏赐。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If any man's work shall be burned, he shall suffer loss: but he himself shall be saved; yet so as by fire. |
| 中文 | 人的工程若被烧了，他就要受亏损。自己却要得救。虽然得救乃像从火里经过的一样。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Know ye not that ye are the temple of God, and that the Spirit of God dwelleth in you? |
| 中文 | 岂不知你们是神的殿，神的灵住在你们里头吗。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | If any man defile the temple of God, him shall God destroy; for the temple of God is holy, which temple ye are. |
| 中文 | 若有人毁坏神的殿，神必要毁坏那人。因为神的殿是圣的，这殿就是你们。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Let no man deceive himself. If any man among you seemeth to be wise in this world, let him become a fool, that he may be wise. |
| 中文 | 人不可自欺。你们中间若有人，在这世界自以为有智慧，倒不如变作愚拙，好成为有智慧的。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | For the wisdom of this world is foolishness with God. For it is written, He taketh the wise in their own craftiness. |
| 中文 | 因这世界的智慧，在神看是愚拙。如经上记着说，主叫有智慧的中了自己的诡计。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And again, The Lord knoweth the thoughts of the wise, that they are vain. |
| 中文 | 又说，主知道智慧人的意念是虚妄的。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Therefore let no man glory in men. For all things are yours; |
| 中文 | 所以无论谁，都不可拿人夸口。因为万有全是你们的。 |

## 22

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 或保罗，或亚波罗，或矶法，或世界，或生，或死，或现今的事，或将来的事，全是你们的。 |

## 23

| --- | --- |

| 中文 | 并且你们是属基督的。基督又是属神的。 |
