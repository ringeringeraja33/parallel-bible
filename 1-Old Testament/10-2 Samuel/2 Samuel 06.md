# 2 Samuel 6

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Again, David gathered together all the chosen men of Israel, thirty thousand. |
| 中文 | 大卫又聚集以色列中所有挑选的人三万。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And David arose, and went with all the people that were with him from Baale of Judah, to bring up from thence the ark of God, whose name is called by the name of the LORD of hosts that dwelleth between the cherubims. |
| 中文 | 大卫起身，率领跟随他的众人前往，要从巴拉犹大将神的约柜运来。这约柜就是坐在二基路伯上万军之耶和华留名的约柜。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And they set the ark of God upon a new cart, and brought it out of the house of Abinadab that was in Gibeah: and Uzzah and Ahio, the sons of Abinadab, drave the new cart. |
| 中文 | 他们将神的约柜从冈上亚比拿达的家里抬出来，放在新车上。亚比拿达的两个儿子乌撒和亚希约赶这新车。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And they brought it out of the house of Abinadab which was at Gibeah, accompanying the ark of God: and Ahio went before the ark. |
| 中文 | 他们将神的约柜从冈上亚比拿达家里抬出来的时候，亚希约在柜前行走。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And David and all the house of Israel played before the LORD on all manner of instruments made of fir wood, even on harps, and on psalteries, and on timbrels, and on cornets, and on cymbals. |
| 中文 | 大卫和以色列的全家在耶和华面前，用松木制造的各样乐器和琴，瑟，鼓，钹，锣，作乐跳舞。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And when they came to Nachon's threshingfloor, Uzzah put forth his hand to the ark of God, and took hold of it; for the oxen shook it. |
| 中文 | 到了拿艮的禾场，因为牛失前蹄（或作惊跳），乌撒就伸手扶住神的约柜。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the anger of the LORD was kindled against Uzzah; and God smote him there for his error; and there he died by the ark of God. |
| 中文 | 神耶和华向乌撒发怒，因这错误击杀他，他就死在神的约柜旁。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And David was displeased, because the LORD had made a breach upon Uzzah: and he called the name of the place Perezuzzah to this day. |
| 中文 | 大卫因耶和华击杀（原文作闯杀）乌撒，心里愁烦，就称那地方为毗列斯乌撒，直到今日。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And David was afraid of the LORD that day, and said, How shall the ark of the LORD come to me? |
| 中文 | 那日，大卫惧怕耶和华，说，耶和华的约柜怎可运到我这里来。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | So David would not remove the ark of the LORD unto him into the city of David: but David carried it aside into the house of Obededom the Gittite. |
| 中文 | 于是大卫不肯将耶和华的约柜运进大卫的城，却运到迦特人俄别以东的家中。 |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And the ark of the LORD continued in the house of Obededom the Gittite three months: and the LORD blessed Obededom, and all his household. |
| 中文 | 耶和华的约柜在迦特人俄别以东家中三个月。耶和华赐福给俄别以东和他的全家。 |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And it was told king David, saying, The LORD hath blessed the house of Obededom, and all that pertaineth unto him, because of the ark of God. So David went and brought up the ark of God from the house of Obededom into the city of David with gladness. |
| 中文 | 有人告诉大卫王说，耶和华因为约柜赐福给俄别以东的家和一切属他的。大卫就去，欢欢喜喜地将神的约柜从俄别以东家中抬到大卫的城里。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |

## 16
