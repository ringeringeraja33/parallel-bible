# Psalms 78 / 诗篇 78

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Give ear, O my people, to my law: incline your ears to the words of my mouth. |
| 中文 | （亚萨的训诲诗）我的民哪，你门要留心听我的训诲，侧耳听我口中的话。 |

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |

| --- | --- |

| --- | --- |
