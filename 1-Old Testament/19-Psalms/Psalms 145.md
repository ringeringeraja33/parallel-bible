# Psalms 145 / 诗篇 145

## 1

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will extol thee, my God, O king; and I will bless thy name for ever and ever. |
| 中文 | （大卫的赞美诗）我的神我的王阿，我要尊崇你。我要永永远远称颂你的名。 |

## 2

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Every day will I bless thee; and I will praise thy name for ever and ever. |
| 中文 | 我要天天称颂你，也要永永远远赞美你的名。 |

## 3

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Great is the LORD, and greatly to be praised; and his greatness is unsearchable. |
| 中文 | 耶和华本为大，该受大赞美。其大无法测度。 |

## 4

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | One generation shall praise thy works to another, and shall declare thy mighty acts. |
| 中文 | 这代要对那代颂赞你的作为，也要传扬你的大能。 |

## 5

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | I will speak of the glorious honour of thy majesty, and of thy wondrous works. |
| 中文 | 我要默念你威严的尊荣，和你奇妙的作为。 |

## 6

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | And men shall speak of the might of thy terrible acts: and I will declare thy greatness. |
| 中文 | 人要传说你可畏之事的能力。我也要传扬你的大德。 |

## 7

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They shall abundantly utter the memory of thy great goodness, and shall sing of thy righteousness. |
| 中文 | 他们记念你的大恩，就要传出来，并要歌唱你的公义。 |

## 8

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD is gracious, and full of compassion; slow to anger, and of great mercy. |
| 中文 | 耶和华有恩惠，有怜悯，不轻易发怒，大有慈爱。 |

## 9

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD is good to all: and his tender mercies are over all his works. |
| 中文 | 耶和华善待万民，他的慈悲，覆庇他一切所造的。 |

## 10

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | All thy works shall praise thee, O LORD; and thy saints shall bless thee. |
| 中文 | 耶和华阿，你一切所造，的都要称谢你。你的圣民也要称颂你， |

## 11

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | They shall speak of the glory of thy kingdom, and talk of thy power; |
| 中文 | 传说你国的荣耀，谈论你的大能， |

## 12

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | To make known to the sons of men his mighty acts, and the glorious majesty of his kingdom. |
| 中文 | 好叫世人知道你大能的作为，并你国度威严的荣耀。 |

## 13

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thy kingdom is an everlasting kingdom, and thy dominion endureth throughout all generations. |
| 中文 | 你的国是永远的国，你执掌的权柄，存到万代。 |

## 14

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD upholdeth all that fall, and raiseth up all those that be bowed down. |
| 中文 | 凡跌倒的，耶和华将他们扶持。凡被压下的，将他们扶起。 |

## 15

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The eyes of all wait upon thee; and thou givest them their meat in due season. |
| 中文 | 万民都举目仰望你。你随时给他们食物。 |

## 16

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | Thou openest thine hand, and satisfiest the desire of every living thing. |
| 中文 | 你张手，使有生气的都随愿饱足。 |

## 17

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD is righteous in all his ways, and holy in all his works. |
| 中文 | 耶和华在他一切所行的，无不公义。在他一切所做的，都有慈爱。 |

## 18

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD is nigh unto all them that call upon him, to all that call upon him in truth. |
| 中文 | 凡求告耶和华的，就是诚心求告他的，耶和华便与他们相近。 |

## 19

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | He will fulfil the desire of them that fear him: he also will hear their cry, and will save them. |
| 中文 | 敬畏他的，他必成就他们的心愿。也必听他们的呼求，拯救他们。 |

## 20

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | The LORD preserveth all them that love him: but all the wicked will he destroy. |
| 中文 | 耶和华保护一切爱他的人，却要灭绝一切的恶人。 |

## 21

| Language | Content |
| --- | --- |
| English | My mouth shall speak the praise of the LORD: and let all flesh bless his holy name for ever and ever. |
| 中文 | 我的口要说出赞美耶和华的话。惟愿凡有血气的，都永永远远称颂他的圣名。 |
